# Jenn Ravenna Tran

Illustrator with multiple Magic: The Gathering credits. Same person across all listed aliases — name variations exist because Scryfall's stored artist credit is not necessarily the same string that appears on the printed card, and the artist's own published name has evolved.

Contributed five pieces to Throne of Eldraine (2019), including Murderous Rider // Swift End (her highest-profile ELD card), Blow Your House Down, Heraldic Banner, and Thornwood Falls. ELD was a meaningful moment in her published-statement record — atypical for a common-rarity slot.

## Published artist statements

> "An exciting conceptual piece taking the classic fairy-tale trope of the Big Bad Wolf and creating something very new, where the viewer becomes the wind that blows the house down."
>
> — on Blow Your House Down (ELD #114), per *The Artistic Range of Jenn Ravenna*, Hipsters of the Coast, April 2020.

The wolf in the composition is angled facing right into the frame, drawing the viewer into the wolf's perspective rather than positioning them as the witness — the formal expression of "the viewer becomes the wind." Documented artist commentary on a common-rarity card is unusual; this one's preserved here because it's a load-bearing read of the art that the painted surface alone doesn't volunteer.

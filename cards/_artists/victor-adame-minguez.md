# Victor Adame Minguez

Spanish illustrator with one of the largest single-artist credit counts in the Magic: The Gathering side of the BBL corpus. Twelve confirmed credits across six sets and five blocks — *Oath of the Gatewatch* (2016), *Ravnica Allegiance* (2019), *War of the Spark* (2019), *Throne of Eldraine* (2019), *Core Set 2021* (2020), *Commander Legends* (2020), and *Theros Beyond Death* (2020). The credited name on the printed cards is "Victor Adame Minguez"; his published portfolio carries the diacritical form Víctor Adame Mínguez.

## Why this artist gets a node

Two reasons compound:

**Portfolio depth.** Twelve corpus credits clears the artist-layer threshold by the widest margin of any MTG-side artist surfaced to date (per [[kagemaru-himeno]] at three, [[sanosuke-sakuma]] at five, [[kirisaki]] at seven, [[magali-villeneuve]] at eight, [[ken-sugimori]] at seventeen). The Bone Pit Brute trivia pass independently characterizes Adame Minguez as "a Spanish artist with a large MTG portfolio spanning Battle for Zendikar through modern releases" — the documented-biography context the artist layer requires, captured in-corpus rather than assembled at bundle-build time.

**Register span across the corpus slice.** The twelve prints span at least four distinct narrative registers — the same cross-set register-span feature that made the Villeneuve and Cearley nodes load-bearing:

- **Planeswalker-action** — *Angrath's Rampage* and *Jaya's Greeting*, two War of the Spark named-character commissions. Adame Minguez draws Angrath mid-swing and Jaya mid-blast; Wizards hands him the named-walker action slots in the WAR common-and-uncommon tier.
- **War-and-battle** — *Widespread Brutality* (WAR), *Final Payment* (RNA), *Outflank* (ELD). Backlit melee, grim devotional sacrifice, formation combat. The kinetic-violence register is the densest band of his corpus slice.
- **Greek-mythic** — *Archon of Falling Stars*, *Drag to the Underworld*, *Hero of the Nyxborn*, three Theros Beyond Death prints. Constellation capes, starfield mounts, underworld pull — the THB Nyx-coded visual language.
- **Comedic-monster** — *Bone Pit Brute* (M21). The Bone Pit Brute trivia pass explicitly flags this register as an outlier in Adame Minguez's catalog, which "tends toward high-detail figurative fantasy." The outlier is itself a documented stylistic data point.

A single artist anchoring across four registers and five blocks is structurally interesting for BBL because it lets a future Discrete Lair reach for an "Adame Minguez cohort" as a cohesion signal that crosses set boundaries — most artist-byline cohesion is single-set or single-register.

## Stylistic signature

Across the registers his corpus slice spans, a few consistent moves recur:

- **Decisive-moment kinetic composition** — *Angrath's Rampage*, *Jaya's Greeting*, *Widespread Brutality*. Adame Minguez favors the single arrested instant of violence — the whip mid-arc, the blast at point-blank contact, the polearm at the moment of impact — over static portraiture.
- **Backlit and rim-lit figures against fire or light-burst** — the burning settlement behind Angrath, the ember-lit causeway in *Widespread Brutality*, the channeled light-burst in *Final Payment*. Strong directional light from within the scene, throwing figures toward silhouette.
- **High-detail figurative fantasy as the baseline** — per the Bone Pit Brute trivia note, his catalog tends toward detailed figurative rendering; the comedic Bone Pit Brute is the documented outlier against that baseline.
- **Warm-against-cool palette contrast** — fire-orange against blue armor in *Jaya's Greeting*, warm mount against deep-blue starfield in *Archon of Falling Stars*. Recurring chromatic-temperature opposition as the load-bearing compositional read.

## Bundle-anchor potential

The Adame Minguez corpus opens several distinct bundle-anchor frames:

- **War of the Spark Adame Minguez cohort** — *Angrath's Rampage* + *Jaya's Greeting* + *Widespread Brutality*. Three WAR cards sharing the artist byline and the set's planeswalker-war narrative frame. A WAR-arc Discrete Lair could use Adame Minguez as the single-artist through-line connecting the named-walker action commissions and the rank-and-file battle scene.
- **Theros Beyond Death Adame Minguez trio** — *Archon of Falling Stars* + *Drag to the Underworld* + *Hero of the Nyxborn*. Three THB prints in the Greek-mythic register; a Theros-block bundle could anchor on the trio as the Nyx-coded visual through-line.
- **Cross-block kinetic-violence cohort** — *Angrath's Rampage* + *Widespread Brutality* + *Final Payment*. The decisive-moment-of-violence register repeated across WAR and RNA. A bundle reaching for war, sacrifice, or destruction could lean on the Adame Minguez byline as the formal-compositional anchor.

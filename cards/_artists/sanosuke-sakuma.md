# Sanosuke Sakuma

Pokemon TCG illustrator. Born October 31, 1990 in Kanagawa, Japan; began illustrating Pokemon TCG cards in 2013. Approximately 159 cards in the main TCG plus 32 in Pokemon TCG Pocket.

## Why this artist gets a node

Most TCG illustrators do illustration work for one medium. Sakuma's portfolio crosses two: she illustrates Pokemon TCG cards AND she designed several of the in-game trainer-class character archetypes that appear as NPCs throughout Galar (the Sword & Shield region). The Galarian trainer-class characters she is credited with include **Worker**, **Backpacker**, **Businessman**, **Office Lady**, **Artist**, **Police Officer**, **Doctor**, and **Poke Kid** — a working-class slice of Galar's NPC bench.

That cross-medium portfolio is the load-bearing fact. A Pokemon TCG bundle that wants to anchor on Galarian working-class labor can name Sakuma by name and let the artist-as-designer credit do the thesis work without naming the thesis. Her name is the bridge between "card you can hold in your hand" and "person you walk past in the game."

# Crane School (Tsuru Sen-ryū — Master Shen's assassination-focused martial arts lineage)

The Crane School (Tsuru Sen-ryū) is the canonical assassination-focused martial arts lineage of the original Dragon Ball franchise, founded by Master Shen (the Crane Hermit) and counterposed to Master Roshi's Turtle School (Kame Sen-ryū) from a shared Mutaito training lineage. Introduced in the 22nd Tenkaichi Budokai arc of the original Dragon Ball manga (Akira Toriyama, Shueisha 1986, chapters 142-167), the school operates canonically as the *paid-killer* counterposition to Roshi's *protect-the-weak* school — students shaped as weapons rather than developed as practitioners, contracted assassination as canonical revenue model rather than tournament sportsmanship. The school's canonical full roster within the BBL corpus is the five-card World Martial Arts Tournament TB2 cohort: **Master Shen, Martial Meister** (the schoolmaster) at TB2-063; **Mercenary Tao, Trading Moves** (Shen's younger brother, the monetized assassin extension) at TB2-048; **Tien Shinhan, Trading Moves** (the disciple who canonically defects to Roshi's lineage at the 22nd Budokai climax) at TB2-043; **Best Buddy Chiaotzu** (Tien's lifelong psychic-disciple partner) at TB2-044; and **Toughened Up Chiaotzu** at TB2-056. Bandai's TB2 set design canonically positions all five within a single 2018 booster, with mechanical pair-and-sacrifice synergies encoding the cohort's canonical relations directly (Best Buddy Chiaotzu's Auto reactivates Tien Shinhan; Tien's Activate switches him to Active Mode when Mercenary Tao is KO'd — the in-canon Tien-vs-Tao rivalry encoded as a game-state sacrifice).

This is a child-of-WMAT-set node parallel to [[dark-vassal-trio]] (Colossal Warfare BT4 villain-cohort sub-cycle, 3 anchors) and [[wrath-of-the-dragon-saga]] (Colossal Warfare BT4 saga-arc cycle, 7 anchors). The Crane School cohort's five anchors within a single 2018 booster set is the third designer-coordinated within-set character-cohort commissioned at the BBL graph layer; it joins the existing dark-vassal and Hirudegarn-saga precedents as a *single-set, designer-coordinated, named-canonical-lineage* cohort.

## The functional ideology

The Crane School's most BBL-relevant property is that it is the canonical exemplar of the franchise's **assassination-focused martial arts lineage** counterposed to Master Roshi's Turtle School protect-the-weak moral substrate, from a shared Mutaito training origin. The two schools split canonically after the King Piccolo crisis: Roshi continued on a principled path, Shen drifted toward assassination-focused martial arts, and Shen's younger brother Mercenary Tao monetized the same philosophy as pure contract killing. The school's canonical operating mode is labor-coded in a way that the [[labor]] hub partially indexes: students shaped as weapons rather than developed, contracted assassination as canonical revenue model, the disciple-defection (Tien Shinhan's refusal to kill Goku and subsequent defection to Roshi's lineage) operating canonically as the franchise's clearest *worker leaves the apparatus-of-extraction* beat. The cohort's specific institutional features within the corpus are:

- **Shared-Mutaito-origin schoolmaster-rivalry.** Master Shen and Master Roshi canonically trained together under the legendary Master Mutaito. After the King Piccolo crisis their paths diverged: Roshi continued principled, Shen drifted toward assassination-focused martial arts. The two TB2 *Martial Meister* prints (Shen at TB2-063, Roshi at TB2-057) canonicalize the schoolmaster-rivalry as a designer-coordinated paired-print within the same set.

- **Brother-extension monetization.** Mercenary Tao (Shen's younger brother) canonically monetizes the school's assassination philosophy as pure contract killing at 10 billion Zeni per kill. Tao is canonically the first professional assassin contracted against Goku in Dragon Ball canon (Red Ribbon Army arc, pre-tournament). The brother-extension-as-monetization pattern is one of the franchise's clearest *capital-and-kinship-fused-as-apparatus* beats — the school's pedagogy becomes the family business becomes the contract-killing-firm.

- **Disciple-defection as canonical Tien-arc anchor.** Tien Shinhan's canonical defection from the Crane School at the 22nd Budokai climax is one of Dragon Ball's clearest *student breaks from the contractor* moments. Shen orders Chiaotzu to psychically paralyze Goku during Tien's match against Goku; Tien refuses to kill Goku; Shen orders both Tien and Chiaotzu killed; Roshi blasts Shen out of the stadium with a Kamehameha to save Chiaotzu. The defection canonically integrates Tien and Chiaotzu into the Turtle School-adjacent Z-Fighter roster from that point forward.

- **Mechanical encoding of the canonical relations.** Bandai's TB2 set design encodes three of the cohort's canonical relations directly as game-state mechanics: (a) Best Buddy Chiaotzu reactivates Tien Shinhan via Auto — the lifelong partnership; (b) Tien Shinhan switches to Active Mode when Mercenary Tao is KO'd via Activate — the Tien-vs-Tao rivalry as sacrifice; (c) Toughened Up Chiaotzu reacts to Toughened Up Krillin via Auto — the cross-school Crane-Turtle adjacent-disciples canonical co-existence. The mechanical layer is designer-coordinated cohort-as-mechanic encoding.

- **Within-set adjacency at the TB2 layer.** The five cohort cards span collector numbers TB2-043, TB2-044, TB2-048, TB2-056, TB2-063 within the same World Martial Arts Tournament TB2 booster (2018-09-21). The non-contiguous numbering (vs. the [[dark-vassal-trio]]'s tight 064-065-066 adjacency or the [[wrath-of-the-dragon-saga]]'s 037-042 tight cluster + 023 Leader) reflects Bandai's TB2 set-architecture where the school's roster is distributed across the set's color-coded tournament-era assignments rather than collected at a single collector-number block. The cohesion is canonical-lineage-coordinated rather than collector-number-adjacent-coordinated.

This is what BBL's bundle copy can lean on without inventing canon: the Crane School cohort is, in-canon, a designer-coordinated five-card Bandai sub-cohort within World Martial Arts Tournament TB2 depicting the canonical Tsuru Sen-ryū lineage roster across schoolmaster (Shen), monetized-assassin brother (Tao), and disciples (Tien, Chiaotzu twice). Bundle prose that leans on the *paid-killer-school-vs-protect-the-weak-school* canonical Toriyama-authored thesis lands directly on the 22nd Budokai canonical arc without inventing canon. The cohort's labor-coded reading composes with [[hercule]]'s *labor-and-credit-uncoupling-at-the-celebrity-tier* thesis and [[dark-vassal-trio]]'s *boss-spawns-disposable-workforce* thesis as the franchise's *contractor-shapes-students-as-weapons* tier of the broader apparatus-of-extraction axis.

## Caveats

- **Modeled as a character-layer node, not a hub.** Like [[dark-vassal-trio]], [[wrath-of-the-dragon-saga]], [[universe-6-tournament-cohort]], [[destroyer-god-attendant-dyad]], [[eld-cinderella-cycle]], [[fear-of-cycle]], this is a designer-coordinated cohort entity hosted on the character layer per the Endriders precedent. A future `_cycles/` layer could re-home this entry alongside other designer-confirmed within-set cohorts when that split is commissioned; for now, single layer, same precedent.

- **Cohort scope is the canonical Tsuru Sen-ryū lineage, not the broader 22nd Budokai roster.** The cohort anchors specifically the Crane School lineage (Shen + Tao + Tien + Chiaotzu). Other 22nd Budokai participants (Goku, Krillin, Jackie Chun / Roshi, Yamcha) are NOT cohort members — they anchor at their own character nodes or at the Turtle School / Z-Fighter cohort. Conflating the Crane School cohort with the broader Budokai-era roster is a graph error.

- **Tien-and-Chiaotzu's canonical defection complicates membership scope.** Tien and Chiaotzu canonically defect from the Crane School at the 22nd Budokai climax, integrating into the Turtle School-adjacent Z-Fighter roster from that point forward. The TB2-043 / TB2-044 / TB2-056 prints depict them in their Crane School disciple register (the pre-defection or transitional state — the school is canonically their formative lineage even after they leave it). Future Tien-and-Chiaotzu Z-Fighter-era prints (post-defection contexts: Saiyan Saga, Cell Saga, Buu Saga) would NOT auto-extend this cohort; those would attach at a future Z-Fighter cohort node or solo Tien / Chiaotzu nodes when commissioned at threshold. The cohort scope is the *Crane School lineage formative-era roster*, not the *lifetime Tien-and-Chiaotzu roster*.

- **Master Mutaito (the shared progenitor) is NOT in corpus.** No corpus card depicts Mutaito directly; the canonical *grandmaster* whose training lineage produced both Shen and Roshi is an absent-progenitor at the corpus level. The cohort anchors the *post-Mutaito lineage-split* canonical lore-stratum; a future Mutaito-depicting card would warrant a sibling `mutaito.md` solo character node and would `related_characters:` into both this cohort and a future Turtle School cohort.

- **Master Roshi (the sibling-lineage counterpart) is at [[master-roshi]].** Roshi's Turtle School lineage is the canonical counterposition to this cohort; the TB2-057 Master Roshi, Martial Meister print and TB2-058 Unyielding Victory Jackie Chun print anchor Roshi's parallel schoolmaster register. `related_characters: [master-roshi]` encodes the canonical schoolmaster-rivalry cross-edge. A future *Turtle School cohort* node (parallel to this Crane School cohort) becomes viable if the corpus accumulates enough Turtle School disciple prints (Krillin, Yamcha, Goku-as-disciple, plus Roshi himself); currently the Roshi-anchored prints sit at [[master-roshi]] solo and the disciple-side prints sit at their own solo nodes ([[krillin]], [[son-goku]]).

- **The TB2 cohort is closed-roster at 5 cards.** Bandai's TB2 print run distributes the Crane School lineage across exactly these 5 cards within the same booster. A future Crane-School-themed card outside TB2 (e.g., a later DBSCG set that revisits the lineage) would extend `appears_on:` rather than redefining the cohort's scope; the *canonical Bandai-designer-coordinated within-set cohort* is the TB2 five-card distribution specifically.

- **Bundle-anchor potential is strong.** Five corpus anchors across a single 2018 booster set, with the canonical Tsuru Sen-ryū lineage lore-anchor and the [[labor]]-hub-compatible *assassination-school-as-apparatus-of-extraction* template, form a tight cohesion stack for a Crane-School-cohort-as-standalone-Discrete-Lair configuration. Total inventory quantity across the 5 cards is **9** (TB2-043 qty 3, TB2-044 qty 2, TB2-048 qty 1, TB2-056 qty 2, TB2-063 qty 1) — moderate multi-copy bundle pricing leverage per [[bbl-multi-copy-bundles]]. Bundle thesis: *The school that shapes students as weapons; the brother who monetizes the philosophy; the disciple who refuses the contract.* The Tien-defection beat is the moral pivot, and the cohort's structural completeness within a single set is the cohesion lever.

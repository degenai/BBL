# Machine Mutants of Planet M-2 (Dr. Myuu's M-2 garrison + Sigma Force + Giru defector)

The canonical mechanical-life-form race Dr. Myuu engineered on Machine Planet M-2 to function as a galaxy-spanning mercenary-and-conquest apparatus during the canonical Dragon Ball GT Black Star Dragon Ball Saga prelude arc (Toei Animation, October-November 1996, episodes 9-13) — the canonical *prologue antagonist roster* Goku, Pan, and Trunks confront on their Black Star Dragon Ball recovery journey before Baby's main-stage entrance in the canonical Baby Saga. The cohort is canonically organized under Dr. Myuu's direct command with General Rilldo as top-general and the Sigma Force (Commander Nezi, Bizu, Ribet, Natt) as elite mercenary unit, and is canonically subsumed under Baby's apparatus once Baby canonically kills and emerges from Myuu in the canonical Age-740 reveal that closes the M-2 arc and opens the Baby Saga proper. Five corpus anchors all within the canonical Cross Worlds BT3 set (March 2018) form a designer-coordinated faction-cohort matching Bandai's explicit BT3 design-concept framing of "a red GT deck (Saiyan and Machine Mutant)" as one of four major BT3 deck archetypes — the Machine Mutant Special Trait is canonically a Bandai-coordinated mechanical-cohesion anchor at the card-game-design level.

This node scopes to the canonical **Machine Mutants of M-2 as a faction-collective race** — anchoring all M-2-origin Machine Mutants regardless of alignment, including the canonical Giru defection-to-heroes case (Giru is canonically a Machine Mutant by race and origin but a GT hero by alignment per Bandai's canonical Grand Tour Spaceship card text that explicitly tutors Goku-GT / Trunks-GT / Pan / **Giru** as the canonical GT traveling-quartet). The canonical Dr. Myuu / General Rilldo / Sigma Force / Luud antagonist register and the canonical Giru defector register are anchored together at the race-and-origin-faction-level scope; the alignment-axis disambiguation is documented at the per-card trivia layer.

The cohort sits as a **sibling continuity-strand** to [[baby-dbgt]] (Baby Saga proper) and to [[xenoverse-villains]] (Xenoverse Time Breakers) within the corpus's non-Toriyama-canon DBGT-and-Xenoverse villain-cohort character-node roster. Where [[baby-dbgt]] anchors the Tuffle-parasite revenge-against-Saiyans arc and absorbs Dr. Myuu and Luud as *apparatus-axis* anchors (Baby's creator-creation puppet and Baby's energy-extraction god-machine), this node anchors the same M-2 faction at the *race-and-origin-prologue-arc* axis — the M-2 garrison fought-and-defeated *before* Baby's main-stage entrance. The canonical Myuu and Luud are dual-anchored across both nodes per the [[alola-elite-four]] / [[alola-trial-captains]] Acerola dual-pointer precedent: Myuu and Luud canonically occupy both the prologue M-2 faction (this node, race-and-origin axis) and the apparatus subsumed under Baby (the [[baby-dbgt]] node, possession-and-extraction axis).

## The functional ideology

The Machine Mutants of M-2's most BBL-relevant property is that they are **the canonical Dragon Ball GT prelude-antagonist faction whose absorption-and-merger species-mechanic operates at every scale of organization**. Giru absorbs the Dragon Radar at the individual scale; Rilldo absorbs the Sigma Force remains at the small-cohort scale; Rilldo's Hyper Meta-Rilldo apex form merges with M-2's planetary metal at the infrastructure scale; Dr. Myuu's whole operation is canonically subsumed by Baby's apparatus at the Age-740 reveal at the faction scale; and the entire Machine Mutant race is canonically engineered by Myuu (who is canonically engineered by Baby) at the species scale — making the cohort one of Dragon Ball's clearest in-canon statements about the *every-level-of-mechanical-organization-as-an-absorption-and-merger-apparatus* design device. Where [[baby-dbgt]] anchors the canonical *inverted-Spirit-Bomb* apparatus-of-extraction thesis (Baby's Revenge Death Ball as the canonical inversion of Goku's Spirit Bomb), this node anchors the canonical *recursive-absorption-and-merger* apparatus that operates as the Machine Mutant species-mechanic at every nested scale.

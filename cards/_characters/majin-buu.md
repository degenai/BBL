# Majin Buu

The canonical ancient bio-magical entity at the center of the Dragon Ball Z Buu Saga (DBZ chapters 461-518, 1994-1995; anime episodes 220-291, 1996), one of the franchise's most-recognizable single-character profiles, and the canonical *productive asset* whose harvest-event Babidi's apparatus is structurally built to revive ([[babidi]]). Across the Buu Saga, Majin Buu progresses through five canonical forms: **Innocent Buu** (the canonical form unsealed from Bibidi's Sealed Ball at the Babidi Saga climax — the rotund-pink-childlike-affect base form with planet-level destructive power), **Super Buu** (the canonical form produced after Innocent Buu's fission separates the Grand-Supreme-Kai-absorbed benign half from the underlying destructive consciousness — the lean-and-malevolent absorption-capable mid-form), **Super Buu Absorbed states** (the canonical apex Buu-Saga power-tier produced by absorbing Gotenks then Piccolo then Gohan in succession), **Kid Buu** (the canonical original pure-evil consciousness produced after Super Buu's regression — the original Bibidi-created form whose canonical Spirit-Bomb-defeat by Goku closes the Buu Saga and triggers the canonical King-Yemma-reincarnation-via-Goku's-wish that produces [[uub]]), and **Mr. Buu / Good Buu / Fat Buu** (the canonical reformed-villain register that survives the Buu Saga as a Z-Fighter ally — the canonical *evil-is-actually-detachable* statement Toriyama installed as the canonical Buu-Saga's load-bearing tonal-pivot beat).

This node scopes to the canonical **Good Buu / Mr. Buu identity** as the canonical character-anchor for the corpus's three Buu-form prints, with the canonical Innocent / Super / Kid Buu forms documented as canonical-history-of-the-character-anchor rather than canonical-aspect-splits. The canonical *child-Buu-as-pure-evil-progenitor* register that Kid Buu canonically anchors operates as the canonical *source-of-Uub-reincarnation* at the [[uub]] cross-edge: King Yemma's afterlife-judgment-of-Goku's-wish reincarnates Kid Buu's evil spirit into a human-Earthling body without the canonical evil-component, retaining the raw ki-power signature — the canonical *evil-spirit-reformed-by-human-rebirth-without-memory* design move. A future `kid-buu.md` solo node split would refactor the Kid-Buu-form into its own anchor if the corpus accumulates Kid-Buu-specific prints (currently no Kid-Buu-anchored corpus prints; the BT1-047 Energy Boosted Majin Buu canonically depicts the Innocent / Fat Buu register per its trivia bullet).

Three canonical Buu-form registers operate across the corpus:

- **Innocent Buu / Fat Buu (Galactic Battle BT1 register)** — one corpus card depicting the canonical Buu-Saga-opening rotund-pink-childlike-affect base form. The canonical *Buu predates Bibidi* clarification per the Dragon Ball DAIMA 2024 retcon and Toriyama's later canonical-source-statement notes that Bibidi is canonically the *one-who-knew-how-to-awaken-Buu*, not the canonical-creator-of-Buu per the original-canon framing. The BT1-047 print depicts this form.
- **Mr. Buu / Good Buu (Colossal Warfare BT4 register)** — one corpus card depicting the canonical reformed-villain Z-Fighter-ally register operating in the canonical GT Baby Saga continuity. The canonical *one-of-few-canonical-characters-who-evaded-Baby-possession* status the BT4-015 trivia bullet foregrounds canonically affirms the canonical Mr-Buu-as-Z-Fighter-ally register at the GT continuity. The Mr. Buu canonical *the-Buu-form-that-survives-the-Buu-Saga-and-continues-into-subsequent-continuities* status is the canonical character-anchor for this node.
- **Mr. Buu / Good Buu (World Martial Arts Tournament TB2 register)** — one corpus card depicting the canonical Mr. Buu participating in the canonical World Martial Arts Tournament arc as Mr. Satan's disciple and Z-Fighter ally. The TB2-028 print canonically mechanically-pairs with TB2-022 Scuffle Time Son Goten as a named designer-coordinated Scuffle Time synergy-pair within the WMAT TB2 set — the canonical *Mr-Buu-and-child-Goten-as-paired-tournament-arc-cohort* register.

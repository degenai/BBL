# Zendikari Resistance (BFZ + OGW multi-triangle meta-cycle)

The Wizards-of-the-Coast designer-coordinated two-set Eldrazi-invasion arc's *resistance-side* cohesion anchor across the *Battle for Zendikar* + *Oath of the Gatewatch* block (BFZ September 2015 + OGW January 2016, per MTG Wiki's Battle for Zendikar block page). This node is the BBL graph's **first multi-triangle meta-cycle anchor** — a single character-layer node that organizes two within-set three-card thesis-verb triangles (one in BFZ, one in OGW) under one designer-coordinated block-level cohesion frame. Per MTG Wiki's BFZ + OGW set-design pages and the canonical Wizards storyline archive, the two sets jointly form the Eldrazi-invasion arc whose resistance-side participants (the Zendikari Ally-mechanic militia, the Sea Gate survivors, the Affa-settlement underground-refuge defenders, the Gatewatch planeswalker command tier) compose a single cohesive military coalition rendered across the block's combined ~430 cards. This node anchors the resistance-side; the [[eldrazi]] node anchors the apparatus-side. Together the two nodes characterize the canonical adversary-coupling at the block-level scope.

## What this node anchors

Card art and flavor text that depicts the Zendikari resistance-side of the Eldrazi-invasion arc: Sea Gate survivors, organized Ally-resistance army members, Affa-settlement-canonical-underground-refuge defenders, Zendikari humans / kor / merfolk / elves operating in coordinated-defense register against the Eldrazi brood. Cards whose flavor text quotes resistance-coalition voices (the canonical Make a Stand flavor *"For a moment they stood tall, all fear forgotten"*; the canonical Affa Protector flavor *"I hold on to the memory of the world before the Eldrazi came"*; the canonical Reckless Cohort *"Mine died at Sea Gate"* survivor framing) anchor here. The multi-triangle meta-cycle structure of this node is what distinguishes it from sibling faction-cohort nodes: it does NOT replace the kor race-cohort node ([[kor]]) or the Gatewatch planeswalker-coalition (currently distributed across [[jace-beleren]] / [[chandra-nalaar]] / [[kiora]] solo nodes); it organizes the *block-level designer-coordinated resistance cohesion frame* that the kor-race-cohort and the Gatewatch-coalition both participate within.

**Multi-triangle meta-cycle composition:**

This node organizes two within-set three-card triangles whose three-verb thesis-axis-coverage is identical in shape and complementary in canonical anchor:

**BFZ within-set triangle:**

- **Kozilek's Sentinel** (BFZ-129) — labor-verb-corrupted anchor (apparatus-of-extraction side; Eldrazi drone-caste depicted from the resistance's adversarial perspective as the geological-inevitability-of-consumption rendered at workforce-tier of the brood). **OGW within-set triangle:**

# Guidelight Voyagers

The blue-aligned Grand Prix team in *Aetherdrift* (DFT, 2025-02-14): an "uncanny fleet of automatons captained by a single hive-mind entity called Mendicant Core" (Wizards: *Planeswalker's Guide to Aetherdrift Part 1*). The team arrived on Avishkar (formerly Kaladesh) through an Omenpath that opened around them in the aftermath of the Phyrexian invasion. They entered the Ghirapur Grand Prix not for prize or glory but in the hope that recreating the transit conditions of the race might return them to their unknown homeworld.

## What this node anchors

Card art and flavor text that depicts the Guidelight Voyagers as a faction: their automaton members, their racing rigs, the Mendicant Core hive-mind framing, and the canonical speech pattern of category-prefixed utterances ("Analysis:", "Exclamatory:") that marks an automaton as a distributed extension of the Core's intelligence. The Guidelight Voyagers are the BBL corpus's clearest in-set anchor for a labor-coded reading of the apparatus-of-automation: workers fielded by a singular controlling intelligence, racing not for themselves but as ten thousand instruments of one will trying to find its way home.

## Caveats

- The Guidelight Voyagers are a faction-collective (modeled here as a `character` node per the [[endriders]] precedent), not a single named individual. Mendicant Core *is* a single named entity but functions as the collective's distributed captain rather than an independent character; both Guidelight cards in corpus depict member-automatons rather than the Core itself. If a card depicting Mendicant Core specifically enters the corpus, the right move is a sibling character node (`mendicant-core.md`) that edges here, not a re-home of this entry.
- **Guidelight Pathmaker** is a third named Guidelight card in DFT (per the trivia pass on Guidelight Optimizer); not currently in the corpus. When it lands, it edges here.
- **Far Fortune, End Boss** is the [[endriders]] team captain card; Mendicant Core would be the analogous captain card for Guidelight Voyagers. Neither captain card is currently in the corpus.
- Avishkar (formerly Kaladesh) is a *place*, not a faction. Card art set on Avishkar but not depicting Guidelight automatons is still plane-relevant without belonging to this node. If the corpus accumulates enough Avishkar-set cards to anchor a place-level node, that would be a sibling.

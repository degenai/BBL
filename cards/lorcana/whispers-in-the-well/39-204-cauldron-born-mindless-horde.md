## Trivia

- **Flavor text** — "My beloved warriors have come to life! All the dead of centuries past! Never has anyone created an army like this!" — The Horned King. The speaker is the primary antagonist of *The Black Cauldron* (1985), a skeletal warlord obsessed with using the Black Cauldron to raise an invincible undead army. This is the Horned King's moment of triumph before the army ultimately destroys him. `[card frontmatter; Disney Wiki: Horned King; Wikipedia: The Black Cauldron (film)]`

- **Set context** — Cauldron Born are the undead warriors summoned by the Black Cauldron in Disney's *The Black Cauldron* (1985). The film's production was notably troubled — deleted scenes showed the Cauldron Born's green mist melting flesh from soldiers, content cut to secure a PG rating rather than PG-13. The surviving film still carries Disney's first PG rating. `[Wikipedia: The Black Cauldron (film); WebSearch snippet via TV Tropes]`

- **Design** — Common Amethyst character, 5 ink cost, 6 Strength / 7 Willpower, Storyborn classification. At that cost-to-stat ratio, the Cauldron Born are among the higher raw-power bodies available at the common rarity in Set 10. The card has no keyword abilities — their threat is purely the numbers, consistent with "mindless horde" framing. `[TCGPlayer product listing; WebSearch snippet via Mushu Report wiki]`

- **Franchise debut** — The Black Cauldron IP makes its first-ever Lorcana appearance in Whispers in the Well (Set 10, November 2025). The Cauldron Born card is the common-rarity entry point into a villain cluster anchored by The Horned King - Wicked Ruler (Enchanted, Legendary) and The Black Cauldron item card. `[Official Ravensburger press release; Lorcana Player: The Black Cauldron franchise list]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Vladimir is a Common Emerald card (6-cost, 5 Strength / 6 Willpower / 2 Lore) in Whispers in the Well (set 10), the final Lorcana release of 2025, which debuted November 14, 2025 (prerelease November 7). The set introduced new Disney IP to Lorcana including The Black Cauldron and Gargoyles characters. `[LorcanaPlayer.com: Whispers in the Well set page; Beckett News: Whispers in the Well Checklist]`
- **Flavor text** — "His commitment to excellent craftsmanship is unparalleled." The card subtitle 'Ceramic Unicorn Fan' and this flavor text both reference Vladimir's defining character trait in Disney's Tangled (2010): his secret hobby of collecting ceramic unicorns, revealed during the song 'I've Got a Dream' in which the other Pub Thugs sing 'And Vladimir collects ceramic unicorns!' — the comedy deriving from the total mismatch between his massive, menacing frame and his delicate hobby. `[Disney Wiki: Vladimir; Tangled Wiki: Vladimir; LorcanaJSON]`
- **Design** — The HIGH STANDARDS ability ('Whenever this character quests, you may banish chosen item') is a mechanical nod to Vladimir's exacting taste in ceramic unicorns: he doesn't just collect them, he curates them. Illustrated by Jochem van Gool. `[LorcanaJSON card data; LorcanaPlayer.com card page]`
- **Design** — Vladimir is a Storyborn Ally in Lorcana, meaning he represents a direct glimmer of the original animated character rather than a Floodborn (transformed) version. His classification as a common reflects the Pub Thugs' supporting-cast status in Tangled, even though Vladimir is one of the more physically memorable members of the group. `[LorcanaPlayer.com: Vladimir - Ceramic Unicorn Fan card page]`

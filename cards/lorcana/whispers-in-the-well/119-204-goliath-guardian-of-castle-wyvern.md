## Trivia

- **Set context** — Whispers in the Well (set 10, November 14 2025) marks the Gargoyles franchise's first appearance in Disney Lorcana. The set introduces 12 Gargoyles characters, with Goliath, Demona, Broadway, Elisa Maza, and others confirmed. Gargoyles' inclusion was announced alongside Darkwing Duck as Whispers in the Well's headline franchises. `[Ravensburger CASE FILE #1 — disneylorcana.com; LorcanaPlayer.com set overview]`
- **Design — STONE BY DAY mechanic** — The shared drawback keyword STONE BY DAY (characters can't ready if you have 3 or more cards in hand) is the Lorcana design team's direct translation of the show's core premise — gargoyles turn to stone during the day, rendering them helpless, and become warriors only at night. GamesRadar's preview noted the mechanic "turns a foundational piece of lore from the animated series into tangible mechanics that are more than just fluff." Hand management (staying at 2 or fewer cards) is therefore built into the Gargoyles archetype by design. `[GamesRadar preview, gamesradar.com; Lorcana France TCG, X/@LorcanaFr]`
- **Mechanical role** — This specific version (cost 4, Strength 5, Willpower 5, Ruby, 1 lore) functions as a Gargoyle tribal payoff: BE CAREFUL, ALL OF YOU grants 1 lore whenever any of your Gargoyle characters challenges another character, making it a lore-engine centerpiece in Ruby-Steel Gargoyles builds. A companion deckbuilding guide on Gaming Trend names Tinker Bell: Insistent Fairy as a key enabler — get her on board early to drop 5-strength Gargoyles and generate lore while thinning your hand for the Stone by Day constraint. `[LorcanaPlayer.com card review; gamingtrend.com Lorcana deckbuilding guide: Ruby Steel Gargoyles]`
- **IP context — Goliath in source canon** — In the animated series, Goliath did not name himself; the name was given to him by the humans of Castle Wyvern because of his enormous stature (6 ft. 10 in., 400 lbs.). He was the head of the Wyvern Clan in 994 Scotland, betrayed by the Captain of the Guard and frozen in stone for 1,000 years until the castle rose above the clouds atop Xanatos's New York skyscraper. `[GargWiki: Goliath; Disney Wiki: Goliath]`

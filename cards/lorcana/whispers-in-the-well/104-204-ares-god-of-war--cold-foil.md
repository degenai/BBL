## Trivia

- **Set context** — Ares appears in Whispers in the Well (Set 10, released November 14, 2025), Lorcana's mystery/noir-themed set that introduced the Boost mechanic and Whisper-type cards. The Hercules franchise has been part of Lorcana since The First Chapter (Set 1), but Ares as a named individual card debuts here in Set 10 — no earlier Ares printing exists in the game as of this set's release. `[Lorcana Player: Hercules franchise card list; Lorcana Player: Ares - God of War card page]`

- **Design** — Ares carries the Reckless keyword, which forces the character to challenge every turn if able and bars questing entirely. His CALL TO BATTLE ability lets players ready a chosen character whenever a card is placed under one of their characters or locations — tying him mechanically to the Boost system introduced in Set 10. Card artist is Kiersten Hale. `[Lorcana Player: Ares - God of War 104/204; card frontmatter]`

- **Character context** — In Disney's 1997 *Hercules* film, Ares has a minor cameo at Zeus and Hera's Olympus celebration; his expanded role comes in the *Hercules* animated TV series (1998–1999), where he is a recurring antagonist voiced by Jay Thomas, acting as a brash war-god who champions Sparta over Athena's Athens and is served by his sons Fear and Terror. The card's "God of War" subtitle and Deity classification directly reflect his mythological and franchise role. `[Disney Wiki: Ares (Hercules); Wikipedia: List of Disney's Hercules characters]`

- **Mechanical context** — At 2 ink cost with 3 Strength / 3 Willpower and Reckless, Ares is a pure aggressive threat: he cannot quest, must challenge each turn, and enables combo readying via CALL TO BATTLE. His stats make him a credible early attacker in Ruby-aggro builds. `[WebSearch snippet via Red Claw Gaming / Troll and Toad product listings]`

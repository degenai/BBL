## Trivia

- **Set context** — Whispers in the Well is Lorcana's tenth set (Set 10), released November 14, 2025. Its theme is film noir, mystery, and the supernatural. The subterranean ruins setting for this card reimagines Kristoff's mountain ice-harvesting identity into an archaeological excavation context, consistent with the set's motif of familiar Disney characters recast in underground investigative roles. `[Mushu Report — Whispers in the Well; Disney Lorcana press release October 2025]`
- **IP verification** — Kristoff Bjorgman is the deuteragonist of Disney's Frozen (2013) — a rugged, Sami-descended orphan who became an ice harvester in the mountains of Arendelle alongside his reindeer Sven. He is characterized as a practical loner whose partnership with Princess Anna develops into romance. IP confirmed via card name and Frozen character record. `[Wikipedia: Kristoff (Frozen); Frozen Wiki (Fandom)]`
- **Mechanical profile** — An Amber-ink Storyborn Ally Rare costing 3 ink (2 strength, 2 lore, 3 willpower). BOOST 1 lets you pay 1 ink once per turn to bank the top card of your deck facedown under Kristoff; WORTH MINING converts that banked card into inkwell mana whenever he quests. The loop provides repeatable inkwell acceleration starting as early as turn 4, making him a ramp engine in Amber builds. `[TCGBulk card database; Mushu Report — Kristoff Mining the Ruins; LorcanaPlayer.com starter deck breakdown]`
- **Design** — The "Mining the Ruins" subtitle is a narrative reframe of Kristoff's core identity: ice harvester becomes underground excavator. Artist credited is Alejandro Hernandez. `[Card frontmatter artist field; Lorcana set design via Disney Lorcana press release]`

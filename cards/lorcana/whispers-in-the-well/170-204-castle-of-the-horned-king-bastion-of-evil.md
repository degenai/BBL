## Trivia

- **Set context** — The Black Cauldron franchise makes its Lorcana debut in Whispers in the Well (Set 10, November 14, 2025), described by Ravensburger as one of the "long-awaited properties" joining the game for the first time. The press release specifically named The Black Cauldron alongside Disney Gargoyles as inaugural arrivals. `[Official Ravensburger Whispers in the Well press release, October 2025; Lorcana Player: The Black Cauldron franchise list]`

- **Design** — Castle of the Horned King is an Amethyst location card costing 1 ink. Its INTO THE GLOOM ability (once per turn, whenever a character quests here, you may ready a chosen item) synergizes with the Horned King's item-focused playstyle, including The Black Cauldron item card (no. 32/204 in the same set). The castle's low ink cost makes it one of the cheapest locations in the game to field. `[WebSearch snippets via Trinket Mage, Mighty Meeple product listings; Lorcana Player card database]`

- **Film context** — In Disney's *The Black Cauldron* (1985), the Horned King's castle in the land of Prydain serves as the primary seat of his power and the site of the climactic Cauldron Born summoning. The film was Disney's first PG-rated animated feature and remains one of the studio's darkest productions. `[Wikipedia: The Black Cauldron (film)]`

- **Community note** — The Horned King's Enchanted card from this set (Wicked Ruler, no. 226/204) was reported trading near $303 USD shortly after release, signaling strong collector demand for The Black Cauldron's Lorcana debut wave. The Castle location card at rare rarity sits at the more accessible tier of that same villain cluster. `[Screen Rant: Whispers in the Well most valuable cards]`

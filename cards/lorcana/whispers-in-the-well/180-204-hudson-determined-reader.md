## Trivia

- **IP context — Hudson's literacy arc** — Hudson was illiterate through most of his thousand-year life. In the animated series, he befriended a blind man named Jeffrey Robbins, who taught him to read. Hudson kept his gargoyle nature secret from Jeffrey for fear of losing the friendship. The "Determined Reader" subtitle directly references this character arc — Hudson mastering literacy is one of the show's recurring character-growth threads. `[Disney Wiki: Hudson; GargWiki: Hudson]`
- **Set context** — Hudson was the former leader of the Wyvern Clan before handing leadership to Goliath after losing his left eye in a battle with the Archmage. As of the show's present day, he serves as elder advisor to the Manhattan Clan, making the scholarly/reader framing of this card consistent with his role as clan wisdom-keeper rather than frontline warrior. `[GargWiki: Hudson; Disney Wiki: Hudson]`
- **Design — STONE BY DAY + FINDING ANSWERS** — Hudson's FINDING ANSWERS ability (draw a card, then discard a card) pairs tension with the STONE BY DAY constraint: drawing fuels hand size, which triggers the readying penalty. The card rewards players who can actively cycle — draw the card and immediately discard to stay at or below the 2-card threshold. Steel-color placement is appropriate; Steel typically covers support and knowledge roles in Lorcana's ink taxonomy. `[Lorcana-api.com card data; LorcanaPlayer.com card review]`
- **Mechanical profile** — Cost 3, Steel, Storyborn, Common. At cost 3 with a looting effect (draw + discard), Hudson slots into hand-management support builds that keep gargoyle readying online while refreshing toward key pieces. `[LorcanaPlayer.com; Lorcana-api.com]`

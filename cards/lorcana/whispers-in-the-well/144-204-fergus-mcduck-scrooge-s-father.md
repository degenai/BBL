## Trivia

- **Set context** — Whispers in the Well is Lorcana's tenth set (Set 10), released November 14, 2025 (prerelease November 7). Its theme is film noir, mystery, and the supernatural — set beneath the Great Illuminary where half-formed glimmers called Whispers begin to stir. The set introduced 204 cards and a new card type (Whispers). `[Mushu Report — Whispers in the Well; LorcanaPlayer.com; Disney Lorcana official press release October 2025]`
- **IP verification** — Fergus McDuck is Scrooge's father in DuckTales (2017 reboot), voiced by Graham McTavish. He is the patriarch of Clan McDuck — a proud Scottish clan from Dismal Downs — and his key DuckTales episode is "The Secret(s) of Castle McDuck!" (Season 1, Episode 21, August 4, 2018), in which Scrooge and family explore secret underground tunnels beneath Castle McDuck, directly echoing this card's dungeon setting. `[DuckTales Wiki (Fandom) — Fergus McDuck; TV Tropes recap S1E21]`
- **Flavor text** — "Ach, close the secret entrance! You're letting all the mysterious air out!" — Fergus McDuck. `[Card flavor_text (canonical Lorcana JSON capture)]`
- **Mechanical profile** — Fergus is a Sapphire-ink Storyborn character with TOUGHEN UP: when played, give a chosen friendly character Ward until the start of your next turn (opponents cannot choose that character except to challenge). At 3 cost and inkable, he fits Ward-protection and DuckTales-tribal strategies. `[TCGPlayer listing — Fergus McDuck 144/204; Star City Games Whispers in the Well card list]`
- **Artist** — Card art is by Kenneth Anderson, a freelance character designer and illustrator with credits across multiple Lorcana sets including Reign of Jafar. Anderson has cited Scrooge McDuck as one of his favorite characters to illustrate. `[Zatu Games — An Interview With Disney Lorcana Artist: Kenneth Anderson; Mushu Report — Kenneth Anderson artist page]`

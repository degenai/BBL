## Trivia

- **IP context — Finnick's role in Zootopia** — Finnick is Nick Wilde's partner-in-crime in Disney's 2016 animated film *Zootopia*. A fennec fox, Finnick's signature con involves disguising himself in a toddler elephant costume (and separately a bunny suit) to pose as Nick's "son," allowing Nick to scam merchants with a plausible cover story. His deep, gravelly voice — sharply contrasting his small, baby-faced appearance — is a recurring comedic payoff throughout the film. He was voiced by the late Tommy "Tiny" Lister. `[Disney Wiki: Finnick; IMDb: Zootopia — Tom Lister Jr. as Finnick]`
- **Art identity confirmation** — The depicted costume (grey bunny suit with pink bow, pacifier in mouth) is Finnick's bunny disguise specifically, distinct from the elephant toddler outfit used in other scenes.

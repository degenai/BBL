## Trivia

- **Set context** — Jim Hawkins - Stubborn Cabin Boy is card 173/204 in Azurite Sea (Lorcana set 6, released November 15, 2024). Azurite Sea is a pirate-adventure themed set. Jim Hawkins is one of Ravensburger's marquee characters for this wave: he appears in at least five named versions across Into the Inklands and Azurite Sea, and the Azurite Sea Starter Deck (Amber/Ruby) was themed around Jim Hawkins - Honorable Pirate paired with Tigger. [Lorcana Player: Azurite Sea overview, via WebSearch]
- **Mechanical design** — This card is a 2-cost Steel Common, 4 Strength, with ability COME ON, COME ON, COME ON! — during your turn, whenever a card is put into your inkwell, this character gains Challenger +2. The inkwell-scaling Challenger bonus rewards aggressive tempo play, echoing Jim's impulsive, opportunistic character in the film. No flavor text. Illustrated by Ursula Dorada. [card frontmatter; Lorcana databases via WebSearch]
- **Source character** — In Treasure Planet (2002, dir. Ron Clements and John Musker), Jim Hawkins — full name James Pleiades Hawkins, voiced by Joseph Gordon-Levitt — is a troubled, fatherless teenager assigned as cabin boy under the cyborg ship's cook John Silver. The film is a sci-fi adaptation of Robert Louis Stevenson's Treasure Island in a space-sailing universe. Jim's arc from delinquent to hero is centered on his surrogate father-son relationship with Silver. [Disney Wiki: Jim Hawkins (Treasure Planet), via WebSearch]
- **Version landscape** — Lorcana's Jim Hawkins prints as of Azurite Sea: Thrill Seeker (Into the Inklands, 110/204, Ruby), Space Traveler (Into the Inklands, Ruby Storyborn with location-play abilities), Honorable Pirate (Azurite Sea, Starter Deck face), Stubborn Cabin Boy (Azurite Sea, 173/204, this card), and Rigging Specialist (Azurite Sea, 183/204, Steel Floodborn with Shift 3). The "Stubborn Cabin Boy" subtitle frames an early-arc beat — before Jim earns recognition. [Lorcana Player: Jim Hawkins cards, via WebSearch]

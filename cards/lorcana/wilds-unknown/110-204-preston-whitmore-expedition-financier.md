## Trivia

- **Set context** — Preston Whitmore - Expedition Financier is a Common Ruby character in Wilds Unknown (set 12), released May 15, 2026. Wilds Unknown is the first Lorcana set to feature Pixar characters; Atlantis: The Lost Empire's crew — Preston Whitmore, Gaetan Moliere, Milo Thatch, and Kida — appears alongside Toy Story and The Incredibles characters. `[Ravensburger/disneylorcana.com: Wilds Unknown press release; FantasyLandNews.com]`
- **Design history** — Preston Whitmore was originally voiced by Lloyd Bridges, who died on March 10, 1998 before the film's 2001 release and recorded only a few lines. John Mahoney (Frasier) was recast; his performance was energetic enough that the character was redesigned as more eccentric, deepening the backstory of his lifelong friendship with Milo's grandfather Thaddeus Thatch. This is one of the more unusual casting-history stories in Disney animation. `[Wikipedia: Atlantis: The Lost Empire; Disney Wiki: Preston B. Whitmore]`
- **Flavor text** — "I promised all of you I'd keep your secrets. And Preston Whitmore always keeps a promise." The PRICE OF PROGRESS ability (discard the top 2 cards of your deck on entry) maps to the cost of keeping that promise: valuable resources destroyed to protect the expedition's secrets. The art — Whitmore burning documents over an open flame — is the literal visualization of this flavor text. `[LorcanaJSON card data; Disney Wiki: Preston B. Whitmore]`
- **Set context** — Atlantis: The Lost Empire (2001) earned approximately $185 million worldwide against a $90-120 million budget, making it a commercial disappointment that shelved planned sequels and theme park attractions. The film has since developed a significant cult following; its representation in Wilds Unknown with multiple named crew members receiving individual cards reflects its rehabilitation in Disney fan esteem. `[Wikipedia: Atlantis: The Lost Empire; CBR: Atlantis underrated Disney classic]`

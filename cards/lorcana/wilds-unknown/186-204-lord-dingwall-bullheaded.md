## Trivia

- **Set context** — Lord Dingwall - Bullheaded (no. 186/204) is among the Brave characters making their Lorcana debut in Wilds Unknown (Set 12, May 15, 2026) — the first expansion to introduce Pixar IP into the game. He appears alongside his son Wee Dingwall (Scrappy Lad, no. 176/204) in the same set, preserving the film's father-son dynamic. Brave joins Toy Story and The Incredibles as the set's inaugural Pixar franchises. `[WebSearch: lorcanaplayer.com Wilds Unknown card list; Mushu Report: Lord Dingwall]`

- **IP context** — In Disney/Pixar's Brave (2012), Lord Dingwall is the lean, ferociously-tempered leader of Clan Dingwall — one of three Highland clan lords who bring their sons as suitors for Princess Merida's hand. Despite being the smallest of the three lords physically, he has no hesitation challenging adversaries far larger than himself. He was voiced by the late Robbie Coltrane. `[Disney Wiki: Lord Dingwall, via WebSearch]`

- **Flavor text** — "Ach, look at the size o' that flower! Let's clear that beastie out before it causes any trouble." — The line captures Dingwall's reflexive pugnacity: he sees a large flower and immediately frames it as a threat requiring violent intervention, which is the joke the card name "Bullheaded" hangs on. `[Card flavor_text, frontmatter]`

- **Mechanical context** — FIGHTIN' TALK allows Lord Dingwall to enter play exhausted and grant a chosen character Challenger +3 for the turn. As a 5-cost 5/6 Uncommon Steel-ink Storyborn Ally, he functions as a combat-tempo piece — paying his own entry with exhaustion to accelerate another character's attack power. `[Mushu Report: Lord Dingwall - Bullheaded, via WebFetch; card oracle_text]`

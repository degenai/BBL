## Trivia

- **Character lore** — Professor Norton Nimnul is the recurring human antagonist of Chip 'n Dale: Rescue Rangers, the Disney animated series that ran 1989–1990 as part of the Disney Afternoon television block. He is voiced by Jim Cummings and operates as a mad scientist whose schemes, while elaborate, are typically illogical. He is notable as one of the few human characters in the show — and one of the only humans apparently aware of the Rescue Rangers' existence. `[Disney Wiki: Professor Norton Nimnul, via WebSearch snippet; Wikipedia: Chip 'n Dale: Rescue Rangers, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "Another successful test! This brilliant devitalizer ray technology will be my finest invention yet!" — Norton Nimnul. `[Card frontmatter: flavor_text field]` The flavor text and DEVITALIZER RAY ability form a closed loop: the card mechanically is Nimnul's invention. Once per turn, whenever you play an item, a chosen opposing character gets -2 Strength for the turn. `[Card frontmatter: oracle_text field]`
- **Set context** — Wilds Unknown is the twelfth Disney Lorcana set, released May 15, 2026 (prerelease May 8, 2026). It is the first Lorcana set to include Pixar characters alongside Disney animated properties. Rescue Rangers returned in this set after their first Lorcana appearance in Into the Inklands; Bleeding Cool received an exclusive preview of the Rescue Rangers cards. Nimnul is in Sapphire ink — the game's logic and intelligence-themed ink — appropriate for a scientist villain. `[Bleeding Cool: Rescue Rangers Return with Disney Lorcana: Wilds Unknown (Exclusive), via WebSearch snippet; disneylorcana.com press release, via WebSearch snippet]`

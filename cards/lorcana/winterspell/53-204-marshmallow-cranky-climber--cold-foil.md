## Trivia

- **Set context** — Marshmallow - Cranky Climber (no. 53/204) is an Amethyst-ink card in Winterspell (Lorcana Set 11, released February 13, 2026) — a set whose entire narrative centers on a magical winter event freezing the Inklands. Marshmallow's ice-construct identity fits the set's cold theming directly: the character is a snow-golem created by Elsa in Disney's Frozen (2013). `[Mushu Report: Marshmallow - Cranky Climber; WebSearch: chaoscards.co.uk Winterspell article]`

- **IP context** — In Frozen, Marshmallow is a giant snow-monster created by Elsa to expel Anna and Kristoff from her ice palace after she accidentally harmed Anna. Unlike Olaf, who was born from Elsa's happiness, Marshmallow was created from Elsa's fear and protective instinct. After the film's events, Marshmallow inherits the ice palace and lives atop the North Mountain with the Snowgies. `[Disney Wiki: Marshmallow; The Codex Wiki: Marshmallow, via WebSearch]`

- **Flavor text** — "GO AWAY!!" — a verbatim line Marshmallow delivers when expelling Anna and Kristoff from Elsa's ice palace in Frozen. The card captures the character's defining moment of territorial rage in two words. `[Card flavor_text, frontmatter; Disney film: Frozen, 2013]`

- **Mechanical context** — ICY BLAST (triggers on quest: each opponent cannot ready more than 1 character at start of their next turn) is a board-wide tap-lock effect. As a 5-cost 5/5 Rare Dreamborn Ally, Marshmallow quests for 2 lore while locking down opponent boards — the ability is particularly punishing in multi-opponent formats where the tap-limit affects all players simultaneously. `[Mushu Report: Marshmallow - Cranky Climber, via WebFetch; card oracle_text]`

- **Design** — The inventory copy is a Cold Foil variant — Winterspell's premium cold-stamped foiling treatment named after the set's ice theme, distinct from standard holographic foil. Illustrated by Ian MacDonald. `[WebSearch: facetofacegames.com Winterspell Cold Foil listing; card frontmatter: artist]`

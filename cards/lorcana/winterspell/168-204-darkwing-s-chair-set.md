## Trivia

- **Set context** — Darkwing's Chair Set is a Rare Amber item card in Winterspell (set 11, released February 20, 2026), the set where Darkwing Duck makes his Lorcana debut. As an item card, it has no Strength, Willpower, or Lore stats; its value is entirely mechanical — specifically the named synergy with Darkwing Duck character cards. `[LorcanaJSON card data; Star City Games: Darkwing's Chair Set listing]`
- **Design** — In the 1991-1992 animated series, Darkwing's chair set consists of two blue chairs at Drake Mallard's civilian home at 537 Avian Way. Pushing the head of a bronze statuette between them activates a spin mechanism that transports occupants to Darkwing Tower — his secret lair inside one of the towers of the Audubon Bay Bridge in St. Canard. The spin also functions as Drake's identity-protection: visiting guests who accidentally lean against the statuette spin without noticing, and Drake smoothly redirects them. This is depicted in the episode 'Calm a Chameleon.' `[Darkwing Duck Wiki: Darkwing's chair set; Darkwing Duck Wiki: Audubon Bay Bridge]`
- **Mechanical** — SECRET ENTRANCE (top card of deck into inkwell facedown and exerted on play) + SUDDEN SPIN (exert + banish: remove up to 2 damage from chosen character; 4 damage if that character is Darkwing Duck). The named bonus makes this item mechanically inert without Darkwing Duck on field — hard synergy that mirrors the prop's in-universe function as infrastructure built exclusively for him. Illustrated by Danielle Powers. `[LorcanaJSON card data]`
- **Design** — This card has no flavor text. All narrative freight is carried by the ability names: SECRET ENTRANCE and SUDDEN SPIN are both direct references to the chair set's in-series function. The purple armchair in the art matches Darkwing's costume color — a deliberate visual link between the domestic cover and the vigilante identity it conceals. `[LorcanaJSON card data]`

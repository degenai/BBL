## Trivia

- **Set context** — Part of Reign of Jafar (set 8), released May 30, 2025, Disney Lorcana's eighth expansion. The set's narrative frames Jafar's takeover of Archazia's Island, drawing characters from across the Disney catalog into a single conflict, including this TaleSpin entrant. `[Mushu Report: Reign of Jafar wiki]` `[lorcanaplayer.com/set/reign-of-jafar/]`
- **Franchise** — Mushu Report's Lorcana wiki places this card under the **TaleSpin** franchise — Disney's 1990 Disney Afternoon series that transplanted Jungle Book characters into a 1930s-era aviation-adventure setting with a recurring cast of air pirates and mercenaries. `[Mushu Report: He Who Steals and Runs Away card page]`
- **Design** — Illustrated by Luis Huerta. As a 3-cost Emerald Action, it banishes a chosen item and replaces itself by drawing a card — a clean tempo exchange, trading opponent's item advantage for a fresh draw. `[DigitalTQ: reign-of-jafar/he-who-steals-and-runs-away-114]`
- **Flavor text** — "Lives to steal another day!" — a riff on the proverb "he who fights and runs away, lives to fight another day," recast as a boast rather than a tactical retreat. `[card frontmatter; confirmed lorcanaplayer.com]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Reign of Jafar (set 8) brings the full Rescue Rangers crew together: Chip, Dale, Gadget, Monterey Jack, and Zipper all appear, marking the franchise's complete-team debut in Lorcana. `[lorcanaplayer.com: Chip 'n Dale: Rescue Rangers franchise page]`
- **IP verified** — Canonical identification confirmed. The BEST MATES ability explicitly names Monterey Jack — Zipper's inseparable partner from the show — and Mushu Report / lorcanaplayer.com both categorize this card under the Chip 'n Dale: Rescue Rangers franchise. `[Mushu Report: Zipper - Flying Ranger; lorcanaplayer.com]`
- **Lore** — In Chip 'n Dale: Rescue Rangers (1989–1990), Zipper is the only Ranger who communicates exclusively through squeaks — no intelligible speech — yet consistently pulls off the tasks no one else can, particularly flight, infiltration, and lock-picking. The card's BURST OF SPEED Evasive ability reflects this: the tiniest Ranger slips past anything in front of him. `[Disney Wiki: Zipper]`
- **Mechanical** — At 3-cost Steel Uncommon (2/4 stats) with conditional Evasive, Zipper is fragile solo but drops to 2-cost when Monterey Jack is already on board — a real tempo incentive to run both in Rescue Rangers synergy builds. `[lorcanaplayer.com: Zipper - Flying Ranger card review]`

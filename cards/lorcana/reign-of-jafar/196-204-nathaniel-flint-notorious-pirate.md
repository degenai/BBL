## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Nathaniel Flint is the posthumous overarching antagonist of Disney's 2002 animated film Treasure Planet, a sci-fi retelling of Robert Louis Stevenson's {Treasure Island}. Flint never appears alive in the film; his hidden hoard and fearsome reputation drive the entire plot. The card flavor text — "Flint's secret trove was never found, but stories have persisted that it remains hidden..." — mirrors this precisely. `[Disney Wiki: Captain Nathaniel Flint]` `[card frontmatter]`
- **IP verified** — Confirmed via exact card-name match, Mushu Report franchise categorization (Storyborn / Villain / Alien / Pirate / Captain under Treasure Planet), and Disney Wiki's description of Flint's unnamed alien species with a reptilian multi-eyed face — consistent with the art's non-human features and glowing eyes. Wizards-internal Disney character; no external IP risk. `[Mushu Report: Nathaniel Flint - Notorious Pirate; Disney Wiki]`
- **Design** — Illustrated by Edu Francisco. Mechanically: he only arrives when blood is already in the water. `[lorcanaplayer.com: Nathaniel Flint - Notorious Pirate]`

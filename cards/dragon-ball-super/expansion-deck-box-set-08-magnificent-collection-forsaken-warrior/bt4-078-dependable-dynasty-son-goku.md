## Trivia

- **Set context** — BT4-078 originates in Colossal Warfare (BT4), the fourth main DBS Card Game booster, and was reprinted as a promotional card in Magnificent Collection: Forsaken Warrior (DBS-BE08), released November 22, 2019. The Forsaken Warrior product pairs a Broly structure deck with 18 new promo cards (36 copies total), allowing older staples like this Goku to reach new players in a tournament-legal format. `[TCGplayer: Magnificent Collection Forsaken Warrior product page; Wild Things Games BT4-078 PR listing]`
- **Mechanical** — Yellow Battle card, Power 15,000, cost 2 Yellow. Skill: Swap 3 (YY) targeting a "Goku's Lineage" card with energy cost 3 — exchange Dependable Dynasty Son Goku from the field for a higher-cost Goku's Lineage card from hand, returning this card to hand. A setup-ramp tool that keeps itself recyclable across the game's tempo swings. `[Pricecharting.com: Dependable Dynasty Son Goku BT4-078; Wild Things Games BT4-078 retailer listing]`
- **Design** — The "Saiyan Saga" era designation anchors this Goku in his earliest Dragon Ball Z power tier — pre-Super Saiyan, pre-Namek — consistent with the base-form, grinning, running pose. The "Dependable Dynasty" title frames him as patriarch of a warrior lineage rather than peak-power combatant, a card-design choice that makes this Goku function as a foundation-layer rather than a finisher. `[Pricecharting.com: BT4-078 card entry]`

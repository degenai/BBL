## Trivia

- **Set / card context** — P-028 is a DBS Card Game Promotion Card — distributed through events or store channels, not booster packs. It is a Leader flip card: the base side is "Frieza" and the awakened side is "Bionic Strike Mecha Frieza." Mecha Frieza is the mechanically-rebuilt form of Frieza following his death at Future Trunks's hands in the Dragon Ball Z Trunks Saga. `[Card name text; WebSearch: dbs-cardgame.com strategy]`
- **Competitive / errata history** — P-028 was added to the official DBS Card Game Watch List for enabling turn-two kill strategies: the Bionic Strike Mecha Frieza awakened side reduced Extra Card costs while trading life for cards, enabling powerful plays by turn two. Bandai subsequently issued an official errata to P-028's [Permanent] skill. `[WebSearch: dbs-cardgame.com/us-en/strategy/errata/p028-frieza.php]`
- **Image quality note** — The reference image is captured at 260x363 (low quality) from the official DBS Card Game website's promo image host. Mechanical details distinguishing the Mecha Frieza awakened side are not resolvable at this resolution. `[Card frontmatter: image_quality: low]`

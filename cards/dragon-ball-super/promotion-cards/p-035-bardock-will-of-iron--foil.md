## Trivia

- **Set / distribution context** — P-035 is a Judge Promo card distributed through the Official Tournament Pack Vol.3 (Cross Worlds season), available in sanctioned DBS TCG tournaments from March 9, 2018 onward. Judge promos are not obtainable through retail purchase — they are given to judges who run official Bandai tournament events, making this a rarer pull channel than a store-stock rare. The foil variant in this inventory is the same art as the judge-distribution version with a foil treatment. [dbs-cardgame.com: Shop Tournaments Vol.3 event page; dbscards.fr P-035 entry]
- **Mechanical profile** — Yellow Battle card, power 20,000, energy cost 4, Combo Power 10,000. Special traits: Saiyan/Great Ape. Skills: Double Strike + Blocker. Auto skill: "During your turn, at the end of the battle after you combo with this card, choose up to 1 of your Bardock, and evolve it into this card." The Evolve-via-combo mechanic allows P-035 to be placed from the combo zone onto an existing Bardock Battle Card without paying full energy cost, making the Double Strike / Blocker package cheaper to deploy than its 4-cost face value suggests. [WebSearch snippet: dbscards.fr P-035; dbs-cardgame.com promo list]
- **Lore context — Bardock and the Great Ape form** — Bardock is the canonical low-class Saiyan warrior, father of Goku (Kakarot) and Raditz, first depicted in the 1990 Toei TV special *Dragon Ball Z: Bardock — The Father of Goku*. The Great Ape (Oozaru) transformation occurs when a Saiyan with a tail looks at a full moon, multiplying their power tenfold but suppressing rational control. The TV special shows Bardock and his squad transforming into Great Apes to destroy an entire civilization — and Bardock is depicted as retaining memory of his Great Ape actions, unlike typical Saiyans. The Oozaru form depicted on P-035 directly references this special. [Wikipedia: Bardock; Kanzenshuu forum: "Bardock Special canon?" thread]
- **Character canon revision** — Bardock's characterization was significantly rewritten by Akira Toriyama in the 2013 manga bonus chapter *Dragon Ball Minus: The Departure of the Fated Child* (collected in *Jaco the Galactic Patrolman*), which Toriyama confirmed was inspired by his affection for the character's TV-special story. In *Dragon Ball Minus*, Bardock is a caring father who, sensing Frieza's intent, arranges to send infant Kakarot to Earth. This rewrites the TV special's amnesiac-prophet Bardock into a protective parent driven by instinct. The DBS Card Game's "Bardock Saga" era stamp covers both interpretations across different prints; P-035 places Bardock at the Oozaru-form layer that predates the *Dragon Ball Minus* revision. [Wikipedia: Bardock; CBR: "Dragon Ball Z: What's Still Canon From Bardock: The Father of Goku?"]
- **Saiyan / Bardock cluster link** — P-035 shares the Saiyan/Great Ape trait profile with the broader Saiyan-subarchetype cards across BT1-BT4. It is the only Great-Ape-depicted card in the BBL corpus as of this wave, making it the sole Oozaru-form anchor in the Saiyan character cluster. The Evolve-via-combo skill ties it mechanically to other Bardock-named Battle Cards (standard Bardock commons and rares from the main sets). [dbs-cardgame.com promo card list]

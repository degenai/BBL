## Trivia

- **Set context** — Giru is a Machine Mutant encountered by Goku and company on Planet Imegga in Dragon Ball GT. He accidentally swallows the Dragon Radar Trunks dropped, absorbing its function, which forces the group to bring him along as a living Dragon Ball locator. Goku gives him the name "Giru" because the robot repeatedly says "Giru! Giru!" — a naming pattern typical of Dragon Ball's comedic register. [Source: dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Giru / official Dragon Ball site character showcase no. 205]
- **Design** — Giru is one of the few antagonist-origin characters in Dragon Ball GT who fully transitions to the heroes' side. Despite originating on Machine Planet M-2 (Dr. Myuu's base), he bonds with Pan and is described as her best friend. His "Handy" title in this card name references his utility role — he literally carries Dragon Radar capability inside his body. [Source: dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Giru]
- **Mechanical** — Giru can absorb the functionality of other machines he consumes, gaining their capabilities — a trait that mirrors the Machine Mutant faction's overall theme of mechanical assimilation. In the DBS card game his cards sit in the Black Star Dragon Ball Saga cluster alongside Pan, Goku GT, and Dr. Myuu's faction. [Source: dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Giru / dbs-cardgame.com card list]

## Trivia

- **Set/lore context** — The Masked Saiyan is Bardock — Goku's father — pulled from the moment just before his death at Frieza's Supernova and brainwashed by Towa using a Demon Realm mask that provides a steady supply of Dark Ki. His identity as Bardock is the central mystery of the Masked Saiyan Saga in Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 and Dragon Ball Heroes; breaking the mask frees him and purges the Dark Ki. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Masked Saiyan Saga, via WebSearch; Xenoverse 2 Wiki: Masked Saiyan, via WebSearch]`
- **Design** — The Masked Saiyan concept originated in Dragon Ball Online (2010) before being adapted into Dragon Ball Heroes and Xenoverse 2. The white mask with pink markings is Towa's Demon Realm technology — its design makes the wearer unrecognizable, suppressing Bardock's identity and will entirely. The mask's destruction is the condition for his liberation. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Masked Saiyan Saga, via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical** — SD3-04 is part of the Dark Invasion starter deck (DBS-SD03). The SD3 release came with a promo card "Increasing Evil Masked Saiyan" described as "incredibly powerful," marking the Masked Saiyan as the deck's flagship threat card and its narrative centerpiece. `[WebSearch: DBS Series 3 roundup strategy page]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Cross Worlds (BT3) released March 9, 2018 as the third main booster set for the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, comprising 127 cards. It is the first set to heavily feature the Xenoverse / Dragon Ball Online timeline, introducing the Time Breakers faction alongside heroic time-guardians like the Supreme Kai of Time. `[WebSearch: Bleeding Cool Cross Worlds Checklist; Amazon BT3 listing]`
- **Design** — Chronoa (Supreme Kai of Time) debuted in Dragon Ball Online (2010) before becoming a central figure in Xenoverse and Xenoverse 2. She is the keeper of the Time Vault and commands the Time Patrol — her pink twin-tail hairstyle and petite stature are her definitive visual identifiers across all media appearances. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Chronoa, via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical** — BT3-113 and BT3-122 (Time's Judgement) give Chronoa dual card representation in the same booster set — both a Battle Card (this card) and a named-ability event card. Having both in the same set at Common rarity is uncommon and signals her narrative centrality to the Xenoverse arc Cross Worlds adapts. `[WebSearch: DBS BT3 card list]`

## Trivia

- **Mechanics** — BT3-058 carries [Dual Attack] — allowing it to switch to Active Mode after attacking, enabling two attacks per turn — plus an [Auto] on-play ability that KOs an opponent's Battle Card on entry. Power 20,000, energy cost 5 (three green), combo power 10,000. This removal-on-entry plus Dual Attack combination made it a tempo-aggressive Uncommon in the BT3 format. [Source: Rochester CCG product listing via WebSearch snippet]
- **Set context** — Cross Worlds (DBS-B03, March 9, 2018) was the third main DBS card game set and used the green card color for this Goku variant — encoding Android 13 Saga affiliation — distinguishing it from the red-coded Baby Saga Goku cards that followed in BT4. [Source: dbs-cardgame.com BT3 product page]
- **Design** — Goku's orange-and-blue Turtle School gi (passed down from Master Roshi) is his defining visual constant across all Dragon Ball eras and one of the most recognized character designs in anime history. The "pressure assault" compositional archetype — low-angle framing, blazing aura, forward-leaning combat stance — recurs across dozens of Goku cards in the DBS card game to convey raw forward momentum as a readable shorthand. [Source: Dragon Ball GT Wikipedia]

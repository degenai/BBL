## Trivia

- **Set/lore context** — Towa is a demon scientist from the Demon Realm and younger sister of Demon King Dabura. Her goal across Dragon Ball Online and the Xenoverse series is to gather energy from powerful warriors across time to break the seal on the Demon Realm — a scheme she executes with her creation Mira and enslaved weapon Masked Saiyan Bardock. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Towa, via WebSearch]`
- **Design** — Towa originated in Dragon Ball Online (2010 Korean MMORPG) and carried into Xenoverse and Xenoverse 2 as a primary antagonist. Her partner Mira was confirmed as Toriyama-designed; Towa follows the same Toriyama-supervised pipeline. She is the only named female villain in the Xenoverse main cast. `[CBR: 10 Things About Towa, via WebSearch; CBR: Dragon Ball DAIMA Towa article, via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical** — In BT3, Towa (BT3-115) and Mira (BT3-116) appear in sequential card numbers — a design pattern in the set that signals narrative partnerships. The card is a Common in the BT3 booster's Dark Empire cluster, part of the same black-card faction later anchored by the SD3 Dark Invasion starter deck. `[WebSearch: DBS BT3 card list]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Luud is a Machine Mutant created by Dr. Myuu and worshipped as a god of destruction by a manipulated cult on Planet Luud. His power-absorption system works via a "Doll Beam" that converts organic beings into dolls, which are fed into a cauldron to fuel his energy level toward level 3 — the maximum state — with the collected energy then intended to funnel to Baby for his restoration. [Source: villains.fandom.com/wiki/Luud / WebSearch snippet]
- **Design** — Luud's defeat requires cooperation from inside and outside simultaneously: Pan, absorbed within Luud's core, attacks the nucleus from inside while Goku strikes from outside. The card title "Power-absorbing" directly names his Doll Beam mechanic. [Source: villains.fandom.com/wiki/Luud]
- **Mechanical** — Luud sits in the Uncommon slot (BT3-016) within the Machine Mutant antagonist cluster in Cross Worlds, which spans BT3-016 through BT3-020 and forms the primary villain faction block for the Black Star Dragon Ball Saga side of the set. [Source: dbs-cardgame.com card list]

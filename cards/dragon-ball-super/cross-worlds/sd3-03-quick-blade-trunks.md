## Trivia

- **Set/lore context** — SD3-03 is from "The Dark Invasion" starter deck (DBS-SD03), the black/green Xenoverse-themed deck released alongside Cross Worlds in 2018. In Xenoverse lore Future Trunks serves as Chronoa's Time Patrol lieutenant; his presence in the Dark Invasion deck (the antagonist-faction deck) reflects the game's framing of Trunks as the player's guide, bridging hero and Dark Empire narrative. `[WebSearch: DBS SD3 Dark Invasion product listing; WebSearch: Series 3 roundup strategy page]`
- **Design** — Future Trunks is canonically the greatest swordsman in Dragon Ball. His most iconic sword moment is the "Sword of Hope" in the Dragon Ball Super Goku Black arc, where he channeled Earth's survivors' ki into his blade to bisect Fused Zamasu. The blue ki energy crackling around the blade in this card's art is consistent with that ki-sword aesthetic. `[CBR: Future Trunks Sword Power Explained, via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical** — Per the Cross Worlds strategy page, "Chain Attack Trunks" (the SD3 deck's key attacker) is designed to reach the board by turns 2-3 when paired with BT3-011. The SD3 deck's synergy uses Trunks as the pressure card while Masked Saiyan and Mira provide intimidation and power — mirroring the Xenoverse narrative where Trunks is the player-guide hunting the Dark Empire. `[WebSearch: DBS Series 3 roundup strategy page]`

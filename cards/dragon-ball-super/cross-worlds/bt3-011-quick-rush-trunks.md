## Trivia

- **Set context** — Cross Worlds (DBS-B03) is the third booster set for the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, released March 9, 2018. The set draws from multiple Dragon Ball eras including Dragon Ball GT's Black Star Dragon Ball Saga and the Majin Buu Saga, making it one of the most chronologically wide-ranging early sets in the game `[TCGPlayer: Cross Worlds set listing]`.
- **Design** — BT3-011 is an Uncommon Battle card tagged with the special traits Saiyan/Earthling and era label Black Star Dragon Ball Saga, placing this card's Trunks firmly in Dragon Ball GT continuity rather than the Future Trunks timeline from Dragon Ball Z. The card carries the [Critical] skill: damage dealt goes directly to the opponent's life area instead of their hand `[WebSearch snippet: dbs-cardgame.com BT3-011]`.
- **Character context** — GT-era Trunks is the adult president of Capsule Corporation who reluctantly joins Goku and Pan's quest to recover the Black Star Dragon Balls. His signature look — purple hair, brown jacket, blue clothing — distinguishes him from both Kid Trunks (lavender hair, no jacket) and Future Trunks (whose anime hair drifted from purple to blue in Dragon Ball Super). The purple hair retained in this card art is Toei Animation's original coloring choice for the character `[Wikipedia: Trunks (Dragon Ball)]`.

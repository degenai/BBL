## Trivia

- **Character origin** — Android 13 is the main antagonist of Dragon Ball Z: Super Android 13!, released in Japanese theaters July 11, 1992. He was completed not by Gero himself but by Gero's underground supercomputer after Gero's death — built with the sole directive of killing Goku and carrying out the Red Ribbon Army's posthumous revenge. `[Wikipedia: Dragon Ball Z: Super Android 13!; Dragon Ball Wiki: Android 13, via WebSearch]`
- **Absorption ability** — Android 13 can absorb the core components of Androids 14 and 15 to transform into "Super Android 13," a hulking, exponentially more powerful form. Cell's creators noted this absorption mechanic may represent a prototype to Cell's own absorption design. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Android 13, via WebSearch]`
- **Set cluster** — BT3-070 (Android 13, Uncommon) shares its set with BT3-067 (Dr. Gero, Common), making Cross Worlds the only DBS-TCG set to pair the architect and his posthumous creation in the same release. Android 13's Red Ribbon Army cap visually marks this lineage even though Gero was already dead when 13 was activated. `[dbs-cardgame.com BT3 card list, via WebSearch; Dragon Ball Wiki: Android 13, via WebSearch]`

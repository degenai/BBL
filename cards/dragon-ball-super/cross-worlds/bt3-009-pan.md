## Trivia

- **Set context** — Pan is the daughter of Gohan and Videl, making her one-quarter Saiyan and the granddaughter of both Goku and Mr. Satan. In Dragon Ball GT she is one of three protagonists who travel the universe hunting Black Star Dragon Balls — notable as one of the youngest main-cast members to anchor a Dragon Ball arc. [Source: dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Pan / official Dragon Ball site character showcase]
- **Design** — Pan is one of only two post-Frieza Saiyans who never achieved Super Saiyan form. Creator Akira Toriyama stated he could not determine what a female Super Saiyan would look like and felt both Pan and Bulla lacked sufficient in-story incentive to transform — a design choice that drew ongoing fan discussion throughout GT's run. [Source: dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Pan]
- **Mechanical** — In the DBS card game, Pan appears as a Leader card in Cross Worlds (BT3), anchoring the Black Star Dragon Ball Saga mechanic cluster. The BT3 designer's note identifies Pan as one of the set's three featured leader characters alongside Dr. Myuu and Son Goku. [Source: dbs-cardgame.com set overview]

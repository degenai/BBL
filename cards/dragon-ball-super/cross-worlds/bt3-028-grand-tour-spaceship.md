## Trivia

- **Set context** — Cross Worlds (DBS-B03, March 9, 2018) is the third main DBS card game set and the first to introduce Dragon Ball GT's Black Star Dragon Ball Saga as a featured arc. Characters like Pan and Dr. Myuu debut as named cards in this set, establishing the GT era as a playable identity distinct from Super-era cards. [Source: dbs-cardgame.com BT3 product page; WebSearch snippet]
- **Mechanics** — The Grand Tour Spaceship's card text lets you look at the top 7 cards of your deck and play a "Son Goku: GT", "Trunks: GT", "Pan", or "Giru" with 15,000 or less power — mechanically replicating the story beat where the four crew members are assembled aboard the ship. The card is categorized as an EXTRA card, serving as deck infrastructure rather than a combat unit. [Source: Big Orbit Cards: BT3-028 via WebSearch snippet]
- **Character lore** — In Dragon Ball GT, the Grand Tour Spaceship was designed by Bulma and crewed by Goku (in child form due to Emperor Pilaf's wish), Trunks, and Pan — who joined by sneaking aboard, displacing her father Gohan's intended slot. The ship traveled to over a dozen planets as the crew retrieved Black Star Dragon Balls, with a one-year countdown before Earth's destruction as the stakes. [Source: Dragon Ball Wiki: Grand Tour Spaceship]

## Trivia

- **Set context** — BT3-112 identifies the character as Trunks: Xeno, placed in the Dark Demon Realm Saga era — a storyline originating in Super Dragon Ball Heroes, the Japanese arcade card game. Xeno Trunks is a Time Patroller variant of Future Trunks recruited by Chronoa (Supreme Kai of Time) after Cell's defeat in his own timeline. He fights the Time Breakers and Dark Empire across multiple time periods, and is distinct from both GT Trunks and main-timeline Future Trunks. [Source: ComicBook.com / CBR.com on Xeno Trunks]
- **Mechanical** — The card's Over Realm 3 skill is the defining mechanic of the Black color in Cross Worlds: if the player has 3 or more cards in the Drop Area, the card can be played by sending all Drop Area cards to the Warp; it then returns to hand when sent to the Warp from the Battle Area or deck. The BT3 designer's note explicitly names Over Realm as Black's signature feature and a deck-building diversity tool. [Source: dbs-cardgame.com designer's note / DBZ Exchange listing BT3-112]
- **Mechanical** — BT3-112 carries a tournament restriction — the card cannot be placed in a main deck or side deck for official tournament play. This status was in effect from the March 2018 release date and typically indicated competitive-level power concerns in the early DBS card game era. [Source: dbs-cardgame.com official card list / DBZ Exchange listing]

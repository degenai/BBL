## Trivia

- **Set context** — Pint-sized Warrior Son Goku (BT3-006) is a red Common from Cross Worlds (DBS-B03), released March 9, 2018. BT3 was the third main DBS Card Game booster, notable for introducing black as a new color and [Over Realm] as a deck-construction mechanic designed to diversify competitive builds [Source: dbs-cardgame.com/europe-en/strategy/cross-worlds/designers-note.php].
- **Mechanical** — The card's skill: "[Auto] When you play this card, look at up to 7 cards from the top of your deck. Choose up to 1 red 'Son Goku: GT' among them and add it to your hand. Then shuffle your deck." The official character tag is "Son Goku: GT" — a deep-dig tutor for Dragon Ball GT-era Goku variants. Seven-card dig is unusually deep for a 1-cost common, making it a strong consistency piece in GT Goku decks [Source: WebSearch snippets from coretcg.com and alternateworldscomics.com BT3-006 listings].
- **IP note** — The depicted child Goku is specifically the Dragon Ball GT version: in GT (1996-1997 anime), Goku is accidentally wished back to child form by the Black Star Dragon Balls, retaining all adult power in a child's body. The "Pint-sized Warrior" title references this directly. This distinguishes the card from BT4-079 (Unbroken Dynasty Son Goku), which carries the "Son Goku: Childhood" tag for classic Dragon Ball pre-Z continuity [Source: Wikipedia: Dragon Ball GT; WebSearch: GT overview].

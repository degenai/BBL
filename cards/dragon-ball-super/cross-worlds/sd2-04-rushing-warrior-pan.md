## Trivia

- **Set/lore context** — SD2-04 is from "The Extreme Evolution" starter deck (DBS-SD02), one of two starter decks released alongside Cross Worlds in 2018. The Extreme Evolution is a red-color GT-arc deck built around Pan and Goku as leaders, with Pan functioning as a draw and power-boost engine in combos. `[WebSearch: DBS SD2 Extreme Evolution product listing; WebSearch: DBS Series 3 roundup strategy page]`
- **Design** — Pan is the granddaughter of Goku and Mr. Satan — a quarter-Saiyan, three-quarters Earthling. In Dragon Ball GT she first appears at age 10 and is one of the main characters for the Universal Dragon Ball hunt arc, traveling with Goku and Trunks across space. Her inability to achieve Super Saiyan despite her Saiyan heritage has been a persistent subject of fan debate. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Pan, via WebSearch; Screen Rant: Dragon Ball GT Pan article, via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical** — Per the Cross Worlds strategy page, Pan's SD2 card pairs with the SD2-01 Goku leader and BT3-011 Trunks to produce quick board presence by turns 2-3. Her design intent is as a combo enabler and draw engine rather than a standalone attacker — consistent with her narrative role as a support character alongside Goku and Trunks in GT. `[WebSearch: DBS SD2 Extreme Evolution strategy; DBS Series 3 roundup page]`

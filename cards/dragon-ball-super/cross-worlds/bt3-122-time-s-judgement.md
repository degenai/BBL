## Trivia

- **Set/lore context** — BT3-122 is the second Chronoa card in the BT3 Cross Worlds booster and is numbered directly after Dark Plot (BT3-121) — justice card placed immediately after the conspiracy card. The set appears to structure the Xenoverse narrative conflict as a literal card sequence, with the Time Breakers' scheme (BT3-121) answered by the Supreme Kai of Time's judgement (BT3-122). `[WebSearch: DBS BT3 card list; WebSearch: Bleeding Cool Cross Worlds Checklist]`
- **Design** — Chronoa's "Holy Time Judgement" is a canonical attack in the Xenoverse games — energy spheres formed in both hands and thrown to surround and explode the target. The card name "Time's Judgement" directly references this move, making the card a mechanical translation of a specific in-game ability rather than a generic power event. `[WebSearch: GameFAQs Xenoverse 2 Chronoa moveset thread]`
- **Mechanical** — BT3-113 and BT3-122 represent Chronoa's dual presence in Cross Worlds: a Battle Card (her as a unit) and an Event card (her signature ability). Having both a character card and a named-ability card for the same figure at Common rarity in the same set is uncommon in the DBSCG and signals Chronoa's narrative centrality to the Xenoverse arc. `[WebSearch: DBS BT3 card list]`

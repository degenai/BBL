## Trivia

- **Set context** — Ledgic is the personal bodyguard of Don Kee, ruler of Planet Imecka, encountered early in Dragon Ball GT's Black Star Dragon Ball Saga. He is notable as the first opponent in GT to force Goku to transform into Super Saiyan — a meaningful power-scaling threshold that the early GT arc used to establish the threat level of the universe-spanning journey ahead. [Source: dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Ledgic]
- **Design** — Despite being a mercenary working for a tyrant, Ledgic refuses Don Kee's money after the fight and declines further service, citing personal honor. He also asks Goku explicitly not to hold back because he knows Goku is a Saiyan — an unusual characterization for a one-arc antagonist in Dragon Ball where villains typically underestimate the hero. [Source: dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Ledgic]
- **Mechanical** — Ledgic fights using two swords and a spear in the GT anime. In the DBS card game his Uncommon slot (BT3-015) places him within the Black Star Dragon Ball Saga antagonist cluster. His alien design — blue skin, elongated cobra-shaped head, spiked red armor — marks the Planet Imecka arc as one of GT's more visually inventive alien-world episodes. [Source: dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Ledgic / dbs-cardgame.com]

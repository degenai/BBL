## Trivia

- **Set context** — General Rilldo is Dr. Myuu's top general and commander of Machine Planet M-2 in Dragon Ball GT. In the anime, Goku states that Rilldo's base-form power level exceeds that of Majin Buu — placing him above the final villain of Dragon Ball Z at his initial appearance. His "Hidden Ability" card title refers to his multi-stage transformation system: each form reveals progressively greater power, culminating in the Hyper Meta-Rilldo form. [Source: villains.fandom.com/wiki/General_Rilldo / WebSearch snippet]
- **Design** — In Hyper Meta-Rilldo form, Rilldo becomes a being of liquid metal capable of reshaping and liquefying metal in his environment, effectively merging with Planet M-2 itself. The planet's functions are linked to Rilldo's biology, making him one with the infrastructure he commands. [Source: villains.fandom.com/wiki/General_Rilldo]
- **Mechanical** — Rilldo anchors the Machine Mutant antagonist cluster in Cross Worlds (BT3) alongside Dr. Myuu (BT3-017) and Luud (BT3-016). As the highest-numbered card in the Machine Mutant group within the Black Star Dragon Ball Saga block, he serves as the faction's power capstone. [Source: dbs-cardgame.com card list]

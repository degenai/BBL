## Trivia

- **Set context** — SD2-01 is the Leader card of the "THE EXTREME EVOLUTION" Starter Deck (DBS-SD02), released alongside the Cross Worlds booster set (DBS-B03) in 2018. The starter contains 51 cards total, including 5 exclusive Starter Rare cards not available in booster packs — making this card a starter-only pull. [dbs-cardgame.com official product page via WebSearch; dbzexchange.com listing via WebSearch]
- **Mechanical** — As a dual-face Leader card, SD2-01 uses the DBSCG Awaken mechanic: when the player's life total drops to 3 or less, the card flips to its "Rising Spirit Super Saiyan Son Goku" side, granting a +5000 power bonus during the player's turn. Every DBSCG deck is built around its Leader's Awaken condition — this is the core game loop, not a bonus feature. [DBSCG official rules via WebSearch snippet]
- **IP context** — Son Goku is the primary protagonist of Akira Toriyama's Dragon Ball franchise (Shueisha/Toei Animation). The DBS Card Game is produced by Bandai under license. The base-form advancing pose with one hand extended — visible on this card's front face — is Goku's signature beckoning gesture, recognizable across Dragon Ball Z and Dragon Ball Super. [Bandai DBS Card Game official site via WebSearch]
- **Set context** — Cross Worlds (BT3) was the third main booster set for DBSCG, released March 2018. Its accompanying starter decks introduced new players to the Awaken mechanic with pre-built 51-card decks tuned around a single Leader card's transformation arc. [WebSearch snippet: Amazon BT3 release / Bleeding Cool Cross Worlds checklist]

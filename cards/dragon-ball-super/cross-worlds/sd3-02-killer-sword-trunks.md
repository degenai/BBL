## Trivia

- **Set / design context** — SD3-02 is a Starter Rare from the Dark Invasion starter deck (DBS-SD03), released March 9, 2018 as part of the Cross Worlds expansion. The SD3 deck was built around black-color Warp-cycling strategy, and Killer Sword Trunks is its pivot card: [Xeno-Evolve] (4) plays it in Active Mode by sending a card to the Warp, then its Auto recovers a 3-or-less-cost Battle Card from Warp, generating a recursive loop. [WebSearch: DBS official card search SD3-02 / TCGPlayer Cross Worlds]
- **Set-lore context** — The depicted figure is Trunks: Xeno, not the main-series Future Trunks. Xeno Trunks is a non-canon character exclusive to the Dragon Ball Heroes arcade/promotional card game, whose timeline diverges from the main series after Cell's defeat: he is recruited into the Time Patrol by Chronoa (Supreme Kai of Time) and becomes central protagonist of the Dark Demon Realm Saga — the first original story arc in Dragon Ball Heroes. The card's Special Traits (Saiyan/Earthling), Dark Demon Realm Saga era tag, and [Xeno-Evolve] mechanic all confirm this identification. [WebSearch: Xeno Trunks VS Battles Wiki / CBR Xeno Goku explainer / DBZ Exchange SD3-02]
- **IP qualification** — The `suspected_ip` flag reads 'Future Trunks' but the more precise identity is Trunks: Xeno. The two characters share the same base origin (both are Future Trunks from alternate timelines) but are treated as distinct in the Dragon Ball Heroes property. Recommend updating `suspected_ip` to 'Trunks: Xeno' in frontmatter. [WebSearch: Kanzenshuu Xeno suffix article]

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Cross Worlds (DBS-B03) released March 9, 2018. Uub's card is indexed under the Black Star Dragon Ball Saga era, placing this version of the character in Dragon Ball GT continuity — after five years of training with Goku following the end of Dragon Ball Z's Majin Buu arc `[WebSearch snippet: dbs-cardgame.com BT3-014]`.
- **Design** — BT3-014 is a Common Battle card (Red, 20000 power) with a Double Strike mechanic: it deals 2 damage to the opponent's life area instead of 1 when attacking. The unusually high power number (20000) for a Common reflects Uub's in-universe reputation as one of the strongest human fighters despite his unassuming child appearance `[WebSearch snippet: Hidden Power Uub TCGPlayer]`.
- **Character context** — Uub is the human reincarnation of Kid Buu, the purest form of Majin Buu. King Yemma reincarnated Kid Buu in response to Goku's wish for a worthy future opponent. As a human, Uub retains the Majin's raw power but loses its regeneration and body-manipulation abilities. The card's title "Hidden Power" directly names this premise: a child who outwardly appears ordinary while containing the compressed destructive force of the Majin Buu lineage, reformed and redirected `[dragon-ball-official.com: Weekly Character Showcase Uub; Game Rant: Dragon Ball Who Is Uub]`.

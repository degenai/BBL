## Trivia

- **Set context** — SD2-02 is exclusive to Starter Deck 2: The Extreme Evolution (DBS-SD02), a dedicated red Dragon Ball GT deck released alongside the Cross Worlds booster set (BT3, 2018). The official product page states the deck is "focused on the GT Saga" and includes five starter-exclusive cards — SD2-02 is a Starter Rare (ST rarity) unavailable in BT3 booster packs. `[dbs-cardgame.com/us-en/product/dbs-sd02.php]`
- **Mechanical design** — Red Battle card, 25,000 power, Son Goku: GT character tag. EX-Evolve skill: place 1 card from hand in Drop Area on a red Son Goku: GT with energy cost 4 or more to evolve into this card. When your cards attack, this card gains +5,000 power for the turn; if power reaches 40,000 or more, it gains Triple Strike (deals 3 damage counters instead of 1). The escalating power threshold mirrors the GT narrative arc of Goku pushing past each transformation ceiling. `[WebSearch: thehaventabletop.com SD2-02 listing; WebSearch: Devastation Store SD2-02]`
- **Lore context** — In Dragon Ball GT, Son Goku uses Super Saiyan 3 during the Baby Saga — the arc where Baby (a parasitic Tuffle mutant) possesses most of Earth's population. SS3 proves insufficient; Goku ultimately achieves Super Saiyan 4, GT's signature apex form, during this arc. The Son Goku: GT character tag distinguishes this card from the many Son Goku cards in the Z or DBS continuities. `[WebSearch: Dragon Ball Wiki DBS Card Game article]`
- **Visual note** — Super Saiyan 3 is visually identified by two unique markers absent in all other Super Saiyan tiers: (1) extremely long flowing golden hair reaching below the waist, and (2) completely absent eyebrows. Both are confirmed in the vision pass. These markers make SS3 Son Goku one of the most unambiguously identifiable character-transformation depictions in the franchise. `[Dragon Ball franchise canon — widely documented]`

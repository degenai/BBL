## Trivia

- **Set context** — BT3-058 is an Uncommon foil Battle Card from Cross Worlds (released March 9, 2018), depicting Goku in Super Saiyan 1 — the baseline transformation first achieved against Frieza on Namek. Within BT3, Goku appears across base form (Leader BT3-032), SSJ1 (BT3-058), and SSJ3 (flip side of BT3-032), forming a deliberate transformation ladder within a single set. `[dbs-cardgame.com BT3 card list, via WebSearch]`
- **Foil variant** — The foil treatment on BT3-058 applies shimmer to the golden energy aura effects, making the SSJ energy visually prominent. In the DBS Card Game, foil Uncommons are chase variants seeded at lower frequency than non-foil counterparts — a cosmetic differentiation with no mechanical difference. `[dbs-cardgame.com product page, via WebSearch]`
- **Card title** — "Pressure Assault" names an aggressive attack archetype in DBS-TCG design language. Cards in this framing typically reward forward-facing combat pressure — consistent with SSJ Goku's canonical role as a high-aggression mid-tier fighter. The Uncommon rarity places this in the workhorse slot of a deck rather than the top-end finisher. `[dbs-cardgame.com BT3 card list, via WebSearch]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Unending Awakening (BT3-027) is a red Common Counter card from Cross Worlds (DBS-B03, March 9, 2018). Unlike Battle cards, Counter cards in the DBS Card Game illustrate thematic concepts rather than specific named characters. This card's art depicts the Super Saiyan awakening archetype without tying the figure to any individual character [Source: WebSearch snippets from coretcg.com, trollandtoad.com, dbzexchange.com BT3-027 listings].
- **Mechanical** — The card's skill: "[Counter: Attack] Negate the attack. Then, choose up to 1 of your red Saiyan Leader Cards. It gains +5000 power for the duration of the turn." A universal red Saiyan support counter with no character lock — works with any red Saiyan Leader, making it a flexible generic inclusion [Source: WebSearch snippets from coretcg.com and dbzexchange.com BT3-027 listings].
- **Art archetype** — The screaming child in orange gi with erupting golden aura is one of Dragon Ball's most recognizable visual motifs — the Super Saiyan awakening under extreme emotional stress. Its most famous in-story instance is young Gohan's Super Saiyan 2 breakthrough against Cell (DBZ, 1993). The art evokes the archetype without depicting either Gohan or Goku specifically; the figure is deliberately unnamed in the card's official character field [Source: Dragon Ball Wiki (Fandom): Super Saiyan; WebSearch: card database listings].

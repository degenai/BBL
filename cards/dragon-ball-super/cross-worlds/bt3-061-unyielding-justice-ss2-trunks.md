## Trivia

- **Set context** — Unyielding Justice SS2 Trunks (BT3-061) is a green Uncommon Battle card from Cross Worlds (DBS-B03, March 9, 2018). Official character tag: "Trunks: Future / Saiyan / Earthling," Future Trunks Saga era. Cross Worlds incorporated the Future Trunks arc (from Dragon Ball Super's 2016 anime) as a key narrative thread alongside its introduction of black cards and [Over Realm] [Source: dbs-cardgame.com/europe-en/strategy/cross-worlds/designers-note.php; WebSearch: BT3-061 listings].
- **Mechanical** — BT3-061 is a 20000-power Uncommon Battle card with 5000 combo power — high base power for its rarity tier in BT3, positioning Future Trunks as a legitimate threat piece in green decks. It is tagged for the Future Trunks Saga era, making it searchable by Future Trunks Saga-specific support cards in the DBS Card Game system [Source: WebSearch snippets from manaconfluence.com and tcgplayer.com BT3-061 listings].
- **IP lore** — Future Trunks in Dragon Ball Super achieves Super Saiyan 2 strength during the Goku Black / Zero Mortal Plan arc (DBS anime, 2016-2017). He also develops "Super Saiyan Rage" — a boosted form of SS2 unique to him — used to fight Goku Black and Fused Zamasu. The "Unyielding Justice" title reflects his core motivation: protecting a future timeline already devastated by Androids, then threatened again by Zamasu [Source: CBR: Goku Black & Zamasu's Plan Explained; Dragon Ball Wiki (Fandom): Future Trunks Saga].

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Dr. Myuu is a Machine Mutant and the primary antagonist of the Black Star Dragon Ball Saga and secondary antagonist of the Baby and Super 17 Sagas in Dragon Ball GT. He created Machine Mutants including General Rilldo, Luud, and Giru, with the stated goal of transforming all living beings into Machine Mutants under his control. [Source: dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Dr._Myuu / CBR.com]
- **Design** — The card subtitle "Under Baby's Control" references one of GT's central plot twists: Baby, the parasitic Machine Mutant villain, was ultimately revealed to have created and programmed Dr. Myuu — not the other way around. Myuu appeared to be Baby's creator, but Baby had controlled a host body to engineer Myuu into building Baby a more powerful body for himself. Myuu was killed when Baby suddenly emerged from inside him. [Source: CBR.com / dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Dr._Myuu]
- **Design** — After death, Dr. Myuu reappears in Hell and teams with Dr. Gero to create a duplicate of Android 17. The two Android 17s fuse into Super 17, who then turns on and kills both Gero and Myuu — making Myuu one of the few Dragon Ball GT characters killed twice across different sagas. [Source: dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Dr._Myuu]

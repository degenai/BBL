## Trivia

- **Set context** — Trunks, Bridge to the Future (BT3-062) is a green Common Battle card from Cross Worlds (DBS-B03, March 9, 2018). Official character tag: "Trunks: Future / Saiyan / Earthling," Future Trunks Saga era. It pairs directly with Unyielding Justice SS2 Trunks (BT3-061) in the same set as BT3's two-card Future Trunks representation — base form and Super Saiyan 2 [Source: dbs-cardgame.com card list (BT3-062); dbzexchange.com BT3-062 listing].
- **Mechanical** — BT3-062's skill: "When you play this card, look at up to 2 cards from the top of your deck. Choose up to 1 Saiyan among them and add it to your hand. Then, place the rest of the cards in the Drop Area." A 1-cost, 5000-power deck sculptor that tutors any Saiyan card — not just Future Trunks variants — giving it broader utility than a character-locked searcher [Source: WebSearch snippets from dbzexchange.com and trollandtoad.com BT3-062 listings].
- **Design** — The red scarf and dark jacket are Future Trunks' canonical costume in the Dragon Ball Super Future Trunks arc (DBS anime, 2016-2017) — distinct from his earlier DBZ look (Capsule Corp jacket). "Bridge to the Future" references Trunks' literal function in DBS: he travels via time machine from a destroyed future timeline to the present, serving as the bridge between timelines and the catalyst for the Goku Black arc [Source: CBR: Everything About Future Trunks' Timeline in DBZ; Wikipedia: Dragon Ball Z: The History of Trunks].

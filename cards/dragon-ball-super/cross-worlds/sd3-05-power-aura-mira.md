## Trivia

- **Set/lore context** — Mira is an artificial being created by Towa from the Demon Realm — a composite of Earthling, Demon Realm race, Android components, and Saiyan DNA assembled specifically to become the strongest being in the universe. His goal of collecting Goku's DNA drives the main plot of the Xenoverse games. He is the Demon King successor to Dabura. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Mira, via WebSearch]`
- **Design** — Mira was confirmed as a Toriyama-designed character by Dragon Ball Xenoverse producer Masayuki Hirano. He originated in Dragon Ball Online (2010) and served as the final boss of both Xenoverse 1 and Xenoverse 2's main stories. The teal chest emblem visible on this card is consistent across all canonical Mira appearances. `[CBR: 10 Things About Mira, via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical** — As SD3-05, Mira is the fifth card in the Dark Invasion starter deck — positioned as the deck's power-declaration finisher after Trunks (SD3-03) and Masked Saiyan (SD3-04) establish the board. The deck's structure mirrors the Xenoverse narrative: Masked Saiyan as the threat that draws out heroes, Mira as the final boss behind it. `[WebSearch: DBS SD3 Dark Invasion product listing; WebSearch: Series 3 roundup strategy page]`

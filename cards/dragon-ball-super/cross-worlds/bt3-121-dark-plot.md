## Trivia

- **Mechanical** — Dark Plot (BT3-121) is a Black-color Extra card in Cross Worlds. Its skill: [Activate Main]: Choose up to 1 Battle Card in your Warp with an energy cost of 4 or less and play it. If you use this skill to play a Battle Card with [Over Realm], switch up to 3 of your energy to Active Mode — a recursion engine for the Over Realm mechanic central to the set's Dark Demon Realm strategy. `[WebSearch: PriceCharting BT3-121; WebSearch: CoreTCG BT3-121 listing]`
- **Set/lore context** — The title "Dark Plot" names the Time Breakers' central scheme across Dragon Ball Online and the Xenoverse series: Towa and Mira's plan to collect warrior energy across historical timelines in order to break the Demon Realm seal. Card BT3-121 is positioned in the final stretch of the BT3 booster directly after BT3-115 (Towa) and BT3-116 (Mira), placing the plot-naming card after its architects. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Towa, via WebSearch; WebSearch: DBS BT3 black cards strategy page]`
- **Design** — BT3-121 is a Black Extra card — consistent with the Dark Demon Realm Saga's color identity in the DBSCG, where black represents the Dark Empire faction. The SD3 "Dark Invasion" starter deck released alongside BT3 uses the same black/green pairing and centers the same narrative arc. `[WebSearch: DBS SD3 Dark Invasion product listing]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — BT3-007 is a Common Red Battle card in Dragon Ball Super Card Game set Cross Worlds (DBS-B03), released March 9, 2018. Character is tagged "Son Goku: GT" in the Black Star Dragon Ball Saga era — the opening arc of Dragon Ball GT where adult Goku is reverted to a child body and travels the universe with Pan and Trunks hunting the Black Star Dragon Balls. [Source: dbs-cardgame.com card list]
- **Mechanical** — The card's skill tutors another Son Goku: GT card with 5000 or less power from the deck at the end of a combo battle, functioning as a search engine for GT Goku chains. Power is 10000 at 2 red energy cost with 1 combo energy. [Source: DBZ Exchange product listing BT3-007 / dbs-cardgame.com]
- **Design** — Cross Worlds was the first DBS card game set to integrate Dragon Ball GT characters at scale alongside DBS-era characters. The set's designer note cites cross-franchise deck construction diversity as the core design goal, with the GT's Black Star Dragon Ball Saga and the Dark Demon Realm Saga represented together in a single 135-card set. [Source: dbs-cardgame.com designer's note / WebSearch snippet]

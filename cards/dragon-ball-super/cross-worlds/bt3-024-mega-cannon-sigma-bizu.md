## Trivia

- **Set context** — BT3 Cross Worlds was explicitly designed to support a Dragon Ball GT red deck built around Saiyans and Machine Mutants. The set's designer notes name "a red GT deck (Saiyan and Machine Mutant)" as one of four major deck archetypes, with GT-specific support cards to enable theme-focused builds. Bizu and the Sigma Force are central to the Machine Mutant half of that GT identity. `[dbs-cardgame.com: Cross Worlds Designer's Note, via WebSearch]`
- **Character** — Bizu is one of four Machine Mutants comprising the Sigma Force — General Rilldo's elite mercenary unit on Planet M-2. The four members (Commander Nezi, Bizu, Ribet, and Natt) can merge their bodies into the Sigma Force Cannon (Super Mega Cannon Sigma), with a combined power level of 10,000,000 per Dragon Ball Z: Scouter Battle Taikan Kamehameha. Bizu's name derives from "vise," a piece of construction hardware. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Sigma Force, via WebSearch]`
- **Narrative role** — Despite the Sigma Force's intimidating fusion power, Bizu and his unit are dispatched by Super Saiyan Goku, after which General Rilldo absorbs their remains to boost his own power. The Sigma Force function as a prologue threat in GT, establishing Machine Mutant danger before Rilldo and Dr. Myuu become the main focus. `[CBR: Dragon Ball GT Villains Beaten Too Easily, via WebSearch]`
- **Design note** — The card title "Mega Cannon Sigma, Bizu" names the individual member (Bizu) within the Mega Cannon Sigma subgroup, distinguishing it from the full Sigma Force fusion. This suggests Bandai designed individual-member cards to support the Machine Mutant fusion mechanic — consistent with BT3's [EX-Evolve] keyword, which evolves individual pieces into combined titans. `[dbs-cardgame.com: Cross Worlds Designer's Note, via WebSearch]`

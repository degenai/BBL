## Trivia

- **Set context** — SD2-05 originates in DBS-SD02 "The Extreme Evolution" starter deck (January 2020) and was reprinted in Mythic Booster (MB-01) with new alternate art and a gold stamp. The Mythic Booster's 63-card reprint pool covered starter decks, promos, and early main set cards, making it the primary secondary-market access point for players who missed the original starter printings. `[dbs-cardgame.com: Mythic Booster MB-01 product page; TotalCards.net: MB-01 Chain Attack Trunks SD2-05]`
- **Mechanical** — Red Battle card, Power 20,000, cost 4 Red. Two skills: (1) this card can attack Battle Cards in Active Mode — removing the standard Rest Mode restriction — and (2) when played, the controller may play a Battle Card with power 15,000 or less from hand. The on-play chaining ability is the card's name-giving trait: a single play action deploys two units, generating immediate field presence and the pressure of an above-curve 20,000-power body that can swing into Active Mode targets. `[TotalCards.net: Dragon Ball Super MB01 Chain Attack Trunks SD2-05; WebSearch: Mythic Booster SD2-05 card details]`
- **Design** — Future Trunks is the time-traveling warrior from a destroyed future timeline who travels to the present in DBS episodes 47-76 (the "Future Trunks arc") seeking help against Goku Black and Fused Zamasu. His Super Galick Gun and the Father-Son Galick Gun (combining his blast with Vegeta's in episode 65) are the arc's most referenced combat moments. The night-void setting with electric lightning aura in the card art is visually consistent with the Future Trunks arc's apocalyptic-future aesthetic. `[CBR: Future Trunks' Life in Dragon Ball Z and Super; ComicBook.com: Dragon Ball Super Debuts Vegeta and Trunks Father-Son Attack]`

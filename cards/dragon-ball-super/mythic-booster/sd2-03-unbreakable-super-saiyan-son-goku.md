## Trivia

- **Set context** — SD2-03 originates in DBS-SD02 "The Extreme Evolution" starter deck (January 2020) and was reprinted in Mythic Booster (MB-01), which collects 63 reprints from starter decks, promos, and pre-Unison Warrior boosters — each with new alternate art and a gold stamp. MB-01 contains the first Secret Rare reprints in DBS Card Game history, making it a notable milestone release as well as an accessibility product. `[dbs-cardgame.com: Mythic Booster MB-01 product page; TrollAndToad: Dragon Ball Super Mythic Booster MB-01]`
- **Mechanical** — The card carries Barrier, preventing it from being targeted by the opponent's skills on the turn it is played. Retailer research notes it functions as a setup piece for EX-Evolve chains into higher-cost Goku cards — the Barrier buys the turn needed for the evolution payoff to come online. The "Unbreakable" subtitle maps exactly to this function: a body opponents cannot touch when it arrives. `[TotalCards.net: MB-01 Unbreakable Super Saiyan Son Goku SD2-03]`
- **Design** — The art layers a blue energy element into the golden Super Saiyan aura — visually ambiguous between standard Super Saiyan and Super Saiyan Blue. The card's collector number (SD2-03 from the "Extreme Evolution" starter) anchors it as standard Super Saiyan mechanically. The mixed-aura aesthetic may be an intentional design nod toward Goku's further transformation potential, consistent with the "Extreme Evolution" deck's GT-era theme of constant growth. `[TotalCards.net: SD2-03 listing; dbs-cardgame.com: DBS-SD02 product page]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — P-035 is a promotional Battle card from Official Tournament Pack Vol.3 — CROSS WORLDS, distributed at sanctioned Dragon Ball Super Card Game tournaments starting March 9, 2018. Like other Judge Promotion cards, it was distributed to certified judges via tournament kits and is not available through retail — secondary market only for players. [WebSearch: DBS card game promo search results; progamingcrew.com DBS Judge cards explained]
- **Mechanical design** — Yellow Battle card, power 20000, traits: Saiyan / Great Ape. Ability: during your turn, at the end of a battle in which this card is used as a combo, evolve a `<Bardock>` card in your Battle Area into this card — representing Bardock's mid-battle transformation into the Great Ape form. [WebSearch: DBS P-035 Bardock promo ability text snippet]
- **Great Ape (Oozaru) lore** — Bardock's Oozaru transformation is depicted in the Dragon Ball Z: Bardock — The Father of Goku TV special (1990, Toei). In the opening sequence Bardock and his squad operate as Great Apes; Bardock is portrayed as unusual in retaining full memory and awareness during the transformation, distinguishing him from most Saiyans who go berserk. [WebSearch: Dragon Ball Wiki Great Ape transformation; Wikipedia: Bardock]
- **Canon shift** — Bardock's canonical status was substantially revised by Dragon Ball Super: Broly (2018), which adopted the Dragon Ball Minus version of his story (a father concerned for his son, not a soldier-prophet) as the new official canon. P-035 was released in the Cross Worlds tournament window (March 2018) — precisely when this canonical transition was occurring — drawing from the pre-Broly-film Bardock Saga framing. [WebSearch: ComicBook.com — How Does Dragon Ball Super: Broly Retcon Bardock?]

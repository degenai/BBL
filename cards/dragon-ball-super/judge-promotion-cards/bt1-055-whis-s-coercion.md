## Trivia

- **Set context** — BT1-055 originates from Galactic Battle (DBS-B01), the launch set of the Dragon Ball Super Card Game (July 2017). The copy in inventory is the foil Judge Promotion version, distributed to sanctioned tournament judges starting June 21, 2019, and later included in the Special Anniversary Box DBS-BE06. The base print was also reprinted in Starter Deck 12 (Spirit of Potara). `[TrollAndToad listing; DBS-cardgame.com, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Mechanical context** — As an Extra card with Counter:Attack, Whis's Coercion negates an opponent's attack and switches up to one of your energy cards to Active Mode. Counter:Attack cards are reactive hand-traps in the DBS TCG; the energy untap bonus is unusually efficient for a Common rarity card. `[CoreTCG listing; DBS-cardgame.com, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Character lore** — Whis is the Guide Angel Attendant of Universe 7's God of Destruction Beerus, and also serves as Beerus's martial arts teacher. He is a child of the Grand Minister and was trained by his older sister Vados (Angel Attendant of Universe 6), who was reportedly the stronger sibling during their early training — though Whis later expressed uncertainty about whether that gap persisted. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Whis, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Lore note — Angels as structural coercers** — Angels in Dragon Ball Super exist outside the cycle of life and death and are permanently in a state of Ultra Instinct. The card title "Coercion" reflects Whis's functional role: he does not merely advise Beerus; he constrains and trains him, acting as supervisor over a deity whose destruction could destabilize his universe. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Whis, via WebSearch snippet]`

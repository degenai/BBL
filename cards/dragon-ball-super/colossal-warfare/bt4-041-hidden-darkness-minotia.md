## Trivia

- **Lore: the sacrificed brother** — Minotia is Tapion's younger brother and fellow Konatsian warrior. He carried the lower half of Hirudegarn sealed within his body. Unlike Tapion, Minotia was found and freed by Hoi on the unnamed distant planet he was dispatched to, then killed when Hirudegarn's lower half materialized and crushed him — making his death the film's inciting pivot that allows Hirudegarn's lower half to fully manifest `[Wikipedia: Dragon Ball Z: Wrath of the Dragon]` `[WebSearch: Minotia Dragon Ball Z lore]`.
- **Lore: strategic target** — Hoi's decision to free and kill Minotia first was calculated: killing the younger, presumably less combat-ready brother broke the lower-half seal and allowed partial reassembly of Hirudegarn without yet triggering Tapion's upper-half defense. Minotia's death is the domino that drives the entire film's threat `[Villains Wiki: Hoi]` `[WebSearch: Hoi Kashvar Wrath of the Dragon]`.
- **Set design** — Minotia is a minor character who appears briefly in the film and dies early — limited screen time relative to Tapion. His inclusion as a Common (BT4-041) in a tight Saga cluster (cards no. 039 through 042) alongside three other Wrath of the Dragon characters signals intentional completionism in Colossal Warfare's coverage of the film `[Bleeding Cool: Colossal Warfare checklist]`.

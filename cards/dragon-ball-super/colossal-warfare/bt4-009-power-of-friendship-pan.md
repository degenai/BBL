## Trivia

- **Mechanical design** — BT4-009 is a red Common Battle card: 20000 power, 5000 combo power, 5-red energy cost. Auto ability on play: look at the top 5 cards of your deck, choose up to 1 Battle Card with 15000 or less power bearing GT in the character name, and play it — a direct tutor for any GT-named card. This makes Pan a toolbox piece in GT aggro: paying 5 energy to search and deploy a GT Battle Card for free. Trait: Saiyan/Earthling, Era: Baby Saga. `[Source: WebSearch: TCGPlayer product page BT4-009]`
- **Character lore** — Pan is the daughter of Son Gohan and Videl, and the granddaughter of Son Goku — making her a quarter-Saiyan, one of very few in the franchise. She was introduced as a small child in the final chapters of Dragon Ball Z and becomes a main protagonist in Dragon Ball GT's opening space arc alongside Goku and Trunks. Notably she cannot achieve Super Saiyan form despite her Saiyan lineage, a creative choice that has generated substantial fan discussion. `[Source: dragon-ball-official.com: Weekly Character Showcase Pan; Wikipedia: Dragon Ball GT]`
- **Set context** — Pan is one of a handful of BT4 protagonists who evaded Baby's possession entirely during the Baby Saga. Her tutor ability in the DBS TCG — fetching GT allies — directly models her in-story role as the practical, resourceful member of the GT trio who keeps the team together while Goku and Trunks provide raw power. `[Source: WebSearch: Baby Dragon Ball GT lore; TCGPlayer BT4-009 product data]`

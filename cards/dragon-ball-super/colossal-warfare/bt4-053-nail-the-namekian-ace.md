## Trivia

- **IP context — Nail** — Nail served as Grand Elder Guru's personal bodyguard and was the strongest Namekian warrior on Planet Namek during the Frieza Saga. After being mortally wounded stalling Frieza, he proposed merging with Piccolo. The Namekian assimilation is permanent: Piccolo absorbed Nail's energy, knowledge, and fighting skill, gaining enough power to challenge Frieza's second form and force a transformation to the third. Nail remains a background presence within Piccolo's consciousness in subsequent arcs. `[Dragon Ball Wiki / Nailicy.com: Piccolo Nail fusion status via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical design** — BT4-053 is a Rare Green Battle card with 10,000 power. Its Auto Bond 2 skill draws 1 card and grants +5,000 power per attack when 2 or more Battle Cards are in play. The official BT4 strategy page singles this card out and advises playing a 1-cost Namekian on turn 1 to activate Bond immediately — positioning Nail as the engine card in the BT4 Namekian deck. `[dbs-cardgame.com: Series 4 Green Strategy Page]`
- **Set context — consecutive Namekian block** — BT4's green color was officially described as "mostly focused on Namekians and Lord Slug Saga." The run BT4-052 through BT4-057 forms a complete Namekian hierarchy in one numbered block: Guru (patriarch), Nail (warrior ace), Kami (Earth-guardian), Dende (healer), Popo (aide), Saonel (Universe 6 counterpart). Nail at BT4-053 is the warrior core of that hierarchy. `[dbs-cardgame.com: Series 4 Green Strategy Page]`

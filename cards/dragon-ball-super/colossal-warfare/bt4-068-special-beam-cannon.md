## Trivia

- **Set context** — Special Beam Cannon (BT4-068) is a green Uncommon from Colossal Warfare (DBS-B04), the fourth main DBS Card Game booster, released July 2018. BT4's design concept introduced the [Bond] keyword specifically for Namekian characters, making Piccolo a first-class archetype anchor in this set [Source: dbs-cardgame.com/asia/strategy/series4/design-concept.php].
- **Move lore** — The technique's Japanese name is Makankosappo (魔貫光殺砲), meaning "Demon's Penetrating, Killing Light Gun." Piccolo developed it after the 23rd Tenkaichi Budokai intending to use it against Goku; he instead deployed it against Raditz in Dragon Ball Z episode 4 (first aired May 17, 1989). The signature two-beam structure — one straight, one coiling around it — is exactly what the card art depicts [Source: Dragon Ball Wiki (Fandom): Special Beam Cannon].
- **Mechanical** — Card skill text reads: "[Activate: Battle] If your Leader Card is Piccolo, it gains +10000 power for the duration of the battle, then choose up to 1 card in your opponent's hand and place it in their Drop Area." The power boost is Piccolo-Leader-locked, mechanically encoding the idea that only a Piccolo-piloted deck can properly harness the technique [Source: WebSearch snippets from levelupgamesmd.com and bigorbitcards.co.uk product listings].

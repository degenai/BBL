## Trivia

- **Mechanical design** — BT4-012 is a green Uncommon Battle card: 5000 base power, 5000 combo power, 1-green energy cost. Auto ability: when this card attacks, you may add a life card to hand; if you do, the card gains +10000 power (to 15000) and a Critical-analog life-to-Drop-Area effect for the turn. This life-trade mechanic rewards calculated aggression — you burn a life buffer to spike power and pressure. Character: Trunks: GT, Trait: Saiyan/Earthling, Era: Baby Saga. `[Source: WebSearch: DBZ Exchange BT4-012; TrollAndToad product page]`
- **Design / print history** — BT4-012 was also distributed as a promo in the 2019 DBS TCG Event Pack 03, bearing a PR stamp rather than the standard Uncommon frame. The Event Pack version has a separate product listing distinct from the Colossal Warfare booster printing. `[Source: WebSearch: CoreTCG Event Pack 03 listing; Big Orbit Cards promo listing]`
- **Character lore** — Trunks in Dragon Ball GT is the teenage son of Vegeta and Bulma who has become President of Capsule Corporation — though he does not take the role seriously. He joins Goku and Pan on the space mission to retrieve the Black Star Dragon Balls at the start of GT. A notable creation fact: Akira Toriyama himself did not know who Future Trunks was when that character debuted in Dragon Ball Z — he decided Trunks was Vegeta and Bulma's son only afterward, a reveal that reportedly shocked his editorial team. `[Source: Screen Rant: 'Not Even Dragon Ball's Creator Knew Who Future Trunks Was When He Debuted'; Wikipedia: Trunks (Dragon Ball)]`

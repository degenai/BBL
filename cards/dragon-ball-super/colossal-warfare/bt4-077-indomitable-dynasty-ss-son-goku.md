## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Colossal Warfare (BT4) introduced the Swap mechanic to the Dragon Ball Super Card Game: return a card to hand and immediately play a stronger card for reduced energy. Goku's Lineage archetype was among the primary beneficiaries, enabling a chain of escalating Goku forms. BT4-077 is a mid-chain enabler — not a board threat, but a value link to more powerful Goku cards. [WebSearch: DBS BT4 Swap mechanic]
- **Mechanical** — BT4-077: Uncommon Yellow, 3 cost, 15000 power. Swap 4 allows swapping this card from play for a "Goku's Lineage" card costing 4 from hand. The card is also immune to opponent card selection effects. At $0.04 market value it is among the lowest-priced uncommons in the set, indicating the Swap chain has been power-crept by later BT sets. [DBZ Exchange: BT4-077; card frontmatter market_price]
- **Design** — The "Indomitable Dynasty" title framing is consistent with BT4's Goku cluster, which includes Unbroken Dynasty Son Goku (BT4-079) and Deadly Golden Great Ape Son Goku (BT4-080) — a three-card mini-cycle covering Super Saiyan, standard, and Great Ape forms of Goku across the same set. [Colossal Warfare checklist: bleedingcool.com; card frontmatter]

## Connections

- [[son-goku]] — SS-form Goku slotted into the BT4 *Goku's Lineage* yellow Swap-chain mechanic — Bandai's card-game-mechanical encoding of the canonical Goku family-tree (Bardock → Goku across all incarnations → Gohan → Goten → Pan). BT4-077's [Swap 4] is the mid-chain intermediate: a value-link that returns to hand and pulls a higher-cost Goku's Lineage card forward, sitting in the Swap escalation between BT4-079 base-Goku ([Swap 2]) and BT4-080 Golden Great Ape ([Swap 5]). The "Dynasty" subtitle is the cluster's connective tissue — three Goku forms inside a designed-as-one mini-cycle (Unbroken / Indomitable / Deadly Golden Great Ape). `[Source: dbs-cardgame.com BT4 strategy: yellow Goku's Lineage]`

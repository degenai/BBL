## Trivia

- **Set context** — Colossal Warfare (BT4) was released July 2018 as the fourth main DBS card game booster set. Its primary antagonist theme is the Xenoverse villain Demigra; heroes are organized around a "Goku's Lineage" sub-archetype tracing Goku's family from Bardock onward. BT4 introduced keyword skills including [Bond], [Swap], [Deflect], and [Dark Over Realm]. `[Bleeding Cool: Colossal Warfare Checklist]` `[dbs-cardgame.com design-concept page]`
- **Mechanical design** — BT4-030 is a Super Rare Blue Battle card with 20,000 power and a 4(B) energy cost. Its Activate: Main skill pulls cards from the player's life zone to hand, scaling power by total energy count, and grants Triple Strike when 5 or more energy are in play — a late-game finisher built around sacrificing safety reserves for burst damage. The "At All Costs" title is a mechanical metaphor for Vegeta's recurring narrative arc of staking his own life for power. `[DBZ Exchange: BT4-030]`
- **IP verified** — Super Saiyan Blue (SSGSS) is Vegeta's form combining Super Saiyan transformation with god-ki mastery achieved through training with Beerus and Whis. In Dragon Ball Super's Universe Survival arc — the era BT4 draws from — Blue-form Vegeta is a primary protagonist in the Tournament of Power. The electric-blue and gold aura in the art is the canonical visual signature of the SSB form. `[CBR / Dragon Ball Wiki via WebSearch]`

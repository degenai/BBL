## Trivia

- **IP context — Mr. Popo** — Mr. Popo is a djinn who has served Earth's Guardian for hundreds of years; by Dragon Ball Z he was over 1,000 years old. His duties at the Lookout include training warriors, tending the palace grounds, supporting the current Guardian, and reconstructing Shenron when needed. He is not Namekian — his species is unspecified in canon — distinguishing him from the Namekian Kami he serves alongside. He uses a Magic Carpet for transport and can teleport anywhere on Earth while seated on it. `[Wikipedia: Mr. Popo via WebSearch]`
- **Setting — Kami's Lookout** — The white dome architecture in the background is the Lookout, floating high above Earth. Kami trained Goku there before the 23rd World Martial Arts Tournament. BT4-054 "Kami, the Watcher" shows Kami individually at the same sky-elevation location, while BT4-056 shows the duo together with the dome's architecture foregrounded — a split-card representation of a single canonical place. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Kami's Lookout via WebSearch]`
- **Design note — duo Common** — BT4-056 is one of the few cards in the BT4 Namekian block to depict two named characters simultaneously. The placement of the duo card at BT4-056, adjacent to Kami (BT4-054) and Saonel (BT4-057), anchors the Popo-Kami relationship in the consecutive print order, reinforcing the Lookout as a thematic anchor point for the block. `[dbs-cardgame.com: BT4 card list via WebSearch]`

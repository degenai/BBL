## Trivia

- **IP context — Mystic/Ultimate form** — Gohan's Ultimate (also called Mystic or Potential Unleashed) form does not involve a visible transformation — the hair stays dark and no aura color signals the shift. The power was unlocked by Old Kai through a ritual lasting over 25 hours. At debut in Dragon Ball Z's Buu Saga, Ultimate Gohan's power exceeded Super Saiyan 3 Goku's. The "Newfound Power" title and the large energy sphere in the art directly reference this latent-potential awakening. `[CBR: Mystic Gohan Explained]`
- **Set context** — Colossal Warfare (BT4, July 2018) mixed Dragon Ball Super's Universe Survival Saga with classic DBZ arc characters. The green cluster was explicitly built around Namekians and Piccolo fusion lineage; Gohan's Ultimate form fits as a non-Namekian but Piccolo-adjacent hero whose power trajectory (Guru's unlock on Namek, then Old Kai's unlock) mirrors the Namekian theme of power bestowal. `[dbs-cardgame.com: Series 4 Green Strategy Page]`
- **Design note** — BT4-048 is an Uncommon, placing Ultimate Gohan at a lower rarity than Vegeta (Super Rare, BT4-030) within the same set. The rarity gap reflects the card game's combat role hierarchy: Vegeta is a finisher bomb, Gohan is a mid-range support piece. `[dbs-cardgame.com BT4 cardlist via WebSearch]`

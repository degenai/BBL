## Trivia

- **Set/lore context** — Hirudegarn is described in Dragon Ball lore as a "Phantom Majin" — a supernatural creature of destruction rather than a natural organism — created or transformed by the Kashvar, an alien race of evil sorcerers who used it as an extinction-level weapon against all non-Kashvar life `[Villains Wiki: Hirudegarn]` `[Wikipedia: Dragon Ball Z: Wrath of the Dragon]`.
- **Two forms** — This card's insectoid armored mass depicts Hirudegarn's first (pre-metamorphosis) form: a towering behemoth with skull-like head, long tail, and armored carapace. After metamorphosis, the creature gains insect wings, horns, and shoulder blades — that second form is shown on BT4-038 (Hirudegarn, the Wanderer). The "Impenetrable Defense" title reflects the first form's mechanical role in DBS card game play, where it is described as nearly impossible to defeat once summoned `[Villains Wiki: Hirudegarn]` `[WebSearch: DBS BT4 design concept]`.
- **Sealed in two brothers** — Hirudegarn was bisected by an enchanted sword and its upper half sealed within Tapion and lower half within Minotia — meaning this card depicts the partially-reassembled form after Hoi freed Hirudegarn's lower half from Minotia's body. Minotia's death was the trigger for partial reassembly `[Wikipedia: Dragon Ball Z: Wrath of the Dragon]` `[WebSearch: Minotia Hirudegarn sealed halves]`.

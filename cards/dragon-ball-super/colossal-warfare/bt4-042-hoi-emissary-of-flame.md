## Trivia

- **Lore: last of the Kashvar** — Hoi is the secondary antagonist of DBZ: Wrath of the Dragon and the last surviving member of the Kashvar — an alien race of evil sorcerers who deemed themselves superior to all other species. His stated goal is to use Hirudegarn to "cleanse" the universe of all non-Kashvars `[Villains Wiki: Hoi]` `[WebSearch: Hoi Kashvar villain role]`.
- **Lore: manipulation over force** — Hoi's scheme is entirely based on deception: he manipulates the Z-Fighters into opening Tapion's Music Box by fabricating a cover story, presenting himself as a desperate victim seeking heroes' help. He is not a fighter — he uses others as instruments, making his "Emissary" framing apt: he operates through ceremony and proxy rather than direct combat `[Villains Wiki: Hoi]` `[Wikipedia: Dragon Ball Z: Wrath of the Dragon]`.
- **Lore: ironic death** — After engineering Hirudegarn's full resurrection, Hoi is killed by the monster itself — inadvertently crushed underfoot — ending the Kashvar bloodline entirely. The creature he summoned to be his instrument of genocide becomes his executioner, leaving Hirudegarn without even the nominal "control" Hoi claimed to have `[Villains Wiki: Hoi]` `[WebSearch: Hoi death Hirudegarn crushes]`.

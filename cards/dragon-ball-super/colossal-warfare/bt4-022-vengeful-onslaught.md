## Trivia

- **Character lore** — In Dragon Ball GT's Baby Saga, Baby first targets Trunks as one of his early major host victims before ultimately possessing Vegeta. The confrontation depicted — youth fighter vs. blue parasite form — reflects the early arc phase before Baby achieves his most powerful Vegeta possession. [Source: Dragon Ball Villains Wiki: Baby]
- **Design** — Baby's blue-skinned Tuffle Parasite form is his natural body — small, lean, blue — before he enters and amplifies his host's physique. The design contrast between Baby's compact alien form and Trunks's humanoid Saiyan build efficiently codes the parasite-vs-host dynamic in a single frame. [Source: Dragon Ball Villains Wiki: Baby]
- **Set context** — Colossal Warfare (DBS-B04, July 2018) designates Trunks: GT cards with Baby Saga era labels, making Trunks a supporting card type in Baby-themed deck strategies — the card game mechanically encodes the GT storyline's Trunks-as-early-target narrative into deck-building dynamics. [Source: Bleeding Cool: Colossal Warfare Checklist]

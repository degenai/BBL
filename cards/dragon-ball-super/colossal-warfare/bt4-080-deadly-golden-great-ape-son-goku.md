## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — The Golden Great Ape is a Dragon Ball GT-exclusive transformation: a Saiyan with a tail looks at a full moon or full planetary body while already a Super Saiyan, producing golden fur instead of standard brown. In GT's Baby Saga, Old Kai regrows Goku's tail so he can access this form — controlling the transformation leads to Super Saiyan 4. BT4-080's card text requires the "Son Goku : GT" Leader and designates the Baby Saga era, locking this card to GT-specific deck builds. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Golden Great Ape (via WebSearch); DBZ Exchange: BT4-080]
- **Mechanical** — BT4-080 carries 25000 power (the highest among BT4 Goku commons), a Swap 5 for Goku's Lineage cards, and a tutor on play: search your deck for a Goku's Lineage card with cost 5 or less and add it to hand. The Leader restriction ("cannot be played unless Leader is Son Goku : GT") makes it a hard GT-deck-only card — unusable in general Goku's Lineage builds. [DBZ Exchange: BT4-080; getthosemons.co.nz: BT4-080]
- **Design** — BT4-080 sits at the end of the BT4 Goku cluster (BT4-077 SS Goku, BT4-079 base Goku, BT4-080 Golden Great Ape), functioning as the cluster's apex common at 25000 power with the highest Swap cost. It previews the GT transformation chain at common rarity for players who cannot pull the Secret Rare Super Saiyan 4 cards in the same set. [Cardmarket: Colossal Warfare set page; WebSearch: BT4 set details]

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Unbroken Dynasty Son Goku (BT4-079) is a yellow Common from Colossal Warfare (DBS-B04, July 2018), officially tagged as character "Son Goku: Childhood / Saiyan / Goku's Lineage." BT4's yellow cards were purpose-designed around Goku's Lineage — tracing the family line from Bardock through child Goku outward — making this card a structural entry-point for the set's dynasty theme [Source: dbs-cardgame.com/asia/strategy/series4/design-concept.php].
- **Mechanical** — The card's [Swap 2] skill lets a player swap into any "Goku's Lineage" card with energy cost 2 from hand. BT4 introduced [Swap] as a new keyword designed to chain Goku's Lineage cards for deck consistency and hand cycling. Child Goku functions as the lineage's opening link — the dynasty begins here [Source: dbs-cardgame.com/us-en/strategy/series4/yellow.php; WebSearch snippet from alterealitygames.com BT4-079 listing].
- **Design** — The staff depicted is the Nyoibō (Power Pole), a gift from Goku's adoptive grandfather Gohan and present from Dragon Ball chapter 1 onward. Goku's childhood design is itself rooted in Sun Wukong from the Chinese novel Journey to the West — Toriyama adapted the staff-wielding monkey king into a child with a monkey tail [Source: CBR: Goku's Power Pole trivia; Wikipedia: Goku].

## Trivia

- **Set context** — BT4 Colossal Warfare's official design concept states: "In the world of Colossal Warfare, the vile Xenoverse villain Demigra leads an army of colossal foes against our heroes." Demigra is the narrative anchor of the entire set — the set is named after the scale of his assault [Source: dbs-cardgame.com/asia/strategy/series4/design-concept.php].
- **IP lore** — Demigra originates from the 2015 video game Dragon Ball Xenoverse as its primary antagonist. He is a demonic magician imprisoned in the Crack of Time for 75 million years by the Supreme Kai of Time (Chronoa). His stated goal: destroy history and claim the title of God of Time. BT4 is one of the earliest mainline DBS card game sets to center a Xenoverse-exclusive villain as the primary faction antagonist [Source: Dragon Ball Wiki (Fandom): Demigra; Villains Wiki: Demigra].
- **Mechanical** — A linked Temporal Darkness Demigra card in BT4 features the new [Dark Over Realm] keyword — a more powerful version of BT3's [Over Realm] where played cards are not sent to the Warp at end of turn, mechanically representing Demigra's dominion over time and history. Heavenly Wizard Demigra (BT4-107) slots into the Demigra-leader black deck strategy introduced in this set [Source: dbs-cardgame.com/asia/strategy/series4/design-concept.php via WebSearch].

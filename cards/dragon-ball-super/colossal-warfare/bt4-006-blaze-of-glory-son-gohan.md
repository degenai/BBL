## Trivia

- **Mechanical design** — BT4-006 is a red Common Battle card: 15000 power, 5000 combo power, 2-red energy cost. Auto ability: when this card attacks or combos, if the Leader is red, place up to 3 cards from the top of your deck into your Drop Area — a self-mill trigger that feeds Drop Area-based strategies in the red Baby Saga shell. Trait: Saiyan/Earthling. `[Source: WebSearch: DBZ Exchange BT4-006; TrollAndToad product page]`
- **Set context / character lore** — Son Gohan's card trait of Saiyan/Earthling reflects his hybrid biology: half-Saiyan (from Goku) and half-human (from Chi-Chi). This dual-race trait is shared across BT4's "hybrid lineage" cluster: Gohan (BT4-006), Pan (BT4-009 — quarter-Saiyan), and Trunks (BT4-012 — half-Saiyan/Earthling). In Dragon Ball GT, Gohan is notably de-powered relative to his Cell Saga peak, having retired from training to pursue academia. `[Source: Wikipedia: Dragon Ball GT; WebSearch: DBZ Exchange BT4-006 / BT4-009 trait data]`
- **Character lore** — Gohan is the only GT-era Saiyan in BT4's red pool to not achieve Super Saiyan during the Baby Saga arc in the anime — his appearance here marks his role as a possessed Z Fighter (Baby took Gohan as a host before upgrading to Vegeta). His appearance on a hero-coded card rather than a Baby-possessed card reflects the TCG's choice to depict pre-possession Gohan. `[Source: Wikipedia: Dragon Ball GT; WebSearch: Baby Saga Dragon Ball Wiki]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Dark Kamehameha (BT4-121) is a black Uncommon Extra card from Colossal Warfare (DBS-B04, July 2018). The card is Mira-Leader-locked: its full effect is "If your Leader Card is Mira, it gains +15000 power for the duration of the battle, and if it's your turn, choose up to 1 of your opponent's Battle Cards with energy cost 4 or less and send it to their Warp." It is a high-impact support piece for the BT4 Time Breakers faction [Source: WebSearch snippets from levelupgamesmd.com and coretcg.com BT4-121 listings].
- **IP note** — Vision flagged the depicted figure as "Corrupted Goku or dark-controlled fighter" at medium confidence. The Mira Leader lock in the card text is decisive: this is a Time Breakers / Mira-faction card, not a Goku card. The white hair matches Mira's canonical silver-white design, not any Goku transformation. The "Dark Kamehameha" in Xenoverse lore is a technique deployed by fighters under dark-energy / Time Breaker mind control — the move is not Goku's even if it shares the Kamehameha name [Source: WebSearch: Demigra/Time Breakers lore via villains.fandom.com; BT4-121 card text].
- **Mechanical** — BT4 introduced [Dark Over Realm] as an upgrade to BT3's [Over Realm], attached to Demigra and his Time Breaker faction. Dark Kamehameha is the faction's high-burst Extra: +15000 power plus free removal of any 4-cost-or-less opponent Battle Card on your turn when Mira leads. It is one of the more efficient Extra cards in BT4's black pool [Source: dbs-cardgame.com/asia/strategy/series4/design-concept.php via WebSearch; BT4-121 card text].

## Trivia

- **IP canonicalization** — The Masked Saiyan is Xeno Bardock — Goku's father — captured and placed under Time Breaker mind control by Towa after his battle with Chilled. Towa's mask delivers a steady supply of Dark Ki to keep him subjugated; breaking the mask frees him. This identity is established across Dragon Ball Online, Dragon Ball Heroes (God Mission 1), and Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Xeno Bardock, via WebSearch]`
- **Set context** — The "Time Trauma" subtitle is Bandai's card-game naming for this form, not a canonical lore title. It reflects the character's function: Bardock is weaponized against timelines he fought to protect, his Saiyan battle-instinct turned into a delivery mechanism for Time Breaker violence. `[Xeno Bardock: Videogaming Wiki, via WebSearch]`
- **Dual wiring** — BT4-117 carries both xenoverse-villains and saiyan character tags — one of the few BT4 cards bridging the Time Breaker villain cluster and the Saiyan bloodline cluster. Bardock is simultaneously the Time Breakers' weapon AND a Saiyan warrior being exploited, making this card a natural node for both cohorts. `[Wave 65 Edgelord saiyan-node sidecar]`
- **Community resonance** — Xeno Bardock / Masked Saiyan is one of Dragon Ball Heroes' most popular character expansions. His inclusion in BT4 signals Colossal Warfare's ambition to serve Heroes-centric collectors, extending Bardock's mythology far beyond his single-episode DBZ special origin. `[CBR: All Forms of Bardock Explained, via WebSearch]`

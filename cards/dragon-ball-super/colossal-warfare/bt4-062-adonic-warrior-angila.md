## Trivia

- **Set context** — Colossal Warfare (BT4) released July 13, 2018 as the fourth main booster set and final set of the Dragon Ball Super Card Game's Over Realm block, containing 218 total cards and introducing the Bond, Swap, and Deflect mechanics. [Cardmarket: Colossal Warfare set page]
- **IP correction** — The card's oracle text lists Era: Lord Slug Saga and special trait Slug's Army, placing Angila firmly in Dragon Ball Z: Lord Slug (Movie 4, 1991) — not Cooler's Revenge as initially flagged. Angila is one of Lord Slug's three elite henchmen alongside Medamatcha and Wings. Cooler's Armored Squadron consists of Salza, Dore, and Neiz — entirely different characters. [DBZ Exchange: BT4-062 card listing; Dragon Ball Wiki: Angila (via WebSearch)]
- **Mechanical** — BT4-062 carries [Critical] and a [Bond 2] "Slug's Army" trigger: when attacking into a two-card Slug's Army board, the opponent must discard a card from hand. The 3-cost / 15000-power statline is the standard commons floor for BT4. [DBZ Exchange: BT4-062 card listing]

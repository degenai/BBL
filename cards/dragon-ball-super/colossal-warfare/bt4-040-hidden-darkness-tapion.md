## Trivia

- **Lore: the skull literalized** — The skull imagery on this card literalizes Tapion's lore: Hirudegarn's first form has a skull-shaped head, and Tapion contains the monster's upper body within himself. When Tapion cannot play his Hero's Flute, Hirudegarn's top half begins to manifest — the skull overlay on this card depicts precisely that surfacing horror `[Villains Wiki: Hirudegarn]` `[WebSearch: Tapion Konatsian lore]`.
- **Design: paired card structure** — The "Hidden Darkness" title pairs deliberately with BT4-041 (Hidden Darkness Minotia) — both cards use the same title prefix to signal the parallel fate of the two brothers: each carrying a half of the same monster, their heroism indistinguishable from their curse. This narrative pairing across two cards is a design choice characteristic of Colossal Warfare's Hirudegarn Saga arc `[Bleeding Cool: Colossal Warfare checklist]` `[WebSearch: DBS BT4 design overview]`.
- **Collector note** — BT4-040 is a Common with a market price of approximately $0.18 (May 2026), making it notably the highest-valued common in this 8-card inventory wave — likely because the skull-over-warrior duality composition resonates as a collector image beyond its gameplay utility `[inventory data: market_price 0.18]`.

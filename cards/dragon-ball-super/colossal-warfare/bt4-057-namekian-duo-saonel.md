## Trivia

- **IP context — Saonel** — Saonel is a Namekian from Universe 6 who, alongside partner Pilina, absorbed most of their universe's Namekian population before the Tournament of Power. This made the pair nearly inexhaustible — ki and regeneration were amplified by the collective power of an entire people. They fought Piccolo and Gohan across multiple episodes before being eliminated when Piccolo and Gohan combined a full-power Masenko to knock them out of bounds. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Saonel via WebSearch]` `[Screen Rant: Universe 6 Team via WebSearch]`
- **"Duo" naming** — The card title "Namekian Duo Saonel" references Saonel's inseparable pairing with Pilina throughout the Tournament of Power. Both are Namekians who individually absorbed entire populations; neither appears without the other in the arc. Saonel gets the named card in BT4; check for a paired Pilina print in the same set. `[WebSearch: Bleeding Cool BT4 Checklist]`
- **Set placement** — BT4-057 is the last card in the consecutive Namekian block (BT4-052 through BT4-057) and the only Universe 6 representative in a block otherwise populated by classic DBZ-era Namekians (Guru, Nail, Kami, Dende, Popo). Saonel's position as the terminal card in the run places a Dragon Ball Super–era Namekian as the endpoint of a DBZ-origin lineage, reflecting BT4's design brief of mixing the two eras. `[dbs-cardgame.com: Series 4 Green Strategy Page]`

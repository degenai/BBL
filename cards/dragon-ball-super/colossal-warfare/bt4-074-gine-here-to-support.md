## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Gine first appeared in the manga special Dragon Ball Minus: The Departure of the Fated Child (April 2014) before being fully animated in Dragon Ball Super: Broly (2018). She is Goku's mother and Bardock's mate — notable as one of the few Saiyans depicted with genuine emotional bonds. The Dragon Ball official site states that Goku inherited his kindness directly from her, making her characterization foundational to Goku's core identity. [Dragon Ball official site: Weekly Character Showcase no. 74 — Gine; CBR: Everything We Know About Goku's Mother, Gine]
- **Mechanical** — BT4-074's oracle text: [Auto] When you attack or combo with this card, if your Leader Card is yellow, place up to 3 cards from the top of your deck in your Drop Area. This milling effect is structurally sacrificial — Gine discards resources so the board grows stronger, echoing her canon act of sacrificing her son to give him a better chance. 15000 power / 5000 combo is standard yellow support. [DBZ Exchange: BT4-074 card listing]
- **Design** — Gine and Bardock are described in Dragon Ball Super as "among the rare Saiyans who are joined by an emotional bond as opposed to a practical reproductive purpose" — their partnership is canonically unique in a species Toriyama depicted as broadly indifferent to family. This distinguishes Gine as one of the franchise's earliest explorations of Saiyan emotional capacity. [Dragon Ball official site: Weekly Character Showcase no. 74 — Gine]

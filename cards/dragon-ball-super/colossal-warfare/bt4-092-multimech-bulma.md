## Trivia

- **Set context** — Colossal Warfare (BT4) released for tournament play on July 13, 2018, as the fourth main booster set in the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, spanning 218 cards across multiple eras. [bleedingcool.com: Colossal Warfare Checklist]
- **Mechanical** — Multimech Bulma (BT4-092, Uncommon, Yellow, 20,000 power) carries an [EX-Evolve] skill: plays on top of any Bulma with energy cost 3 or more. Its [Auto] on the turn played mills up to 2 cards into the Drop Area, refreshes up to 1 energy to Active Mode, and draws 1 card — a compact value engine enabling Bulma-named evolution chains. [WebSearch: CoreTCG / DBZExchange BT4-092]
- **Design** — BT4-092 and BT4-093 appear on adjacent collector numbers in the same set, forming a paired inventor register for Bulma within Colossal Warfare — distinct from the mother-register at BT4-013. Both are yellow-aligned with the Special trait, signaling design intent to separate Bulma's roles across the set. [WebSearch: dbs-cardgame.com card list]
- **Set lore** — Bulma is the daughter of Dr. Brief, founder of Capsule Corporation (est. Age 712 in Dragon Ball canon). Her debut panel in the original Dragon Ball manga had her standing beside a Capsule Corp motorbike — establishing the vehicle-and-invention throughline that this card's mechanical helmet continues. [Wikipedia: Bulma; WebSearch: Dragon Ball Wiki Capsule Corporation]

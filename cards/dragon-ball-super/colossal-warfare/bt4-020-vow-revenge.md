## Trivia

- **Set context** — Colossal Warfare (DBS-B04) released July 2018 as the fourth main set of the Dragon Ball Super Card Game. It is the franchise's primary Baby Saga expansion, featuring Baby as a Leader Card with Machine Mutant / Great Ape / Saiyan special traits — the set was explicitly designed around GT's Baby arc villain cluster. [Source: dbs-cardgame.com product page; Bleeding Cool Colossal Warfare Checklist]
- **Character lore** — Baby is a Tuffle parasite — a survivor of the Saiyan genocide of the Tuffle race — who possesses Vegeta and channels negative energy harvested from all possessed Earthlings. His vow of revenge against the Saiyans is the narrative axis of the entire Baby Saga arc in Dragon Ball GT. [Source: Dragon Ball Villains Wiki: Baby]
- **Mechanics** — The [Revenge] keyword is a named Auto mechanic in the DBS card game: it KOs an attacking card at end of battle when this card is used as a guard. Given the card name "Vow Revenge," the mechanic and the character's core motivation are directly mirrored in the card design. [Source: dbs-cardgame.com card FAQ rules page]

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Colossal Warfare (DBS-B04) released July 13, 2018 as the final set of the Over Realm block — the largest DBS booster to that point at 218 total cards. Bandai's own strategy notes state explicitly: "Red in series 4 is mostly focused on Great Ape Baby." BT4-002 is the red Machine Mutant Leader card the entire BT4 red pool is built around. `[Source: dbs-cardgame.com/us-en/strategy/series4/red.php]`
- **Mechanical design** — BT4-002 is a double-sided Leader card. The front (Baby, humanoid Tuffle Parasite form) awakens when life drops to 4 or fewer, flipping to Rampaging Great Ape Baby on the back. The awakened back side draws a card on every attack and forces both players' Battle Cards to lose -10000 power for the turn — a board-erosion effect that punishes wide boards. Pairs directly with BT4-018 (Baby, Vengeance Unleashed) per Bandai strategy notes. `[Source: WebSearch: DBZ Exchange BT4-002; dbs-cardgame.com strategy page]`
- **Character lore** — Baby is the last survivor of the Tuffles, a race exterminated by Saiyans on Planet Vegeta before Dragon Ball Z's timeline. His origin contains a canonical bootstrap paradox: Baby created Dr. Myuu (the scientist credited as Baby's inventor) in Age 740 — ten years after Baby's own creation in Age 730. Baby engineered his own creation retroactively. `[Source: CBR: 'Dragon Ball GT's Baby Created Dr. Myuu Oddity, Explained'; Wikipedia: Dragon Ball GT]`

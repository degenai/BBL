## Trivia

- **IP context — Kami** — Kami (Japanese: "God") was a Namekian whose original name was lost; the evil half of his soul split off and became King Piccolo. He served as Earth's Guardian, created the Earth Dragon Balls, trained Goku and Krillin before the 23rd World Martial Arts Tournament, and permanently merged with Piccolo during the Android Saga. The merger restored his forgotten identity and transformed Piccolo into a Super Namek. His white robes bearing a red "God" kanji and his staff are his signature items — both visible in the Vision description. `[CBR: How Kami Became Earth's Guardian via WebSearch]`
- **Setting** — The sky-elevated background with soft muted tones on BT4-054 corresponds to Kami's Lookout, the floating platform high above Earth that serves as the Guardian's residence. BT4-056 "Popo, Guardian's Aide" depicts the same location from a slightly wider angle showing the dome architecture. The two cards together represent the Lookout setting as a paired location within the BT4 Namekian cluster. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Kami via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical context** — BT4-054 is an Uncommon in the BT4 Namekian block. The BT4 green cluster was designed around [Bond] with "Piccolo, Kami's Successor" (BT4-046) as the designated Namekian Leader Card — the title explicitly referencing Kami. Kami's Watcher slots into the Bond-activation support suite for that leader. `[dbs-cardgame.com: Series 4 Green Strategy Page]`

## Trivia

- **Mechanical design** — BT4-015 is a red Common Battle card: 10000 power, 5000 combo power. Era: Baby Saga. The card's presence in BT4's Baby Saga era pool is lore-consistent: Mr. Buu (Good Buu) was one of the very few characters who resisted Baby's possession during Dragon Ball GT — Baby infected most of Earth but not Good Buu or Mr. Satan. `[Source: WebSearch: LevelUpGamesmd BT4-015; WebSearch: Baby Dragon Ball GT Tuffle parasite villain lore]`
- **Character lore** — Mr. Buu (Good Buu) split from Pure Evil Buu through fission at the end of Dragon Ball Z's Buu Saga, befriended Mr. Satan, and retired from destruction. He became a student of Mr. Satan's martial arts school and a recurring Z Fighter ally. The DBS TCG uses the name "Mr. Buu" specifically to distinguish this reformed Good Buu incarnation from his destructive predecessors (Kid Buu, Super Buu) who appear separately across DBS card sets. `[Source: Wikipedia: Majin Buu; WebSearch: Good Buu Dragon Ball Wiki via dragonball.fandom.com]`
- **Design** — Buu's visual identity — pink, rotund, stubby antennae, yellow-and-white outfit — is one of Dragon Ball's most recognizable silhouettes, requiring no name text to identify. The DBS TCG has printed multiple Buu forms across sets: Good Buu / Mr. Buu (this card), Kid Buu (FB04-095 in Fusion World), and energy-boosted forms (BT1-047 in Galactic Battle). BT4-015 is the baseline Good Buu as Z Fighter ally. `[Source: WebSearch: Game Rant: All of Buu's Transformations Explained; WebSearch: Majin Buu Kid FB04-095 TCGPlayer listing]`

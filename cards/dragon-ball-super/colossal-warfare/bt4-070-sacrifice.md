## Trivia

- **Mechanical** — BT4-070 is a Green Extra card: [Counter: Attack] — negate the attack. If your Leader is a Namekian, you may add a card from your life to hand when activating this Counter; if you do, you may activate it without paying its energy cost. The effect encodes the sacrifice theme directly: trading life cards for hand advantage mirrors Nail's canon act of offering himself so Piccolo could absorb him and grow stronger. [DBZ Exchange: BT4-070 card listing]
- **Set / lore context** — Nail was a Warrior-type Namekian with a power level of 42,000 — the strongest Namekian when Frieza attacked Planet Namek. He fought Frieza to buy time, then allowed Piccolo to absorb him, permanently boosting Piccolo's power enough to fight Frieza's second form. Namekian Fusion (unlike Fusion Dance or Potara) is permanent and cannot be undone. [Dragon Ball official site: Weekly Character Showcase no. 132 — Nail; CBR: What Makes Namekian Fusion Unique]
- **IP qualification** — The two-figure composition (smaller collapsed Namekian, looming larger figure above) is consistent with either the Nail-Piccolo fusion scene or Nail's battle against Frieza. Neither the card's oracle text nor flavor text names the depicted figures explicitly. The scene attribution to Nail and/or Piccolo remains plausible but unconfirmed without a Bandai source tying this specific art to that scene. [triviabot qualification; DBZ Exchange: BT4-070]

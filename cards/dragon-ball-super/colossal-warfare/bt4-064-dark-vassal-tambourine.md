## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Tambourine, Cymbal, and Drum form the "Dark Vassals" — all mutant Namekian sons spawned by King Piccolo in the original Dragon Ball King Piccolo Saga. Their BT4 cards are consecutive commons (nos. 064-066) in Colossal Warfare, designed as an intentional triplet that synergizes with the King Piccolo Bond archetype. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Dark Vassals (via WebSearch)]
- **Naming** — All of King Piccolo's children are named after musical instruments (Tambourine, Cymbal, Drum, Piano, Piccolo Jr.), following the naming convention established by King Piccolo himself — whose name derives from the piccolo flute. This is one of Toriyama's earliest consistent naming schemes in Dragon Ball. [Kanzenshuu forum: Piano, Drum and Tambourine-namekians?]
- **Mechanical** — BT4-064 is the cheapest Dark Vassal at 2 energy cost with 15000 power and [Critical], reflecting Tambourine's role as King Piccolo's primary frontline hunter. Critical denies the opponent card advantage by sending damaged life to the Drop Area rather than the hand. In Dragon Ball canon, Tambourine was described as the most powerful of King Piccolo's spawned sons at the time of his creation. [DBZ Exchange: BT4-064 card listing]

## Trivia

- **IP context — Grand Elder Guru** — Guru is the patriarch of virtually all living Namekians, having single-handedly repopulated the species after a catastrophic climate event centuries prior. He produced over 100 children. He is the creator of the Namekian Dragon Balls, which become permanently inert upon his death unless authority is transferred. His signature ability — placing a hand on a person's head to unlock their dormant power — was used on Krillin (raised to ~13,000), Gohan (raised to ~14,000), and Dende (gained healing abilities rather than combat strength) during the Namek Saga. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Grand Elder Guru via WebSearch]` `[Kanzenshuu: Guru power-unlock thread via WebSearch]`
- **Design note — rarity inversion** — BT4-052 is a Common card, the lowest rarity tier in the BT4 Namekian block. Guru is narratively the supreme Namekian patriarch, yet in the card game occupies a support-role slot consistent with his story function: he never fights, only enables. This mirrors a recurring DBS card game design pattern where lore significance and combat rarity diverge for non-fighter characters. `[dbs-cardgame.com: BT4 green strategy / card list via WebSearch]`
- **Set context — Namekian block** — BT4's consecutive run BT4-052 through BT4-057 (Guru, Nail, Kami, Dende, Popo, Saonel) forms a complete Namekian hierarchy in a single numbered block: patriarch elder, warrior ace, Earth-guardian, healer, aide, and Universe 6 counterpart. The BT4 green color was officially described as "mostly focused on Namekians and Lord Slug Saga." `[dbs-cardgame.com: Series 4 Green Strategy Page]`

## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Drum is King Piccolo's most powerful spawned mutant son during the King Piccolo Saga — stronger than both Tambourine and Cymbal — notable for defeating Tien Shinhan, one of the strongest human fighters in the series. Drum was ultimately killed by Kid Goku with a single punch, demonstrating Goku's growth after training with Korin. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Drum (via WebSearch)]
- **Naming** — All three Dark Vassal commons (BT4-064 Tambourine, BT4-065 Cymbal, BT4-066 Drum) bear percussion instrument names, continuing King Piccolo's musical naming convention — one of Toriyama's earliest systematic naming schemes in Dragon Ball. [Kanzenshuu forum: Piano, Drum and Tambourine-namekians?]
- **Mechanical** — The three Dark Vassal commons sit in consecutive slots (nos. 064-066), suggesting intentional design as a playset unit for King Piccolo Bond decks. Drum's massive-build archetype in canon maps to high-power commons that reward board presence — the Bond mechanic in BT4 triggers stronger effects when multiple Namekian battle cards are in play. [WebSearch: DBS BT4 King Piccolo Bond archetype; Cardmarket: Colossal Warfare]

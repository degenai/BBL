## Trivia

- **Set context / oracle link** — Phantom Flame Cannon is an Uncommon Blue Extra card from Colossal Warfare (BT4, 2018). Its oracle text: "If your Leader Card is Hirudegarn, it gains +10,000 power for the duration of the battle, then choose up to 1 card in your opponent's hand and place it on top of their deck." This places the card in the Wrath of the Dragon arc cluster alongside adjacent BT4-042 "Hoi, Emissary of Flame." `[DBZ Exchange: BT4-043]` `[Bleeding Cool: BT4 Checklist]`
- **IP qualification** — The vision flagged the depicted green reptilian humanoid as Hoi. Research finds Hoi canonically wears a dark blue cloak with white undershirt and has prominent whiskers — no green skin is described in any wiki source. Hirudegarn, the oracle-linked character, is a colossal skull-headed monster, not a slender humanoid. The depicted figure's identity cannot be confirmed without direct comparison to BT4-042 "Hoi, Emissary of Flame." The DBS card game may use a stylized visual redesign for Hoi not reflected in the anime design. `[Dragon Ball Wiki via WebSearch]` `[Villains Wiki: Hoi via WebSearch]`
- **Lore context** — Hoi was the last surviving Kashvar — an alien race of malevolent warlocks — and the secondary antagonist of Dragon Ball Z: Wrath of the Dragon (1995). He manipulated the Z Fighters into releasing Tapion and ultimately Hirudegarn, intending to weaponize the monster to rule the cosmos. He was accidentally crushed to death by Hirudegarn after it broke free of his control. `[Wikipedia: Dragon Ball Z: Wrath of the Dragon via WebSearch]`

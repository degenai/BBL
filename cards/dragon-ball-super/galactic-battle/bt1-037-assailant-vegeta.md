## Trivia

- **Set context / character placement** — BT1-037 is tagged to the Resurrection F Saga era on the official DBS card database, placing Vegeta within the storyline of Dragon Ball Z: Resurrection F (2015), where both he and Goku train under Whis and Beerus to achieve the SSGSS transformation. The "Assailant" title foregrounds his role as aggressive combatant — the same arc where Vegeta's pride and rivalry with Goku peak dramatically. `[dbs-cardgame.com official card list; CoreTCG card entry]`
- **Set history** — Galactic Battle (BT1), released July 28, 2017, was the first English-language DBS Card Game set — and therefore the first time Vegeta appeared as a named card in the English product line. The set shipped multiple Vegeta variants (BT1-037 Uncommon, BT1-038 Common, BT1-028 double-faced SSGSS Rare), establishing Vegeta as a structural pillar of the game's launch roster alongside Goku. `[Bleedingcool.com Galactic Battle Checklist; PSA Set Registry article]`
- **Mechanical profile** — BT1-037 is a 3-cost Blue Battle card with 15,000 power and the Critical keyword (damage goes to opponent's Drop Area instead of hand), making it an efficient aggression piece at below-curve cost. Combo power is 5,000. The official Galactic Battle strategy guide on dbs-cardgame.com identifies Vegeta-family cards as cornerstones of the Blue aggro archetype. `[CoreTCG card entry; dbs-cardgame.com Galactic Battle Strategy Guide]`

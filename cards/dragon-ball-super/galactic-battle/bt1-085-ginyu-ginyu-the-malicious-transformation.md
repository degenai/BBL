## Trivia

- **Design** — BT1-085 is a flip Leader Card in Galactic Battle: the front side (Ginyu) has a Life Card flip mechanic tied to Ginyu Force card identity; the awakened back side (Ginyu, The Malicious Transformation) adds Double Strike and a +5000 power bonus when two or more Ginyu Force cards are in play. The mechanic thematically encodes Ginyu's body-swap ability — the card "transforms" just as Ginyu inhabits new bodies. [dbscards.fr: BT1-085-UC card mechanics]
- **Character context** — Captain Ginyu's "Body Change" technique ("Change Now!") allows him to swap bodies by firing a beam of light. During the Frieza Saga, Ginyu used it on Goku after inflicting a severe self-injury to trade into a more powerful body — but his inability to access Goku's full power, combined with Goku intercepting a second swap attempt, left Ginyu permanently trapped in a Namekian frog's body. [CBR: 35 Years Ago, Dragon Ball Z Debuted the Most Busted Anime Ability of All Time]
- **Set context** — Captain Ginyu leads the Ginyu Force, an elite mercenary squad in Frieza's Planet Trade Organization known for camp-theatrical pose routines that contrast with their genuine combat power. Multiple Ginyu Force members have cards in BT1 (Guldo at BT1-099), reflecting the squad's collective identity within the set. [Dragon Ball official site: Weekly Character Showcase no. 117: Ginyu!]

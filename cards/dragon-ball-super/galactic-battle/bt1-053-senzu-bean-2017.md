## Trivia

- **Set context** — Senzu Beans are grown exclusively by Korin, the immortal cat deity residing atop Korin Tower. When consumed, a Senzu Bean instantly restores the consumer to full health and energy. The canonical supply chain runs Korin (grows) to Yajirobe (delivers and assists with gardening) to the Z Fighters. Korin is designated the "God of Martial Arts" in Dragon Ball lore — a divine figure whose tower predates the Lookout in the vertical cosmology of Earth-Z. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Senzu Bean, via WebSearch snippet; Saiyan Warrior: Korin Tower Secrets]
- **Mechanical** — BT1-053 is a Common Blue Extra card with a 1-Blue energy cost. Its Activate:Battle skill grants up to 1 of your cards +5000 power for the turn, then switches up to 2 of your energy to Active Mode. The effect encodes Senzu Beans' canonical dual function: combat-power restoration and energy recovery. [DBZ Exchange: BT1-053 listing, via WebSearch snippet]
- **Design** — BT1-053 depicts Goku in his orange gi carrying a cloth sack of Senzu Beans — an item card using a named character as the delivery vehicle for the item's mechanical effect. The card has received multiple reprints including Mythic Booster (MB01) and a gold-stamped Special Anniversary Box variant, indicating strong print-run longevity for a Common from the inaugural set. [The Side Deck / Big Orbit Cards: Senzu Bean reprint listings]
- **Set context** — Korin Tower is one of Dragon Ball's oldest recurring locations, introduced in the original Dragon Ball manga (pre-Z). Korin is the mentor figure who preceded Kami and the Lookout in the series's vertical spiritual geography. The Senzu Bean supply chain (Korin → Yajirobe → Z Fighters) is among the series's most enduring logistical fixtures — a comedic counterpoint to escalating power scales. [Saiyan Warrior: Korin character page / CBR: Yajirobe and Korin, via WebSearch snippets]

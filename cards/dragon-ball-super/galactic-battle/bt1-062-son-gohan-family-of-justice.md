## Trivia

- **Set context** — Galactic Battle (BT1) was the inaugural English-language booster set for the DBS Card Game, released July 28, 2017. With 114 cards (60 Commons, 30 Uncommons, 12 Rares, 8 Super Rares, 4 Special Rares), it established the game's core color system, card types, and leader mechanic as the foundational template for all subsequent sets. `[PSA CardFacts: 2017 Dragon Ball Super Series 1 Galactic Battle]`
- **Mechanical** — Green Battle card, Power 10,000, cost 2 Green. Skill: Blocker — when another of your cards is attacked, switch Gohan to Rest Mode and redirect the attack to him instead. The Special Trait "Saiyan/Earthling" reflects Gohan's canonical dual heritage as Goku's (Saiyan) and Chi-Chi's (Earthling) son — a trait that distinguishes him from pure-Saiyan characters in the game's type system. `[CoreTCG: Son Gohan Family of Justice BT1-062; Bandai official card search]`
- **Design** — The "Family of Justice" subtitle and Broly Saga era designation link this card to DBS film continuity, where Gohan is framed as a pillar of the Son family legacy. However, the art depicts a young Super Saiyan Gohan visually consistent with his Cell Saga teenager form. The card layers the emotional weight of past heroism (the boy who surpassed his father) onto a present-era framing — Gohan as heir and protector, not just prodigy. `[PSA Set Registry: Collecting 2017 Dragon Ball Super Card Game; CoreTCG BT1-062 listing]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Galactic Battle (DBS-B01) was the first booster set for the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, released July 28, 2017. Its 114-card roster spans two primary arc pools: the God of Destruction Champa Arc (Universe 6 Saiyans) and the Broly Saga. Cabba appears in the Champa Arc pool as a Universe 6 representative. `[WebSearch: PSA Set Registry Galactic Battle 2017; WebSearch: CoreTCG BT1-027]`
- **Character context** — Cabba is a Saiyan from Universe 6's Planet Sadala, introduced in Dragon Ball Super Chapter 8. He achieves Super Saiyan for the first time during his tournament match against Vegeta, who provokes Cabba's rage by threatening to destroy Planet Sadala. The card title "Cabba's Awakening" directly names this breakthrough moment. `[en.dragon-ball-official.com: Weekly Character Showcase #9 Cabba]`
- **Design detail** — BT1-027 is a red Common Extra card (0 energy cost) with an [Auto] skill granting one of your red cards +6000 power for the duration of a battle. The zero-cost buff makes it a tempo piece for red aggro builds. `[WebSearch: CoreTCG BT1-027; WebSearch: BigOrbitCards BT1-027]`
- **Lore detail** — Cabba's battle suit is designed in the style of old Saiyan armor from Universe 7's pre-Frieza Force era. Vegeta explicitly remarks on this during their fight, noting the armor resembles what Universe 7 Saiyans wore before conscription into the Frieza Force — a visual bridge between the two universes' Saiyan heritage. `[en.dragon-ball-official.com: Weekly Character Showcase #9 Cabba, Trivia section]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — BT1-109 is a Yellow Extra card from Galactic Battle (BT1), the inaugural English DBS Card Game booster (July 28, 2017). The card also received an Event Pack Promo printing, confirming it was distributed at official Bandai tournament events during the game's first competitive year — early evidence of the Frieza Force archetype's competitive viability from launch. `[PSA CardFacts: 2017 Dragon Ball Super Series 1 Galactic Battle; TrollAndToad: Frieza's Call BT1-109 Event Pack Promo listing]`
- **Mechanical** — Yellow Extra card, cost 1 Yellow. Skill: "[Activate: Main] Choose 1 Frieza's Army card of energy cost 2 or less from your deck, and play it in Rest Mode. Then, shuffle your deck." A tempo generator: at 1 energy it deploys a 2-cost Frieza's Army unit for free, converting a single action into field presence and card advantage. Frieza-type decks built around this effect were among the game's earliest functional archetypes. `[CoreTCG: Frieza's Call BT1-109; DBZ Exchange: BT1-109]`
- **Design** — The wide-shot art centers anonymous rank-and-file Frieza Force soldiers rather than Frieza himself — a deliberate framing for a "Call" card. The Frieza Force's standardized purple-gray armor and fleet-scale deployment style is consistent across Dragon Ball Z and Dragon Ball Super, and the rounded mothership in the background is visually linked to Frieza's personal ship design. The card does not need Frieza's face to establish his authority; the empire's aesthetic does it. `[WebSearch: Dragon Ball Super TCG BT1-109 card text; Dragon Ball Wiki Fandom: Frieza's Army]`

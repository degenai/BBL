## Trivia

- **Set context** — Human Shield Krillin (BT1-072) is a Common in Galactic Battle. Krillin is the strongest human fighter in the Dragon Ball Z roster and Goku's oldest Earth-based friend — Akira Toriyama created him specifically to give Goku a peer for contrast and depth, as his editor flagged Goku as "too plain" without a rival companion. [Wikipedia: Krillin]
- **Stewardship register** — Krillin holds the canonical record for most deaths of any Dragon Ball character: three confirmed deaths in the main timeline — killed by Tambourine (Dragon Ball), blown apart by Frieza on Namek (this death triggered Goku's first Super Saiyan transformation), and turned to chocolate and eaten by Super Buu (Buu Saga). The 'Human Shield' card title literalizes the role he inhabits across the entire franchise: a lower-power fighter who shows up anyway, absorbs the cost, and keeps going. [CBR: How Many Times Does Krillin Really Die?; Game Rant: All of Krillin's Deaths, Ranked]
- **Stewardship register** — Krillin's late-series arc is explicitly custody-of-relationships: he spared Android 18's life during the Cell Arc when Bulma's remote would have destroyed her, married her between the Cell and Buu Sagas, and had a daughter (Marron) — making him the Z-Warrior who most concretely converted battlefield loyalty into domestic stewardship. [Wikipedia: Android 18; CBR: Dragon Ball Z: Krillin Was Right to Spare Android 18's Life During the Cell Arc]
- **Mechanical** — The card name and defensive-block art position Krillin as a protection-type card in BT1's battle system, consistent with his narrative function: the character whose work-shape is absorbing damage and protecting others while Saiyans handle apex-power combat. [Wikipedia: Krillin; CBR: Every Time Krillin Has Died In Dragon Ball]

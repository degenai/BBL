## Trivia

- **Character role** — Appule is a mid-level Frieza Force soldier in the Namek Saga — above unnamed grunts but below Zarbon, Dodoria, and Cui. On Namek he discovered a village destroyed by Vegeta and reported back to Frieza. He was killed when Vegeta broke out of a rejuvenation tank [Dragon Ball Official Site: Weekly Character Showcase no. 224: Appule].
- **Official recognition** — Despite his minor story role, Appule received a dedicated Dragon Ball character spotlight: Weekly Character Showcase no. 224 on the official Bandai/Toei Dragon Ball website (published August 2025). His inclusion in BT1 and the showcase both reflect the card game's interest in representing the Frieza Force's full depth beyond its named lieutenants [Dragon Ball Official Site: Weekly Character Showcase no. 224].
- **Design note** — Appule's purple skin with yellow spots, elongated head ridges, and absence of a nose are specific to his unnamed alien species — one of dozens composing Frieza's empire. His visual distinctiveness made him recognizable enough to appear in Dragon Ball video games including Dragon Ball Legends (card code DBL-EVT-25E) [WebSearch: legends.dbz.space Appule character entry].
- **Set context** — BT1-102 sits at the low end of Galactic Battle's collector number range — the deep Frieza Force tier. The set's Frieza archetype was structured with a tiered hierarchy: Ginyu Force elites (BT1-09x range) above rank-and-file soldiers (BT1-10x range) [PSA Set Registry: Galactic Battle 2017].

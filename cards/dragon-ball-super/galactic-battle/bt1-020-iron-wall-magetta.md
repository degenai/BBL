## Trivia

- **Set context** — Iron Wall Magetta (BT1-020) is an Uncommon from Galactic Battle (BT1), the inaugural main set of the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, released July 28, 2017. The set launched with 114 cards and introduced the Champa Saga / Universe 6 Saga as its primary narrative frame `[PSA CardFacts; The Games Corner AU]`.
- **Character** — Magetta is a Metalman from Galactic System 66,950 in Universe 6. Despite appearing robotic, Metalmen are a biological race whose bodies are filled with magma; when Magetta is under stress a steam pipe atop his head vents heat — a literal physiological tell `[Dragon Ball Official Site, Showcase no. 188: Otta Magetta]`.
- **Mechanical design** — The card skill reads "This card cannot be KO-ed in battle," a direct translation of Magetta's anime trait: his metal body absorbed Super Saiyan Vegeta's Full Power Final Flash without being knocked out. On the card, the Champa Saga era trait is listed as "Alien" `[Official DBS card list, BT1-020; Dragon Ball Wiki: Auta Magetta, via WebSearch]`.
- **Canonical weakness** — Magetta's sole exploitable flaw is emotional: the entire Metalman race loses the will to fight when insulted. Vegeta won their Tournament of Destroyers bout not by overpowering Magetta but by calling him a "pathetic pile of junk," causing Magetta to cry and stop fighting. The card name "Iron Wall" contrasts sharply with this fragility `[Dragon Ball Official Site, Showcase no. 188; Dragon Ball Wiki: Auta Magetta, via WebSearch]`.
- **Tournament of Destroyers placement** — Magetta was selected by Vados for Universe 6's five-fighter team alongside Hit, Cabba, Frost, and Botamo. He famously passed the required written exam with exactly 50% — the steam pipe reportedly fired throughout `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Auta Magetta / Universe 6 Saga, via WebSearch]`.

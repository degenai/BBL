## Trivia

- **Set context** — Galactic Battle (DBS-B01, July 28, 2017) is the inaugural set of the Dragon Ball Super Card Game — the first 114-card English-language release, containing 60 commons, 30 uncommons, 12 rares, 8 super rares, and 4 special rares. BT1-060 Son Goku is one of the most mass-produced common prints in the game's launch wave. [PSA Set Registry: Galactic Battle, 2017]
- **Card profile** — BT1-060 is a Green Battle card, cost 2 (1 Green required), 15,000 power, 5,000 combo power, Saiyan special trait, Battle of Gods Saga era. [CoreTCG product listing; WebSearch snippet]
- **IP verification** — The depicted figure is Son Goku, canonical protagonist of the Dragon Ball franchise (Akira Toriyama / Toei Animation, 1984-present), confirmed by Bandai's official character-tag field for BT1-060: character `Son Goku`, era `Battle of Gods Saga`. The Battle of Gods Saga is the Dragon Ball Super opening arc where Goku first encounters Beerus, God of Destruction, and unlocks Super Saiyan God. [dbs-cardgame.com character database; son-goku.md character node]
- **Print history note** — Galactic Battle sealed booster boxes were retired in late 2018; by Q3 2020 supply dried up globally and prices rose significantly, reaching approximately $600-$700 per sealed box by February 2021. Individual BT1 commons including this card retain very low raw-single market value ($0.14 market price) reflecting the high print run of the launch wave. [PSA Set Registry: Galactic Battle; WebSearch snippet]

## Trivia

- **Set context** — BT1-073 is a Special Rare (SPR) Battle card from Galactic Battle (DBS-B01), the inaugural booster set of the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, released 2017. Galactic Battle drew heavily from the classic DBZ film roster; Broly's inclusion in the first set signals his franchise-marquee status. Cards were available at sanctioned tournaments starting July 28, 2017. [WebSearch: PSA CardFacts BT1-073; Galactic Battle set overview]
- **Mechanical design** — Green Battle card, power 30000, energy cost 6. When played: choose up to 2 cards from the opponent's hand at random and place them in the Drop Area — a hand-disruption ability reflecting Broly's obliterative, uncontrollable power. Both SR and SPR (alternate art) prints exist for this card. [WebSearch: TCGplayer BT1-073 listing; PSA CardFacts BT1-073]
- **Character identity** — The depicted Broly is the Koyama/Toei version from DBZ Movie 8: Broly — The Legendary Super Saiyan (Japan, March 6, 1993), not the Toriyama-designed DBS Broly (2018). The two are distinct characters; the Toei Broly was created by writer Takao Koyama and has no Toriyama-authored manga appearance. The circular waist gem visible in this card's art is the control device Paragus installed to suppress Broly's power — a signature identifier of the Toei character. [WebSearch: Dragon Ball Wiki DBZ Movie 8; Wikipedia: Dragon Ball Z: Broly — The Legendary Super Saiyan]
- **Lore detail** — In Movie 8, Broly's hatred of Goku originates from an infant memory: newborn Goku's incessant crying in an adjacent pod on Planet Vegeta drove the infant Broly into uncontrollable rage. The Toei Broly became the franchise's most recognizable film antagonist of the 1990s, spawning two direct sequels (Second Coming, Bio-Broly). [WebSearch: Dragon Ball Wiki DBZ Movie 8 plot summary]

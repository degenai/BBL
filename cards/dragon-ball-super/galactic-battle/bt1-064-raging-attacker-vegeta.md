## Trivia

- **Set context** — BT1-064 is from Galactic Battle (DBS-B01), the inaugural main booster set of the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, released July 28, 2017. Galactic Battle had 110 cards across 4 rarities and established the game's four-color framework; green cards were designed around destruction effects and reactive board control, with Raging Attacker Vegeta singled out on the official strategy page as a key green-color showcase: "Armed with a powerful counter-attack skill, Raging Attacker Vegeta can drop in during your opponent's turn and wipe out one of their Battle Cards." [dbs-cardgame.com: Galactic Battle Red and Green Cards strategy page]
- **Mechanical profile** — Green Battle card, Rare, power 15,000. The "Counter:Attack" skill allows the card to be played from hand during the opponent's turn when one of your other Battle Cards is attacked — a reactive disruption tool that wipes an opposing Battle Card, directly encoding Vegeta's canonical aggressor-who-strikes-back character mode. [CoolStuffInc listing; dbs-cardgame.com strategy page]
- **Character era** — The card does not carry the Super Saiyan Blue costume; the dark Saiyan battle armor and upswept golden energy aura place it in the standard Super Saiyan / Saiyan Saga visual register. The Galactic Battle set's green-color Vegeta cards anchor on Vegeta's Z-era warrior-prince identity rather than the DBS god-tier continuation, consistent with the set's emphasis on "Saiyan" as the foundational trait for green-color identity. [dbs-cardgame.com set roster; Vegeta character node `_characters/vegeta.md`]
- **Saiyan cluster link** — "Saiyan" is the primary card trait shared by Vegeta, Goku, Bardock, and their descendants across the BT1 and BT2 corpus cards; BT1-064's Counter:Attack mechanic makes it a natural fit alongside other Saiyan-trait battle cards in an aggression-focused green deck. The card sits within the broader Saiyan-trait subarchetype that Bandai built out across BT1-BT4. [dbs-cardgame.com BT1 card list]

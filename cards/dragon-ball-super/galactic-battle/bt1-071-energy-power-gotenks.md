## Trivia

- **Set context** — Galactic Battle (BT1), released July 28, 2017, was the first English DBS Card Game booster. Including Gotenks as a common in the inaugural set signals how central fusion characters are to the game's identity — the fusion mechanic would anchor multiple competitive archetypes across subsequent sets including Union Force (BT2) and beyond. `[PSA CardFacts: 2017 Dragon Ball Super Series 1 Galactic Battle]`
- **Mechanical** — Green Battle card, Power 15,000, cost 3 Green. Auto skill: "When this card attacks a Leader Card, your opponent chooses 1 card from their hand and places it in the Drop Area." At 3 energy, 15,000 power is above-curve for cost, and the hand-disruption trigger on Leader attacks turns every swing into a two-for-one threat — opponents must decide whether to block and prevent the discard or allow the Leader hit and lose a card. `[The Side Deck: Energy Power Gotenks BT1-071; TCGplayer: Energy Power Gotenks Galactic Battle]`
- **Design** — Gotenks is the Fusion Dance product of Goten and Trunks, using the technique Goku learned from the Metamorans in Other World and taught to his son. All Fusion Dance characters wear canonical Metamoran attire: black-and-white cropped vest, white sash, baggy white pants, black wristbands. The fusion inherits Trunks' brashness and Goten's fearlessness — producing a child warrior who consistently underestimates opponents and overestimates himself. The "cocky confidence" in the art is not stylistic license; it is canonical character behavior. `[Saiyan Warrior: Gotenks character profile; GameRant: Dragon Ball — All Forms of Gotenks Explained]`

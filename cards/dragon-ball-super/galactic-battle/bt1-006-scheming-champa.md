## Trivia

- **Set context** — Champa is the God of Destruction of Universe 6 and the twin brother of Beerus (Universe 7). They are the only known sibling pair among DBS's twelve Gods of Destruction. Born in the same universe, one moved to preside over its twin — the mirror-universe structure underpinning the entire Champa Arc. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Champa, via WebSearch snippet]
- **Set context** — The Champa Arc's inciting premise is Champa organizing a tournament between U6 and U7 fighters to obtain Universe 7's Earth (U6's Earth was destroyed), motivated by its superior cuisine. The food-rivalry framing — two destroyer gods competing over culinary bragging rights — is a recurring comedic motif that humanizes otherwise omnipotent figures. [Den of Geek: DBS Episode 28 Review — The 6th Universe's Destroyer, His Name Is Champa]
- **Design** — Vados notes within the series that the physical difference between Champa and Beerus visually signals which twin is stronger — Champa's rotund build contrasting Beerus's lean frame. This is a deliberate Toriyama design choice: relative power encoded through body type. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Champa / WebSearch snippet citing Vados dialogue]
- **Design** — Champa's name follows the alcohol-based GoD naming pun: Champa = Champagne. His attendant Vados = Calvados. The full quad (Beerus/Champa + Whis/Vados) is a four-way alcohol pun seeded from Toriyama's "virus/beer" mishearing. [Wikipedia: Beerus, via WebSearch snippet]

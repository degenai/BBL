## Trivia

- **Set context** — Galactic Battle (BT1) was the inaugural English DBS Card Game booster, released July 28, 2017. The "Battle of Gods Saga" era designation places this Vegeta in the events of DBS's first arc and the 2013 Battle of Gods film — the arc where Vegeta's protective rage over Bulma produces one of his most character-defining moments in the entire franchise. `[PSA CardFacts: 2017 Dragon Ball Super Series 1 Galactic Battle]`
- **Mechanical** — Green Battle card, Power 10,000, cost 2 Green. Skill: "When this card is attacked, KO the attacking card after the battle. This card cannot be attacked by a Leader Card." A punishing trade deterrent — opponents must weigh whether attacking Vegeta is worth losing their Battle Card. Leader Card immunity removes the most common attack vector, forcing opponents to either commit a disposable unit or leave him on the board. `[TrollAndToad: Tenacious Vegeta BT1-066; TCG Collectibles BT1-066 listing]`
- **Design** — The "Tenacious" subtitle maps directly to Vegeta's defining trait across the franchise: refusal to yield regardless of the power gap. In the Battle of Gods arc specifically, Vegeta goes Super Saiyan from rage at Beerus slapping Bulma — his emotional ceiling cracks in protection of his family rather than in pursuit of strength. The card's aggressive lunge and fierce scowl (not a roar, not a power-up) captures that combative pride in its most compressed form. `[GameRant: Dragon Ball — Vegeta's Galick Gun Explained; Wikipedia: Trunks (Dragon Ball) — referencing Vegeta's Saiyan pride characterization]`

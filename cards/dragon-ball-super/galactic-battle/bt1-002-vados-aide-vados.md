## Trivia

- **Set context** — Vados is the angel attendant of Champa (Universe 6's God of Destruction) and the elder sister of Whis, attendant to Universe 7's Beerus. Both are children of the Grand Minister. The Vados-Whis pair is the angel half of the U6/U7 twin-dyad structure: each destroyer god is paired with a sibling angel from the same Grand Minister family tree. Vados trained Whis and claims to be slightly stronger; Whis disputes this. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Vados, via WebSearch snippet]
- **Design** — Vados's name follows the alcohol-based naming convention: Calvados is a French apple brandy from Normandy. The four-way pun (Beerus = beer/virus, Champa = Champagne, Whis = whisky, Vados = Calvados) spans both twin dyads, reflecting Toriyama's seeded naming system for the DBS multiverse cosmology. [Wikipedia: Beerus, via WebSearch snippet]
- **Set context** — BT1-002 is one of the lowest-numbered cards in Galactic Battle, placing Vados at the front of the U6 cohort block. The numeric grouping (Vados BT1-002, Hit BT1-003, Champa BT1-006/007) reflects the set's narrative organization by universe alignment. [Trading Card Database: 2017 Dragon Ball Super Card Game BT1 Galactic Battle]
- **Set context** — The U6/U7 parallel structure (twin destroyer gods each attended by a sibling angel from the Grand Minister family) is deliberate Toriyama and Toyotarou designer intent for DBS multiverse cosmology, introduced during the God of Destruction Champa Arc. The four figures — Beerus, Champa, Whis, Vados — constitute the founding cosmological quad of the DBS multiverse. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Champa / Vados, via WebSearch snippets]

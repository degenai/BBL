## Trivia

- **Set context** — BT1-080 is an Extra card from Galactic Battle (BT1, July 2017), situated in the green color cluster adjacent to the Broly archetype cards (BT1-075, 081). Its character is Vegeta, confirmed by the Judge Foil promotional variant of this exact card explicitly titled "Full Power Energy Vegeta" [eBay: Full Power Energy Vegeta JUDGE FOIL DBS BT1-080].
- **Mechanical design** — The card's Counter Attack skill negates an incoming attack, then KOs an opponent Battle card with energy cost 1 or less — a defensive burst that also clears small blockers. The Counter Attack keyword was part of the DBS TCG's foundational rule structure introduced in BT1 [WebSearch: pricecharting.com and Troll and Toad BT1-080 listings].
- **Lore reference** — Vegeta's "Full Power" state in Dragon Ball Z refers to his attempt to push the Super Saiyan transformation beyond its normal limits to match Cell's Perfect Form during the Cell Games arc — a strategy that proved temporarily effective but was ultimately surpassed by Son Gohan's ascent. The white-gold aura and armor stance on this card is consistent with that arc [Wikipedia: Dragon Ball Z].
- **Collector note** — A Judge Foil promotional version of BT1-080 was produced; Judge Foils in the DBS card game were distributed to official tournament judges as recognition items, making this variant a collector's piece beyond its gameplay utility [eBay: Full Power Energy Vegeta JUDGE FOIL DBS BT1-080].

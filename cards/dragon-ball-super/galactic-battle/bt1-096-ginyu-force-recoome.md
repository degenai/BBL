## Trivia

- **Cohort context** — Recoome is one of five members of the Ginyu Force (Captain Ginyu, Guldo, Burter, Jeice, Recoome) — Frieza's elite mercenary squad sent to Namek in the Namek Saga of Dragon Ball Z. The squad's naming convention is a dairy pun: Recoome derives from "creme," Burter from "butter," Jeice from "cheese," Guldo from "yogurt," and Ginyu from the Japanese word for milk [WebSearch: multiple sources on Ginyu Force naming].
- **Character profile** — Recoome is the Force's most flamboyant member — he strikes battle poses more often than Captain Ginyu himself and is described as a dim-witted bully who enjoys toying with opponents. Despite the goofy exterior he is considered the second-strongest Ginyu Force member after the captain. His poses originated from childhood dance training, per Dragon Ball lore [Dragon Ball Official Site: Weekly Character Showcase no. 79].
- **Power-scaling note** — Recoome was defeated twice on Namek: once by Vegeta (energy wave) and then by Goku (a single punch to the midsection that knocked him out instantly). The contrast between how much damage he absorbed from Vegeta, Krillin, and Gohan before Goku arrived made his one-punch KO a defining power-scaling moment in the series [WebSearch: CBR "Recoome is Dragon Ball Z's Scariest Ginyu Force Member"].
- **Set context** — BT1-096 is a Common from Galactic Battle (BT1, July 2017). The set's Frieza Force archetype (including Ginyu Force members as support Battle cards) was one of the two flagship launch strategies alongside the Broly green deck [PSA Set Registry: Galactic Battle 2017].

## Trivia

- **Character context** — Paragus is a Saiyan from Dragon Ball Z: Broly – The Legendary Super Saiyan (Movie 8, 1993). After King Vegeta ordered infant Broly's execution due to his 10,000 power-level birth, Broly instinctively shielded himself and his father and they escaped into space. Paragus subsequently developed a mind-control device — synced between a bracelet on his wrist and a crown on Broly's head — to suppress and weaponize his son's uncontrollable power, using Broly to construct "New Planet Vegeta" and exact revenge on King Vegeta's bloodline. `[Wikipedia: Dragon Ball Z: Broly – The Legendary Super Saiyan]`
- **Mechanical design** — BT1-077 is a green Common Battle card (2 energy, 10000 power, 5000 combo, Special Trait: Saiyan). Its [Auto] on-play ability reads: discard 1 card from hand, then choose and activate 1 Broly's Ring (BT1-081) from your deck, then shuffle. Paragus directly tutors the control device — the card mechanically enacts his lore role as the retriever of the suppressor. `[WebSearch: therealmofadventure.com BT1-077; WebSearch: levelupgamesmd.com BT1-077]`
- **Lore irony** — In the film, Paragus's ultimate fate is to be killed by Broly himself after the mind-control crown is destroyed and Broly transforms into the Legendary Super Saiyan. Paragus attempts to escape in a space pod before Broly confronts and kills him. The card title "Controller of Monsters" carries this irony: the controller becomes the victim of the monster he built his revenge plan around. `[Wikipedia: Dragon Ball Z: Broly – The Legendary Super Saiyan]`

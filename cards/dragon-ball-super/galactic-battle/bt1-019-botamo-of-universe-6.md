## Trivia

- **Set context** — Galactic Battle (BT1) released July 28, 2017 as the debut DBS-TCG booster. Botamo appears alongside Frost (BT1-016) in the same set, placing both Universe 6 Tournament of Destroyers fighters in the inaugural card pool. `[WebSearch: Galactic Battle BT1 release 2017]`
- **Character lore** — Botamo is one of five fighters selected by Vados for Universe 6's team at the Tournament of Destroyers (alongside Frost, Magetta, Cabba, and Hit). He faced Goku first. His defining trait is a rubber-like body that renders him immune to punches, kicks, and ki blasts — damage simply disappears into his body without effect. Goku defeated him not by force but by dragging him to the ring's edge by his legs and throwing him out via ring-out. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Tournament of Destroyers / Botamo, via WebSearch; CBR: Universe 6 Tournament every match, via WebSearch]`
- **Recurring elimination pattern** — Botamo returned for the Tournament of Power and was again eliminated by ring-out: Vegeta tied his arms into a knot, exploiting the same mobility vulnerability Goku had found at the Tournament of Destroyers. The unkillable-but-immobilizable pattern defines his role as a high-durability, low-maneuverability fighter. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Botamo, via WebSearch]`

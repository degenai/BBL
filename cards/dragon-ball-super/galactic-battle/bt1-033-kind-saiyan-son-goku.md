## Trivia

- **Set context** — Galactic Battle (BT1) is the inaugural main booster set of the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, released July 28, 2017. The set contains 114 cards (60 Common, 30 Uncommon, 12 Rare, 8 Super Rare, 4 Special Rare) and established the game's Blue/Red/Green/Yellow color system. BT1-033 is a Common Blue-color Battle card. `[WebSearch snippet: TCDB Galactic Battle 2017, dbs-cardgame.com card list]`

- **Mechanical role** — BT1-033 is a Blue-color Battle card with 5000 Power and 1 energy cost. Its skill lets the player look at up to 7 cards from the top of their deck, add up to 1 Blue Son Goku card to hand, then shuffle. As a low-cost searcher for Son Goku cards, it functions as an early-game setup piece — thematically fitting for a card titled "Kind Saiyan" (helping the hand rather than fighting). `[WebSearch snippet: dbs-cardgame.com BT1-033, TrollAndToad listing]`

- **Character context** — Son Goku is the central protagonist of Dragon Ball Super, the 2015 manga and anime sequel by Akira Toriyama. The "Kind Saiyan" title references a canonical tension in Goku's character: he is a member of the warrior Saiyan race whose defining trait is nonetheless his guileless goodwill rather than his combat instinct. `[WebSearch snippet: Wikipedia Dragon Ball Super]`

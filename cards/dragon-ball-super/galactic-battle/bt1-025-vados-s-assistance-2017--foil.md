## Trivia

- **Set context** — BT1-025 is from Galactic Battle (DBS-B01), the inaugural booster set of the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, released July 28, 2017. The 114-card set was the first competitive-legal product in the game and established the format's baseline. Vados's Assistance is a Common-rarity EXTRA card with a red color identity and 1 energy cost. `[PSA Set Registry: DBS BT1]` `[dbs-cardgame.com card search]`

- **Character context** — Vados is an Angel and the attendant and martial arts trainer of Champa, the God of Destruction of Universe 6. She is one of twelve children of the Grand Minister and the older sister of Whis (attendant to Beerus of Universe 7). Her power is stated to equal or exceed Whis's, placing her among the strongest beings in the Dragon Ball Super cosmology. `[Dragon Ball Wiki (Fandom): Vados]`

- **Mechanical note** — In the DBS card game, BT1-025's effect negates an incoming attack and then allows you to switch up to 1 of your Battle Cards to Active Mode — a defensive tempo tool consistent with Vados's attendant/support role in the anime. The card has a red color identity, aligning with Champa's Universe 6 faction. `[dbs-cardgame.com card search BT1-025]` `[totalcards.net listing]`

- **Lore context** — Vados debuted in the Dragon Ball Super anime and manga during the Universe 6 arc, which introduced the Tournament of Destroyers structure that became a recurring format throughout the series. Her inclusion in the launch set reflects her status as a new tier-one character in the DBS era, alongside Whis and Beerus. `[Dragon Ball Wiki (Fandom): Vados]`

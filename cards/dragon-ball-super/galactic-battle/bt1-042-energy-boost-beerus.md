## Trivia

- **Set context** — Galactic Battle (BT1) was the inaugural main booster set for the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, released July 28, 2017, comprising 114 cards. It is the foundational set for the game and the primary print run that introduced U6/U7 cosmology characters to the card game format. [Trading Card Database: 2017 Dragon Ball Super Card Game BT1 Galactic Battle]
- **Design** — Beerus is the God of Destruction of Universe 7 and the twin brother of Champa (U6). His design was partially inspired by a Cornish Rex cat that lived with Akira Toriyama during development; the naming derives from "virus," which Toriyama reportedly misread as "beer" — seeding the alcohol-themed naming pun used for all subsequent GoDs and attendants (Whis = whisky, Vados = Calvados, Champa = Champagne). [Wikipedia: Beerus]
- **Mechanical** — BT1-042 is a Blue Battle card with 20,000 power, energy cost 4, Double Strike, and an Auto skill that KOs an opponent Battle Card when played with 5 or more energy. The energy-threshold KO maps mechanically to Beerus's in-universe role: destructive power that activates at full capacity. [Big Orbit Cards / Games Haven TCG: BT1-042 listing]
- **Set context** — Beerus and Champa are the only known sibling pair among DBS's twelve Gods of Destruction; each presides over one of a canonically paired universe (U7 and U6). This twin-destroyer structure is foundational to DBS multiverse cosmology, introduced in the God of Destruction Champa Arc. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Champa, via WebSearch snippet]

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Galactic Battle (DBS-B01) was the inaugural booster set for the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, released July 28, 2017. Its 114-card pool spans the Battle of Gods, Resurrection F, and Universe 6 Tournament arcs plus the original Broly film continuity — a multi-arc design pattern that every subsequent DBS-B main series would follow. `[TCDB: 2017 Dragon Ball Super Card Game BT1 Galactic Battle; Bleeding Cool: Galactic Battle Checklist]`
- **Mechanical design** — BT1-059 is a Super Rare Green Battle card (25,000 Power) with an Evolve ability (GGG2: Son Goku) that grants [Double Strike], upgrading to [Triple Strike] via [Permanent] if Krillin is in the Drop Area — a deliberate reward for running the Turtle School ensemble. Its [Auto] on-play wipes opposing Battle Cards with combined cost 6 or less, a broad-sweeper in the format's early-game context. `[TrollAndToad: Awakening Rage Son Goku BT1-059]`
- **Lore context** — The "Awakening Rage" title references the canonical Dragon Ball Z mechanism for Super Saiyan transformation: extreme emotional distress as the power catalyst. Bandai's character tag for BT1-059 is Son Goku (standard Z-and-DBS adult), era: Frieza Saga — placing this print within the Resurrection F arc where SSB is newly achieved. `[dbs-cardgame.com card database; Dragon Ball Wiki: Super Saiyan]`
- **Design layering** — The first set includes multiple Goku prints across transformation tiers: BT1-030 (Rare, SSB Goku) and BT1-032 (Super Rare, SSB Goku) occupy the top of the power stack while BT1-059 anchors the earlier Super Saiyan tier — Bandai deliberately laddering Goku's canonical transformation arc across rarity slots within a single set. `[Bleeding Cool: Dragon Ball Super Card Game Galactic Battle Checklist]`

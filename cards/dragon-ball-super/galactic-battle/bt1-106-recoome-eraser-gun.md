## Trivia

- **Set context** — Recoome Eraser Gun (BT1-106) is an Uncommon Yellow Extra card from Galactic Battle (BT1), the launch set of the Dragon Ball Super Card Game (July 28, 2017). As an Extra card it functions as a Counter: Attack — when your Leader Card is Yellow, it negates an attack and prevents all opposing Battle Cards from attacking for that turn `[House Rules Gaming / Big Orbit Cards product listings, via WebSearch; PSA CardFacts]`.
- **Technique** — The Recoome Eraser Gun is a mouth-fired energy wave; Recoome charges pink energy in his mouth and releases it as a massive blast capable of distorting terrain. In DBZ it was directed at Vegeta on Planet Namek; Gohan saved Vegeta at the last instant while Krillin struck Recoome from behind, interrupting the beam and shattering most of Recoome's teeth — the attack's defining comedic beat `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Recoome Eraser Gun, via WebSearch]`.
- **Character role** — Recoome is the most physically imposing member of the Ginyu Force and canonically its most flamboyant: he poses before every special move. In the Namek arc he single-handedly incapacitated Vegeta, Krillin, and Gohan before Goku's arrival. His effortless dominance then served as the benchmark by which Goku's Namek-trained power was measured — Goku neutralized him in a single strike `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Recoome; TV Tropes: Dragon Ball Z Namek Arc Recap, via WebSearch]`.
- **Ginyu Force cohort signal** — The Ginyu Force fills the narrative gap between Frieza's standard henchmen and Frieza himself, functioning as a tiered power escalation device. Recoome, as the Force's "muscle," anchors that tier. His appearance in BT1 alongside other Ginyu Force-adjacent cards makes BT1 a natural cohort anchor for any Ginyu Force lair `[Screen Rant: Ginyu Force analysis, via WebSearch]`.

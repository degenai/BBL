## Trivia

- **Set context** — Cabba is a Universe 6 Saiyan, one of the first U6 Saiyans encountered in DBS canon. Unlike Universe 7 Saiyans, U6 Saiyans lack tails and inhabit Planet Sadala. Cabba had never witnessed or heard of the Super Saiyan transformation before the Champa Arc tournament, making his first transformation a genuine discovery rather than a training milestone. [Dragon Ball Official Site: Weekly Character Showcase no. 9 — Cabba, confirmed via WebSearch snippet]
- **Set context** — Cabba's Super Saiyan transformation was unlocked during his tournament match against Vegeta in the Champa Arc. Vegeta triggered it by threatening to destroy Planet Sadala and Cabba's family, provoking rage sufficient to ignite the form — mirroring the classic Saiyan grief/rage catalyst pattern while establishing that U6 Saiyans carry the same latent transformation potential. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Cabba / CBR: Cabba Explained, via WebSearch snippets]
- **Design** — The card title "Raging Cabba" encodes the rage-catalyst narrative. BT1-013 depicts the Super Saiyan state rather than Cabba's base form, anchoring the most narratively loaded beat of his tournament arc as the card's primary image — a deliberate card design choice. [Trading Card Database: BT1 Galactic Battle set listing]
- **Set context** — Cabba subsequently helped Caulifla achieve the Super Saiyan transformation, making him the transmission node for the form within Universe 6. Cabba, Caulifla, and Kale all achieved transformations almost immediately after becoming aware of them — noted in canon as unusual compared to Universe 7 Saiyan training requirements. [CBR: Dragon Ball Super Universe 6 Saiyans / Screen Rant: Universe 6 Saiyans strength, via WebSearch snippets]

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Galactic Battle (BT1) was the first English main booster set for the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, released July 28, 2017. Its 114 cards established the Blue/Red/Green/Yellow color system the game still uses, and Special Rare copies from BT1 were selling for $600–$700 per sealed box by early 2021 as the set became scarce. `[PSA Set Registry: Galactic Battle article; Bleedingcool.com Galactic Battle Checklist]`
- **Set lore / era** — BT1-032 is assigned to the Champa Saga era on the official DBS card database, placing it in the Universal Tournament story arc from Dragon Ball Super. The card belongs to the Blue color family alongside the Beerus Leader archetype, reflecting SSGSS's divine-ki blue aesthetic. `[dbs-cardgame.com official card list]`
- **Character / transformation** — SSGSS (Super Saiyan God Super Saiyan, also called Super Saiyan Blue) is Goku's divine transformation achieved after training with Beerus and Whis, first shown in Dragon Ball Z: Resurrection F (2015) and introduced in Dragon Ball Super Episode 24 ("Clash! Frieza vs. Goku — This Is the Result of My Training!"). The form is visually defined by blue hair, blue eyes, and a crackling blue-white aura — the visual signature on this card. `[Game Rant: Super Saiyan Blue Explained; Dragon Ball Wiki Fandom via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Mechanical profile** — BT1-032 is a 4-cost Blue Battle card with 15,000 base power, the Critical keyword (damage dealt goes to opponent's Drop Area instead of hand), and a Permanent ability granting +10,000 power during your turn when you control 5 or more energy — making it a 25,000-power attacker in the mid-to-late game. Combo power is 10,000. `[DBZExchange product listing; CoreTCG card entry]`

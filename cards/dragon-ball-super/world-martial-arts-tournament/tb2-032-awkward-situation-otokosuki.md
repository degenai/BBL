## Trivia

- **Character / set-lore context** — Otokosuki is a canon Dragon Ball Z character who competes in the 28th World Martial Arts Tournament. He is one of two explicitly gay characters in the original Dragon Ball series (alongside General Blue), and his tournament scenes are played for comedic discomfort — particularly an anime-exclusive scene where he flirtatiously winks at Trunks during the matchup announcement, causing Trunks to recoil. [WebSearch: Dragon Ball Wiki Fandom - Otokosuki / LGBT Characters Wikia - Otokosuki]
- **Mechanical design** — TB2-032's game mechanic encodes the source scene directly: the card can be played for free (bypassing the 4[B] energy cost) if 'Awkward Situation Trunks' is already in the Battle Area and 'Awkward Situation Otokosuki' is not — literally recreating Otokosuki chasing Trunks as a game-state trigger. Color: Blue, Power: 15000, Common rarity. [WebSearch: DBZ Exchange TB2-032 / Orange Men retailer listing]
- **Art register** — The pink-and-sparkle visual language deployed in this card art deliberately borrows shojo romance anime aesthetic codes (screen-tone sparkles, soft lavender background, wide-eyed flustered expression) into a martial arts tournament context. This is a direct visual reference to how Otokosuki's scenes were animated in Dragon Ball Z, where romantic-comedy visual grammar was applied to signal the discomfort of the other fighters. [WebSearch: Bleeding Cool TB02 checklist / Dragon Ball Wiki Otokosuki]

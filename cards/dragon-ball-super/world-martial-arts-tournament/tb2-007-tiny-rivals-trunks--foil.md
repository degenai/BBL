## Trivia

- **Set context** — The World Martial Arts Tournament themed booster (DBS-TB02) was released by Bandai in September 2018 and focuses on the 25th World Martial Arts Tournament arc of Dragon Ball Z — the Budokai arc where Kid Trunks competed. As an Uncommon Foil (TB2-007), this is the foil-variant tier of the set's mid-rarity pull. `[WebSearch: Dragon Ball Super World Martial Arts Tournament TB2 September 2018]`
- **Tournament canon** — Kid Trunks competed in the Junior Division of the 25th World Martial Arts Tournament and defeated his best friend Goten in the finals, becoming Junior Champion. The "Tiny Rivals" title references the Trunks/Goten rivalry — both are demi-Saiyan children of similar age and power, sons of the two most prominent Z Fighters, and are consistently depicted as each other's primary competition. `[WebSearch: Trunks Dragon Ball Z 25th World Martial Arts Tournament Junior Division Goten]`
- **Sword note** — Kid Trunks received the Brave Sword from the hero Tapion at the end of the non-canon film Dragon Ball Z: Wrath of the Dragon. Future Trunks carries an entirely separate sword from a different timeline; the two blades are visually similar but canonically unrelated. The sword iconography in this card likely invokes the broader Trunks-as-sword-wielder archetype rather than a specific canon sword-acquisition event. `[WebSearch: Future Trunks sword Kid Trunks Tapion Wrath of the Dragon timeline distinction]`

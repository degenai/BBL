## Trivia

- **Character lore** — Videl is Mr. Satan's daughter, raised by him after her mother's death in infancy. She trained in martial arts hoping to equal her father — and in practice exceeded him without knowing it. She is noted as the only known female Martial Arts Champion in any tournament division in the Dragon Ball franchise. She later blackmailed Gohan into teaching her to fly by threatening to expose his Great Saiyaman identity. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Videl / Wikipedia: Videl]
- **"Heroic Duo" framing** — The card title references Videl's crime-fighting partnership with Gohan as the Great Saiyawoman / Great Saiyaman duo before and during the 25th World Tournament arc. The pairing began as a blackmail arrangement and evolved into marriage: Videl becomes Gohan's wife and the mother of Pan (a quarter-Saiyan who appears in Dragon Ball GT). [Dragon Ball Wiki: Videl / CBR: Dragon Ball Gohan Wife Relationship History]
- **Set placement** — Appearing in the World Martial Arts Tournament set (TB2, September 2018) alongside her father Hercule (TB2-001), Videl anchors the set's human-fighter representation of the 25th Tournament. The "Heroic Duo" title positions her as part of the Gohan pair — her counterpart card presumably being a Gohan entry in the same set. [dbs-cardgame.com: WMAT Design Concept / TCGplayer: World Martial Arts Tournament]

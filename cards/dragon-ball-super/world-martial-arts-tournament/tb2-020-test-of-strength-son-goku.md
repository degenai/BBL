## Trivia

- **Set context** — The World Martial Arts Tournament themed booster (DBS-TB02) was released by Bandai in September 2018. TB2-020 is a Rare-rarity Battle Card — the mid-tier pull below Super Rare. The set contains 12 Rare cards among its 77 unique cards. `[WebSearch: Dragon Ball Super World Martial Arts Tournament TB2 September 2018 card breakdown]`
- **Gi and base-form framing** — The orange martial arts gi is Goku's canonical tournament uniform worn consistently across all Dragon Ball series from the original Dragon Ball through Dragon Ball Super. The base-form, no-aura composition of this card contrasts with the Super Saiyan close-up of TB2-002. Goku often deliberately suppresses his power early in tournament matches to assess opponents — the "test of strength" title frames the base-form read as discipline, not limitation. `[WebSearch: Dragon Ball World Martial Arts Tournament Goku base form orange gi]`
- **Pair design** — TB2-002 (Super Rare) and TB2-020 (Rare) form a two-card Goku representation pair within DBS-TB02: one as the peak-power Super Saiyan portrait, one as the disciplined base-form striker. The DBS TCG regularly uses multi-card character representation within a single set to show different modes or contexts of the same character. `[DBS-TB02 card list, DBZ Exchange: TB2-020]`

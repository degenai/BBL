## Trivia

- **Set context** — TB2-036 is a Common from the World Martial Arts Tournament Themed Booster (DBS-TB02), distinct from the Uncommon double-sided TB2-034. At $0.07 market value it sits near the floor of the set — named iconic characters in DBS TCG as Commons typically serve as combo fodder; their pull value is eclipsed by the Rare and Super Rare slots. [WebSearch: PriceCharting Dragon Ball Super World Martial Arts Tournament]
- **Lore context** — 'Trading Blows' captures Goku's preferred combat style at this tournament: close-quarters exchange rather than ki-beam exchanges. At the 28th Tournament, Goku was deliberately matching Uub's pace rather than overwhelming him — the 'trading' framing reflects restraint, not limitation. [WebSearch: Game Rant Dragon Ball Who Is Uub / Dragon Ball Official Site Uub]
- **Character history** — Son Goku first appeared in Akira Toriyama's Dragon Ball manga in 1984 (Shonen Jump, issue 51). The 28th World Martial Arts Tournament depicted in this set marks one of his few tournament appearances where winning was explicitly not the goal: Goku entered to scout Uub as his successor and training partner. The tournament ends with Goku flying off with Uub to train him — the last scene of Dragon Ball Z proper. [WebSearch: Game Rant Dragon Ball Who Is Uub / Dragon Ball Official Site]

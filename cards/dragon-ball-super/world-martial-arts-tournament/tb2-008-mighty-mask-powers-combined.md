## Trivia

- **Character lore** — Mighty Mask is the borrowed identity Goten and Kid Trunks use to enter the adult division of the 25th World Martial Arts Tournament. The real Mighty Mask never competed: Trunks lured him away with the promise of meeting an attractive woman, knocked him out, and stole his costume. The two boys fought inside the costume until Android 18 used a Destructo Disc to cut it in half during their match, exposing them and causing immediate disqualification — teams are forbidden in tournament rules. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Mighty Mask]
- **Design / card title** — "Powers Combined" is a direct reference to the moment Trunks and Goten simultaneously transformed into Super Saiyans inside the Mighty Mask costume during the fight against Android 18. The dual transformation visually inflated the costume and was the visual tell that confirmed 18's suspicion she was fighting two children rather than one fighter. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Mighty Mask / Dragon Ball Wiki: World Martial Arts Tournament]
- **Cluster significance** — Mighty Mask is the diegetic "combined form" of the TB2-004/007 Goten-Trunks Link Card pair. In the World Martial Arts Tournament set's design logic, TB2-008 functions as the narrative resolution of the Tiny Rivals duo — the costume is what Goten and Trunks become when their rivalry becomes collaboration. [Official DBS Card Game: WMAT Design Concept]

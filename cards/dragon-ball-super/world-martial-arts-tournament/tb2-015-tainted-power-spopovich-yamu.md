## Trivia

- **Character lore** — Spopovich and Yamu were Babidi's sleeper agents at the 25th World Martial Arts Tournament. Both were former human martial artists whose traces of evil made them susceptible to Babidi's Manipulation Sorcery. Yamu was the more calculating of the two, twice restraining Spopovich from killing Videl mid-match because the mission — gathering energy — took priority. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Yamu; Villains Wiki: Spopovich]`
- **Story pivot** — The energy-drain scene is the narrative hinge of the arc: Spopovich pinned a freshly transformed Super Saiyan 2 Gohan in place while Yamu stabbed him with the Energy Absorber device. This act collapses the tournament story into the Babidi Saga. After delivering the energy, Babidi killed both men immediately — Spopovich via exploding magic, Yamu via Pui Pui's blast on Babidi's order. `[CBR: Buu Saga World Tournament recap; Dragon Ball Wiki: Yamu]`
- **Title framing** — 'Tainted Power' names their condition precisely: the energy they harvested and the transformation they triggered was never theirs to use. They were vessels for a plan they didn't understand, executed by a sorcerer who never intended them to survive it. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Yamu]`

## Trivia

- **Lore context** — Master Shen holds the title Crane Hermit (Tsuru Sennin) and leads the Crane School, whose philosophy centers on killing opponents rather than tournament sportsmanship. He trained Tien Shinhan and Chiaotzu as weapons — students shaped for assassination, not martial development. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Master Shen, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Origin and fall** — Shen and Roshi trained together under the legendary Master Mutaito. After the King Piccolo crisis their paths diverged: Roshi continued on a principled path; Shen drifted toward assassination-focused martial arts. Younger brother Mercenary Tao (TB2-048) monetized that same philosophy as pure contract killing. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Master Shen; CBR: Forgotten Dragon Ball Decision Ruined Tien's Life]`
- **Canonical 'boss reveals true nature' moment** — At the 22nd World Martial Arts Tournament, Shen ordered Chiaotzu to psychically paralyze Goku during Tien's match. When Tien refused to kill Goku and defected, Shen ordered both killed. Roshi blasted Shen out of the stadium with a Kamehameha to save Chiaotzu. `[CBR: Forgotten Dragon Ball Decision Ruined Tien's Life, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Stewardship inverted** — Tien's eventual defection from the Crane School is one of Dragon Ball's clearest 'student breaks from the contractor' moments: the pupil refusing the apparatus-of-extraction his master built, choosing Roshi's Turtle School path instead. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Master Shen, via WebSearch snippet]`

## Connections

- [[crane-school-cohort]] — Master Shen (the Crane Hermit, Tsuru Sennin) is the cohort's schoolmaster — the canonical founder of the Tsuru Sen-ryū whose assassination-focused pedagogy shaped Tien and Chiaotzu as weapons and whose younger brother Mercenary Tao monetized the same philosophy as pure contract killing. Shen and Roshi canonically trained together under the legendary Master Mutaito; after the King Piccolo crisis their paths diverged into Crane (paid-killer) and Turtle (protect-the-weak) lineages, and TB2's parallel-construction *Martial Meister* card titles (Shen TB2-063, Roshi TB2-057) canonicalize that schoolmaster-rivalry as a designer-coordinated paired-print within the same 2018 booster. The 22nd Budokai climax — Shen ordering Chiaotzu to paralyze Goku, Tien refusing the contract and defecting, Shen ordering both disciples killed, Roshi's Kamehameha blasting him out of the stadium to save Chiaotzu — is the canonical moment the cohort's apparatus collapses under its own pedagogy, the *contractor* exposed and the *workers* freed.

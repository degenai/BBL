## Trivia

- **Set context** — World Martial Arts Tournament (DBS-TB02) is a 2018 Bandai themed booster set focused on the Babidi arc. Yakon is one of Babidi's three principal henchmen (with Pui Pui and Dabura), sent to drain energy to fuel Majin Buu's resurrection. His Common rarity in this set places him at the base of the villain roster — appropriately, since he is the weakest of the three henchmen (Babidi rates his power at 800 Kili vs. Goku's 3,000 Kili as a Super Saiyan) `[dbs-cardgame.com: TB02 card list; dragon-ball-official.com: Character Showcase Yakon no. 163]`.
- **Design** — TB2-017's game mechanic makes the card grow stronger under pressure (gaining +5000 power per card stacked beneath it as opponents attack), then self-destructs when 5 or more cards accumulate. This is a direct mechanical translation of Yakon's in-story defeat: he grows stronger by absorbing Goku's light energy during battle, then Goku overloads him with Super Saiyan output until Yakon literally explodes. The card's self-destruction threshold maps to his fictional overconsumption death `[WebSearch snippet: Razor's Edge Yakon card details]`.
- **Character context** — Yakon originates from the Planet of Darkness, a world permanently shrouded in shadow. His species evolved to see in the dark and absorb light as sustenance. He is one of the few Dragon Ball Z antagonists defeated through the mechanic of his own ability rather than brute force — Goku doesn't overpower him in combat, he weaponizes Yakon's appetite against him. The card name "Razor's Edge" references Yakon's large slashing claws, which are his primary physical weapon `[dragon-ball-official.com: Weekly Character Showcase Yakon no. 163; WebSearch snippet: Dragon Ball Wiki Yakon]`.

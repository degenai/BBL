## Trivia

- **Set context** — TB2-034 is an Uncommon from the World Martial Arts Tournament Themed Booster (DBS-TB02, 2018), a 125-card set drawing exclusively from the 28th World Martial Arts Tournament arc of Dragon Ball Z. The double-slash naming convention ('Son Goku // Stopping Power Son Goku') indicates participation in TB02's Link Card mechanic — two-card pairs that unlock additional effects when fielded together. [WebSearch: Bleeding Cool TB02 checklist / DBZ Exchange TB02 booster box]
- **Character design** — The orange gi and dark-blue undershirt visible in the card art are Goku's defining visual signature, worn consistently across Dragon Ball, Dragon Ball Z, and early Dragon Ball Super. This design originated with Akira Toriyama in the 1984 launch of the Dragon Ball manga and has remained functionally unchanged for four decades. [WebSearch: Dragon Ball Official Site]
- **Lore context** — 'Stopping Power' frames Goku's role at the 28th Tournament not as a combatant seeking victory but as a scout: he entered the tournament specifically to find and test Uub, deliberately holding back and provoking the young fighter to measure his potential. Goku's goal was succession rather than winning — the 28th Tournament is the only major tournament in Dragon Ball Z where Goku competed without intent to claim the title. [WebSearch: Game Rant Dragon Ball Who Is Uub / Dragon Ball Official Site Uub]

## Trivia

- **Set context** — World Martial Arts Tournament (DBS-TB02) is a 2018 Bandai themed booster set focused on the 25th World Martial Arts Tournament arc and the Babidi/Majin Buu buildup. Dabura is the arc's principal enforcer before Buu's resurrection, and his presence here as a Common card reflects the set's intent to populate the Babidi Saga villain roster across all rarity tiers `[dbs-cardgame.com: TB02 product page]`.
- **Design** — TB2-014 is a Link Card (Common, Red, 20000 power, energy cost 4) tagged with special traits Demon Realm Race/World Tournament. It gains Barrier when its link partner Dark Duo Babidi is in the battle area — meaning Dabura and Babidi must be deployed together for maximum effect, mirroring their codependent relationship in the source material. Its play effect persistently Warps two cards from the opponent's hand for as long as Dabura stays on the field, representing Dabura's role as a sustained, dominant battlefield presence `[dbzexchange.com: TB2-014; WebSearch snippet: Dark Duo Dabura card details]`.
- **Character context** — Dabura is the Supreme King of the Demon Realm, enslaved to Babidi's will for approximately 300 years before Dragon Ball Z's events. His power is compared to Perfect Cell's. After Majin Buu turns him into a cookie and eats him, Lord Enma — recognizing that Dabura would relish Hell — sends him to Heaven instead, where the demon king reforms entirely. Dragon Ball DAIMA (2024) retroactively establishes that Dabura and Babidi originate from another universe, a new layer of lore added decades after the character's debut `[dragon-ball-official.com: Weekly Character Showcase Dabura; CBR: Dragon Ball DAIMA Confirms These DBZ Villains Were From Another Universe All Along]`.

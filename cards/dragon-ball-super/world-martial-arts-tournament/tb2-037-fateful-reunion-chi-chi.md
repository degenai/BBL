## Trivia

- **Lore context** — The 'Fateful Reunion' title directly references Chi-Chi's canonical 23rd World Martial Arts Tournament appearance, where she competed under the alias 'Anonymous' (preliminary number 59) and faced Goku in a match before he recognized her. This is her first tournament combat appearance as an adult. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Chi-Chi, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Lineage anchor** — Chi-Chi is the daughter of the Ox-King, wife of Goku, mother of Gohan and Goten, grandmother of Pan (through Gohan and Videl), and great-great-grandmother of Goku Jr. in the GT timeline — making her the matrilineal human anchor for a 4-generation Son family fighting lineage. `[Wikipedia: Chi-Chi (Dragon Ball); CBR: Goku's Complete Family Tree]`
- **Quarter-Saiyan chain** — Pan (TB2-024) is a quarter-Saiyan: Goku (Saiyan) → Gohan (half-Saiyan) → Pan (quarter-Saiyan). Chi-Chi is the human grounding force at each generational transition, with Pan inheriting fighting spirit through both the Saiyan and human lines. `[Wikipedia: Chi-Chi (Dragon Ball); Dragon Ball Wiki: Pan]`
- **Rarity note** — TB2-037 is a Rare, the same rarity tier as Pan (TB2-024), reflecting the set's consistent treatment of the Son family female fighters as mid-tier pulls. `[dbs-cardgame.com/us-en/product/dbs-tb02.php]`

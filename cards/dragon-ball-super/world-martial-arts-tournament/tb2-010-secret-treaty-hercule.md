## Trivia

- **Set context** — DBS-TB02 World Martial Arts Tournament is Bandai's second themed booster for the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, released September 21, 2018. The set spans multiple in-universe tournament eras — each color covers a different historical WMAT — with 77 unique card designs across Normal and Foil variants. [dbs-cardgame.com product page; WebSearch retailer listings]
- **Tournament position** — Hercule (Mr. Satan in Japanese) first won the 24th World Martial Arts Tournament legitimately, defeating Spopovich and Jewel in a bracket the Z-Fighters did not enter. He held the WMAT championship from the 24th through at least the 28th tournament, winning repeatedly because the Z-Fighters voluntarily withdrew or threw their matches to preserve his public image. [Wikipedia: Mr. Satan; CBR, "The World Protector's Mr. Satan, Explained"]
- **Card name lore** — "Secret Treaty" references the unspoken arrangement between Hercule and the Z-Fighters throughout Dragon Ball Z: they defeat the real threats (Cell, Buu), he takes public credit, the world's peace is preserved and his celebrity is maintained. The card's Era field is listed as "The Evil Wizard Babidi Saga" — the 25th WMAT arc in which this dynamic is most visible. [DBS card database via WebSearch]
- **Mechanical note** — TB2-010 is a Red Battle Card (Power 5000, cost 1) with Special Trait Earthling/World Tournament. Its [Activate:Main] effect lets you Rest this card plus one other World Tournament card to draw 1 card — a low-ceiling draw-smoothing role matching Hercule's in-universe support function rather than frontline combat value. [dbs-cardgame.com card database via WebSearch]

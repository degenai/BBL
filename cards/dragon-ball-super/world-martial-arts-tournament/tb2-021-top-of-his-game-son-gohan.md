## Trivia

- **Story context** — At the 25th World Martial Arts Tournament, Gohan agreed to demonstrate his Super Saiyan 2 transformation at Kibito's request — making himself an open target. Spopovich and Yamu immediately swarmed him and drained his energy to power Majin Buu's revival. The card title 'Top of His Game' is accurate and ironic simultaneously: Gohan was at his power peak and caught completely off-guard. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: World Tournament Saga; CBR: World Tournament Saga]`
- **Character arc** — This tournament appearance represents Gohan's narrative high-water mark in Dragon Ball Z. Community discussions frequently note that the 25th World Tournament is where Gohan's potential was most visibly demonstrated — and most immediately exploited — before later series arcs shifted his focus away from combat toward scholarship. `[CBR: Dragon Ball Z Who Would Have Won the 25th World Martial Arts Tournament]`
- **Set context** — In TB02, Gohan cards carry the 'World Tournament' special trait linking all 25th Tenkaichi Budokai participants. The set released September 21, 2019, with 125 cards total. `[dbs-cardgame.com: DBS-TB02 product page]`

## Trivia

- **Character lore** — Kid Trunks won the Junior Division championship at the 25th World Martial Arts Tournament, defeating Goten by ring-out. The win was not entirely clean: Trunks used his left arm, became a Super Saiyan, and fired an energy blast — all three things he had agreed not to do. Goten had also cheated by transforming, so the moral accounting is roughly even. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: World Martial Arts Tournament; Screen Rant: Z Warriors who won tournaments]`
- **Design** — Comedy-register card titles like 'Awkward Situation' are uncommon in the DBS card game, which trends toward power-proclamation titles. It signals a social or lobby moment rather than a combat scene — one of the few slice-of-life register cards in TB02, reflecting the tournament arc's dual nature as sports event and apocalyptic prelude. `[WebSearch: DBS TB02 card list]`
- **Character dynamics** — Vegeta specifically wanted his son to defeat Goku's son, adding parental stakes to what would otherwise be a kids' roughhousing match. The 'Awkward Situation' title may read against that gravity: a kid who is supposedly the scion of a proud Saiyan prince, caught mid-embarrassment in a tournament lobby. `[CBR: Dragon Ball Goten vs Trunks — Trunks Wins Even if He Loses]`

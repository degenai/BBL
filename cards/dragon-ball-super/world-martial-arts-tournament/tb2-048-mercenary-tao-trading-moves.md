## Trivia

- **Lore context** — Mercenary Tao (Tao Pai Pai) is the younger brother of Master Shen and the first professional assassin contracted to kill Goku in Dragon Ball canon. He charges ten billion Zeni per kill (100 million in the Japanese version) — labor purely on contract, no ideology, no loyalty. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Mercenary Tao, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Design history** — After being badly injured in the Red Ribbon Army arc, Tao paid to have his body extensively converted into a cyborg, gaining hidden blade and cannon weapons in his arms and a powered-up Super Dodonpa. He is one of Dragon Ball's earliest examples of the 'body-as-commodity' villain archetype: capital spent on self-modification to remain marketable as a killer. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Mercenary Tao, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Crane School lineage** — Tao's signature energy technique, the Dodonpa, is the Crane School's foundational move — directly connecting him to his brother Master Shen and by extension to Tien Shinhan and Chiaotzu. His mercenary practice is the monetized extension of Shen's assassination-focused philosophy. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Mercenary Tao, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Community resonance** — Community sentiment: Tao is frequently cited as an underrated early-series villain who punched above his narrative weight, noted as the first character to defeat Goku in single combat. `[Kanzenshuu forum: Tao Pai Pai, via WebSearch snippet]`

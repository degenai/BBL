## Trivia

- **Set context** — TB2-046 is part of DBS-TB02, the World Martial Arts Tournament Themed Booster, released September 21, 2018 — a 125-card set spanning the classic Dragon Ball tournament sagas. `[dbs-cardgame.com/us-en/product/dbs-tb02.php]`
- **Lore context** — Kami is the Namekian Guardian of Earth, the 'good half' produced when a nameless Namekian split his evil from himself. That evil half became King Piccolo. The previous Earth Guardian sensed the darkness in the arriving Namekian and relinquished the title only after the split was complete. `[CBR: How Kami Became Earth's Guardian; en.dragon-ball-official.com/news/01_1657.html]`
- **Design history** — During the Android Saga, Kami permanently fused with Piccolo to boost his power against the incoming android threat, temporarily leaving Earth without a Guardian until Dende took over. This fusion is the canonical endpoint of Kami's independent existence — the stewardship-tier elder dissolves into the next generation's protector. `[CBR: Kami/Piccolo/Dende Connection]`
- **Stewardship lineage** — Kami served as a mentor to young Goku and as an overseer to Gohan during training at the Lookout, functioning as the direct predecessor of Piccolo's own mentor role with Gohan. The stewardship chain runs: Kami → (fuses with) Piccolo → Gohan's trainer. `[en.dragon-ball-official.com/news/01_1657.html]`

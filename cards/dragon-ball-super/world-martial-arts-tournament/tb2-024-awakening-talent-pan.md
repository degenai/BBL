## Trivia

- **Lineage** — Pan is the daughter of Gohan and Videl, granddaughter of Goku and Mr. Satan, and great-granddaughter of both Ox-King and Bardock — a quarter-Saiyan, the furthest dilution of Saiyan blood in the main Dragon Ball family tree to demonstrate combat ability. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Pan; Wikipedia: Chi-Chi (Dragon Ball)]`
- **4-generation chain** — Pan represents four living generations of the Son family line: Goku (great-grandparent) → Gohan (parent) → Pan → Goku Jr. (GT timeline). Chi-Chi (TB2-037) is her paternal grandmother, the human anchor in each generational transition. `[CBR: Goku's Complete Family Tree; Dragon Ball Official Site: Pan showcase]`
- **Latent power** — Despite being only a quarter-Saiyan, Pan demonstrated the ability to fly as an infant in Dragon Ball Super — a feat that surprised veteran characters. In GT she is one of three primary protagonists. The card title 'Awakening Talent' directly references this exceptional latent capacity recognized across the franchise. `[Dragon Ball Official Site: Pan Weekly Showcase, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Inventory anchor note** — TB2-024 is the fifth canonical Pan anchor card in the overall inventory and a Rare rarity, same tier as Chi-Chi (TB2-037) — the set treats the female fighter side of the Son lineage consistently at mid-pull rarity. `[dbs-cardgame.com/us-en/product/dbs-tb02.php]`

## Trivia

- **Tournament position** — Krillin competed in the 21st, 22nd, and 23rd World Martial Arts Tournaments. At the 21st he reached the semi-finals before losing to Jackie Chun (Master Roshi in disguise); at the 22nd he defeated Chiaotzu via cleverness and again reached the semi-finals before losing to Goku; at the 23rd he was eliminated in the quarter-finals by Piccolo Jr. in a fight widely considered one of his best in the franchise. [CBR, "Every Martial Artist Who Fought in Dragon Ball's World Tournaments"; Kanzenshuu Rewatch thread via WebSearch]
- **Design detail** — Krillin's six forehead dots are incense-burn marks from his original Orin Temple monk training, predating his arrival at the Kame House. Toriyama explained in the Daizenshuu 2: Story Guide (1995) that Krillin has a physical idiosyncrasy allowing him to breathe through his skin — which is why he has no nose, a fact his 21st-tournament opponent Bacterian was unable to weaponize against him. [Kanzenshuu Rewatch thread citing Daizenshuu 2 via WebSearch]
- **Mechanical note** — TB2-041 is a Blue Rare Battle Card (Power 10000, cost 2) with a [When Played] effect: KO up to 1 of your opponent's Battle Cards with energy cost 2 or less. Compact, targeted removal that slots into the World Tournament trait engine without heavy energy investment. [DBS card database via WebSearch]
- **Krillin vs. Bacterian** — The 21st tournament Krillin/Bacterian match (anime episode 21, "Smells Like Trouble") is the fight where Goku shouts from ringside that Krillin has no nose and therefore cannot smell Bacterian's stench. Krillin wins instantly after that reminder, then ends the fight by flatulating in Bacterian's face. Both fighters appear in TB2 — Krillin as TB2-041, Bacterian as TB2-061. [CBR episode review; Dragon Ball Wiki Fandom via WebSearch]

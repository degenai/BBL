## Trivia

- **Set context** — World Martial Arts Tournament (TB2) launched September 21, 2018 as the second themed booster for the Dragon Ball Super Card Game. Unlike series boosters, TB2 is self-contained: complete competitive decks can be built from a single box. Each color focuses on a different tournament era from Dragon Ball history, and the set introduced Link Cards — mechanically paired cards whose art and effects work together. [Official DBS Card Game: WMAT Design Concept]
- **Character lore** — Hercule (real name Mark — a pun on Japanese "akuma," meaning devil/demon) is the reigning World Martial Arts Champion, a title won legitimately by beating normal humans during years when Goku and company were off-planet. He publicly claimed credit for Cell's defeat, making him Earth's most celebrated hero. He is Videl's father and, later, Majin Buu's unlikely best friend. Toriyama noted the character originates from a culture that does not use surnames. [Wikipedia: Mr. Satan / Dragon Ball Wiki: Mr. Satan]
- **Slot context** — TB2-001 holds the first card number in the World Martial Arts Tournament set — the literal opening slot of the set roster. For a character who is the canonical face of the World Tournament for normal humans, the numbering is narratively fitting even if mechanically modest at $0.15 market price. [TCGplayer: World Martial Arts Tournament]

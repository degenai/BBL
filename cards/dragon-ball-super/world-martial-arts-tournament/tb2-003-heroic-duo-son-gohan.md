## Trivia

- **Set context** — World Martial Arts Tournament (DBS-TB02) is a themed booster set released by Bandai in 2018, containing 125 cards focused on the 25th World Martial Arts Tournament arc and the Babidi/Majin Buu buildup. Leader cards in the set include Hercule, Uub, Son Goku, Jackie Chun, and the Announcer — a who's-who of the tournament arc's human side `[dbs-cardgame.com: TB02 product page]`.
- **Design** — TB2-003 is a Common Battle card (Red, 10000 power) with special traits Saiyan/Earthling/World Tournament and the era label The Evil Wizard Babidi Saga. Its entry effect KOs an opponent's Battle Card with 10000 power or less — "removal on entry" design that reflects Gohan's in-story role as the fighter who defeated Babidi's enforcer Dabura during this arc `[WebSearch snippet: Heroic Duo Son Gohan TCG details]`.
- **Character context** — The "Heroic Duo" naming pairs Gohan with his father Goku as the two dominant Saiyans present during the World Tournament/Babidi arc. Gohan is tagged as Son Gohan: Adolescence in the DBS TCG — the 16-year-old green-gi version who had slacked on training and grown leaner before Old Kai unlocked his full latent potential as Ultimate Gohan. Gohan is the canonical combatant who fights and defeats Dabura in the Babidi arc, making him the direct counter to TB2-014 (Dark Duo Dabura) in the same set's internal lore `[CBR: Dragon Ball Gohan Complete Timeline; Kanzenshuu: Gohan age breakdown]`.

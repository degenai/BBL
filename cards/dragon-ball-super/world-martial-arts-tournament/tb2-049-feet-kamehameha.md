## Trivia

- **Set context** — DBS-TB02 World Martial Arts Tournament was released September 21, 2018, as the second Themed Booster for the Dragon Ball Super Card Game (MASTERS format). The 125-card set draws exclusively from the original Dragon Ball tournament arcs — the 21st through 23rd Tenkaichi Budokai — spotlighting fighters, techniques, and support characters from that era. `[WebSearch: Bleeding Cool checklist, DBZ Exchange booster box page]`
- **Design** — TB2-049 is a green-color Common Extra card. Its skill: if your Leader Card is a green Son Goku, the active Battle Card gains +15,000 power and Critical (opponent places life cards in Drop Area rather than hand) for the duration of the battle. The mechanic explicitly anchors this technique to Son Goku, confirming the depicted figure. `[WebSearch: DBZ Exchange / House Rules Gaming product pages]`
- **Technique history** — The Feet Kamehameha debuted in Dragon Ball anime episode 146 and manga chapter 188, during Goku's fight with Piccolo Jr. at the 23rd World Martial Arts Tournament. Knocked head-first toward the ground, Goku fired the Kamehameha from his feet to reverse his fall and launch a double-punch counter. It has not appeared in the main manga or anime since — a single-use tournament-specific technique with an outsized legacy in Dragon Ball games and cards. `[WebSearch: CBR 'Dragon Ball: Why The Feet Kamehameha Should Come Back'; Quora thread]`

## Trivia

- **Design** — TB2-056 is a yellow-color Common Battle card with only 1,000 base power (1 yellow energy, 5,000 combo). It carries Blocker and a reactive auto-ability: when Toughened Up Krillin (TB2-053) is played, Chiaotzu draws 1 card and gains +14,000 power for the turn. The design reflects Chiaotzu's canonical role — low individual threat, high support value, functioning as a force-multiplier for his allies. `[WebSearch: Carta Magica TB2-056; Big Orbit Cards TB2-056]`
- **Set / lore context** — Chiaotzu is a disciple of the Crane School and reached the finals of the 22nd World Martial Arts Tournament in his first entry. His primary weapon is telekinesis — he can freeze opponents in place and communicate telepathically. Krillin defeated him at the 22nd Tenkaichi Budokai by asking arithmetic questions mid-match, disrupting his finger-counting and breaking the telekinetic hold. `[WebSearch: mnacardz.com 'The Powers and Abilities of Chiaotzu in Dragon Ball'; CBR '22nd World Martial Arts Tournament' article]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — At 1,000 base power Chiaotzu cannot meaningfully attack — but the +14,000 burst on Krillin's arrival effectively turns him into a 15,000-power surprise blocker for one turn. The Blocker keyword means he absorbs attacks the opponent directs at other cards, trading his minimal power for tactical disruption. The green energy art in the card (vs. his yellow color identity) is a DBS card game visual convention separating art palette from color coding. `[WebSearch: Carta Magica TB2-056 (color: Yellow); mnacardz.com Chiaotzu abilities]`

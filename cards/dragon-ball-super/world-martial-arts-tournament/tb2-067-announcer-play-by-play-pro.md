## Trivia

- **Set context** — TB2 (DBS-TB02) is the second themed booster set for the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, released October 2018 by Bandai. The entire set is dedicated to the World Martial Arts Tournament arc, with cards carrying the Earthling/World Tournament special trait to support tournament-themed leader strategies. `[Bandai DBS-TB02 product page, via WebSearch]`
- **Design** — TB2-067 is the third in a three-card Announcer mini-cycle within DBS-TB02: TB2-065 (Announcer // Announcer, Referee Veteran), TB2-066 (Announcer, Ever-Curious), and TB2-067 (Announcer, Play-By-Play Pro). Each version represents a distinct facet of the character's broadcast role, from officiating to color commentary. `[Bleedingcool DBS-TB02 checklist, via WebSearch; Big Orbit Cards listings, via WebSearch]`
- **Lore** — The World Tournament Announcer is a recurring background figure throughout Dragon Ball, Dragon Ball Z, Dragon Ball GT, and Dragon Ball Super — one of the franchise's longest-running unnamed characters. His blonde hair and suit are consistent visual identifiers across all series appearances. The card's draw-2 ability (active when 2+ World Tournament cards are in the Battle Area) directly models his role as a hype-man whose presence amplifies the tournament spectacle. `[Dragon Ball Wiki Fandom: World Tournament Announcer, via WebSearch]`

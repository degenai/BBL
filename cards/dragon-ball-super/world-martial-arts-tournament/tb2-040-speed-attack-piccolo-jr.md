## Trivia

- **Lore context** — Piccolo Jr. is the complete reincarnation of King Piccolo, born from an egg spat out as the Demon King died, inheriting all of his father's memories. He entered the 23rd World Martial Arts Tournament under the alias 'Ma Junior' to conceal his identity before his confrontation with Goku — who ultimately won that match when Piccolo landed outside the ring. `[Wikipedia: Piccolo (Dragon Ball)]`
- **Villain-to-mentor arc** — Piccolo Jr.'s arc is Dragon Ball's most structurally complete villain-to-stewardship pipeline: demon-king reincarnate → tournament rival → Gohan's harsh but devoted trainer → sacrificed himself protecting Gohan from Nappa (the first canonical moment of his redemption) → fused permanently with Kami. The TB2-040 card captures the earliest stage of this trajectory. `[Wikipedia: Piccolo (Dragon Ball); Saiyan Warrior: From Demon King to Dad of the Year]`
- **Stewardship chain** — As Gohan's mentor, Piccolo is the stewardship figure for the third Son generation. The chain runs: Kami (Guardian, TB2-046) → Piccolo (Kami's other half, eventual fusion) → Gohan's trainer → Pan's father is trained. Piccolo unlocks the solo Piccolo character node per wave 71 Edgelord flag; this card provides the fifth canonical anchor. `[Wikipedia: Piccolo (Dragon Ball)]`
- **Mechanical note** — TB2-040 is a Common rarity card, yet at $0.36 market price it is the highest-value common in DBS-TB02 — Piccolo's secondary-market presence vastly exceeds his print-rarity assignment in this set. `[pricecharting.com/console/dragon-ball-super-world-martial-arts-tournament]`

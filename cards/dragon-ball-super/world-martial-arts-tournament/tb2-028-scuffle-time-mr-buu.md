## Trivia

- **Set context** — TB2-028 is a Common from the World Martial Arts Tournament themed booster (DBS-TB02), the second themed expansion for the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, released September 21, 2018. TB2 is built around the tournament arc as a recurring competitive framing — hence the "Scuffle Time" title rather than a crisis or saga framing `[WebSearch: DBS TB02 release date]` `[dbs-cardgame.com TB02 product page]`.
- **Mechanical synergy** — Scuffle Time Mr. Buu has the Barrier keyword (cannot be chosen by opponent card effects) and a triggered ability: when placed at the bottom of its owner's deck, if Scuffle Time Son Goten (TB2-022) is in the Battle Area, return one opponent Battle Card to hand. The reverse is also true — Scuffle Time Son Goten gains +10,000 power and Active Mode when Scuffle Time Mr. Buu is in play. These two cards form an explicit named synergy pair within TB2 `[WebSearch: Scuffle Time Son Goten TB2 synergy]` `[CoreTCG: TB2-028 product listing]`.
- **Lore: reformed villain** — Good Buu (Mr. Buu) is the product of Innocent Buu's fission into Good and Evil halves — the Good portion was later given the name Mr. Buu by the main cast after Evil Buu was defeated. His reformation is notably mundane in the lore: Mr. Satan simply explained to Buu that he didn't have to kill people, and Buu accepted this. He subsequently trained under Mr. Satan and competed in World Martial Arts Tournaments as a fighter and ally rather than an antagonist `[Wikipedia: Majin Buu]` `[Dragon Ball Fandom: Good Buu]`.

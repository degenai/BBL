## Trivia

- **Character context** — The depicted fighter is Uub, the reincarnated human form of Kid Buu. Born from Goku's wish moments before defeating Buu with the Super Spirit Bomb, Uub enters the 28th World Martial Arts Tournament as a ten-year-old seeking prize money to feed his impoverished family. His match against Goku is the final story beat of Dragon Ball Z before the time skip — Goku deliberately provokes Uub to anger to unlock his suppressed power. [WebSearch: Dragon Ball Official Site Uub character showcase / Game Rant 'Who Is Uub?']
- **Mechanical design** — TB2-033's skill activates only when the Leader Card is Uub, granting +10000 power for the turn and a card draw. Without an Uub Leader it functions as an Uncommon battle card with no trigger — making it a dedicated Uub-deck support piece rather than a generic include. Uncommon rarity, Green color. [WebSearch snippet: CoreTCG TB2-033 listing]
- **Title context** — 'Shocking Latent Ability' directly references the in-universe reveal: Uub's ki dwarfs his apparent potential, suppressed by inexperience and lack of training. Goku had to goad him into rage to access it during their tournament bout. The title functions as both plot summary and card-mechanic flavor — the power is there, it just needs a trigger condition to surface. [WebSearch: Game Rant Dragon Ball Who Is Uub / Dragon Ball Official Site Uub]

## Connections

- [[babidi]] — Direct character depiction and anchor card for the Babidi cohort. This is the alien sorcerer son of Bibidi himself, ringmaster of the Buu Saga's revival operation and the namesake antagonist of DBS-TB02 *World Martial Arts Tournament*. Every other card in the TB02 villain cluster (Dabura, Spopovich & Yamu, Pui Pui, Yakon, the Spaceship) wires here through Babidi's Manipulation Sorcery hierarchy; this card *is* the foreman atop that vertical chain.

## Trivia

- **Set context** — TB2-013 is a Common from DBS-TB02 (Themed Booster pack World Martial Arts Tournament), released September 21, 2019 by Bandai. The 125-card set focuses on the 25th Tenkaichi Budokai arc and the early Buu Saga, with 30 Commons, 18 Uncommons, 12 Rares, 8 Super Rares, 8 Special Rares, and 1 Secret Rare. `[dbs-cardgame.com: DBS-TB02 product page]`
- **Character lore** — Babidi is the alien sorcerer son/clone of Bibidi, who orchestrated Majin Buu's revival. His plan required gathering energy from Earth's fighters at the 25th World Martial Arts Tournament — the exact event this card set is themed around. Babidi maintained control over Buu via the threat of re-sealing him, though Buu eventually destroyed Babidi to act freely. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Babidi]`
- **Art context** — The 'Dark Duo' title pairs Babidi with Majin Buu, and the organic pink interior background in the art is consistent with Majin Buu's body interior — a location Babidi frequently operated from and commanded Buu within. Their relationship was grotesque codependency: Babidi needed Buu as muscle; Buu tolerated Babidi just long enough to destroy him. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Babidi]`

## Trivia

- **Design** — TB2-057 is a yellow-color Uncommon Battle card (1 yellow energy, 6,000 power, 5,000 combo). It carries Barrier (cannot be targeted by opponent skills) and an Activate Main once-per-turn ability: switch Master Shen, Martial Meister (TB2) in the Battle Area to Rest Mode to draw 1 card. Roshi exhausts his rival for card advantage — Turtle School vs. Crane School as a gameplay resource mechanic. `[WebSearch: TrollAndToad TB2-057; TCGPlayer TB2-057]`
- **Set / lore context** — Master Roshi competed in the 21st and 22nd World Martial Arts Tournaments disguised as 'Jackie Chun' — wearing a wig to avoid recognition by his students. His intent was to ensure Goku and Krillin would lose, believing easy victory would cause them to stop improving. At the 22nd tournament he forfeited against Tien Shinhan rather than risk injury that might expose his identity. The alias 'Jackie Chun' is a reference to Jackie Chan. `[WebSearch: CBR 'Dragon Ball: How Master Roshi Got the Nickname Jackie Chun'; Wikipedia Master Roshi]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — Roshi's Barrier keyword makes him unusually resilient for a 6,000-power Uncommon — opponent skills simply cannot select him. Combined with the Shen-draws-a-card engine, he functions as a sticky draw piece in World Tournament builds. The Barrier keyword echoes Roshi's lore composure: untouchable not because of overwhelming force, but because of positioning and knowing what your opponent cannot do to you. `[WebSearch: TrollAndToad TB2-057; Chaos Cards TB2-057 listing]`

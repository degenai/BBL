## Trivia

- **Lore context** — Jackie Chun is the tournament alias Master Roshi adopted by gluing a white hair toupee onto his head and removing his glasses. He entered the 21st World Martial Arts Tournament under this name so his students would not know they were fighting their own master. `[CBR: How Master Roshi Got the Nickname Jackie Chun]`
- **Design history** — Roshi's stated rationale: if Goku or Krillin won the tournament, they might believe themselves the strongest in the world and stop training. By entering as a formidable unknown opponent, Roshi ensured they would face defeat and remain driven. Corrupted-stewardship-by-mask: mentorship exercised through deliberate concealment. `[CBR: How Master Roshi Got the Nickname Jackie Chun]`
- **Mechanical note** — This card is a Super Rare (TB2-058) in DBS-TB02, released September 21, 2018 — among the highest-rarity pulls in the themed booster, appropriate for the tournament's iconic disguised elder. `[dbs-cardgame.com/us-en/product/dbs-tb02.php]`
- **Rival lineage** — Roshi and Master Shen trained together under the legendary Master Mutaito, making the Jackie Chun and Master Shen (TB2-063) pairing within this set a canonical rivalry between two students of the same origin who chose opposite paths. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Master Shen, via WebSearch snippet]`

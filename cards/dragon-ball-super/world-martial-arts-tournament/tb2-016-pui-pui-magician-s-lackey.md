## Trivia

- **Character lore** — Pui Pui is the guardian of the first floor of Babidi's Spaceship and the weakest of Babidi's combat-tier henchmen. He hails from Planet Zoon, a world with 10x Earth's gravity — a fact he weaponizes as psychological intimidation. When Vegeta fought him on his home planet, Vegeta dispatched him in base form with contemptuous ease. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Pui Pui; Official Dragon Ball site character showcase no. 173]`
- **Narrative role** — Pui Pui executed the dirty work of killing Yamu on Babidi's order after the energy drain, then immediately served as the first boss of Babidi's Spaceship gauntlet. He represents the lowest tier of Babidi's hierarchy: expendable, arrogant, and easily discarded the moment his function was complete. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Pui Pui]`
- **Title framing** — 'Magician's Lackey' is a precise rank description: Pui Pui is not a general, not a strategist, and not trusted with anything approaching Babidi's plans. He guards a door. The title is accurate to the point of being almost dismissive. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Pui Pui]`

## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Mr. Satan (real name Mark; known as Hercule in some English dubs) legitimately won the 24th World Martial Arts Tournament against non-superhuman fighters during the years when Goku and other Z fighters were absent from the events. He then leveraged that title into celebrity status — something Master Roshi, Tien, and Goku had never done. He became the first tournament champion to successfully convert the title into cultural fame and ongoing media presence. `[WebSearch: Wikipedia 'Mr. Satan'; CBR 'Dragon Ball: The World Protector's Mr. Satan, Explained']`
- **Design** — TB2-064 is a yellow-color Common Extra card (1 energy). Its effect: if a Son Goku: Childhood Battle Card is in the Battle Area, draw two cards. Mr. Satan as a draw engine that only fires in Goku's presence is deliberately ironic — in lore, Mr. Satan claims credit for Goku's victories and would not acknowledge needing Goku to function at all. `[WebSearch: dbscards.fr French card DB TB2-064; DBZ Exchange TB2-064]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — Two-card draw for 1 energy is a strong return if the condition is met, making TB2-064 a premium payoff in decks running Son Goku: Childhood as a Battle Card. As an Extra (not a Battle Card), Mr. Satan contributes nothing to the board state — he is pure enablement, which tracks: in the Dragon Ball narrative his function is to take credit for outcomes he did not produce, not to produce them himself. `[WebSearch: DBZ Exchange TB2-064; dbscards.fr TB2-064]`

## Trivia

- **"Hidden Power" title** — Shin attended the 25th World Martial Arts Tournament in disguise, not to compete, but to covertly monitor Babidi and prevent the awakening of Majin Buu. The card title "Hidden Power" directly references this concealment of divine authority within a mundane tournament setting — a god watching from the stands. This makes him one of the few characters in the TB2 set whose presence at the tournament was deliberate subterfuge rather than competition. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Shin / CBR: Supreme Kai Trivia]
- **Character lore** — East Supreme Kai (Shin) is the youngest of the four original Supreme Kais responsible for the cardinal quadrants of Universe 7. He survived Majin Buu's first rampage — which killed the other three — by sustaining severe injuries. In Dragon Ball Super he fuses with his bodyguard Kibito via Potara earrings to become Kibito Kai, later separating via a wish to Porunga. Despite his divine rank — higher than Grand Kaio — he is described as the least powerful of the four original Supreme Kais. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Shin / CBR: Supreme Kai Trivia / Dragon Ball Official Site: Weekly Character Showcase]
- **Rarity inversion** — Shin is an Uncommon at $0.05 market price. His divine authority in the franchise (ranking above Grand Kaio, directly subordinate to Grand Zeno) does not map to card scarcity in the DBS TCG — a common pattern where narrative importance and print rarity diverge at the common/uncommon tier. [TCGplayer: World Martial Arts Tournament]

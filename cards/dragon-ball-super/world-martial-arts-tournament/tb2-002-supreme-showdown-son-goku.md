## Trivia

- **Set context** — The World Martial Arts Tournament themed booster (DBS-TB02) was released by Bandai in September 2018. As a Super Rare (SR), TB2-002 is among the premium pulls in a set of 77 unique cards (125 with foil variants). The extreme close-up portrait composition is the DBS TCG's visual language for high-rarity Goku cards — proximity signals power intensity rather than action posing. `[WebSearch: Dragon Ball Super World Martial Arts Tournament TB2 September 2018 card count]`
- **Tournament lore** — Goku was granted one day of life by King Yemma to compete in the 25th World Martial Arts Tournament (the Budokai arc). His scheduled match against Vegeta — canonically one of the most anticipated matchups in the series — was never completed, interrupted by the Babidi plot. The "supreme showdown" in the title may reference that unrealized fight as much as any actual on-screen battle. `[WebSearch: Dragon Ball Z 25th World Martial Arts Tournament Goku one day alive Budokai Majin Buu interruption]`
- **Rarity note** — TB2-002 is the second card in the set's numbering (after the Secret Rare slot), placing it at the front of the Super Rare tier. In the DBS TCG, low collector numbers within a rarity tier typically correspond to marquee characters — Son Goku at no. 002 establishes him as the face of this themed product. `[DBS-TB02 card list, DBZ Exchange: TB2-002]`

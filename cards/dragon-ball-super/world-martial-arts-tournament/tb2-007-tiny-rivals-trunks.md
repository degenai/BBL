## Trivia

- **Character lore** — Kid Trunks is Vegeta and Bulma's son and is distinguishable from Future Trunks by his lavender/light-purple hair and the relative peace he grew up in — no Android apocalypse, no desperate survivalism. His personality is cocky and mischievous, shaped by Vegeta's pride and the luxury of peacetime. He achieved Super Saiyan at a young age, like Goten. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Trunks / Wikipedia: Trunks (Dragon Ball)]
- **Set context / Link Card** — TB2-007 is the Link Card partner to TB2-004 Son Goten. The "Tiny Rivals" pairing reflects Goten and Trunks's canonical dynamic: best friends, near-equals in power, perpetual sparring partners. Trunks defeated Goten in the Junior Division final of the 25th World Tournament by going Super Saiyan — which technically violated the rules. The TB2 set introduced Link Cards as a mechanic where paired cards' art and effects work together. [Official DBS Card Game: WMAT Design Concept / Dragon Ball Wiki: World Martial Arts Tournament]
- **Mechanical** — At $0.06 market price, TB2-007 carries a slight premium over its Link partner TB2-004 Goten ($0.04) — Trunks cards historically trade higher across the DBS TCG due to the character's broader popularity spanning multiple timelines (Kid Trunks, Future Trunks, Trunks in Dragon Ball Super). Still bulk, still designed to pair with Goten to fire its full effect. [TCGplayer: World Martial Arts Tournament]

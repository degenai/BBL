## Trivia

- **Set context** — At the 25th World Martial Arts Tournament, Piccolo qualified for the adult bracket but forfeited his match against Supreme Kai (Shin) without fighting — he could sense Shin's divine power was beyond him, and Shin needed him to pursue Babidi's forces instead. Piccolo then journeyed with Shin and was later turned to stone by Dabura inside the spaceship. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: World Tournament Saga; Wikipedia: Piccolo (Dragon Ball)]`
- **Character arc** — Piccolo began the Dragon Ball franchise as Goku's arch-enemy and then became Gohan's reluctant kidnapper-turned-mentor. By the Buu Saga that relationship had deepened into something the series never cleanly named. The 'Begrudging Respect' title does it in two words. After the tournament, Piccolo trained Goten and Trunks to master the Fusion technique, and was eventually absorbed by Majin Buu alongside Gotenks — temporarily giving Buu tactical intelligence. `[Wikipedia: Piccolo (Dragon Ball)]`
- **Design** — Piccolo is TB02's only Uncommon in this run (TB2-027), a step above the Common-tier henchmen and location cards in the same set — accurately reflecting his narrative standing as an allied anti-hero rather than a villain or fodder fighter. `[dbs-cardgame.com: DBS-TB02 product page (WebSearch)]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — DBS-TB02 "World Martial Arts Tournament" is a Bandai Themed Booster released in late 2018 (US: approximately September–October 2018), covering fighters across Dragon Ball, Dragon Ball Z, and Dragon Ball Super tournament eras in a single set. The set is distinct from the main numbered booster series — TB02 is a second tournament-themed booster, designed for limited sealed-play events as well as single-card collecting. `[dbs-cardgame.com official product page, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **IP context** — Chi-Chi is the daughter of the Ox-King and a competitor in the 23rd World Martial Arts Tournament (Tenka'ichi Budōkai) in the original Dragon Ball manga/anime, where she fought Goku while concealing her identity under a battle helmet. She is characterized as the strongest human woman in the Dragon Ball universe at the time of that tournament. The blue armor-helmet outfit depicted on this card directly references that canonical appearance. `[Wikipedia: Chi-Chi (Dragon Ball), via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Design** — TB2-038 is a Green Battle card: Power 12000, Energy Cost 2[GG], type Earthling/World Tournament. Its [Super Combo] skill triggers an Auto ability when used as a combo card — if your Leader Card is "World Tournament" and you have 5 or fewer life, draw 1 card and gain +10000 combo power for the turn. This frames Chi-Chi mechanically as a desperation counter-tool in World Tournament Leader builds rather than a front-line threat. Deck limit: 4 copies across all [Super Combo] cards. `[dbs-cardgame.com card list, via WebSearch snippet]`

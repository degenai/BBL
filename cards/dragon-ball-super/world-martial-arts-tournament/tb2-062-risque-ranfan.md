## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Ranfan was the only female fighter to reach the quarter-finals of the 21st World Martial Arts Tournament. Her fighting style weaponizes her appearance: she feigns vulnerability to make opponents hesitate, delivers crushing blows in the opening, and if that fails, removes her outer garments to shock or embarrass opponents into dropping their guard. She lost to Nam, who closed his eyes to resist the distraction. `[WebSearch: Dragon Ball Wiki Fandom search result snippet; TV Tropes Dragon Ball Tournament Characters]`
- **Design** — TB2-062 is a yellow-color Common Battle card (1 yellow energy, 5,000 power, 5,000 combo). Auto-ability on play: choose up to 1 opponent Battle Card with energy cost 3 or less and switch it to Rest Mode; this card gains +5,000 power for the turn. The Rest Mode effect mechanically mirrors Ranfan's tactic — neutralizing a specific opponent by exploiting a situational vulnerability. `[WebSearch: DBZ Exchange TB2-062; Big Orbit Cards TB2-062]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — At 1 energy with an on-play Rest Mode and +5,000 power buff, Ranfan is a tempo play: she arrives, locks an opposing Common or Uncommon Battle Card, and pushes at 10,000 effective power that turn. The cap at energy-cost-3-or-less means she shuts down lower-cost threats but cannot touch high-cost finishers — consistent with her lore position as a mid-bracket fighter who beats ordinary opponents but falls to genuinely extraordinary ones. `[WebSearch: DBZ Exchange TB2-062; Final Round Games TB2-062]`

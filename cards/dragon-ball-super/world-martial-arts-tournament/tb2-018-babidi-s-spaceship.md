## Trivia

- **Set context** — Babidi's Spaceship functions as the central dungeon of the Babidi Saga. Its interior has 6 levels: the top floors house Babidi's combat guardians in descending strength (Pui Pui, Yakon, Dabura), floor 5 is Babidi's control room, and floor 6 contains the Sealed Ball holding Majin Buu. The ship was buried underground near the 25th World Martial Arts Tournament site — its proximity was deliberate, allowing Spopovich and Yamu to quickly deliver the drained energy from Gohan. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Babidi's Spaceship]`
- **Design** — This is one of the few pure location/stadium cards in TB02 — no figures, only environment. The domed cream-beige interior with ribbed walls is consistent with Babidi's alien-organic aesthetic throughout the anime. The official Dragon Ball site featured this location in its World Travelogue investigation series (File no. 016). `[en.dragon-ball-official.com/news/01_781.html]`
- **IP note** — The vision pass flagged `suspected_ip: Babidi` (med confidence), but this is a location card rather than a character card. The IP belongs to the location itself — Babidi's Spaceship from Dragon Ball Z — not a depicted figure. Confidence bumped to high given the unambiguous card name. `[Card name; Dragon Ball Wiki: Babidi's Spaceship]`

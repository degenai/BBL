## Trivia

- **Tournament position** — Yamcha competed in the 21st, 22nd, and 23rd World Martial Arts Tournaments, losing in the quarter-finals of all three. His 22nd tournament loss to Tien Shinhan is one of the more brutal exits in the original series: Tien not only won but broke Yamcha's leg in a deliberate post-match attack — a moment Kanzenshuu forum discussion specifically cites as a low point in their on-again rivalry. [CBR, "Dragon Ball: World Martial Arts Tournaments Records of the Main Characters"; Kanzenshuu, "Tenshinhan and Yamcha relationship" thread via WebSearch]
- **Character background** — Yamcha is a former desert bandit who trained under Master Roshi at the Turtle School alongside Goku and Krillin. His signature technique, the Roga Fufu Ken ("Wolf Fang Windy Fist"), is a rapid combination of punches and kicks he developed independently before joining the Turtle School — making it one of the few self-originated techniques in the original Dragon Ball Z cast. [Wikipedia: Yamcha; CBR article via WebSearch]
- **Mechanical design** — TB2-054 is a Yellow Rare Battle Card (Power 10000, cost 2Y) with [Barrier]. Its [Permanent] effect: if Unending Moves Tien Shinhan (TB2-055) is in your Battle Area, this card gains +10000 power and [Critical]. The pair-dependency mechanic encodes the Yamcha/Tien rivalry in card-game terms — Yamcha reaches his power ceiling only when Tien is present, inverting the canon dynamic where Tien consistently outpaced him. [DBS card database via WebSearch]
- **Pair design** — TB2-054 and TB2-055 are explicitly designed as a paired combo: Tien's [Activate:Main] can sacrifice Yamcha to draw 2 cards and KO an opponent's resting Battle Card. The Turtle School vs. Crane School rivals become mechanically codependent in the World Tournament themed deck. [DBS card database via WebSearch]

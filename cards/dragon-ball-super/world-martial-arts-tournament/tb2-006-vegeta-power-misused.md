## Trivia

- **Narrative anchor** — "Power Misused" references one of Dragon Ball Z's most pivotal moral failures: during the 25th World Martial Arts Tournament, Majin Vegeta — empowered by Babidi's magic — deliberately fired a Big Bang Attack into the spectator stands, killing dozens of bystanders, to force Goku to fight him. He allowed Babidi to implant the Majin seal specifically for the power boost, knowing full well the cost. The card's title is a direct quote of the event's moral verdict. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Vegeta's Pride / Wikipedia: Vegeta]
- **Set context** — World Martial Arts Tournament (TB2, September 21, 2018) assigns each color to a different tournament era. Red cards in the set are associated with the Buu-era 25th Tournament, where Vegeta's Majin form and the Babidi interruption define the arc. Vegeta is the set's most prominent Red character. [Official DBS Card Game: WMAT Design Concept / TCGplayer: TB2-006]
- **Mechanical** — TB2-006 is a Common Battle card at $0.04 market price. The Majin Vegeta form has multiple representations across DBS TCG sets; this Common version competes against rarer Vegeta prints with stronger effects. The purple energy palette in the art is unusual for Vegeta's Super Saiyan form — it signals the darker Majin-corrupted power rather than his standard golden aura. [TCGplayer: World Martial Arts Tournament]

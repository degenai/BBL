## Trivia

- **Set / character context** — Wild Tiger (Japanese: Mo Kekko, lit. 'Ferocious Bloody Tiger') is a minor but canon Dragon Ball Z tournament fighter who appeared in both the 27th World Martial Arts Tournament (reaching the semi-finals before losing to Majin Buu) and the 28th (where he was quickly defeated by Pan). He is one of several background tournament characters the TB2 Themed Booster elevated to card status alongside major fighters. [WebSearch: Dragon Ball Wiki Fandom - Wild Tiger]
- **Set context** — The World Martial Arts Tournament Themed Booster (DBS-TB02) is a 125-card set by Bandai drawing exclusively from the 28th World Martial Arts Tournament arc of Dragon Ball Z. Packs contain 12 cards, with 24 packs per box; the set introduced Link Cards that pair together to unlock additional effects.

## Trivia

- **Design** — TB2-053 is a yellow-color Common Battle card with 15,000 power (2 yellow energy, 5,000 combo). Skill: if Toughened Up Chiaotzu (TB2-056) is in the Battle Area, Krillin gains +5,000 power and Critical (opponent places life cards in Drop Area). The paired mechanic mirrors their friendship in canon — Chiaotzu's psychic support enabled Krillin's tournament run at the 22nd Tenkaichi Budokai. `[WebSearch: Big Orbit Cards TB2-053; ARKEN DBS TCG listing]`
- **Set / lore context** — Krillin is one of the few non-Saiyan fighters to consistently reach the semi-finals of multiple World Martial Arts Tournaments. He entered the 21st, 22nd, and 23rd Tenkaichi Budokai, reaching semi-finals twice. His quarter-final loss to Piccolo Jr. at the 23rd is considered one of his best fights in the franchise — he was among the first characters after Tien and Chiaotzu to learn flight during that tournament. `[WebSearch: CBR 'Ranking the Best Krillin Fights'; CBR 'Every Martial Artist Who Fought in Dragon Ball's World Tournaments']`
- **Mechanical reputation** — At 15,000 power for 2 energy, Krillin is competitive for a Common, but the Critical keyword only activates with Chiaotzu on the field — a deliberate design constraint that punishes running Krillin without his partner. The dependency is mechanical storytelling: Krillin's ceiling requires the support of someone he trusts. `[WebSearch: Big Orbit Cards TB2-053; Carta Magica TB2-056]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Union Force (DBS-B02) was released November 2017 by Bandai. The set includes cards from the non-canon Dragon Ball Z films alongside the main Cell Saga narrative, making it one of the few DBS TCG products to officially represent film-exclusive characters like Cooler's Armored Squadron. `[WebSearch: Dragon Ball Super Union Force BT2 release 2017]`
- **Character context** — Salza is the captain of Cooler's Armored Squadron — the three-person elite strike team (Salza, Dore, Neiz) serving Cooler in Dragon Ball Z: Cooler's Revenge (1991 film). Salza is described as the strongest of the three. The Squadron's mission was to hunt the Saiyan who defeated Frieza on Earth. `[WebSearch: Salza Dragon Ball Wiki Cooler's Armored Squadron Cooler's Revenge]`
- **Technique naming** — Salza's canonical signature attack is the Salza Blade — a projected energy blade, called Salza Blade Rush in the Budokai Tenkaichi and Raging Blast game series. "Death Razor" uses a razor/slash naming convention consistent with blade-type energy attacks. The DBS TCG frequently creates technique-card names not verbatim from the anime when adapting film-exclusive characters. `[WebSearch: Salza Blade technique Dragon Ball Wiki; DBZ Exchange: BT2-121]`
- **Mechanical effect** — As a Common-rarity Extra card, BT2-121's effect is "Activate Main: Choose 1 of your opponent's Battle Cards in Rest Mode and KO it" for 2 Yellow energy — a direct KO of a resting card, which thematically mirrors the Armored Squadron's ambush-and-eliminate mission profile on Earth. `[DBZ Exchange: BT2-121 Death Razor]`

## Trivia

- **IP verified: Android 18** — The card name explicitly identifies the figure. Android 18's birth name is Lazuli; twin brother Android 17 was born Lapis. Both were human juvenile delinquents forcibly converted into cyborgs by Dr. Gero — the lapis lazuli gemstone split across both names is a canonical Akira Toriyama naming convention `[WebSearch: Android 17 Android 18 Dragon Ball Z Dr Gero twins Lapis Lazuli Cell absorption]`.
- **Stewardship signal** — After the Cell Saga, Android 18 married Krillin. Toriyama described the relationship: "No. 18 found Krillin's seriousness and straightforwardness refreshing, and somehow or other she ended up falling for him." They had a daughter, Marron, and remained a stable couple through Dragon Ball Super `[WebSearch: Android 18 marries Krillin Dragon Ball Z; Screen Rant citing Toriyama]`. This makes Android 18 a stewardship-relevant figure for a potential future Krillin character node.
- **Designer-coordinated twin dyad** — "Twin Sister Android 18" (BT2-091) and "Twin Brother Android 17" (BT2-089) occupy consecutive card numbers in Union Force (BT2, November 2017) with mirrored titles — the canonical sibling pair explicitly encoded as a dyad at the card-number level `[DBS collector_numbers BT2-089 / BT2-091]`.

## Connections

- [[bt2-089-twin-brother-android-17]] — Canonical Toriyama-designed twin dyad printed as a designer-coordinated within-set pair. Both cards share Union Force (BT2, November 2017) at near-adjacent collector numbers (BT2-089 + BT2-091) with deliberately mirrored title templates (`Twin Brother Android 17` / `Twin Sister Android 18`), identical mid-shot composed-stance void-arena framings, and matching `android` + `twin` + `sibling` + `machine` + `creation` tag clusters. The twins are canonically Lapis (Android 17) and Lazuli (Android 18) — human juvenile delinquents Dr. Gero forcibly converted into cybernetic enhanced humans, the lapis lazuli gemstone split across their birth names a Toriyama naming convention. Both were absorbed by Cell (17 to achieve Semi-Perfect form, 18 to achieve Perfect form); 17 died inside Cell during the self-destruct, 18 was expelled alive pre-detonation. Bandai's designer-coordinated dyad printing on adjacent BT2 slots is the canonical TCG encoding of the sibling pair.

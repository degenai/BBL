## Trivia

- **Set context** — BT2-047 is a Common Blue Battle card from Series 2 Booster UNION FORCE (DBS-B02, 2017). Whis appears in the blue color identity in the DBS card game, consistent with his serene, intelligence-focused characterization versus the red/combat-energy palette of Saiyan cards in the same set. `[dbs-cardgame.com: Union Force card list]` `[dbzexchange.com: BT2-047]`
- **Mechanical** — The card's Blocker ability intercepts attacks targeting any of the player's other cards, switching Whis to Rest Mode to absorb the hit. Crucially, Whis cannot be KO'd by opponent card skills or removed from the Battle Area by skills — a mechanical representation of Angel immortality, as Angels in Dragon Ball Super exist beyond the mortal cycle of life and death and cannot be killed by conventional means. `[dbzexchange.com: BT2-047]` `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Whis]`
- **Set / lore context** — Whis is the Guide Angel Attendant of Beerus, God of Destruction of Universe 7, and the younger brother of Vados. Both are children of the Grand Minister. Whis is superior to Beerus in power and exists in a permanent state of Ultra Instinct. He was trained by his older sister Vados roughly a millennium before Dragon Ball Super's timeline. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Whis]` `[Kanzenshuu forum: Is the Grand Priest the father of all 12 angels?]`
- **Design** — Whis and Vados appear in the same UNION FORCE set as Common and Rare support cards respectively, framing the angel-attendant siblings as accessible stabilizing pieces rather than headline threats. This mirrors their narrative role in the anime: present, unflappable, impossible to remove from the board. `[dbs-cardgame.com: Union Force card list]`

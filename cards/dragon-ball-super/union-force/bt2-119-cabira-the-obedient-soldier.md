## Trivia

- **Set context** — Union Force (BT2) was the second main booster set for the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, released November 3, 2017. It drew characters from across the Dragon Ball universe rather than a single arc — making minor antagonists like Cabira eligible alongside major fighters. [PSA Card Facts / BleedingCool: Union Force Checklist]
- **Character lore** — Cabira is one of two named soldiers (alongside Toobi) dispatched by Chilled — Frieza's ancestor and a space pirate — to conquer Planet Plant in Dragon Ball: Episode of Bardock (2011). Bardock, transported back to the planet's past, kills Cabira: a roundhouse kick to the face in the manga, a punch to the stomach in the anime. The card title "The Obedient Soldier" mirrors Cabira's canonical role as a disposable henchman who executes orders without question and dies for it. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Cabira / thecodex.wiki: Chilled]
- **Mechanical** — BT2-119 is a Yellow Battle card with 10,000 Power and 5,000 Combo Power — baseline common stat line for the Union Force era. At $0.10 market price it is bulk filler with no special effects noted in retailer listings. [TCGplayer: BT2-119 Cabira, The Obedient Soldier]

### Related cards
- Toobi, The Obedient Soldier (Union Force) — Cabira's named companion; both are sent by Chilled to Planet Plant and killed by Bardock
- Chilled, Cold-Blooded Conqueror (various DBS sets) — Cabira's commander and Frieza's ancestor; the Chilled Saga villain
- Bardock, Father of Goku (various DBS sets) — the fighter who kills Cabira; his rage at what follows triggers the first Super Saiyan legend

## Trivia

- **Fusion method — Metamoran Fusion Dance** — Gotenks is the Fusion Dance product of Goten and Trunks, trained by Goku and later drilled by Piccolo inside the Hyperbolic Time Chamber. The Metamoran vest (black-and-white sleeveless, visible in Vision) is the signature garment of this fusion method — distinct from Vegito's Potara gi. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Gotenks; Dragon Ball Wiki: Fusion Dance, via WebSearch]
- **Super Saiyan 3 — child prodigy** — Gotenks achieved Super Saiyan 3 while training in the Hyperbolic Time Chamber — the only character besides Goku to master it during original Dragon Ball Z. The energy drain of Super Saiyan 3 significantly compresses the Fusion Dance's 30-minute window, leaving Gotenks with very limited time to secure victories. [Game Rant: "Dragon Ball: All Forms of Gotenks, Explained", via WebSearch]
- **Absorbed by Super Buu** — Gotenks was eventually absorbed by Super Buu alongside Piccolo, his power used against the Z Warriors until Vegito's intervention. His personality — a mixture of Trunks's arrogance and Goten's cheerfulness — produces extreme cockiness consistent with the "Prodigy" framing in the card title. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Gotenks, via WebSearch]

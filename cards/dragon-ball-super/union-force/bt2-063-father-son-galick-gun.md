## Trivia

- **Set context** — Union Force (BT2) was the second main DBS Card Game English booster, released November 3, 2017 — approximately 97 days after Galactic Battle. The 127-card set expanded the Extra card pool with board-disruption tools, and BT2-063 exemplifies this design: a Blue Extra that returns an opponent's Battle Card to hand at Activate: Main speed, usable at any point during the main phase. `[PSA CardFacts: 2017 Dragon Ball Super Series 2 Union Force; PSA Set Registry: OG Cards of the Franchise article]`
- **Mechanical** — Blue Extra card, cost 2 Blue. Skill: "[Activate: Main] Choose up to 1 of your opponent's Battle Cards and return it to their hand. (Cards stacked under the card are placed in the Drop Area.)" The stack-clause is load-bearing: bouncing an EX-Evolved unit forces the base cards in the stack to the Drop Area rather than returning to hand, punishing opponents who invested multiple cards into a single evolved unit. `[TrollAndToad: Father-Son Galick Gun BT2-063; DBZ Exchange: BT2-063]`
- **Design** — The Father-Son Galick Gun debuted in DBS episode 65 ("Final Judgement? The Ultimate Power of an Absolute God") when Future Trunks fires his Super Galick Gun against Fused Zamasu's Holy Wrath and Vegeta joins with his own Galick Gun, the two beams merging into a single electricity-wreathed blast that overpowers the divine attack. The duo portrait's aura split — warm gold (Trunks, Super Saiyan) on the left, cool blue-white (Vegeta, Super Saiyan Blue) on the right — accurately captures the different transformation levels each character holds during that scene. `[Dragon Ball Wiki Fandom: Father-Son Galick Gun; ComicBook.com: Dragon Ball Super Dub Vegeta and Trunks Father-Son Attack]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — BT2-013 is a Rare Red Battle card from Series 2 Booster UNION FORCE (DBS-B02, 2017). The set's name directly references the Union-Potara mechanic introduced in this series, which mechanically represents the Potara earring fusion between Son Goku and Vegeta. Vegito is the flagship character of the set's named deck archetype. `[dbs-cardgame.com: Union Force card list]` `[dbs-cardgame.com: Designer's Note, Union Force]`
- **Mechanical** — The Union-Potara mechanic requires placing BT2-013 in Active Mode on top of specific Son Goku and Vegeta Battle Cards already in play. The skill then mills 10 cards to the Drop Area and allows the player to evolve into a 25000-power Vegito from the Drop Area — a design where accumulated Goku/Vegeta cards translate into growing power, mirroring the lore logic of the fusion combining both fighters' full strength. With enough Saiyan cards in the Drop Area, attack power can exceed 100,000. `[dbs-cardgame.com: Strategy — SSB Vegito deck]`
- **Set / lore context** — Vegito is the Potara earring fusion of Goku and Vegeta; the name combines "Vegeta" and "Kakarot" (Goku's Saiyan birth name). First appeared fighting Super Buu in Dragon Ball Z. Reappeared in Dragon Ball Super against Fused Zamasu, defusing prematurely due to overexposure to Buu's magic residue inside Zamasu's corrupted body — one of the few canonical mechanisms that dissolves Potara fusion in mortals. `[Dragon Ball Wiki: Vegito]` `[Kanzenshuu: Potara Fusion mechanics thread]`
- **Design** — BT2-013 is one of two Vegito Rares in UNION FORCE alongside BT2-012 (Repeated Force Vegito), together forming the core of the named SSB Vegito deck archetype. Unlike Gotenks (Fusion Dance — temporary, dance-ritual), Vegito represents the Potara method: originally presented as permanent until the Buu saga retcon established a one-hour time limit for mortals. `[dbs-cardgame.com: Strategy — SSB Vegito deck]`

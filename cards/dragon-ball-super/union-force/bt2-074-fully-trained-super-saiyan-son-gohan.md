## Trivia

- **Set context** — Union Force (DBS-B02) is the second main booster set of the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, released November 2017 by Bandai `[WebSearch: Dragon Ball Super Union Force BT2 2017]`. The set draws heavily from Dragon Ball Z's Cell Saga as its primary character source period.
- **IP verified: Son Gohan** — The card name explicitly identifies the depicted figure. During the Cell Saga, Goku and Gohan trained in the Hyperbolic Time Chamber — one year inside equals one day outside — aiming for Super Saiyan Full Power, a mastered transformation state where the form becomes as natural as the base form, eliminating physical strain from staying transformed `[WebSearch: Dragon Ball Z Gohan Super Saiyan Full Power Cell Saga Hyperbolic Time Chamber]`. The title "Fully Trained" maps directly to this milestone.
- **Lore note** — Gohan's Super Saiyan 2 transformation is distinct from the "Fully Trained" Super Saiyan Full Power state on this card. SSJ Full Power was achieved inside the Hyperbolic Time Chamber; SSJ2 first triggered during the Cell Games outside the chamber, upon Android 16's death `[WebSearch: Dragon Ball Z Gohan hyperbolic time chamber SSJ2]`.

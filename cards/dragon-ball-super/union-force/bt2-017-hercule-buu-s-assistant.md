## Trivia

- **Three-saga credit arc** — Hercule (Mr. Satan in the original Japanese) is the reigning World Martial Arts Champion who took public credit for defeating Cell at the Cell Games — a victory actually won by Gohan. He subsequently allowed himself to be credited for defeating Majin Buu (Goku's kill), with the Z Warriors' explicit consent, because they had no interest in public recognition. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Mr. Satan; Wikipedia: Mr. Satan, via WebSearch]
- **Cell Games specifics** — At the Cell Games, Hercule trash-talked the Z Fighters, dismissed their feats as "tricks," and claimed Cell's defeat publicly. Since then he has been worshipped by nearly the entire population of Earth. [CBR: "Dragon Ball Z: Hercule Was (Unintentionally) a Hero During the Cell Games", via WebSearch]
- **Buu Saga pivot** — Hercule's friendship with Fat Buu was the Buu Saga's central moral pivot: Buu's willingness to suppress destruction was tied directly to his affection for Hercule. When Hercule was shot by an assailant, Buu's resulting rage accelerated the saga's escalation toward Super Buu. Hercule's labor here was genuine — but structured entirely through his manufactured champion persona. [CBR: "Dragon Ball: The World Protector's Mr. Satan, Explained", via WebSearch]
- **Universe Survival Arc** — In Dragon Ball Super, Hercule served primarily as emotional anchor for Majin Buu's participation in the Tournament of Power exhibition matches. Buu's ring-clearing rage was directly triggered by Hercule being hurt. Hercule was not selected for Universe 7's tournament team itself. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Universe Survival Saga, via WebSearch]

## Trivia

- **Ancient origin and Babidi's resurrection** — Majin Buu is not Bibidi's creation per Toriyama's clarification: Buu is an ancient being who has existed "since time immemorial" in cycles of destruction and hibernation. Bibidi awakened him and sealed him; Bibidi's son Babidi completed the resurrection during the Dragon Ball Z Buu Saga using energy stolen at the 25th World Martial Arts Tournament. The crack that opened Buu's seal was the massive energy released during Vegeta's fight with Goku. [Wikipedia: Majin Buu; Dragon Ball Wiki: Babidi, via WebSearch; Toriyama interview via WebSearch]
- **Fat Buu — first form depicted** — The form shown here (pink, rotund, antenna, white vest with purple trim) is Fat Buu / Innocent Buu — the first and most childlike form after resurrection. This form later expelled its evil half, producing Evil Buu, then Super Buu, then Kid Buu through successive transformations. [Wikipedia: Majin Buu, via WebSearch]
- **Hercule-Buu friendship as moral pivot** — Hercule's (Mr. Satan's) friendship with Fat Buu was the Buu Saga's central moral hinge: Buu's willingness to suppress destruction was directly tied to his affection for Hercule. When Hercule was shot by an assailant, Buu's resulting rage accelerated the saga's escalation toward Super Buu. The card title "Buu's Assistant" inverts the real dynamic — Hercule served as Buu's emotional anchor, not the other way around. [CBR: "Dragon Ball: The World Protector's Mr. Satan, Explained", via WebSearch]
- **Dragon Ball Daima retcon** — Dragon Ball Daima (2024) introduced a further revision: per that series, Majin Buu was actually created by a Demon Realm witch named Marba at Bibidi's behest — adding a layer on top of Toriyama's earlier "Buu always existed" clarification. The original DBZ explanation remains the canonical baseline for this card's era. [Screen Rant: "Dragon Ball Daima Just Revealed Majin Buu's Origins", via WebSearch]

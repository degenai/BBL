## Trivia

- **Set context** — Union Force (DBS-B02), released February-March 2018, is the home set for this Uncommon. At the Uncommon rarity slot BT2-038 occupies the mass-availability tier — the cards every pack buyer pulls multiples of and that define the practical card-pool for new players. Bandai's BT2 design philosophy placed aggressive base-form Goku prints at lower rarities to enable entry-level Saiyan-blue deck construction without requiring Super Rare or Secret Rare pulls. `[WebSearch: DBS B02 Union Force Checklist; dbs-cardgame.com]`
- **Mechanical design** — BT2-038 is an Uncommon Blue Battle card (15,000 Power, cost 3). The forward-lunging punch composition maps to the low-cost aggressive pressure role base-form Goku Uncommons fill in early DBS sets — cheap threats that chain into higher-cost Goku Evolve targets and force the opponent into a defensive early-game. `[WebSearch: BT2-038 card details]`
- **Color-coding convention** — Bandai established across BT1 and BT2 a deliberate color split for Goku: base-form Goku in blue (representing Universe 7 / Saiyan-blue archetype) while Super Saiyan Goku prints appear in red and green depending on the arc era. BT2-038 is the blue-archetype base anchor that supports SSB-form payoffs like BT2-037 in the same set. `[dbs-cardgame.com; DBSCG strategy page: Union Force]`
- **Expansion Deck context** — The "Mighty Heroes" Expansion Deck released alongside BT2 on March 23, 2018 featured Goku as the deck's primary identity with 14 limited cards designed to synergize with the BT2 booster's Saiyan-ensemble blue shell — Union Force's first experiment with supplementary product design. `[WebSearch: Union Force Expansion Decks 2018]`

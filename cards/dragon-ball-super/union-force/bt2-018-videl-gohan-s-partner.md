## Trivia

- **Family position** — Videl is the daughter of World Champion Hercule (Mr. Satan) and the wife of Son Gohan; their daughter Pan is a future mainstay of the franchise. Videl is the connective tissue between the Satan/Hercule lineage and the Son/Goku lineage — a mortal woman holding together two of Dragon Ball's most structurally important family lines. [Wikipedia: Videl; Dragon Ball Wiki: Videl, via WebSearch]
- **Proactive power-seeker** — Videl deduced Gohan's identity as the Great Saiyaman through investigation, then blackmailed him into teaching her ki manipulation. She trained hard enough to enter the 25th World Martial Arts Tournament herself, making her one of the few mortals in the series to actively pursue combat power rather than receive it. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Videl, via WebSearch]
- **Hair as canon dating signal** — The Vision section identifies Videl's short hair, consistent with her Dragon Ball Super adult design. In Dragon Ball Z she wore signature pigtails; she cut them shorter during the Buu Saga for flight training after Gohan's suggestion. The haircut is a small canon beat fans use to date card art to DBS era vs Z era. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Videl, via WebSearch]
- **Buu Saga emotional beat** — When told by Goku that Gohan had been killed by Buu, Videl tearfully admitted she had fallen in love with him but refused to believe he was dead. Four years after Buu's defeat they married; Pan was born a year after that. [CBR: "Everything You Need To Know About Gohan and Videl's Relationship", via WebSearch]

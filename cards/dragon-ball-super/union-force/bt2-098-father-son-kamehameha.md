## Trivia

- **Set context** — Union Force (DBS-B02) was released November 2017 by Bandai as the second main booster of the Dragon Ball Super Card Game. Extra cards (technique cards) like the Father-Son Kamehameha are a mechanical category distinct from Battle Cards, allowing iconic moves to be played independently of the character card that executes them. `[WebSearch: Dragon Ball Super Union Force BT2 release 2017]`
- **Canonical finishing blow** — The Father-Son Kamehameha is the finishing move of the Cell Games arc (Dragon Ball Z Season 6, Episode 26, "Save the World"). Gohan — SSJ2, fighting with one arm injured after shielding Vegeta — fires the Kamehameha while Goku's spirit manifests from the Other World behind him to amplify the beam and overpower Cell's counterblast. `[WebSearch: Father-Son Kamehameha Dragon Ball Wiki Cell Games]`
- **Labor-credit structure** — The card title credits both "Father" and "Son" equally. Gohan executed the technique physically; Goku contributed a spiritual amplification from the afterlife. Vegeta's unsolicited sideline energy blast — which distracted Cell at the critical moment — is credited to neither, though it was the tactical pivot that allowed Gohan to land the killing blow. Three participants; two named. `[WebSearch: Father-Son Kamehameha Vegeta distraction Cell Games episode Save the World]`

## Trivia

- **IP verified: Android 17** — The card name explicitly identifies the figure. Android 17's birth name is Lapis; his twin sister Android 18 (born Lazuli) share a lapis lazuli gemstone naming pun. The twins were human juvenile delinquents encountered by Dr. Gero, who forcibly converted them into cybernetically enhanced humans to serve as weapons against Goku `[WebSearch: Android 17 Android 18 Dragon Ball Z Dr Gero twins Lapis Lazuli Cell absorption]`.
- **Set context / Cell Saga** — During the Cell Saga, Android 17 was absorbed by Cell to achieve Semi-Perfect form. Unlike Android 18 (who was later expelled alive), Android 17 was still inside Cell when Cell self-destructed — dying in the explosion before being revived via the Dragon Balls after Cell's final defeat `[WebSearch: Android 17 Android 18 Cell absorption Dragon Ball Z]`.
- **Designer-coordinated twin dyad** — Union Force (BT2, November 2017) issues "Twin Brother Android 17" (BT2-089) and "Twin Sister Android 18" (BT2-091) in consecutive card slots with mirrored titles — explicitly encoding the canonical sibling pair as a designer-coordinated dyad at the card-number level `[DBS collector_numbers BT2-089 / BT2-091; WebSearch: Union Force BT2 Bandai 2017]`.

## Connections

- [[bt2-091-twin-sister-android-18]] — Canonical Toriyama-designed twin dyad printed as a designer-coordinated within-set pair. Both cards share Union Force (BT2, November 2017) at near-adjacent collector numbers (BT2-089 + BT2-091) with deliberately mirrored title templates (`Twin Brother Android 17` / `Twin Sister Android 18`), identical mid-shot composed-stance void-arena framings, and matching `android` + `twin` + `sibling` + `machine` + `creation` tag clusters. The twins are canonically Lapis (Android 17) and Lazuli (Android 18) — human juvenile delinquents Dr. Gero forcibly converted into cybernetic enhanced humans, the lapis lazuli gemstone split across their birth names a Toriyama naming convention. Both were absorbed by Cell (17 to achieve Semi-Perfect form, 18 to achieve Perfect form); 17 died inside Cell during the self-destruct, 18 was expelled alive pre-detonation. Bandai's designer-coordinated dyad printing on adjacent BT2 slots is the canonical TCG encoding of the sibling pair.

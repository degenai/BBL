## Trivia

- **Set context** — Union Force (DBS-B02) was the second booster set for the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, released November 3, 2017. BT2-061 is a Common-rarity card in the Gowasu slot for the Universe 10 Supreme Kai character. [PSACardFacts: 2017 Dragon Ball Super Series 2 Union Force, via WebSearch]
- **Stewardship role** — Gowasu is the Supreme Kai (God of Creation) of Universe 10 and the canonical mentor of Zamasu. His stewardship is literal: he taught Zamasu the duties of a god, observing mortals through the Universe 6 vs. 7 exhibition matches — events that ignited Zamasu's contempt for mortal life after seeing Goku's fighting power. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Gowasu, via WebSearch]
- **Betrayed-stewardship beat** — Zamasu murdered Gowasu — his own master — to steal his Potara earring and a Time Ring, which permits time travel without erasure from the timeline. This killing is the canonical act of student-kills-mentor in the Future Trunks arc. In the anime, Whis rewound time to prevent the death; in the manga, Goku Black impales Gowasu when feigning reconciliation. The stolen Time Ring is what makes Zamasu's multi-timeline "Project Zero Mortals" plan possible. [Wikipedia: Zamasu; Dragon Ball Wiki: Protect The Supreme Kai Gowasu Destroy Zamasu!, via WebSearch]
- **Dark energy register** — The purple-black energy rising around Gowasu in this card's art depicts the Supreme Kai in an active divine-power state, contrasting with his usual calm philosophical demeanor in the anime. This visual register is consistent with how the DBS card game frames divine power across the BT2 set — elevated stakes, not passive observation. [Visual grounding in card art; set context from WebSearch]

## Trivia

- **Potara vs Fusion Dance — canonical disambiguation** — Vegito is the Potara Earring fusion of Goku and Vegeta; Gogeta is the Fusion Dance (Metamoran) product. These are two entirely distinct fused beings with different personalities, power profiles, and narrative contexts. This card depicts Vegito specifically, identifiable by the Potara gi (blue and orange layered garments visible in Vision). [Dragon Ball Wiki: Vegito; Dragon Ball Wiki: Fusion Dance, via WebSearch]
- **Formation circumstances** — Vegito first appeared in the Dragon Ball Z Buu Saga when Elder Kai provided Goku with Potara earrings because there was insufficient time to learn the Fusion Dance. Goku initially intended to fuse with Gohan, but Buu absorbed Gohan before Goku could pass the earring — forcing the Goku-Vegeta pairing instead. [CBR: "Vegito: Dragon Ball's Weirdest and Most Powerful Body Fusion, Explained", via WebSearch]
- **Power profile** — Per Daizenshuu 7, Potara fusion produces a stronger warrior than the Fusion Dance, amplified by the rival-boost between Goku and Vegeta — something Metamoran fusion does not have. Super Vegito dominated Super Buu (who had absorbed Piccolo, Gotenks, and Ultimate Gohan) while fighting with only his feet. [Daizenshuu 7, cited via WebSearch snippet; CBR, via WebSearch]
- **De-fusion anomaly** — Vegito de-fused inside Buu's body — an unusual outcome since Potara fusion is normally permanent for non-Supreme Kais. Dragon Ball Super later explained this as caused by the magical properties of Buu's interior environment. [CBR: "Dragon Ball Super Explains Why Vegito De-fused Inside Majin Buu", via WebSearch]

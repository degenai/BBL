## Trivia

- **Set context** — Union Force (BT2, product DBS-B02) released November 3, 2017 as the second main booster set for the Dragon Ball Super Card Game. The set spans multiple sagas — Android/Cell arc, Future Trunks arc, Majin Buu Saga, and Tournament of Power. BT2-010 falls in the Majin Buu Saga grouping, depicting Vegeta in his Super Saiyan 2 form as seen during the Babidi arc. Union Force was also the first DBS-CG set to introduce the Secret Rare rarity tier. `[WebSearch: Bleeding Cool DBS Union Force checklist; dbs-cardgame.com]`
- **Mechanical design** — Common rarity Red Battle card, 15,000 power, 3 energy cost. Skill: at end of battle after comboing from hand, if your Leader Card is red, play this card in Rest Mode — an aggressive extender that keeps Vegeta on board after a combo. The card's Saiyan special trait and Majin Buu Saga tag place it in early Red Saiyan aggro archetypes. `[WebSearch: TrollAndToad BT2-010 listing; WebSearch: CoreTCG BT2-010 listing]`
- **Lore context** — In Dragon Ball Z canon, Vegeta's true Super Saiyan 2 is distinct from his Cell arc "Super Vegeta" (SS Grade 2, which boosted strength at the expense of speed and was not genuine SS2). The actual SS2 form was achieved through years of post-Cell training and is explicitly associated with the Majin arc: Vegeta accepted Babidi's Majin mark partly to unlock that final threshold for his rematch with Goku. `[WebSearch: ComicBook.com — Dragon Ball doubles down on Vegeta's SS2 origin]`
- **Parallel printings** — BT2-010 also exists as Level 1 and Level 2 Judge Promotion Card variants, making the base common print notably more accessible than the judge-promo parallels. `[WebSearch: MnaCardz BT2-010 Judge Lvl 1; WebSearch: GatheringPointGames BT2-010 Judge Lvl 2]`

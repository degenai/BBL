## Trivia

- **Set context** — BT2-004 is from Union Force (DBS-B02), the second main booster of the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, released November 3, 2017. Union Force significantly expanded the deck archetype roster — the official designer's note states the set was built to support 20+ distinct deck types across five colors (versus roughly 4 in BT1). Red decks in BT2 were designed for less-experienced players built around Saiyan-family offense, with BT2-004 positioned near the top of the red Saiyan attack curve. [dbs-cardgame.com: Union Force Red strategy page; tcdb.com: 2017 Dragon Ball Super BT2 Union Force]
- **Mechanical profile** — Red Battle card, Rare (foil version also released), power 20,000 (25,000 on the turn played), energy cost 4. Skills: this card cannot be KO'd by opponent's skills; when played, gains +5,000 power until the beginning of your next turn. The skill-immunity + temporary power boost packages the card as a resilient midrange threat — it survives targeted removal (skill-based KO) while entering as a 25,000 power attacker, then settling at 20,000 in subsequent turns. [WebSearch snippet: DBZExchange BT2-004; GameAcademia Singapore listing]
- **Lore context — Super Saiyan 3** — Super Saiyan 3 (SS3) is the third canonical level of the Super Saiyan transformation. Goku first achieved it while training in Other World after dying against Cell; he reveals it in the manga against Majin Buu (chapter 472, volume 40) and in the anime at episode 245. The defining visual markers — extreme lengthening of the hair, loss of eyebrows, massively amplified golden aura — are the exact features the Vision section identifies on BT2-004's art. SS3 is canonically the most physically taxing Super Saiyan form, draining ki and life span rapidly even for a post-death Goku. [Kanzenshuu FAQ: Super Saiyan 2 name/translation history (cross-confirms SS transformation tier numbering); Wikipedia: Dragon Ball Super Saiyan 3]
- **Son Goku cluster link** — BT2-004 ties to the existing 23-card Son Goku character node (`_characters/son-goku.md`) as a canonical SS3-form print. SS3 Goku's arc context (Buu Saga, the Majin Buu fight) connects across TB2-051 Unyielding Victory Son Goku (childhood era, same World Martial Arts Tournament thematic set) and BT1 Goku prints (Battle of Gods / Saiyan Saga era), giving the Son Goku node prints across five distinct arc phases. [Vegeta character node; son-goku character node]

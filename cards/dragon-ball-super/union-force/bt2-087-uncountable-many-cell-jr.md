## Trivia

- **Set context** — Union Force (DBS-B02) is the second main booster set of the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, released November 2017 by Bandai. The set draws heavily from Dragon Ball Z's Cell Saga as its primary narrative period, making it the densest concentration of Cell-era characters in the early DBS TCG product line. `[WebSearch: Dragon Ball Super Union Force BT2 release 2017]`
- **Bio-mechanical cohort** — Cell Jr. are bio-androids spawned by Perfect Cell via cellular mitosis from his tail during the Cell Games. Seven were created, each matching Cell's own power level at the time. The card title "Uncountable Many" treats them as a horde-cohort rather than named individuals — the seven were functionally identical and deployed simultaneously to break the Z Fighters' morale. `[WebSearch: Dragon Ball Wiki Cell Jr. seven offspring cellular mitosis]`
- **Design note** — The plural title "Uncountable Many" is atypical for a DBS TCG Battle Card, which normally names a single character. The cohort-framing mirrors the in-series narrative role: Cell Jr. were not individuals with arcs but a collective tactical weapon, spawned and dismissed within a single episode of the Cell Games arc. `[DBZ Exchange: BT2-087]`
- **Apparatus note** — Cell's ability to produce seven identical combat-capable offspring via cellular division represents Dr. Gero's bio-mechanical replication logic taken to its extreme: not a weapon that fires once, but one that manufactures threats on demand. The Cell Jr. are the Cell-saga's clearest expression of the android-as-factory-line motif. `[WebSearch: Dragon Ball Wiki Bio-Android Cell Games]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Union Force (DBS-B02) was released November 3, 2017 as the Dragon Ball Super Card Game's second booster set. [dbs-cardgame.com product page, via WebSearch]
- **Cymbal-eating event** — Yajirobe's canonical introduction is his killing of Cymbal during the King Piccolo Saga. He sliced Cymbal in half with his sword, then roasted and ate him — remarking he was "excellent meat." This remains the franchise's most distinctive death-beat in the saga's middle act. The King Piccolo Saga anime aired in Japan starting 1988 (the manga serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shonen Jump from 1984). [Dragon Ball Wiki: Yajirobe; Dragon Ball Wiki: Cymbal; Dragon Ball Wiki: Here Comes Yajirobe, via WebSearch]
- **Korin Tower cohort** — Yajirobe is a permanent resident of Korin Tower, the training landmark beneath The Lookout. He is one of the few mortal humans with consistent access to Korin's Senzu Beans — the Z Warriors' primary healing resource — making him a logistical linchpin across multiple sagas despite never fighting in major battles. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Yajirobe, via WebSearch]
- **Reluctant-hero peak moment** — Yajirobe's single greatest combat contribution in the Z saga is cutting off Vegeta's Great Ape tail during the Saiyan Saga, performed from behind while Vegeta was occupied — entirely consistent with his samurai-archetype-survivor personality. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Yajirobe, via WebSearch]

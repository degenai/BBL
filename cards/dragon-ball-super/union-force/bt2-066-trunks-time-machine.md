## Trivia

- **Set context** — Union Force (DBS-B02) is the second main booster set of the Dragon Ball Super Card Game, released November 3, 2017. [dbs-cardgame.com product page, via WebSearch]
- **Engineering credit** — The Time Machine was built by Future Bulma in the alternate future timeline, constructed in a secret underground basement beneath Capsule Corporation after the Androids destroyed most of civilization. The machine is a Capsule Corp artifact, and Capsule Corp supplies all vehicle and equipment technology across both timelines. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Time Machine; Dragon Ball Wiki: Capsule Corporation, via WebSearch]
- **The HOPE!! inscription** — The inscription "HOPE!!" painted on the machine's side has a canon discrepancy: the manga (Volume 16, chapter 358, Viz translation) attributes the writing to Future Trunks himself ("I wrote this the day I left"); the anime dub credits Future Bulma. The discrepancy is documented at Kanzenshuu and unresolved in official materials. [Kanzenshuu forum: "Who actually wrote HOPE!! on the Time Machine", via WebSearch]
- **Apparatus-of-extraction note** — The Time Machine is the only reason Future Trunks can intervene in the present timeline at all. Future Bulma's engineering labor — performed under Android occupation, with minimal resources — is the foundational act that makes the entire Trunks arc possible. Bulma receives no public credit in-universe; Trunks does. [Dragon Ball Wiki: Future Bulma, via WebSearch]

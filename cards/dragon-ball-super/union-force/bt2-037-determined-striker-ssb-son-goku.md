## Trivia

- **Set context** — Union Force (DBS-B02) was the second main booster set, released February-March 2018, expanding to 123 cards and introducing the Secret Rare rarity tier — the first rarity tier above Super Rare in the DBS card game. The set thematically covers the Buu Saga and Future Trunks Saga, introducing Vegito and Zamasu as its signature high-rarity anchors. `[Bleeding Cool: Dragon Ball Super Card Game Union Force Checklist; WebSearch: DBS BT2 release date]`
- **Mechanical design** — BT2-037 is a Rare Blue Battle card (20,000 Power) with a Counter ability that triggers when the player has 3 or fewer life cards: discarding 2 Saiyan cards from hand reduces the energy cost by 3, enabling a near-free defensive play. The design punishes aggressive life-denial strategies by giving the defending player a late-game tempo reversal — running a Saiyan-heavy blue deck converts the resource cost into an emergency out. `[CoolStuffInc: Determined Striker SSB Son Goku]`
- **Lore context** — Bandai's character tag for BT2-037 is Son Goku, era: Future Trunks Saga — the Dragon Ball Super arc where SSB (Super Saiyan Blue / Super Saiyan God Super Saiyan) is in its mature, mastered state. The card's composed-calm composition directly contrasts the screaming-emergence of BT1-059 Awakening Rage Son Goku one set earlier; Bandai's art direction for SSB Goku encodes the distinction between the raw-emotional Super Saiyan threshold and the controlled-god-ki SSB mastery. `[dbs-cardgame.com; Dragon Ball Wiki: Super Saiyan Blue]`
- **Product note** — Union Force introduced Expansion Decks alongside the main booster: the "Mighty Heroes" Expansion Deck centered on the canonical Saiyan-hero ensemble (Goku, Vegeta, Gohan, Trunks) as the design counterweight to the "Dark Demon's Villains" deck — establishing a supplementary product format that recurred across the DBS card game's lifespan. `[WebSearch: Union Force Expansion Decks 2018]`

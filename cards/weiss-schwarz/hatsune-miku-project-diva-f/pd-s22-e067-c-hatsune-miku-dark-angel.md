## Trivia

- **Set context** — PD/S22 is the Weiss Schwarz booster set adapting Hatsune Miku: Project DIVA F (2013), published by Bushiroad under license from SEGA and Crypton Future Media. The English edition contains 100 card types (8 RR / 20 R / 28 U / 28 C / 16 CX) plus 44 parallel variants, with SP Gold Foil signed cards from voice actress Saki Fujita (voice of Hatsune Miku) randomly inserted per display. `[Weiss Schwarz official product page]`
- **Design — Dark Angel module origin** — The Dark Angel costume first appeared in Hatsune Miku: Project DIVA F, unlocked by clearing the song "Secret Police" (Himitsu Keisatsu) by producer Buriru-P on any difficulty. The module's design — jet black dress, white lace, drilled twintails, feather clips — is a direct homage to that song's aesthetic. The module also appeared in Project DIVA X, Project DIVA Future Tone, and inspired SEGA's Super Premium Figure line. `[Project DIVA Wiki: Dark Angel]`
- **IP context** — Hatsune Miku is a Vocaloid character developed by Crypton Future Media, released August 2007 as a singing synthesizer software. Unlike most TCG characters, she has no single canonical lore narrative — her identity is shaped by thousands of community-produced songs and SEGA's Project DIVA game appearances. The PD/S22 Weiss Schwarz set is one of multiple official TCG adaptations of the franchise. `[Vocaloid Wiki: Hatsune Miku, via WebSearch snippet]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — The PD/S22 set is a licensed Weiss Schwarz expansion based on the 2012 PlayStation 3 rhythm game *Hatsune Miku: Project DIVA F*, produced by Bushiroad; the English edition released November 2013. The set covers all six Crypton Future Media Vocaloids featured in the game. `[WebSearch: Weiss Schwarz PD/S22 retailer listings; Amazon Bushiroad Trial Deck PD/S22]`

- **Design** — The "Bad Boy" costume is a specific unlockable Module in *Hatsune Miku: Project DIVA F*, earned by clearing the song "Unhappy Refrain" on any difficulty and purchasing it for 20,000 DIVA Points. The module alters Len's default appearance: hair becomes a paler yellow, eyes shift to vermillion, and the outfit is a red-and-black geometric jacket, grey headphones, and black tactical pants. It also appeared in *Project DIVA X*. `[Project DIVA Wiki (Fandom): Bad Boy module, via WebSearch snippet]`

- **Character context** — Kagamine Rin and Len (鏡音リン・レン) are Crypton Future Media's second Vocaloid pair (codenamed CV02), first released December 27, 2007 for the VOCALOID2 engine. Their surname 鏡音 (Kagamine) means "mirrored sound" — they were designed as mirror-image androids, with Rin representing the right audio channel and Len the left. Both are voiced by Asami Shimoda. `[Wikipedia: Kagamine Rin/Len, via WebSearch snippet]`

- **Card design** — PD/S22 uses dual art-style variants (A/B) for select character-module combinations, allowing collectors to build aesthetically themed decks around specific in-game costume art. This card (E084a) is the A variant of the Bad Boy module; an E084b variant exists with alternate composition. `[WebSearch: fishpond.com Weiss Schwarz Kagamine Len Bad Boy Art-Style A listing]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — PD/S22 is the Weiss Schwarz booster set adapting Hatsune Miku: Project DIVA F, published by Bushiroad under license from SEGA and Crypton Future Media. The English edition contains 100 card types (8 RR / 20 R / 28 U / 28 C / 16 CX) plus 44 parallel variants, with SP Gold Foil signed cards from Saki Fujita (Hatsune Miku's voice actress) randomly inserted per display. `[Weiss Schwarz official product page: en.ws-tcg.com/products/pd-f/]`
- **Song origin** — "Melancholic" is a Vocaloid song by producer Junky, originally uploaded May 22, 2012, sung by Kagamine Rin's voice bank. The song tells the story of a conflicted tsundere girl experiencing her first crush — one of Junky's most recognized works and a long-running community favorite. `[Vocaloid Wiki: Melancholic, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Module design** — The card depicts Kagamine Rin in her Melancholy module from Project DIVA f: a lime-green hoodie with yellow accessories, black headphones with lime-green and magenta markings, and a coral pink skirt. The module was designed by Chiho-P and later reappeared in Project DIVA Arcade Future Tone. `[Project DIVA Wiki: Melancholy, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Character context** — Kagamine Rin is Crypton Future Media's CV02 (Character Vocal Series 02), a twin vocal package with Kagamine Len. Designed by illustrator KEI around a "mirror image" concept, the twins are among the six core Crypton Vocaloid characters. Rin's default design features short blonde hair, blue eyes, and signature white headphones with a yellow bow. `[Wikipedia: Kagamine Rin/Len]`

## Trivia

- **Design** — Kagamine Rin's "School Swimwear" module is one of the more expensive unlockable costumes in *Hatsune Miku: Project DIVA F*, requiring the player to first clear all songs on Hard difficulty before it becomes purchasable for 60,000 DIVA Points. A distinguishing detail of the module is that Rin's hair shortens to neck-length from her standard cut. The module also appeared in *Project DIVA F 2nd* (clearing 45 songs with Rin) and *Project DIVA X*. `[Project DIVA Wiki (Fandom): Kagamine Rin School Swimwear module, via WebSearch snippet]`

- **Set context** — The PD/S22 English set released November 2013 as a Bushiroad-produced Weiss Schwarz expansion. Cards in the set depict specific in-game Module costumes from the PlayStation 3 rhythm title, making them trading-card illustrations of unlockable in-game outfits rather than original character art. `[WebSearch: Weiss Schwarz PD/S22 English retailer listings]`

- **Character context** — Kagamine Rin and Len share a conceptual design based on the idea of mirror-image androids: their surname 鏡音 (Kagamine) means "mirrored sound," and the pair was designed with Rin for the right audio channel and Len for the left. Both were first released December 27, 2007 as the second Crypton Future Media Vocaloid pair (CV02) for the VOCALOID2 engine. `[Wikipedia: Kagamine Rin/Len, via WebSearch snippet]`

- **Mechanical note** — The School Swimwear variant (E087) is an Uncommon in PD/S22. Higher-rarity Rin cards in the same set depict the Stylish Energy module and other costumes. The module-art tier system means the card's rarity in the set corresponds loosely to the visual impact and unlock difficulty of the depicted in-game costume. `[WebSearch: Weiss Schwarz PD/S22 Kagamine Rin card listings including Stylish Energy R reference]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — The AOT/S35 Booster Pack (English edition) was published by Bushiroad under license from Kodansha and FUNimation, covering the original Attack on Titan anime (Seasons 1-2 era). The official copyright line on the Weiss Schwarz card database reads: "©Hajime Isayama, Kodansha / ATTACK ON TITAN Production Committee. Licensed by Kodansha through FUNimation Productions, Ltd." `[Weiss Schwarz official card database: en.ws-tcg.com]`

- **Character context** — Mikasa Ackerman is the last known descendant of the Azumabito (Hizuru/Eastern) lineage living inside the Walls, and a member of the Ackerman clan — an Eldian bloodline genetically engineered to produce superhuman soldiers. The 'Disheartened' card title names the emotional register: canonically Mikasa is elite militarily but emotionally tethered to Eren, whose trajectory progressively becomes a source of grief rather than purpose. `[Wikipedia: Mikasa Ackerman]`

- **Design** — The AOT/S35 set contains at least nine distinct Mikasa-titled cards across rarity tiers — from the double-rare "Resisting Fate" (RR/SP) at the top to this Common (C) at the bottom. The "Disheartened" print is the lowest-rarity Mikasa in the set, representing emotional collapse rather than her combat peak. `[TCGplayer search results; Amazon product listings for AOT/S35 Mikasa cards]`

- **Set design** — Weiss Schwarz (Weiß Schwarz) is a Bushiroad card game in which licensed IP properties are released as standalone Title sets. The name splits the German words for 'White' and 'Black,' reflecting a dual-faction structure in tournament play. The Attack on Titan English booster was among the earlier English-language releases, predating the Final Season product line (a separate set). `[Weiss Schwarz official website: en.ws-tcg.com]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — This card is from Weiss Schwarz English Edition set MR/W80, the Magia Record: Puella Magi Madoka Magica Side Story (Anime) release by Bushiroad. The set adapts the mobile-game spin-off of the 2011 Puella Magi Madoka Magica series and its three-season anime adaptation produced by Shaft (2020–2022). Card number E081 marks it as a Rare in the English edition. `[ICv2: Bushiroad Magia Record announcement]` `[Wikipedia: Magia Record]`

- **Character context** — Iroha Tamaki is the protagonist of Magia Record, separate from the original Madoka Magica cast. She became a magical girl by wishing to cure her younger sister Ui's illness, then arrived in Kamihama City searching for Ui after the sister disappeared from her memory entirely. Her primary weapon is an arm-mounted crossbow; her magic is healing-based — an unusual specialization in the Madoka framework where most magical girls' powers reflect their wish. `[Puella Magi Wiki: Iroha Tamaki]`

- **Franchise framing** — The anime ran for three seasons: Season 1 (January 2020), Season 2 "The Eve of Awakening" (August–September 2021), and Season 3 "Dawn of a Shallow Dream" (April 2022), all produced by Shaft. The MR/W80 set is cross-compatible with earlier Madoka Magica Weiss Schwarz sets (MM/ prefix), enabling multi-set deck building across the franchise. `[Wikipedia: Magia Record]` `[ws-tcg.com cardlist]`

- **Design note** — The card title "Serious Gaze" emphasizes Iroha's defining narrative quality: quiet determination despite being characterized as shy and self-doubting relative to other magical girl protagonists. This framing distinguishes her from Madoka Kaname, whose personality she resembles but surpasses in resolve. `[Puella Magi Wiki: Iroha Tamaki]`

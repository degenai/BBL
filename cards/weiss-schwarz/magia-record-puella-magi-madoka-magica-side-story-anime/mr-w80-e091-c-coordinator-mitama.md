## Trivia

- **Set context** — MR/W80 is the English-edition Weiss Schwarz set for the TV anime of Puella Magi Madoka Magica Side Story: Magia Record, released April 23, 2021, containing 167 cards. The anime ran in two cours (2020-2021) adapting the Kamihama City story arc from the 2017 Shaft/Aniplex mobile game. `[en.ws-tcg.com card database, WebSearch snippet: littleAKIBA]`

- **Character context** — Mitama Yakumo's title "Coordinator" (調整屋さん, choseiya-san — literally "adjustment-shop operator") is her canonical in-world occupation: she trades magical enhancements of other magical girls' Soul Gems in exchange for Grief Seeds, functioning as an economy hub in Kamihama City. She began as a non-playable shopkeeper NPC in the mobile game before becoming playable in April 2018. `[Puella Magi Wiki: Mitama Yakumo]`

- **Design** — Mitama's character design (primary artist: Punyan) features silver hair, a blue X-shaped Soul Gem worn as a hair ribbon, and a black corset over a white frilled-sleeve shirt with a cadet blue tailcoat — all canonical details reproduced in the card art. Her Doppel (Witch form) is named Totentanz, after the medieval "Dance of Death" allegory. `[Puella Magi Wiki: Mitama Yakumo]`

- **Lore depth** — Mitama's wish to Kyubey was: "I want to become the existence that destroys Kamihama" — considerably darker than her cheerful shopkeeper persona suggests. Her Magical Girl Side Story reveals her true power is not benign adjustment but the ability to "tamper with other magical girls' magic," described as akin to a curse. `[Puella Magi Wiki: Mitama Yakumo]`

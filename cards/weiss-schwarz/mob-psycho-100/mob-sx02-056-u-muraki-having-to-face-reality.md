## Trivia

- **Set context** — This card is from Weiss Schwarz English Edition set MOB/SX02, the Mob Psycho 100 release by Bushiroad. The English edition released in late 2022. The MOB/SX02 designation is unique to the Mob Psycho 100 franchise in the Weiss Schwarz product line. `[ICv2: Mob Psycho 100 for Weiss Schwarz]` `[Bushiroad Global Online Store]`

- **Character context** — Muraki (Muraki Tomohiro) is an esper and former member of Claw's elite unit "Scar," operating within the organization's 7th Division. His psychic ability is Spiritual Projection Technique: creating intangible spirit-clones immune to physical attacks. Despite being able to overwhelm Ritsu Kageyama in combat, he was defeated and humiliated by Arataka Reigen without any psychic power — Reigen questioned his head decoration and shoulder pads and told him to act like an adult. `[Mob Psycho 100 Wiki (Fandom): Muraki]`

- **Design note** — The card title "Having to Face Reality" directly names Muraki's turning point. After Reigen's intervention, Muraki abandoned his cosplay outfit, switched to a suit, and eventually fought alongside the protagonists against Claw. The confrontational scene in the art captures the moment just before that shift. `[Mob Psycho 100 Wiki (Fandom): Muraki]`

- **Community resonance** — Muraki is a minor character in the series but notable for exemplifying Mob Psycho 100's recurring theme: esper power is not the measure of a person. His defeat by a non-psychic's bluntness is often cited as one of the funnier moments in Season 1. Sentiment: `[Mob Psycho 100 Wiki (Fandom): Muraki character notes]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Peonia is a Crown Tundra DLC NPC, *not* a Galar Gym Leader. She traveled to the Crown Tundra hoping to try Dynamax Adventures on her own, only for her overbearing father Peony to insist on a father-daughter "adven-tour." Her Japanese name is Shakuya (commonly "Shaku-chan" to her father). `[Bulbapedia: Peonia, via WebSearch snippet]` `[Pokemon Fandom Wiki: Peony, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Family** — Her father Peony is a former Steel-type Gym Leader and briefly held the Galar Champion title before stepping down. Peony is the younger brother of Chairman Rose (Macro Cosmos), which makes Peonia Rose's niece — though Peony himself is *not* the Chairman of Macro Cosmos as is sometimes assumed; his older brother Rose is. `[Pokemon Fandom Wiki: Peony, via WebSearch snippet]` `[Serebii: Macro Cosmos, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Set context** — Chilling Reign (SWSH6, code CRE, released 2021-06-18) draws on Crown Tundra DLC personnel: Galar Gym Leaders Melony (no. 146/198, Ice/Circhester) and Avery (no. 130/198, Isle of Armor/Psychic) appear alongside Crown Tundra civilian NPCs Peony and Peonia. Co-occurrence in the same set is not equivalent to shared role. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh6-149]` `[Bulbapedia: Melony (Chilling Reign 146), via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Design** — Illustrated by kirisAki across all three Chilling Reign prints: the regular Uncommon (no. 149/198), the Full Art Ultra Rare (no. 196/198), and the Rainbow Rare (no. 219/198). `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh6-149]` `[Limitless TCG: CRE/149, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Mechanical** — Supporter effect lets you put up to 3 Prize cards into your hand, then place that many cards from your hand face down as new Prizes. A niche Prize-manipulation tool — useful for digging for Prize-locked combo pieces. Market price holds around $0.18 normal / $0.20 reverse holo as of 2026-05-12. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh6-149]`

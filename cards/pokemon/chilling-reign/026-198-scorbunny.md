## Trivia

- **Species lore** — Scorbunny is National Pokedex no. 813, the Galar Fire starter introduced in Pokemon Sword and Shield (2019). It evolves into Raboot and then Cinderace. One of three first partner Pokemon of Galar alongside Grookey and Sobble. [PokemonTCG.io: swsh6-26; WebSearch: Bulbapedia snippet]
- **Design** — The orange nose-pad marking is designed to resemble a bandage — a Japanese character-design trope for a rambunctious personality. Sword and Shield director Shigeru Ohmori stated this was intentional to convey "a sense that it could run off or cause trouble." The Fire type may reference the high body temperature of real rabbits (100-103 F / 37-39 C) or Jataka tale no. 316, in which a rabbit jumps into fire as an offering and is not burned. [WebSearch: Bulbapedia snippet; Wikipedia snippet]
- **Flavor text** — "It has special pads on the backs of its feet, and one on its nose. Once it's raring to fight, these pads radiate tremendous heat." [PokemonTCG.io: swsh6-26]
- **Mechanical context** — Single Strike Basic with one-Energy Headbutt (20 damage) — minimal entry-level stats consistent with a first-stage starter. The Single Strike subtype marks it as part of Chilling Reign's dual-mechanic structure. [PokemonTCG.io: swsh6-26]

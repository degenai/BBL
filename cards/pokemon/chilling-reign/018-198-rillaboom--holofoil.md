## Trivia

- **Species lore** — Rillaboom is National Pokedex no. 812, the Stage 2 final evolution of the Galar Grass starter line (Grookey, level 16, to Thwackey; Thwackey to Rillaboom, level 35). Introduced in Pokemon Sword and Shield (2019). [PokemonTCG.io: swsh6-18; WebSearch: Bulbapedia snippet]
- **Design references** — Rillaboom's design references Taiko drumming and gorilla chest-beating as social dominance display. Its hair is based on the Green Man, a pagan Celtic symbol representing growth and renewal found widely across Great Britain. [WebSearch: pokemongohub.net snippet; Bulbapedia snippet]
- **Social lore** — In a Rillaboom troop, the individual with the best drumming technique leads; leadership challenges are settled through drum battles. Galarian folklore holds that as long as Rillaboom drums, the rules of nature are temporarily suspended — predator and prey dance together as companions. [WebSearch: Bulbapedia snippet]
- **Flavor text** — "The one with the best drumming techniques becomes the boss of the troop. It has a gentle disposition and values harmony among its group." [PokemonTCG.io: swsh6-18]
- **Mechanical context** — This print is a Rapid Strike subtype; Raging Repeated Strike scales with Energy discarded, pairing with Rapid Strike Energy for burst damage output. [PokemonTCG.io: swsh6-18]

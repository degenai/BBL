## Trivia

- **Set context** — Siebold is one of the four Elite Four members of the Kalos Pokemon League in Pokemon X and Y (Gen 6, 2013), specializing in Water-type Pokemon. His E4 cohort: Malva (Fire, later revealed as a Team Flare operative), Wikstrom (Steel), and Drasna (Dragon). `[Bulbapedia: Siebold; via WebSearch]`
- **Character design** — Beyond the League, Siebold runs a high-end restaurant in Lumiose City and treats battling as an art form equivalent to fine cuisine. He is characterized as solitary, standoffish, and deeply contemplative — the chef-warrior archetype fully committed. `[Serebii.net: Siebold anime profile; Bulbapedia: Siebold; via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical** — Chilling Reign (SWSH6) was a Sword & Shield expansion released June 18, 2021 introducing Rapid Strike and Single Strike battle-style mechanics. Siebold here is a Rapid Strike Supporter healing 60 damage from up to 2 Rapid Strike Pokemon. Community consensus rated the 60-HP heal underwhelming for the format's damage output — meta decks of the period hit for significantly more. `[WebSearch snippet via TCGPlayer; TCGCollector]`
- **Print variants** — The card appears in three rarities within Chilling Reign: Uncommon (153/198), Rare Ultra Full Art (198/198), and Rare Rainbow (221/198). `[TCGCollector: Chilling Reign Siebold entries; via WebSearch]`

## Connections

- [[galar-gym-challenge]] — Circhester major-division Ice-type Gym Leader, Shield-route. Melony holds the Ice slot at Circhester Stadium in Pokémon Shield (with her son Gordie taking the same slot as a Rock-type in the Sword version) — the canonical version-asymmetric Gym pairing the Galar League formalizes as twin championship brackets. The pale-blue ice ribbon backdrop and warm-elder posture are the publicity-shot register the league uses for its signed Gym-Leader celebrity-athlete roster; in-canon she is publicly listed as the no. 1 member of Gordie's fan club despite their feud, marking the league's televised family-storyline framing. `[Bulbapedia: Melony]` `[Bulbapedia: Galar League]` `[Pokémon Fandom Wiki: Melony, via WebSearch snippet]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — From Sword & Shield—Chilling Reign (SWSH6, code CRE, released 2021-06-18), the second SWSH set built around the Crown Tundra DLC. The expansion centers on Calyrex's two formes — Ice Rider Calyrex VMAX and Shadow Rider Calyrex VMAX — plus the Galarian forms of Articuno, Zapdos, and Moltres; Pokemon.com pitches it as "phantom hauntings, howling blizzards, and wild battles" in the land of spectral snow `[PokemonTCG.io set:swsh6]` `[pokemon.com SWSH6 set page]`.
- **Design** — Illustrator credit on this card (no. 146/198) is "take," who is the official character designer for Melony in Pokémon Sword & Shield itself — not a third-party TCG-only illustrator. "take" also designed other Galar Gym Leaders and Pokémon Legends: Arceus characters Adaman and Irida `[Bulbapedia: take (illustrator), via WebSearch snippet]`. The parallel SWSH7 Gordie Supporter (no. 149, Evolving Skies) is also credited to "take" `[PokemonTCG.io card:swsh7-149]`.
- **Set context** — Melony is the Circhester Gym Leader specializing in Ice-type Pokémon and is fightable only in Pokémon Shield; the Sword version replaces her with her son Gordie, who rejected her Ice specialty in favor of Rock-type. The Circhester slot is one of Galar's signature version-asymmetric Gym pairings `[Pokémon Fandom Wiki: Melony, via WebSearch snippet]` `[Bulbapedia: Circhester Stadium, via WebSearch snippet]`.
- **Set context** — Canonical family: Gordie is Melony's son. Lore frames their relationship as estranged — Melony wanted to step aside and pass the Gym to him, but Gordie refused to inherit her Ice specialty, switched to Rock-type, and the pair fought; Circhester itself is split between their factions. Despite the feud, Melony is publicly listed as the no. 1 member of Gordie's fan club `[Pokémon Fandom Wiki: Melony, via WebSearch snippet]` `[TheGamer: "Melony & Gordie's Feud In Pokemon Is A Reminder To Call Your Mother", via WebSearch snippet]`.
- **Mechanical** — Rules text per PokemonTCG.io: "Attach a Water Energy card from your discard pile to 1 of your Pokémon V. If you do, draw 3 cards. You may play only 1 Supporter card during your turn." Community-rated as a tournament staple of the SWSH era: Pojo ranked her no. 2 in the Top 15 of Chilling Reign, and Pokemon.com's official strategy article identifies her as the primary Energy-acceleration + draw engine for Ice Rider Calyrex VMAX decks. Restriction: targets Pokémon V (and by extension VMAX) only, not basic Pokémon `[PokemonTCG.io card:swsh6-146]` `[Pojo: Top 15 Chilling Reign]` `[pokemon.com: "Top Cards for the Competition in Chilling Reign"]`.

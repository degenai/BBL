## Trivia

- **Species lore** — Deerling is National Pokedex no. 585, a Normal/Grass type introduced in Generation V (Black and White, 2010). It has four seasonal forms — Spring (pink), Summer (green), Autumn (orange), Winter (brown) — that change fur color and scent. Originally, form changed based on the Nintendo DS internal clock season; from Generation VI onward, only the Spring Form was available in the wild, with other forms obtainable via transfer or Pokemon GO. [WebSearch: Bulbapedia snippet]
- **Scent lore** — Deerling's scent shifts with its form: delicate and sweet in spring, grassy in summer, softly sweet in autumn, and no scent at all in winter. It functions as a living seasonal calendar in its in-game lore. [WebSearch: Bulbapedia snippet]
- **Flavor text** — "The turning of the seasons changes the color and scent of this Pokemon's fur. People use it to mark the seasons." [PokemonTCG.io: swsh6-11]
- **Art note** — The card art shows pink-blossom head coloring consistent with Spring Form, but set in a snowy winter environment — an artistic juxtaposition likely driven by Chilling Reign's winter/cold theme. [PokemonTCG.io: swsh6-11 card art; set theme context]

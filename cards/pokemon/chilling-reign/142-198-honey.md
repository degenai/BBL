## Trivia

- **IP note** — This card depicts Honey, the Dojo Matron from The Isle of Armor DLC (Pokemon Sword and Shield, 2020). She is the wife of Mustard (former Galar Champion and dojo master) and the mother of Hyde (a researcher at the dojo). She is NOT the mother of Leon or Hop — their mother is a separate unnamed NPC called "Mum" in the Galar region. These are distinct characters. `[Bulbapedia: Honey (Galar) via WebSearch; Bulbapedia: Hop via WebSearch]`
- **Set context** — Chilling Reign (released June 18, 2021) is the sixth expansion of the Sword and Shield TCG series. The set draws from both the Isle of Armor and Crown Tundra DLC regions, which accounts for the inclusion of Isle of Armor NPCs as Supporter cards. Honey was first released in Japanese S-P Promotional cards before her English Chilling Reign debut. `[PokemonTCG.io API (swsh6-142); Bulbapedia: Honey (Galar) via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical** — The card effect (draw a card for each of the opponent's Benched Pokemon V) scales with the opponent's field presence — stronger the opponent's bench, the more you draw. The effect reflects Honey's in-game role as a resourceful, adaptive support character. Art by Ryuta Fuse. `[PokemonTCG.io API (swsh6-142)]`

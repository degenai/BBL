## Trivia

- **Species lore** — Celebi is National Pokedex no. 251, a Mythical Psychic/Grass type classified as the "Time Travel Pokemon." It is the guardian of Ilex Forest in Johto and is said to exist simultaneously throughout time, with plant life flourishing wherever it has been. [WebSearch: Bulbapedia snippet]
- **Availability history** — The original Crystal event required a Japan-only Mobile Communication Device and was scrapped internationally for over a decade. It became legitimately available without any event or distribution code in the Virtual Console release of Pokemon Crystal on Nintendo 3DS. [WebSearch: Bulbapedia snippet]
- **In-game time travel** — In HeartGold/SoulSilver, bringing a fateful-encounter Celebi to the Ilex Forest shrine triggers a sequence where it takes the player back in time to witness Giovanni abandon his son Silver after his Kanto defeat, then to battle Giovanni at Tohjo Falls. [WebSearch: Bulbapedia snippet]
- **Mechanical context** — As a Pokemon V, Celebi V gives the opponent 2 Prize cards on knockout — the standard V rule. Leaflet Dance (attach any number of Grass Energy from hand) gave it a role as an Energy accelerator in Grass-type Sword & Shield era decks. [PokemonTCG.io: swsh6-7]

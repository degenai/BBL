## Trivia

- **Set context** — Chilling Reign (SWSH6) released June 18, 2021 in the Sword & Shield series, 233 cards total. The set is themed around the Crown Tundra and the Ice Rider/Shadow Rider Calyrex legendaries — Snowy Form Castform is thematically at home here. [PokemonTCG.io: set swsh6]
- **Species lore** — Castform is National Pokedex no. 351, introduced in Generation III (Ruby/Sapphire, 2002). It is described as an artificial Pokemon whose cellular structure is chemically altered by temperature and humidity. Its Forecast Ability gives it four forms — Normal, Sunny (Fire), Rainy (Water), and Snowy (Ice) — each a different type triggered by the current battle weather. [WebSearch: Bulbapedia snippet]
- **Mechanical design** — The Weather Reading Ability ("If you have 8 or more Stadium cards in your discard pile, ignore all Energy in this Pokemon's attack costs") translates Castform's weather-responsiveness into a Stadium-cycling mechanic — the TCG's closest analog to the in-game Forecast system. [PokemonTCG.io: swsh6-34]
- **Flavor text** — "This is Castform's form when caught in a hailstorm. Its whole body is chilled, and its skin is partially frozen!" [PokemonTCG.io: swsh6-34]

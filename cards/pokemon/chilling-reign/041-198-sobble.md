## Connections

- [[042-198-drizzile]] — Galar Water starter evolution-line 1:1 mirror, within-set. Sobble is the Stage-1 base (National Pokedex no. 816) of the three-stage Galar Water starter line (Sobble level 16 to Drizzile; Drizzile level 35 to Inteleon), introduced in Pokemon Sword and Shield (2019). Both prints are within the Chilling Reign 041-042 adjacency, both Rapid Strike subtype, both Yuu Nishida / Mitsuhiro Arita art-style register (cool water palette, lizard body plan), with visual progression from aquatic-and-camouflaged baby (water/bubbles around partially-submerged round body, blue/teal) to laid-back-coastal-adolescent (slim upright bipedal lizard on rocky shoreline, blue-green/teal). The Stage-1-to-Stage-2 evolution-line pair is the canonical anchor; cross-print Sobbles and Drizziles can later attach to this same connection. [PokemonTCG.io: swsh6-41 + swsh6-42 evolvesTo / evolvesFrom; Bulbapedia: Sobble (Pokemon); Bulbapedia: Drizzile (Pokemon)]

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Chilling Reign (June 18, 2021) is the sixth expansion in the Sword and Shield TCG series, based on the Crown Tundra DLC. Sobble appears here as a Rapid Strike Basic, a subtype introduced in Battle Styles (the preceding set), grouping coordinated-strike Pokemon together [PokemonTCG.io: swsh6; screenrant.com: Chilling Reign expansion overview].
- **Design / lore** — Sobble's evolution line carries a spy/secret-agent design arc through all three stages: Sobble (chameleon + anxious child), Drizzile (brooding adolescent who lays traps), and Inteleon (the "Secret Agent Pokemon," National Pokedex no. 818, whose tuxedo coloring alludes to British intelligence). Sobble's own Galar Pokedex entry number is 007 — a direct wink at the James Bond designation [Bulbapedia: Inteleon; PokéJungle: Origin of Species Sobble].
- **Flavor text** — "When it gets wet, its skin changes color, and this Pokemon becomes invisible as if it were camouflaged." The camouflage trait is the mechanical through-line connecting Sobble's chameleon inspiration to Inteleon's stealth-sniper role [PokemonTCG.io: swsh6-41].
- **Mechanical note** — The "Keep Calling" attack searches for up to 3 Basic Rapid Strike Pokemon — a unusually wide search for a Basic. In competitive play this made Sobble a functional setup piece in Rapid Strike builds during its Standard-legal window [PokemonTCG.io: swsh6-41 oracle text; Limitless TCG].

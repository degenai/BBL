## Trivia

- **Set context** — This card is from XY — Steam Siege (set code xy11), released 2016-08-03. Steam Siege was the 11th main XY-series expansion and introduced Dual-type Pokemon cards — cards printed with two types simultaneously — as the set's signature mechanic. `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy11-10]`

- **Flavor text** — "It lives deep in forests. With the leaf on its head, it makes a flute whose song makes listeners uneasy." Illustrated by Ayaka Yoshida. `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy11-10]`

- **Design** — Nuzleaf (National Pokedex no. 274) is the mid-stage of the Seedot line: Seedot → Nuzleaf → Shiftry. The design draws from Japanese yōkai: Nuzleaf is based on the ko-tengu (small tengu), a forest yōkai with a distinctive long nose associated with playing tricks and guarding mountain territories. Its evolved form Shiftry is based on the dai-tengu (great tengu), the more powerful, human-like tengu. The flute-playing Pokedex trait echoes tengu folklore of supernatural forest guardians who create unease in travelers. `[WebSearch: 'Pokemon Based On Yokai' japan-avenue.com; Dextraneous.blog Seedot/Nuzleaf/Shiftry design entry]`

- **Mechanical** — Stage 1 Grass-type, 70 HP. Attacks: Harden (1 Grass energy) prevents damage up to 60 from opponent's next attack; Razor Leaf (2 Colorless energy) deals 20 damage. Fire weakness x2. Legal in Unlimited and Expanded as of the XY era. `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy11-10]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Nymble (no. 009/094) appears in the Mega Evolution—Phantasmal Flames expansion (me2 / PFL), released November 14, 2025 — the second main expansion of the Mega Evolution Series, headlined by Mega Charizard X ex and Mega Gengar ex. The set contains 130 cards including 36 secret rares. `[PokemonTCG.io API; pokemon.com Phantasmal Flames page]`
- **Species** — Nymble is National Pokedex no. 919, a Bug-type Pokemon introduced in Generation IX (Scarlet/Violet). Its evolution Lokix (no. 920) has seen rogue competitive play: a Lokix PFL no. 10 list reached the top 16 of a City League tournament in October 2025, built around Lokix's Jumping Shot attack — which deals 150 damage then shuffles Lokix back into the deck. `[limitlesstcg.com/cards/PFL/10; pokemondb.net/pokedex/nymble]`
- **Flavor text** — "It has its third set of legs folded up. When it's in a tough spot, this Pokemon jumps over 30 feet using the strength of its legs." — Nymble (Phantasmal Flames 009). `[PokemonTCG.io API]` The flavor directly informs the Flail Around attack (coin-flip damage): the unpredictable burst output mirrors the creature's stored kinetic energy in its folded legs.
- **Design** — Illustrated by Yuka Morii, one of the TCG's most recognized long-tenure artists, active since Neo Discovery (2000) with over 209 cards illustrated. Morii hand-sculpts Pokemon in polymer clay, builds miniature sets with real pebbles and leaves, and photographs them. Kotaku described her results as depicting "little creatures living in our world, not just posing." `[CGC Cards artist showcase: Yuka Morii; kotaku.com: Yuka Morii TCG art; hobbylark.com: Who Is Yuka Morii]`
- **Set context** — A separate Nymble Illustration Rare (no. 096/094, illustrated by Nakamura Ippan) exists as an out-of-number secret rare in the same Phantasmal Flames set. Wargamer described it as "the least valuable Illustration Rare from the Phantasmal Flames set" while noting its ambiguous quality: Nymble's insectoid features make "its feelings unknowable" — the mystery named as the card's artistic strength. `[wargamer.com: Nymble Illustration Rare Nakamura Ippan]`

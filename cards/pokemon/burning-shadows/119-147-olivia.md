## Trivia

- **Set context** — Burning Shadows (SM3, August 2017) spotlights the Alola NPC roster in its Supporter lineup. Olivia occupies a unique position in Sun and Moon: she is simultaneously Akala Island's Kahuna (its highest-rank trial authority) and a member of the newly-formed Alola Elite Four — the only character in the games to hold both roles at once. Her Grand Trial takes place at the Ruins of Life. `[Bulbapedia: Olivia, via WebSearch]`
- **Character detail** — Outside of Pokemon battle, Olivia runs a jewelry and accessories shop in Konikoni City. Her Rock-type specialty and lapidary trade reinforce each other — she is framed as someone who finds beauty and utility in stone as both a trainer and a craftsperson. `[Bulbapedia: Olivia, via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical note** — The Olivia Supporter (no. 119/147) tutors up to 2 Pokemon-GX directly to hand, a strong search effect that aligned with the GX-centric Sun and Moon metagame at release. It is Expanded-legal. `[PokemonTCG.io API: sm3-119]`
- **Signature partner** — Olivia's battle partner across all her game appearances is a Midnight Form Lycanroc, the Moon-exclusive evolution path. This gives her a version-specific identity within the Alola Elite Four; she is the only E4 member whose key Pokemon is tied to a version exclusivity. `[Bulbapedia: Olivia's Lycanroc, via WebSearch]`

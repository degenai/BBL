## Trivia

- **Set context** — Burning Shadows (SM3, August 2017). Plumeria is Team Skull's sole Admin — the operational enforcer below Boss Guzma. She is framed in-game as the team's "big sister," enforcing discipline strictly while genuinely caring for the Skull Grunts. She specializes in Poison-type Pokemon and is first encountered at the Akala Outskirts before Olivia's Grand Trial. `[Bulbapedia: Plumeria, via WebSearch]`
- **Character depth** — Team Skull is unusual among Pokemon villain factions: its members are explicitly failed Trial Challengers who couldn't complete the Island Challenge. Plumeria's caretaker role — managing a group of Pokemon League rejects — gives her a social dimension most villain admins lack. Her arc ends with grudging redemption: she hands over the Poisonium Z, apologizes for her role in the conflict, and later challenges the player at the Alola League as a legitimate trainer. `[Bulbapedia: Team Skull, via WebSearch]` `[Bulbapedia: Plumeria, via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical note** — The Plumeria Supporter (no. 120/147) requires discarding 2 cards from hand to discard an Energy from an opponent's Pokemon. The mutual-cost structure — you pay to punish — mirrors her tough-love characterization. Expanded-legal. `[PokemonTCG.io API: sm3-120]`

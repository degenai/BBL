## Trivia

- **Set context** — Burning Shadows (SM3) released August 4, 2017 as the third Sun and Moon series expansion, with 147 printed cards (177 total including secret rares). The trainer supporter lineup spotlights Alola's full NPC cast: Trial Captains, Kahunas, and Elite Four members from Pokemon Sun and Moon. `[PokemonTCG.io API: sm3]`
- **Character lore** — Acerola is the youngest member of the Alola Elite Four and its Ghost-type specialist. She first appears in the games as the Trial Captain of the abandoned Thrifty Megamart on Ula'ula Island, tasked with a Mimikyu-as-Totem trial. She is described as the last living member of an Alolan royal family; designer Shusuke Motomiya confirmed her armband is a royal heirloom from the distant past. `[Bulbapedia: Acerola, via WebSearch]`
- **Design subversion** — Unlike older Ghost-type specialists (Agatha, Phoebe), Acerola places a cheerful child in the role. Official artbooks note that, like Phoebe, Acerola can communicate with Ghost-type Pokemon — the only two trainers across all main-series games with this canonical trait. `[Bulbapedia: Acerola, via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical note** — The Supporter effect (bounce a damaged Pokemon plus all attachments back to hand) mirrors her in-game caretaker role at the Aether House, sheltering injured Pokemon and orphaned children. The card is Expanded-legal; the regular print (no. 112/147) and a Full Art version (no. 142/147, art by Megumi Mizutani) both appeared in Burning Shadows. `[PokemonTCG.io API: sm3-112]`

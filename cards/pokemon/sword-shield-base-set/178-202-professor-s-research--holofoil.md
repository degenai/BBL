## Trivia

- **Set context** — Professor Magnolia is the regional Pokemon Professor of Galar, specializing in research into the Dynamax phenomenon (the distortion of space that causes Pokemon to appear gigantic). She is one of the oldest regional professors depicted in the mainline games and is largely retired from fieldwork, having delegated active research to her granddaughter Sonia. Her Dynamax research was ultimately used by Chairman Rose as the basis for his energy extraction plans. `[Bulbapedia: Professor Magnolia, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Design** — The card's official name in the PokemonTCG.io database is "Professor's Research (Professor Magnolia)" — the parenthetical identifying the depicted professor is canonical, not editorial. This naming convention began in the Sword & Shield series and continues through Scarlet & Violet (e.g., Professor's Research (Professor Sada)). Artist on this Holo Rare is Yusuke Ohmura. `[PokemonTCG.io API swsh1-178]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — Professor's Research (discard hand, draw 7) is the primary draw Supporter of the Sword & Shield era and remains a format staple in Expanded. This swsh1 print is legal in Standard, Expanded, and Unlimited. `[PokemonTCG.io API swsh1-178]`
- **Reprint history** — The Professor's Research effect has been reprinted with different professor art across multiple sets: Professor Oak and Professor Elm in Celebrations (2021), Professor Willow in a Pokemon GO fifth-anniversary promo (2021), Professor Sada and Professor Turo in Scarlet & Violet base (2023), and Professors Birch, Kukui, and Magnolia as SVP Black Star Promos in the Trainer's Toolkit 2025. `[SNKRDUNK magazine: Pokemon TCG Professor's Research reprints, via WebSearch snippet]`

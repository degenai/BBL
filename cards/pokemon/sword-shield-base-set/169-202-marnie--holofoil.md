## Trivia

- **Set context** — Marnie is a rival character in Pokemon Sword & Shield (2019) who specializes in Dark-type Pokemon. Her stated goal is to become Champion to revitalize her hometown Spikemuth, a dilapidated city without its own Power Spot. She eventually succeeds her older brother Piers as Spikemuth's Gym Leader after the player becomes Champion. `[Bulbapedia: Marnie, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Design** — The Sword & Shield base set (ID swsh1) released February 7, 2020, with 202 printed cards. This Holo Rare print (card no. 169/202) was accompanied in the same set by a Full Art (no. 200/202) and Rainbow Rare Secret (no. 208/202). Artist is kirisAki. The card was reprinted in holofoil in Champion's Path and saw multiple additional Japanese-exclusive non-holo reprints across VMAX Starter Sets, VMAX Climax, and the VSTAR Premium Trainer Box. `[PokemonTCG.io API swsh1-169; Bulbapedia: Marnie (Sword & Shield 169), via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — Marnie is classified as both a consistency Supporter and a disruption Supporter: both players shuffle their hands to the bottom of their deck, with the user drawing 5 cards and the opponent drawing 4. It was a competitive staple in Sword & Shield-era aggro decks alongside Professor's Research and Boss's Orders. Legal in Unlimited and Expanded; rotated out of Standard. `[PokemonTCG.io API swsh1-169; suprpetrix.com Pokemon TCG supporter guide, via WebSearch snippet]`

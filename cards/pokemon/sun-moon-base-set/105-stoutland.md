## Trivia

- **Species context** — Stoutland is National Pokedex no. 508, a Normal-type Stage 2 Pokemon (Lillipup → Herdier → Stoutland) introduced in Generation 5 (Unova, Black and White). It is the apex of the only fully-evolved Normal-type dog line in the Unova regional dex. `[Serebii card database, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "Intelligent, good-natured, and valiant, it's a trustworthy partner on rescue teams." The rescue-team role is a consistent canonical thread across Stoutland's Pokedex entries: it is known specifically for rescuing humans stranded in mountains and at sea — paralleling real-world working breeds like St. Bernards and Newfoundlands. `[Card frontmatter flavor_text; Bulbapedia: Stoutland, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Design** — Art is by Kagemaru Himeno, a Pokemon TCG illustrator active since the original Base Set era. The Sun & Moon Base Set (SM1) was released February 3, 2017 as the launch set of the Sun & Moon TCG series. This card is Rare (R) with 150 HP. `[Serebii / PkmnCards, via WebSearch snippet; card frontmatter]`
- **Species design note** — Stoutland's mustache is a canonical social-status marker within the species: mustache length determines rank among Stoutland. The design draws from Shih Tzu, Maltese, Yorkshire Terrier, and Scottish Terrier breeds. `[Bulbapedia: Stoutland, via WebSearch snippet]`

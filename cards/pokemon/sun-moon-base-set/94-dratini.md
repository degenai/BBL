## Trivia

- **Set context** — Dratini (no. 94) is a Basic Dragon-type Common in the Sun and Moon Base Set (SM1), the first Sun and Moon series expansion, released February 3, 2017. The set introduced Alola-region Pokemon and the GX mechanic. Dratini appears as a Kanto Dragon line carryover. `[PokemonTCG.io API: sm1-94]`
- **Design lineage** — The Dratini line is the only Dragon-type introduced in Generation I and remained the only Dragon line until Gold and Silver. Dratini's Japanese name, Miniryu, combines "mini" and "ryu" (the Japanese word for dragon) — its concept is encoded directly in the name. The line draws from ryu (Japanese dragons), Chinese lung dragons that bring good fortune, and possibly the Korean Imugi: a serpentine proto-dragon that transforms into a true dragon only after surviving defined trials, which maps onto the three-stage evolution from Dratini to Dragonite. `[Bulbapedia: Dratini (Pokemon) via WebSearch snippet; facts.net: 38 Facts About Dratini via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Pseudo-legendary anchor** — Dragonite, the line's final form, is the franchise's first "pseudo-legendary" — a fan and later semi-official designation for fully evolved Pokemon with a 600 base stat total that are not game-mechanical Legendaries. Dratini is the Common entry point of this line, making it a budget setup piece for Dragon decks across multiple TCG eras. `[Bulbapedia: Dragonite (Pokemon) via WebSearch snippet; Wikipedia: Dragonite via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "After a 10-hour struggle, a fisherman was able to pull one up and confirm its existence." The entry frames Dratini as a cryptid — its existence required active confirmation rather than observation, echoing sea-serpent folklore where the creature is elusive and rarely witnessed. `[PokemonTCG.io API: sm1-94]`
- **Mechanical note** — Signs of Evolution (1 Grass energy) fetches one each of Dratini, Dragonair, and Dragonite from the deck into hand simultaneously. A full-line-fetch on a Common's attack is unusually efficient setup; it collapses a three-stage evolution's standard multi-turn ramp into a single attack action. `[PokemonTCG.io API: sm1-94]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Brionne is the middle evolution of the Alola Water-type starter line: Popplio evolves into Brionne at level 17 and into Primarina at level 34. The final form Primarina adds Fairy typing, making this one of the few Water starter lines to end as a dual type. Large groups of Brionne are said to dance together on moonlit nights when the sea is calm. `[Bulbapedia: Popplio, WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "It cares deeply for its companions. When its Trainer is feeling down, it performs a cheery dance to try and help." — The emotional-support framing is consistent across the Popplio line's lore identity: Primarina also uses dance in battle, controlling water balloons with its voice in what is described as a theatrical performance. `[PokemonTCG.io API]`
- **Design** — Illustrated by Atsuko Nishida, who is also credited as the original game designer of the Popplio line itself. Nishida is the only Pokemon designer credited across every generation of the mainline games, with credits including Pikachu, Bulbasaur, Charmander, Squirtle, and Togepi. Her illustrating a TCG card of her own species design is a notable convergence of design and illustration credit. `[Wikipedia: Atsuko Nishida, WebSearch snippet]`

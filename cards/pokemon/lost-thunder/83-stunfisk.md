## Trivia

- **Set context** — Stunfisk is a dual-type Ground/Electric Pokemon (National Pokedex no. 618) introduced in Generation V (Unova). This LOT-83 print is the standard Unova form, confirmed by the brown coloration and Electric-type attack in its oracle text. A Galarian regional form was introduced in Sword and Shield — Ground/Steel type — in which the flat body mimics a Poke Ball to lure prey into a bear-trap-style fin clamp. `[PokemonTCG.io API, Bulbapedia: Stunfisk, WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "It conceals itself in the mud of the seashore. Then it waits. When prey touch it, it delivers a jolt of electricity." — Stunfisk's design concept originated from a desire to create "the flattest Pokemon ever," with the species based on flounder and stargazer fish. Its ambush-predator design is unusual in a franchise that defaults to cute or fierce. `[PokemonTCG.io API, WebSearch snippet]`
- **Design** — Illustrated by Ken Sugimori, co-founder of Game Freak and the primary character designer and art director of the Pokemon franchise since its inception. Sugimori designed and finalized most of the original 151 Pokemon. His appearance as a TCG illustrator is relatively infrequent compared to dedicated card artists — his credit here connects this card directly to Game Freak's in-house visual leadership. `[Wikipedia: Ken Sugimori, WebSearch snippet]`

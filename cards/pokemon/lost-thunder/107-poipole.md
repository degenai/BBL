## Trivia

- **Set context** — Poipole is codenamed UB Adhesive by the International Police in Pokemon Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon, placing it within the Ultra Beast classification — extradimensional lifeforms that enter the Pokemon world through Ultra Wormholes. Poipole and its evolution Naganadel (codenamed UB Stinger) are the only Ultra Beasts that form an evolutionary line. `[Bulbapedia: Poipole, WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "This Ultra Beast is well enough liked to be chosen as a first partner in its own world." — This distinguishes Poipole from other Ultra Beasts: it functions as the starter Pokemon equivalent in its home dimension, making it uniquely socialized toward bonding with trainers despite its extradimensional origin. `[PokemonTCG.io API]`
- **Lore connection** — The Aether Foundation's primary antagonist arc in Sun and Moon involves President Lusamine using Cosmog to open Ultra Wormholes and summon Ultra Beasts. Poipole originates from the same wormhole network Lusamine exploited, making it thematically adjacent to the Aether Foundation node in this archive — both are products of the same interdimensional infrastructure weaponized for Lusamine's obsession. `[Serebii.net: Aether Foundation]`
- **Sentiment** — In the Sun and Moon anime, Ash catches a Poipole in the episode "Love at First Twirl!" and later releases it so it can return to its homeworld. Ash's Poipole reappears evolved into Naganadel in "Z-Move Showdown!" — one of the few cases in the anime of a Pokemon evolving after being released. `[Bulbapedia: Ash's Naganadel, WebSearch snippet]`

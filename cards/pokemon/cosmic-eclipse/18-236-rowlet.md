## Trivia

- **Species context** — Rowlet is National Pokedex no. 722, one of three First Partner Pokemon of Alola in Pokemon Sun and Moon (Gen 7, 2016), typed Grass/Flying. It holds the highest base HP of all unevolved starter Pokemon. `[Serebii.net Pokedex 722; Bulbapedia: Rowlet; via WebSearch]`
- **Design** — Based on the barn owl (Tyto alba), introduced to Hawaii. The Grass typing may also nod to the pueo (Asio flammeus sandwichensis), a short-eared owl native only to the Hawaiian archipelago that frequents grassy terrain — a dual reference to Alola's Hawaiian setting. `[Bulbapedia: Rowlet design section; via WebSearch]`
- **Flavor text** — "It sends its feathers, which are as sharp as blades, flying in attack. Its legs are strong, so its kicks are also formidable." The feather-blade motif seeded at base form fully realizes in the final evolution Decidueye, which fires arrow quills from its wings in a tenth of a second. `[PokemonTCG.io: sm12-17 flavorText; frontmatter capture; Bulbapedia: Decidueye; via WebSearch]`
- **Typing anomaly** — Rowlet is the only starter whose secondary typing does not persist through its full evolutionary line: Grass/Flying becomes Grass/Ghost (Alolan Decidueye) or Grass/Fighting (Hisuian Decidueye). No other starter line drops its secondary type at final evolution. `[Bulbapedia: Rowlet; Pokemondb.net; via WebSearch]`

## Trivia

- **Species context** — Cosmog is National Pokedex no. 789, a Psychic-type Legendary introduced in Generation VII (Sun/Moon, 2016), known as the Nebula Pokemon. In Alola's lore it was once known only to the royal family, called "the child of the stars." `[Serebii.net Pokedex 789; WebSearch]`
- **Storyline** — Cosmog (nicknamed Nebby) is the emotional core of Sun/Moon's narrative. Lillie smuggled it out of Aether Paradise; it later evolves under stress from Lusamine's Ultra Wormhole machine into Cosmoem, then into Solgaleo (Sun/Ultra Sun) or Lunala (Moon/Ultra Moon) — making Cosmog the pre-evolutionary stage of both cover legendaries. `[Bulbapedia: Nebby; Pokemon Fandom Wiki: Nebby; via WebSearch]`
- **Flavor text** — "Even though its helpless, gaseous body can be blown away by the slightest breeze, it doesn't seem to care." Consistent with Cosmog's in-game characterization as carefree and oblivious to its own cosmic importance. `[PokemonTCG.io: sm12-99 flavorText; frontmatter capture]`
- **Mechanical** — The sole attack, Ascension, searches for a card that evolves from Cosmog (Cosmoem) and evolves it in place — a one-card accelerated evolution move reflecting the character's narrative arc of unavoidable transformation. `[PokemonTCG.io: sm12-99 oracle_text; frontmatter capture]`

## Connections

- [[62-lillie]] — Named-character-identity edge: this Cosmog IS Nebby, the specific Cosmog at the center of *Pokemon Sun and Moon*'s narrative.

## Trivia

- **Species context** — Pangoro is National Pokedex no. 675, a Fighting/Dark-type introduced in Generation VI (X/Y, 2013). It is Pancham's evolution, acquiring the Dark type at Level 32 evolution when a Dark-type Pokemon is in the player's party. The game frames this as a "bad influence" mechanic. `[Bulbapedia: Pangoro; Pokemondb.net; via WebSearch]`
- **Design** — The bamboo shoot Pangoro carries is functional: it detects minute vibrations and opponent movements through the leaf's sensitivity, providing a sensory combat advantage. The design draws from bancho (Japanese juvenile delinquent gang leaders) combined with giant panda aesthetics and bamboo symbolism from Chinese/Japanese tradition. `[Bulbapedia: Pangoro design section; via WebSearch]`
- **Flavor text** — "This rowdy Pokemon boasts great physical strength. Many Trainers are also smitten by its lively character." A deliberate tonal contrast — despite imposing bulk, Pangoro is described as having an attractive liveliness. `[PokemonTCG.io: sm12-120 flavorText; frontmatter capture]`
- **Mechanical** — This card has Light Punch (40 for 1 Colorless) and Magnum Punch (90 for 3 Colorless), a clean Fighting-archetype attack spread consistent with Pangoro's in-game role as a physical attacker (base Attack: 124 in the main series). `[PokemonTCG.io: sm12-120 oracle_text; frontmatter capture; Serebii.net Pokedex 675; via WebSearch]`

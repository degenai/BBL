## Trivia

- **Set context** — Cosmic Eclipse (SM12) was the final Sun & Moon expansion (released November 1, 2019) and the first to introduce TAG TEAM Supporter cards. Mallow & Lana is one of the TAG TEAM Supporter pairings debuting in this set. `[Pokemon.com press release; WebSearch]`
- **Lore context** — Mallow and Lana are both Trial Captains on Akala Island in Pokemon Sun and Moon. Mallow runs the Lush Jungle Grass-type trial (Totem: Lurantis); Lana runs the Brooklet Hill Water-type trial (Totem: Wishiwashi). They are close friends alongside Akala's Fire-type captain, Kiawe. `[Bulbapedia: Mallow; Bulbapedia: Lana; via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical** — The card switches the Active Pokemon to the Bench; discarding 2 cards from hand additionally heals 120 damage from the retreated Pokemon. Switch-plus-heal mirrors the thematic pairing: Mallow's restorative ingredient-gathering and Lana's Brooklet Hill waters. `[Pokemon.com TCG card database]`
- **Design** — Artist: Ken Sugimori. The color-coded pairing (green for Mallow, blue for Lana) maps directly to their type affinities (Grass and Water), making the art a visual shorthand for both trial identities in a single card. `[PokemonTCG.io; Bulbapedia: Trial Captain, via WebSearch]`

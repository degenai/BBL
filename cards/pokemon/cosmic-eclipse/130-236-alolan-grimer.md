## Trivia

- **Species context** — Alolan Grimer (National Pokedex no. 88, Alolan Form) is a Poison/Dark-type regional variant introduced in Generation VII. The Alolan government deliberately brought Grimer to Alola to address urban garbage problems; populations of approximately 100 are maintained near waste-disposal sites as a recognized public sanitation service. `[Bulbapedia: Grimer (Alolan Form); via WebSearch]`
- **Flavor text** — "There are a hundred or so of them living in Alola's waste-disposal site. They're all hard workers who eat a lot of trash." Directly encodes the state-sanctioned labor role in the species' design. `[PokemonTCG.io: sm12-130 flavorText; frontmatter capture]`
- **Design** — The green-yellow coloration (vs. Kanto Grimer's purple) results from consuming solid garbage rather than sludge; the crystalline white teeth are toxins crystallized from ingested garbage that cannot be broken down. The visual design may reference oil slicks — the rainbow sheen of oil on water — evoking the Hawaiian marine environment Alola is modeled on. `[Bulbapedia: Grimer design section; via WebSearch]`
- **Ecological contrast** — Kanto Grimer feeds on factory wastewater and faces extinction as industrial cleanup progresses; Alolan Grimer was institutionally introduced to consume solid urban waste. National Pokedex no. 88 encodes opposite ecological relationships to remediation across its two regional forms. Alolan Grimer also preys on Trubbish in Alola, an unintended consequence of the waste-disposal program that reduced Trubbish populations. `[Bulbapedia: Grimer; Bulbapedia: Trubbish; via WebSearch]`

## Trivia

- **Species context** — Pancham is National Pokedex no. 674, a Fighting-type introduced in Generation VI (X/Y, 2013) in the Kalos region. Its evolution into Pangoro at Level 32 requires a Dark-type Pokemon to be in the player's party — a party-composition gating mechanic unique to this line in the main series. `[Bulbapedia: Pancham; Pokemondb.net; via WebSearch]`
- **Design** — The Dark-type party requirement encodes a narrative: Pancham gains its Dark typing at evolution via a "bad influence," framing it as a naughty child led astray. The line draws from bancho, the Japanese archetype of juvenile delinquent gang leaders. `[Bulbapedia: Pangoro design section; via WebSearch]`
- **Flavor text** — "It follows Pangoro around like a henchman. When Pancham makes a big mistake, its leaf gets taken away." The leaf is both a personality marker and, for Pangoro, a functional sensory instrument for detecting opponent movements through vibration. `[PokemonTCG.io: sm12-119 flavorText; frontmatter capture]`

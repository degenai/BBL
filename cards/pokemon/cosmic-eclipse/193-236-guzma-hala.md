## Trivia

- **Set context** — Cosmic Eclipse (SM12) was the final expansion of the Sun & Moon Series, released November 1, 2019. It introduced TAG TEAM Supporter cards for the first time in the Pokemon TCG; Guzma & Hala appeared alongside Red & Blue and Cynthia & Caitlin as the debut trio. `[Pokemon.com: TAG TEAM Supporters Take Control article]`
- **Lore context** — In Sun/Moon, Guzma was Hala's apprentice alongside Professor Kukui, aspiring to be a Trial Captain. After crushing defeats against Kukui, he rejected Hala's teachings, abandoned the Z-Ring path, and built Team Skull as a refuge for Alola's rejects and failures. Hala appears at the game's end to promise Guzma mentorship — the TAG TEAM pairing on this card is that fractured arc reunited in print. `[Bulbapedia: Guzma, via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical** — Searches deck for a Stadium; if 2 cards are discarded from hand, may additionally grab a Pokemon Tool and a Special Energy. A three-in-one tutor that served as a flexible consistency engine in Tool- and Stadium-reliant builds during its Standard-legal window. `[Pokemon.com TCG card database; WebSearch]`
- **Design** — Japanese origin: first printed in Alter Genesis before the Cosmic Eclipse English release. Artist: Ken Sugimori, Game Freak's lead character designer, whose involvement in trainer Supporter portraits is consistent across full-art printings. `[PokemonTCG.io; WebSearch]`

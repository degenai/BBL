## Trivia

- **Set context** — Druddigon is a pure Dragon-type from Generation V (Unova) and does not evolve from or into any other Pokemon — one of a small cohort of standalone fully-evolved Dragon-types in the franchise. National Pokedex no. 621. Late-game encounter in Black and White at Chargestone Cave [PokemonTCG.io sm11-157; Bulbapedia: Druddigon, via WebSearch snippet].
- **Flavor text** — "The red skin on its face is harder than rock. In narrow caves, it squares off against its enemies and charges face-first right into them." [PokemonTCG.io sm11-157]
- **Design** — Druddigon's design draws on the red-headed rock agama and European cave dragon archetypes, with gargoyle and troll references in its cold-weather immobility behavior. Its red face is functionally hardened skin, not coloration — a detail the flavor text confirms [Bulbapedia: Druddigon, via WebSearch snippet; PokemonTCG.io sm11-157].
- **Mechanical** — Druddigon's Drag Off attack (switch an opponent's Benched Pokemon into the Active slot before dealing damage) reflects its lore as a territorial ambush predator that forces confrontations in narrow spaces [PokemonTCG.io sm11-157 oracle text].

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Steenee is the Stage 1 Grass-type in the Bounsweet evolution line: Bounsweet (National Pokedex no. 761) evolves into Steenee (no. 762), which evolves into Tsareena (no. 763) when leveled up knowing Stomp. The line is exclusive to the Alola region, introduced in Generation VII [PokemonTCG.io sm11-18; Bulbapedia: Steenee, via WebSearch snippet].
- **Lore depth** — Tsareena, the line's final evolution, is the signature Pokemon of Trial Captain Mallow of Lush Jungle in the Sun and Moon anime. Mallow's Steenee evolved into Tsareena after unexpectedly learning Stomp while defending against Team Rocket — evolution triggered by protective action, not battle achievement [Bulbapedia: Mallow's Tsareena, via WebSearch snippet].
- **Flavor text** — "It's protected by its hard sepals, so it plays with bird Pokemon without worry. They peck it relentlessly, but it doesn't care." [PokemonTCG.io sm11-18]
- **Design** — Artist Kagemaru Himeno is one of the longest-tenured Pokemon TCG illustrators, active since the Base Set era. Himeno frequently depicts smaller, rounder Pokemon in warm outdoor environments [PokemonTCG.io sm11-18].

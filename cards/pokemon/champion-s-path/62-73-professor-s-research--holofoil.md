## Trivia

- **Set context** — Champion's Path (SWSH35) released September 25, 2020 in the Sword & Shield series, 80 cards total. A Galar-themed premium set tied to the gym challenge narrative of Pokemon Sword and Shield. [PokemonTCG.io: set swsh35]
- **Character lore** — Professor Magnolia is the regional Pokemon Professor of Galar, specializing in research into the Dynamax phenomenon. She gives the player the Dynamax Band at the start of the adventure and awards the Master Ball after becoming Champion. She is Sonia's grandmother; Sonia earns the title of Pokemon Professor herself by the end of the games. [WebSearch: Bulbapedia snippet; swordshield.pokemon.com snippet]
- **Card identity** — The PokemonTCG.io card name is "Professor's Research (Professor Magnolia)" — the parenthetical professor naming convention is how The Pokemon Company distinguishes which professor is depicted across this multi-version Supporter card. [PokemonTCG.io: swsh35-62]
- **Mechanical reputation** — Professor's Research is one of the most-played Supporter cards in the Pokemon TCG: discard your hand and draw 7. It has been printed with multiple professors across sets (Magnolia, Juniper, Sada, Turo) — all share identical effect text. [PokemonTCG.io: swsh35-62]
- **Design note** — Of all Sword and Shield's regional characters, Magnolia plays the least active story role — present at the opening, absent through most of the adventure, returning only after the player becomes Champion. Her granddaughter Sonia carries the narrative research thread. [WebSearch: Bulbapedia snippet]

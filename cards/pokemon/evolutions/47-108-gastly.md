## Trivia

- **Set context** — Evolutions (XY12) released November 2, 2016, timed to Pokemon's 20th anniversary. The set is an explicit tribute to the original 1999 Base Set: nearly all card art reprints original 1999 illustrations, preserving the hand-drawn energy symbols and yellow border aesthetic, while updating HP values and mechanics for modern standards. Gastly (no. 47/108) is part of this art-reprint wave. `[PokeBeach.com via WebSearch snippet; PokemonTCG.io API: xy12-47]`
- **Design** — Art is by Keiji Kinebuchi (1956-2002), the primary 3D computer graphics illustrator for the original Pokemon TCG from the late 1990s through Aquapolis. Kinebuchi illustrated over 100 cards in that era; his death in 2002 ended his direct contributions, making the Evolutions reprintings a posthumous 20th-anniversary inclusion of his work. `[Bulbapedia: Keiji Kinebuchi, via WebSearch snippet; pokemonaaah.net, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "A being that exists as a thin gas. It can topple an Indian elephant by enveloping the prey in two seconds." Text carried forward verbatim from the original 1999 Base Set printing. `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy12-47]`
- **Mechanical note** — Gastly carries 40 HP and two attacks: Little Grudge (Psychic, 1 energy) is a punish trigger — if Gastly is KO'd by attack damage on the opponent's next turn, the attacker loses an energy. Nightmare (Psychic + Colorless, 2 energy) deals 20 damage with a coin-flip Sleep infliction. Little Grudge is an unusual design for a Basic: it rewards intentional sacrifice plays, pressuring the opponent's resource management on a 40-HP card. `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy12-47]`

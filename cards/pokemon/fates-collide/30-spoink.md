## Trivia

- **Set context** — Spoink is a Psychic-type common in Fates Collide (XY10, May 2016), the same psychic-themed set as Solosis above. Spoink is a Generation III Pokémon (Hoenn, National Pokedex no. 325) making an XY-era appearance; the set pulls Pokémon across multiple generations. `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy10-30]`
- **Darkest Pokedex lore** — Spoink's Ruby Pokedex entry reads: "Spoink bounces around on its tail. The shock of its bouncing makes its heart pump. As a result, this Pokémon cannot afford to stop bouncing — if it stops, its heart will stop." The implication — that knocking out a Spoink in battle is effectively killing it, and that it must bounce even during sleep — makes it one of the most frequently cited examples of franchise-dark Pokedex lore despite its cute appearance. The flavor text on this card confirms the mechanic without the full existential weight: `"It bounces around on its tail to keep its heart pumping."` `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy10-30]` `[WebSearch: Kotaku "Spoink Will Die If It Ever Stops Bouncing"; pokemondb.net/pokedex/spoink]`
- **Flavor text** — `"It bounces around on its tail to keep its heart pumping. It carries a pearl from Clamperl on its head."` — The pearl detail connects Spoink to Clamperl (National Pokedex no. 366), whose shell is the source; a larger/better pearl amplifies Spoink's psychic power. `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy10-30]`
- **Artist** — Credited to **TOKIYA**, a Pokémon TCG illustrator active across the XY era, known for dynamic action-pose compositions. `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy10-30]`

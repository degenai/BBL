## Trivia

- **Set context** — Chaos Tower is an Uncommon Stadium card in Fates Collide (XY10, May 2, 2016), the English localization of Japan's Awakening of Psychic Kings. The set's marquee cards are Alakazam-EX, M Alakazam-EX, and Zygarde-EX across its multiple formes. No flavor text appears on the card. `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy10-94]` `[WebSearch: Pokebeach Fates Collide set overview]`
- **Name and IP — no canonical game location found** — Despite the dramatic name, "Chaos Tower" does not correspond to a named location in any mainline Pokémon game as of research date. The Japanese card name is カオスタワー (Chaos Tower), carried directly into English without change. The card was initially hypothesized to reference Hoopa Unbound (the "Mischief Pokémon," a prominent Fates Collide pull), but Hoopa's associated location in the games is Dahara City (from the Hoopa film, 2015), not a tower. No canonical connection between the Chaos Tower card and any specific game or anime location has been verified through available sources. The name appears to be an original TCG invention for the set. `[WebSearch: Bulbapedia Chaos Tower Fates Collide 94; WebSearch: Pokebeach Fates Collide set discussion]`
- **Mechanical design — dual-facing Stadium** — Chaos Tower is one of the TCG's few dual-facing Stadium cards: the player physically chooses which direction to play it before putting it on the table, and each orientation grants a different Special Condition immunity (Confused/Poisoned vs. Asleep/Paralyzed). This orientation mechanic is unique enough that the card was given a Staff Promo print for the 2016 National Championship, indicating it saw official tournament spotlight use. `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy10-94]` `[WebSearch: spankyscardshop.com Chaos Tower National Championship Promo Staff]`
- **Artist** — Illustrated by **5ban Graphics**, a studio (not a solo artist) responsible for the majority of EX, Full Art, and Stadium card illustrations in the XY and Sun & Moon eras. Their high-contrast prismatic style is the visual signature of XY-era EX cards. `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy10-94]`

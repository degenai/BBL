## Trivia

- **Set context** — Fates Collide (XY10, released May 2, 2016) is the English localization of Japan's Awakening of Psychic Kings, the third XY BREAK-era expansion. The set's marquee Pokémon are Zygarde, Alakazam-EX, and M Alakazam-EX, making this a psychic-and-order-themed set — Shuckle is a humble Bug/Rock common filling out the 124-card roster. `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy10-1]` `[WebSearch: Pokebeach Fates Collide set overview]`
- **Design / stat lore** — Shuckle (National Pokedex no. 213) holds the record for the highest base Defense and Special Defense of any Pokémon (230 each), yet its Attack and Special Attack are among the lowest in the franchise. Under theoretical optimal conditions (Ice Ball + Defense Curl + Power Trick + specific level/stat spreads), Shuckle is theoretically capable of dealing over 481 million damage in a single hit — a community-famous min-max paradox for a creature that otherwise refuses to fight. `[WebSearch: pokemondb.net/pokebase/225586; screenrant.com Shuckle highest damage]`
- **Berry Juice fermentation lore** — Shuckle stores Berries inside its vase-like shell, and those berries naturally ferment into Berry Juice over time. In Gold and Silver, a held Berry in Shuckle's possession has a 1-in-16 chance of becoming Berry Juice after each battle. In the anime episode "A Better Pill to Swallow" (GS054), an old man's Shuckle mixes multiple Berry Juices together to create elixirs with varying effects, including curing indigestion and attracting wild Pokémon. This fermentation mechanic is among the franchise's quirkiest crafting systems. `[WebSearch: Bulbapedia Berry Juice; pokemon.fandom.com GS054]`
- **Flavor text** — `"It stores Berries inside its shell. To avoid attacks, it hides beneath rocks and remains completely still."` `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy10-1]`
- **Artist** — Art on this card is credited to **sui**, a recurring Pokémon TCG illustrator known for close-up ground-level compositions. `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy10-1]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Fates Collide (XY10) released May 2, 2016 in English, the tenth main expansion of the XY Series. The set is headlined by Zygarde in multiple Formes, alongside Lugia BREAK and Mega Altaria-EX. Total: 124 cards plus 1 secret rare. Braixen appears as no. 12/124, an Uncommon Stage 1. `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy10-12, WebSearch snippet: PokeBeach Fates Collide]`

- **Pokemon context** — Braixen is National Pokedex no. 654, the Stage 1 middle evolution of the Kalos Fire starter line: Fennekin (no. 653) evolves into Braixen at level 16, which evolves into Delphox (no. 655, Fire/Psychic) at level 36. Braixen's design draws from the fennec fox and the kitsune of Japanese mythology — fox spirits associated with fire and shape-shifting into human-like forms — explaining its shift to a bipedal, anthropomorphic silhouette compared to the quadruped Fennekin. `[WebSearch snippet: Bulbapedia Braixen, Pokemon Database Braixen]`

- **Flavor text** — "When the twig is plucked from its tail, friction sets the twig alight. The flame is used to send signals to its allies." The twig is both a weapon and a communication tool, reinforcing the kitsune-mochi design thread: Japanese fox spirits associated with magical wand-like implements. `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy10-12]`

- **Design** — Illustrated by kirisAki, a Pokemon TCG artist known for dynamic action compositions. This card uses a three-quarter facing mid-shot with the flame-twig prominently featured — consistent with kirisAki's tendency toward active poses for Fire-type subjects. `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy10-12]`

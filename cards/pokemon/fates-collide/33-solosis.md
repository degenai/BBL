## Trivia

- **Set context** — Solosis appears as a common Psychic-type in Fates Collide (XY10, May 2016), a set built around psychic and dimensional themes headlined by Alakazam-EX and Zygarde. Solosis's Unova origins and psychic-cell design align neatly with the set's psychic emphasis. `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy10-33]` `[WebSearch: Pokebeach Fates Collide overview]`
- **Design lore — cell biology** — Solosis (National Pokedex no. 577) is explicitly designed after a biological cell: its body represents the nucleus, the green gelatinous surrounding fluid represents cytoplasm, and the curly yellow growth visible on its head resembles the endoplasmic reticulum. The Pokedex notes that Solosis may have evolved to survive in any environment, including the vacuum of space, because the psychic fluid completely seals it off from external conditions. When struck hard, that fluid can leak away. `[WebSearch: Bulbapedia Solosis; bogleech.com/pokemon/p577solosis]`
- **Embryonic design line** — Solosis→Duosion→Reuniclus traces cell division: Solosis is a single cell, Duosion has two nuclei (referencing a dividing cell), and Reuniclus forms a cluster resembling a morula-stage embryo. The entire line may reference the morula and blastocyst stages of early embryonic development, making it one of the franchise's most explicitly biology-grounded design families. `[WebSearch: Bulbapedia Reuniclus; professorrimu.wordpress.com "Science of Multiplying Pokemon"]`
- **Flavor text** — `"They drive away attackers by unleashing psychic power. They can use telepathy to talk with others."` `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy10-33]`
- **Artist** — Credited to **match**, a Pokémon TCG illustrator whose name appears across multiple XY-era commons. `[PokemonTCG.io API: xy10-33]`

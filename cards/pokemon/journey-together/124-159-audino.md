## Trivia

- **Set context** — Audino (no. 124/159) appears in the Scarlet & Violet—Journey Together expansion (sv9), released March 28, 2025 — the ninth main Scarlet & Violet series expansion, carrying Regulation Mark H. `[PokemonTCG.io API]`
- **Design** — Audino is National Pokedex no. 531, classified as the "Hearing Pokemon." According to Ken Sugimori, Audino was designed specifically for the Black & White anime to replace Nurse Joy's Chansey at Pokemon Centers in the Unova region, mirroring Chansey's caregiver role — including the signature nurse hat. The ear-feelers function like stethoscopes, detecting heartbeats and egg-hatching readiness. `[screenrant.com: Audino Was Created to Replace Chansey; Bulbapedia: Nurse Joy's Audino]`
- **Flavor text** — "Audino's sense of hearing is superb. Not even a pebble rolling along over a mile away will escape Audino's ears." — Audino (Journey Together 124). `[PokemonTCG.io API]` The flavor foregrounds Audino's extreme auditory range rather than its caregiver identity — the hearing precision is treated as the defining biological fact.
- **Mechanical** — The Beckon attack (1 Colorless Energy) retrieves any Supporter card from the discard pile, a mechanic DigitalTQ notes first appeared on Kirlia from BREAKthrough. At 110 HP and single-retreat-cost, it functions as a pivot attacker: use Beckon on a stalled turn to recover a key Supporter, then rotate out. `[digitaltq.com Journey Together card page; PokemonTCG.io API]`
- **Design** — Illustrated by Minahamu, a TCG artist who debuted with the Surging Sparks expansion (2024). Minahamu's portfolio through 2025 spans approximately 11 cards across Surging Sparks, White Flare, Journey Together, Ascended Heroes, and Destined Rivals. `[serebii.net TCG Cardex: Minahamu]`

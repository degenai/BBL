## Trivia

- **Set context** — Guardians Rising (Sun & Moon series, released May 5, 2017) is the second Sun & Moon expansion; it introduced the island guardian Tapu Koko-GX and Tapu Lele-GX and is one of two sets to print Akala Island Trial Captains as Supporter cards. Mallow (no. 127/145, Uncommon) is the non-holo print; a Full Art version (no. 145/145) illustrated by Megumi Mizutani also exists in the same set. [Bulbapedia: Guardians Rising (TCG); PokemonTCG.io: sm2-127, sm2-145]
- **Character context** — In Pokémon Sun and Moon, Mallow is the Grass-type Trial Captain stationed at Lush Jungle on Akala Island. Her trial requires players to collect specific ingredients for her signature "Mallow Special" dish — she runs her family's restaurant in Konikoni City and forages Lush Jungle for ingredients. Her signature partner Pokémon is Tsareena (evolved from Steenee during the anime). [Bulbapedia: Mallow; Mallow's Tsareena]
- **Mechanical context** — The Supporter effect — search deck for 2 cards, place both on top in any order — is a deck-setup tool in the vein of Sylveon from Evolutions. Because it puts cards on top rather than directly to hand, it pairs with draw-one effects and top-deck manipulation. Ken Sugimori, the original character designer for the core Pokémon species, illustrated this regular-print Supporter, which is relatively uncommon for TCG cards. [PokemonTCG.io: sm2-127; Bulbapedia: Mallow (Guardians Rising 127)]
- **Akala Island trio** — Mallow is one of three Akala Island Trial Captains alongside Lana (Water-type, Brooklet Hill) and Kiawe (Fire-type, Wela Volcano Park). All three appear as Supporter cards in Guardians Rising. This card anchors the [[alola-trial-captains]] character node in the archive. [Bulbapedia: Guardians Rising (TCG)]

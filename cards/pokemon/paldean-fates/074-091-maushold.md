## Trivia

- **IP verification: Family of Four confirmed** — This card depicts Maushold's Family of Four variant (National Pokedex no. 925), the common form produced by 99% of Tandemaus evolutions at level 25. The Family of Three variant — one offspring instead of two — has a 1% evolution probability and is considered the rare form. Both variants share identical stats; the difference is purely the figure count. The four-figure count in the art confirms the Family of Four identification. `[PokemonTCG.io API: sv4pt5-74; WebSearch: Screen Rant / Gamepur Maushold variant guides]`
- **Species design** — Maushold (National Pokedex no. 925) was introduced in Pokemon Scarlet and Violet (Generation 9, 2022). It is a Stage 1 Normal-type that evolves from Tandemaus (a Couple Pokemon — always depicted as a pair) when the player levels it to 25 with an opposing Pokemon fainting in the active party. The Tandemaus-to-Maushold evolution is one of the few in-game evolutions that adds new figures to the entity rather than transforming a single creature. `[PokemonTCG.io API: evolvesFrom, subtypes fields]`
- **Artist** — KIYOTAKA OSHIYAMA (name stylized in all caps) is an anime director and art department contributor to the Pokemon anime. He entered the TCG in 2021 with Sword & Shield — Battle Styles (Rapid Strike Urshifu VMAX Alternate Art, a high-value chase card). His TCG style is characterized as epic, dynamic, "like an anime movie screenshot turned into a card." His Paldean Fates contributions include Maushold and Mabosstiff. `[Bleedingcool.com: "Pokemon Trading Card Game Artist Spotlight: KIYOTAKA OSHIYAMA"]`
- **Mechanic-flavor lock** — The Family Attack (70 damage times number of Maushold in play) and the flavor text ("When facing strong opponents, the whole group will join the fight") are in explicit design tandem: both describe the same behavior — collective escalation as a unit — in two registers. The mechanic incentivizes running multiple Maushold copies to multiply output, reinforcing the family-unit design thesis at the deck-construction level. `[PokemonTCG.io API: attacks, flavorText fields]`

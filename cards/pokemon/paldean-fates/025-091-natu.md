## Trivia

- **Set context** — Paldean Fates (sv4pt5) released 2024-01-26. Every main-set card is a shiny-colorway Pokemon variant — this is Natu's shiny TCG appearance. The set was distributed exclusively through Elite Trainer Boxes and premium collections, no traditional booster boxes. `[PokemonTCG.io API: sv4pt5 set metadata; PokeBeach: "Paldean Fates Guide" via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Species lore** — Natu is National Pokedex no. 177, introduced in Generation 2 (Gold and Silver, 1999). A Psychic/Flying type classified as the Tiny Bird Pokemon: its wings are not yet fully grown, so it hops and climbs rather than flying. Pokedex lore establishes it as always staring at something — the behavioral seed of its evolutionary line's temporal vision theme. Natu evolves into Xatu at level 25. `[PokemonTCG.io API: nationalPokedexNumbers; Bulbapedia: Natu via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Evolutionary line design** — Xatu (Natu's evolution) is said to see the past with its left eye and the future with its right, and stands motionless out of fear of its own visions. Xatu's design draws on the resplendent quetzal and Native American totem/kachina doll imagery; its English name derives from "xat" (Northwest Coast Native American totem pole type) and "Xaat" (alternate name for the Haida people). The line's temporal-sight motif maps onto pre-Columbian sun-gazing practices. `[Bulbapedia: Xatu design section via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "Because its wings aren't yet fully grown, it has to hop to get around. It is always staring at something." — This is the original Generation 2 Gold version Pokedex entry, used verbatim as the card's flavor text. The "always staring" trait is the behavioral seed of the psychic future-sight lore expanded fully in Xatu. Artist on this card: Teeziro. `[PokemonTCG.io API: flavorText, artist fields]`

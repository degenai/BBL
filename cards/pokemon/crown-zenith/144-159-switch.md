## Trivia

- **Set context** — Crown Zenith (set ID `swsh12pt5`, code CRZ) released 2023-01-20 as the final Sword & Shield-era special expansion in the West before the Scarlet & Violet transition `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh12pt5]`. The set introduced the Galarian Gallery subset of 70 alternate-art cards plus a linked-illustration feature where nine specific Galarian Gallery cards connect into one larger image `[Pokemon Company press release via gamespress.com]`.

But the same frontmatter's `reference_image_source_url` (`swsh12pt5/129_hires.png`), `artist: Toyste Beach`, and `oracle_text` ("Move a basic Energy...") all match Energy Switch no. 129 (Uncommon). The Vision section above describes the Energy Switch art. Either the Collectr CSV labelled this slot wrong, or the vision pass matched against the wrong reference image. Triviabot does not own this fix `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh12pt5-144 vs swsh12pt5-129]`.
- **Design (Energy Switch lineage)** — Energy Switch has 20 printings across Pokemon TCG history, debuting in Aquapolis (2003, art by Mikio Menjo) and recurring through Mega Evolution (2025). For the entire Sword & Shield generation — base SwSh (2020 no. 162), Silver Tempest secret rare (2022 no. 212), Crown Zenith (2023 no. 129) — the art is by Toyste Beach, a 3D-CG studio that has illustrated Pokemon TCG cards since the Boundaries Crossed era (2012) `[PokemonTCG.io API queries for name:'Energy Switch']` `[Bulbapedia: Toyste Beach via WebSearch snippet]`.
- **Design (Switch lineage)** — Plain Switch has 35 printings on record going back to Base Set (1999 no. 95, art by Keiji Kinebuchi), one of the longest-running cards in the Pokemon TCG. Crown Zenith no. 144 is illustrated by Studio Bora Inc., a recurring trainer-card studio for the late-SwSh and Scarlet & Violet generations `[PokemonTCG.io API queries for name:'Switch']`.
- **Mechanical** — Both Switch and Energy Switch are Item-class trainer cards with no per-turn play cap ("You may play any number of Item cards during your turn"). Both are currently Legal in Standard, Expanded, and Unlimited per their Crown Zenith printings; Expanded-legal reprints carry forward all earlier prints of the same card `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh12pt5-144, swsh12pt5-129]` `[Bulbapedia: Expanded format (TCG) via WebSearch snippet]`.

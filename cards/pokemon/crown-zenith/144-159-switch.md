## Trivia

- **Set context** — Crown Zenith (set ID `swsh12pt5`, code CRZ) released 2023-01-20 as the final Sword & Shield-era special expansion in the West before the Scarlet & Violet transition `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh12pt5]`. The set introduced the Galarian Gallery subset of 70 alternate-art cards plus a linked-illustration feature where nine specific Galarian Gallery cards connect into one larger image `[Pokemon Company press release via gamespress.com]`.
- **Design (Switch lineage)** — Plain Switch has 35 printings on record going back to Base Set (1999 no. 95, art by Keiji Kinebuchi), one of the longest-running cards in the Pokemon TCG. Crown Zenith no. 144 is illustrated by Studio Bora Inc., a recurring trainer-card studio for the late-SwSh and Scarlet & Violet generations `[PokemonTCG.io API queries for name:'Switch']`.
- **Mechanical** — Switch is an Item-class trainer card with no per-turn play cap ("You may play any number of Item cards during your turn"). Currently Legal in Standard, Expanded, and Unlimited per the Crown Zenith printing `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh12pt5-144]` `[Bulbapedia: Expanded format (TCG) via WebSearch snippet]`.

## Trivia

- **Pokedex oddity** — Victini is National Pokedex no. 494, placing it after the "final" Pokémon Arceus (493). In the Unova regional Pokedex it holds the number 000 — the only non-glitch Pokémon ever assigned that designation. This was a deliberate design choice making Victini the mythical "zero" that precedes all Unova starters. `[WebSearch snippet: pokemongohub.net "Why is Victini before Snivy"; Bulbapedia Victini]`
- **Victory Pokémon and V-create** — Victini's species classification is "Victory Pokémon," and its signature move V-create is the highest base-power Fire-type move in the game (base power 180). Under normal gameplay Victini cannot learn V-create by leveling up; the move was originally distributed only via event download tied to the 2011 films. `[WebSearch snippet: Bulbapedia Victini; Wikipedia Pokémon Movie Black/White]`
- **2011 dual-film tie-in** — Victini starred in a pair of simultaneously released theatrical films: Pokémon the Movie: White — Victini and Zekrom and Pokémon the Movie: Black — Victini and Reshiram (July 16, 2011, directed by Kunihiko Yuyama). The Japanese theatrical run grossed over $57 million combined. A promotional Victini distributed for the films carried V-create, Searing Shot, Fusion Flare, and Fusion Bolt — moves otherwise unobtainable on the species. `[WebSearch snippet: Wikipedia Pokémon Movie Black/White; pokemon.com movie page]`
- **Flavor text** — "When it shares the infinite energy it creates, that being's entire body will be overflowing with power." `[PokemonTCG.io API swsh8-36]`

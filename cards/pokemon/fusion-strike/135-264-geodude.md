## Trivia

- **Original 151** — Geodude (National Pokedex no. 74) is a Generation I Rock/Ground-type introduced in 1996. Its official species classification is "Rock Pokémon." A Kanto staple and one of the most commonly encountered wild Pokémon in the early routes of Kanto's cave system, often alongside Zubat in Mt. Moon and Rock Tunnel. `[WebSearch snippet: Bulbapedia Geodude; pokemondb.net Geodude]`
- **Kanto vs. Alolan form** — This print is the standard Kanto form (gray body, Rock/Ground typing). The Alolan regional variant introduced in Generation VII (Sun and Moon, 2016) is Rock/Electric, has a slate-gray body with magnetic black eyebrows and hair-like iron sand, and three-fingered hands rather than five. Vision pass confirmed this print shows no magnetic markings or dark coloration, consistent with Kanto form. `[WebSearch snippet: Bulbapedia Geodude Alolan; pokemondb.net Geodude]`
- **Evolution line** — Geodude evolves into Graveler at level 25, which evolves into Golem when traded. Golem is a Rock/Ground-type (Kanto) and one of the few trade-only evolutions in the original 151. `[WebSearch snippet: pokemondb.net Geodude]`
- **Flavor text** — "It uses both hands to climb precipitous cliffs. People who see it in action have been known to take up bouldering." `[PokemonTCG.io API swsh8-135]`

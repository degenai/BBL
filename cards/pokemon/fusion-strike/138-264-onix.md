## Trivia

- **Original 151** — Onix (National Pokedex no. 95) is a Generation I Rock/Ground-type introduced in 1996 with the original Pokémon Red and Blue. At 28'10" (8.8m) tall and 463 lbs (210.0 kg), it was the tallest Rock-type Pokémon at the time of its introduction. `[WebSearch snippet: Bulbapedia Onix; pokemondb.net Onix]`
- **Brock's signature** — Brock, the Pewter City Gym Leader and one of the most prominent recurring characters in the Pokémon anime, received Onix from his father Flint on his 10th birthday. Onix was Brock's first and most powerful Pokémon throughout the early series. Brock eventually lent Onix to his younger brother Forrest, who evolved it into Steelix. `[WebSearch snippet: pokemon.fandom.com/wiki/Onix]`
- **Trade evolution** — Onix evolves into Steelix (Steel/Ground, no. 208, introduced Generation II) when traded while holding a Metal Coat. Pokedex lore states an Onix that lives roughly 100 years will develop a diamond-like internal composition over time, eventually becoming Steelix. `[WebSearch snippet: Bulbapedia Steelix; pokemondb.net Steelix]`
- **Flavor text** — "It rapidly bores through the ground at 50 mph by squirming and twisting its massive, rugged body." `[PokemonTCG.io API swsh8-138]`

## Trivia

- **Original 151** — Mankey (National Pokedex no. 56) is a Generation I Fighting-type introduced in 1996. Its official species classification is "Pig Monkey Pokémon." The species design blends primate anatomy with a pig-like snout — the raised stick in this card's art is consistent with the flavor text theme of perpetual agitation and reactive aggression. `[WebSearch snippet: pokemondb.net Mankey; Bulbapedia Mankey]`
- **Evolution line** — Mankey evolves into Primeape at level 28. In Generation IX (Scarlet and Violet, 2022), Primeape received a third-stage evolution: Annihilape, a Fighting/Ghost dual-type. To trigger the evolution, Primeape must use the move Rage Fist (learned at level 35) exactly 20 times, then gain a level. The Ghost typing was narratively grounded in Primeape's Sun Pokédex entry, which states Primeape can die of rage — Annihilape literalizes that as a spirit. `[WebSearch snippet: Bulbapedia Annihilape; dexerto.com Annihilape evolution guide]`
- **Flavor text** — "If one gets angry, all the others around it will get angry, so silence is a rare visitor in a troop of Mankey." `[PokemonTCG.io API swsh8-133]`

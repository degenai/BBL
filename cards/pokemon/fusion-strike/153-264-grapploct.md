## Trivia

- **Design concept** — Grapploct (National Pokedex no. 853) is categorized as the "Jujitsu Pokémon." Its design blends octopus anatomy with jujitsu and luchador fighter motifs: the yellow face marking resembles a masked wrestler's design, tentacles function as both arms and legs, and one tentacle wraps around its midsection like a belt. Its body is described as pure muscle. `[WebSearch snippet: Bulbapedia Grapploct; pokemon.fandom.com/wiki/Grapploct]`
- **Version exclusivity** — Grapploct is obtainable only in Pokémon Sword in the base games; it is not available in Pokémon Shield without trading. It is a Galar-region species introduced in Generation VIII (Sword and Shield, 2019). `[WebSearch snippet: screenrant.com "How To Find Grapploct in Sword and Shield"; pokemondb.net Grapploct]`
- **Evolution method** — Grapploct evolves from Clobbopus when leveled up while knowing the move Taunt — an unusual condition-based evolution rather than a simple level threshold or held item. `[WebSearch snippet: pokemondb.net Grapploct; imore.com Clobbopus/Grapploct guide]`
- **Flavor text** — "Searching for an opponent to test its skills against, it emerges onto land. Once the battle is over, it returns to the sea." `[PokemonTCG.io API swsh8-153]`
- **Ecological lore** — Grapploct and Golisopod living in Galar are described as natural predator-prey rivals — the winner of their brawls eats the loser. `[WebSearch snippet: pokemondb.net Grapploct Sword Pokedex entry]`

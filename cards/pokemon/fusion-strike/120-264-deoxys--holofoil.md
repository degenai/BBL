## Trivia

- **Set context** — This Holo Rare appears in Fusion Strike (SWSH8, November 12, 2021), the largest Sword and Shield expansion with 264 base-set cards. The Fusion Strike mechanic introduced a third battle style alongside Single Strike and Rapid Strike. `[PokemonTCG.io API swsh8-120; pokellector.com Fusion Strike set page]`
- **Design origin and DNA acronym** — Deoxys (National Pokedex no. 386) was introduced in Generation III (Ruby and Sapphire, 2002). Its name derives from "deoxyribonucleic acid" (DNA). A noted design Easter egg: the initial letters of its three debut formes — Defense, Normal, Attack — spell "DNA." Its central body crystal and chromosome-like silhouette reinforce the double-helix motif. `[WebSearch snippet: Bulbapedia Deoxys; pokemondb.net Deoxys]`
- **Four-forme design** — Deoxys is one of very few Pokémon with four distinct formes: Normal, Attack, Defense, and Speed. Each forme has meaningfully different base stats; Attack Forme peaks at 504 in both Attack and Special Attack. This print depicts Normal Forme — the balanced, foundational expression of the species. `[WebSearch snippet: Bulbapedia Deoxys; PokemonTCG.io API swsh8-120 tags_filter: normal-forme]`
- **Lore origin** — Deoxys originated as a space virus whose DNA mutated upon exposure to a laser beam. It appears where auroras are seen — the swirling purple-blue aurora backdrop on this Fusion Strike print directly illustrates that Pokedex entry. `[PokemonTCG.io API swsh8-120 flavor_text field]`
- **Flavor text** — "DNA from a space virus mutated and became a Pokémon. It appears where auroras are seen." `[PokemonTCG.io API swsh8-120]`

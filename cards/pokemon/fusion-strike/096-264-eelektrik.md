## Trivia

- **Tynamo line** — Eelektrik (National Pokedex no. 603) is the Stage 1 of the three-part Unova Electric eel line: Tynamo (no. 602) → Eelektrik → Eelektross (no. 604, via Thunder Stone). All three were introduced in Generation V (Black and White, 2010). `[WebSearch snippet: Bulbapedia Eelektross; pokemondb.net Eelektrik]`
- **The no-weakness line** — Eelektross, the final evolution, is one of the most cited examples in competitive Pokémon trivia: it is the only Pokémon (and its evolutionary line the only line) with zero type weaknesses under normal battle conditions. Eelektross is pure Electric-type, which normally carries a Ground-type weakness — but its sole ability, Levitate, grants full immunity to Ground moves, closing that gap entirely. The weakness can be restored only by Mold Breaker or Neutralizing Gas abilities in the opponent. `[WebSearch snippet: screenrant.com "The Only Pokémon With No Weaknesses"; Quora Eelektross Levitate]`
- **Eelektrik's own card mechanics** — Eelektrik itself does not have Levitate; that ability belongs only to Eelektross. As the middle stage, Eelektrik retains the normal Electric-type Ground weakness. This card's Thunder attack (80 damage, 20 recoil to itself) reflects the line's glass-cannon energy profile before the final evolution seals the gap. `[PokemonTCG.io API swsh8-96 oracle_text field]`
- **Flavor text** — "These Pokémon have a big appetite. When they spot their prey, they attack it and paralyze it with electricity." `[PokemonTCG.io API swsh8-96]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Black Bolt (ZSV10pt5) released July 18, 2025 as one half of the first-ever dual English Pokemon TCG set, paired with White Flare. The set is a full Unova region retrospective: every Generation V Pokemon gets an Illustration Rare card, and the master set total exceeds 1,100 cards. Pansage appears here as card no. 4 of 86 in the printed set total. `[PokemonTCG.io API: zsv10pt5-4]` `[tcg.pokemon.com: Black Bolt expansion overview]`
- **Design** — Pansage (National Pokedex no. 511, Generation V) is one of three elemental monkey Pokemon designed around the three wise monkeys concept: Pansage (speak no evil, Grass), Pansear (hear no evil, Fire), Panpour (see no evil, Water). The trio was also designed as early-game type-advantage tools keyed to Unova's first gym, which has three different leaders specializing in each of those elements. `[WebSearch snippet via GamesRadar / Serebii Pokedex 511]`
- **Flavor text** — "This Pokemon dwells deep in the forest. Eating a leaf from its head whisks weariness away as if by magic." `[PokemonTCG.io API: zsv10pt5-4]`
- **Design** — Art by kurumitsu. The card's two attacks — Collect (draw a card, 1 Colorless) and Scratch (30 damage, 2 Colorless) — are entry-level moves consistent with Pansage's role as a Common support starter. Regulation mark I places it in current Standard format legal rotation. `[PokemonTCG.io API: zsv10pt5-4]`

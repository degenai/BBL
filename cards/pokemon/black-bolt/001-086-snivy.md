## Trivia

- **Set context** — This card is from Scarlet & Violet: Black Bolt (SV11B), a 2025 Japan-exclusive expansion dedicated entirely to all 156 Generation V (Unova) Pokemon. Released June 6, 2025 in Japan; internationally July 18, 2025. Snivy holds collector number 001/086 — the opening position of the 86-card main set, consistent with its role as Unova's Grass-type starter. [Sports Card Investor: Snivy 2025 Japanese Scarlet & Violet Black Bolt; PokeGuardian: SV11B Black Bolt main set list]
- **Design history** — The Snivy-Servine-Serperior line was designed with Western European aristocracy as its primary cultural reference. Ken Sugimori cited Lady Oscar from The Rose of Versailles as a direct influence. Serperior's chest bears a fleur-de-lis motif, and its sunlight-amplifying ability may reference Louis XIV of France, the "Sun King." The line's culmination Serperior is the official "Regal Pokemon" (National Pokedex no. 497) and sources note it may incorporate design cues from Chinese and Japanese imperial serpent figures — a loong-regality resonance that runs beneath the dominant French-aristocracy frame. [Bulbapedia: Serperior (Pokemon); Veekun: Serperior no. 497]
- **Flavor text** — "It is very intelligent and calm. Being exposed to lots of sunlight makes its movements swifter." This photo-kinetic biology (sunlight powering movement speed) is the canonical Snivy species register and directly informs the line's "Sun King" design thread. [PokemonTCG.io: zsv10pt5-1]
- **Diamond Legendz Zodiac grid** — Snivy anchors the **Wood Snake** cell of the 12-animal Wuxing matrix. Grass-type (Wuxing Wood) + fully serpentine body plan (Snake zodiac animal) is the dual-predicate placement logic. Serperior's official species classification as "Regal Pokemon" and possible imperial-China/Japan design cues extend the loong-regality register beyond the European frame. [Bulbapedia: Serperior (Pokemon); Veekun: Serperior no. 497]
- **Design coordination** — Both Snivy (001/086) and Servine (002/086) in Black Bolt are illustrated by Susumu Maeya — a within-set single-artist coordinated evolution pair. Black Bolt contains at least four such adjacent-collector single-artist evolution-line pairings (Snivy/Servine by Maeya; Pawniard/Bisharp by Oku; Dwebble/Crustle by Tetsu Kayama; Rufflet/Braviary by Kedamahadaitai Yawarakai). [PokemonTCG.io: zsv10pt5-1 and zsv10pt5-2 artist fields]

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Amoonguss (011/086) is an Uncommon card from Scarlet & Violet Black Bolt (set ID: zsv10pt5), released July 18, 2025. Black Bolt is a 86-card main-set print (172 total with secret rares) in the Scarlet & Violet series, regulation mark I. Artist: Kurata So `[PokemonTCG.io API: zsv10pt5-11]`
- **IP verified** — National Pokedex no. 591. Amoonguss is a Grass/Poison-type Stage 1 Pokemon introduced in Generation V (Unova, Black/White). It evolves from Foongus at level 39 `[PokemonTCG.io API: zsv10pt5-11]`
- **Poke Ball mimicry** — Amoonguss's arm caps are patterned to resemble Poke Balls — the same deception mechanic used by Foongus (its pre-evolution) to lure prey and trainers before releasing poison spores. The flavor text states: `"It lures prey close by dancing and waving its arm caps, which resemble Poké Balls, in a swaying motion."` In Black and White, Foongus physically appears on the field disguised as item balls, a trick shared with Voltorb and Electrode from Gen I `[PokemonTCG.io API: zsv10pt5-11 (flavor text); Bulbapedia: Foongus; StrategyWiki, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Competitive identity** — Amoonguss is one of the most consistent VGC (Video Game Championships) and doubles support Pokemon across Gen V through Scarlet & Violet. Its defining role: Spore (100% accuracy sleep move) + Rage Powder (redirect opponent's moves to Amoonguss) + Regenerator ability (heals 1/3 HP on switch-out). Its low Speed stat (base 30) makes it a natural Trick Room partner. Serebii and Smogon consistently rate it as a top-tier doubles support piece `[Serebii: Amoonguss potw-xy/591; Smogon: Amoonguss SM; game8.co Sword and Shield build guide, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **This card's Dangerous Reaction** — The Dangerous Reaction attack (Colorless, 30+) deals 120 additional damage if the opponent's Active Pokemon has a Special Condition — a direct mechanical nod to Amoonguss's role as a status-infliction support piece. Seed Bomb (Grass + Colorless, 60) is the reliable secondary attack `[PokemonTCG.io API: zsv10pt5-11]`

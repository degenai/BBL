## Trivia

- **Design history — Daruma doll and Bodhidharma** — Darumaka (National Pokedex no. 554) is directly based on the Japanese Daruma doll, a folk-art object depicting Bodhidharma, the 5th-century Chinese Buddhist monk who founded Chan Buddhism. Bodhidharma is said to have meditated facing a wall at Shaolin Temple for nine years until his legs atrophied — the origin of the doll's limbless, roly-poly form. Darumaka's round self-righting body mirrors the daruma doll's tip-and-return design, a symbol of perseverance. [Game Rant: Pokemon Darumaka lore explained; Lava Cut Content: Gen 5 Historia (Ken Sugimori Nintendo Dream interview)]
- **Fire type — the daruma kuyō ceremony** — Darumaka's Fire typing is specifically linked to the daruma kuyō, the Japanese end-of-year ceremony in which old daruma dolls are returned to their originating temples and ceremonially burned in large bonfires. The fire inside Darumaka's body (active when burning; quiescent when dimmed) maps this ritual flame-and-rest cycle onto biology. Internal temperature at rest: approximately 600 degrees Celsius. [Game Rant: Pokemon Darumaka lore explained; JapanObjects.com: What are Daruma?]
- **Flavor text** — "When its internal fire is burning, it cannot calm down and it runs around. When the fire diminishes, it falls asleep." The fire-activity/sleep alternation directly encodes the daruma kuyō burning cycle as a living creature's energy state. [PokemonTCG.io: zsv10pt5-13]
- **Set context** — From Scarlet & Violet: Black Bolt (SV11B), the 2025 Japan-exclusive Unova retrospective covering all 156 Generation V Pokemon (released June 6, 2025 in Japan). Darumaka is a Generation V original, making Black Bolt its era of origin for this TCG retrospective treatment. [PokeGuardian: SV11B Black Bolt main set list]
- **Diamond Legendz Zodiac grid** — Darumaka anchors the **Fire Monkey** cell of the 12-animal Wuxing matrix. Fire-type (Wuxing Fire element) + the evolved form Darmanitan's acrobatic, kinetic "Blazing Pokemon" energy maps to the Monkey zodiac animal's quick-witted, energetic character. The Bodhidharma design origin adds a second layer: Bodhidharma is a Chinese Buddhist patriarch, giving this Fire Monkey cell an explicitly East-Asian cross-cultural design ancestry the zodiac grid can honor alongside the Fire-type placement logic. [Bulbapedia: Darumaka (Pokemon); Game Rant: Pokemon Darumaka lore explained]

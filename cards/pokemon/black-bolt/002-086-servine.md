## Trivia

- **Set context** — Servine (002/086) occupies the second position in Scarlet & Violet: Black Bolt (SV11B), the 2025 Japan-exclusive Unova-retrospective expansion (released June 6, 2025 in Japan). The sequential placement directly mirrors the species' evolution order: Snivy evolves into Servine at level 17, placing them in locked adjacent-collector positions at the set's open. [PokeGuardian: SV11B Black Bolt main set list; PokemonTCG.io: zsv10pt5-2]
- **Design history** — Servine is the Stage 1 middle form of the Snivy-Serperior line, designed by Ken Sugimori around Western European aristocracy — Lady Oscar from The Rose of Versailles cited as a direct influence. Within the line, Servine marks the temperament-to-combat-style transition: Snivy's register is intelligent calm and photo-kinetic speed; Servine's register is sliding-evasion and vine-whip strike; Serperior's is regal authority and basilisk-gaze petrification. [Bulbapedia: Serperior (Pokemon), citing Sugimori]
- **Flavor text** — "It moves along the ground as if sliding. Its swift movements befuddle its foes, and it then attacks with a vine whip." The sliding gait and vine-whip attack together define the species' mid-stage elegance-as-combat-style register. [PokemonTCG.io: zsv10pt5-2]
- **Diamond Legendz Zodiac grid** — Servine co-anchors the **Wood Snake** cell of the 12-animal Wuxing matrix alongside Snivy. Grass-type (Wuxing Wood) + serpentine body plan (Snake zodiac animal) is the dual-predicate placement logic shared across the entire evolutionary line. Serperior's official "Regal Pokemon" classification and possible Chinese/Japanese imperial-serpent design cues extend the loong-regality dimension. [Bulbapedia: Serperior (Pokemon); Veekun: Serperior no. 497]
- **Design coordination** — Servine (002/086) and Snivy (001/086) in Black Bolt are both illustrated by Susumu Maeya — a within-set single-artist coordinated evolution pair occupying sequential collector numbers, one of at least four such pairings documented in the Black Bolt set. [PokemonTCG.io: zsv10pt5-1 and zsv10pt5-2 artist fields]

## Trivia

- **Second print in corpus** — This Darkness Ablaze version (2020, art by Mina Nakai) is the second Pansage print in inventory. The earlier BUS-12 (Burning Shadows, 2017, art by Shigenori Negishi) carries different flavor text ("It shares the leaf on its head with weary-looking Pokemon. These leaves are known to relieve stress.") and a single-attack layout (Vine Whip only). This version has a two-attack layout (Seed Bomb + Bite). Two different flavor-text windows into the same species. `[PokemonTCG.io API (swsh3-6) and (sm3-12)]`
- **Set context** — Pansage is National Pokedex no. 511, the Grass-type member of the elemental monkey trio (Pansage/Pansear/Panpour) introduced in Generation V (Unova, Pokemon Black and White, 2010). The trio were designed as early-game Grass/Fire/Water coverage options for players who picked the "wrong" starter — each available only in one version of the paired games. `[PokemonTCG.io API; Bulbapedia: Pansage via WebSearch]`
- **Flavor text** — "It's good at finding berries and gathers them from all over. It's kind enough to share them with friends." `[PokemonTCG.io API flavor_text field (swsh3-6)]`

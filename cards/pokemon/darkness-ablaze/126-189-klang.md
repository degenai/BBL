## Trivia

- **Set context** — Klang is National Pokedex no. 600, the Stage 1 mid-form of the Klink evolution line: Klink (no. 599, evolves at level 38 into Klang) -> Klang (no. 600) -> Klinklang (no. 601, evolves at level 49). Introduced in Generation 5 (Unova) in Pokemon Black and White (2010/2011), the Klink line was among the first Pokemon designed as purely mechanical entities rather than animals with metallic traits. `[pokemondb.net: Klang Pokedex entry; Bulbapedia: Klang, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "When Klang goes all out, the minigear links up perfectly with the outer part of the big gear, and this Pokemon's rotation speed increases sharply." `[pokemon.com TCG database: Darkness Ablaze 126]` The text mechanically describes the same gear-linkage visible in the art: the smaller minigear aligned with the larger gear's outer ring, framing mechanical efficiency as an in-battle power escalation.
- **Design** — Darkness Ablaze no. 126/189, Stage 1, Steel-type, 90 HP, Uncommon, illustrated by NC Empire (released 2020-08-14). NC Empire is a studio illustrator with a large SWSH-era catalog; credited across multiple Steel-type and mechanical-Pokemon cards. Darkness Ablaze (SWSH3) is themed around Charizard VMAX and Eternatus VMAX as its marquee pulls — Klang is an uncommon Steel-type filler. `[pokemon.com TCG database; Serebii: NC Empire artist index, via WebSearch snippet]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Darkness Ablaze (SWSH3) released August 14, 2020. Cubchoo (National Pokedex no. 613) appears as a Water-type common at no. 48/189. Ice-types are consistently classified as Water-type in the Pokemon TCG; the card's Water typing maps to mainline Ice-type `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-48]`.
- **Species context** — Cubchoo is an Ice-type introduced in Pokemon Black & White (Gen 5, Unova, 2010). It evolves into Beartic at level 37. The design was created by a Game Freak staff member who specifically wanted to build a Pokemon around the concept of freezing snot — the mucus mechanic was the origin point, not an add-on `[WebSearch: bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Cubchoo_(Pokemon); WebSearch: pokemaniacal.com/2011/08/11/cubchoo-and-beartic/]`.
- **Design mechanics** — Cubchoo's nasal mucus is a built-in health indicator: when healthy, the snot is thick and sticky and Ice-type moves are stronger; when unhealthy, it becomes watery or absent and moves weaken accordingly. The blue drip visible in the card art signals a healthy specimen `[WebSearch: bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Cubchoo_(Pokemon)]`.
- **Flavor text** — "When this Pokemon is in good health, its snot becomes thicker and stickier. It will smear its snot on anyone it doesn't like." The flavor text reframes the hygiene marker as both diagnostic (health signal) and social (applied as deliberate unpleasantness toward disliked individuals) `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-48]`.

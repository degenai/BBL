## Trivia

- **Design — Pokemon GO debut** — Meltan (National Pokedex no. 808) is a Mythical Steel-type and the first Pokemon ever to debut in Pokemon GO before any mainline series game. Series director Junichi Masuda worked with Niantic to introduce it as a deliberate cross-platform bridge — revealed as a surprise substitution during the September 22, 2018 Community Day, kept secret even from most within the company. Its evolution Melmetal can only be obtained in Let's Go, Pikachu/Eevee by transferring from GO, making the Meltan line one of the franchise's most explicit cross-game dependency designs. `[WebSearch snippet: Wikipedia Meltan; WebSearch snippet: pokemonletsgo.pokemon.com]`
- **Flavor text** — "It dissolves and eats metal. Circulating liquid metal within its body is how it generates energy." — the hex-nut head is the only solid structural feature; the body is fully liquid metal fed by consuming and metabolizing iron and steel from its environment. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-129]`
- **Set context — Common-rarity Mythical** — Meltan appears in Darkness Ablaze as a Common-rarity card (129/189). Mythical Pokemon in the TCG are typically distributed as promotional or restricted prints; a Common-rarity Mythical in a standard set expansion is unusual. The card's low attack values (Headbutt 10, Beam 20) mark it as a setup piece for Melmetal rather than a standalone threat. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-129]`

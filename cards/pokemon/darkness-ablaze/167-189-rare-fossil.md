## Trivia

- **Set context** — Rare Fossil was released in Darkness Ablaze (August 14, 2020), the third Sword & Shield series expansion. It originated in the Japanese Infinity Zone expansion (June 2020) and was also reprinted in Japanese Shiny Star V. `[Bulbapedia: Rare Fossil (Darkness Ablaze 167), via WebSearch; TCGCodex: DAA 167, via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical lineage** — Rare Fossil plays as an Item card but fields as a 70-HP Basic Colorless Pokemon on the bench — a mechanic descending from the original Fossil card from the Base Set era. It holds the highest HP total among Fossil Item cards printed up through Darkness Ablaze, making it slightly more durable as a bench placeholder than its predecessors. `[Pojo.com: Rare Fossil review, via WebSearch; Bulbapedia: Rare Fossil DAA 167, via WebSearch]`
- **Design** — Illustrated by 5ban Graphics. The stone specimen shows multiple embedded fossil impressions (trilobite-like arthropod, spiral shell, fish-like form) representing the variety of fossil-type Pokemon the card can support. The display-specimen framing — lit from above, mounted against a soft gradient — echoes museum natural history presentation aesthetic. `[Frontmatter artist field]`

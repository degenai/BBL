## Trivia

- **Set context** — Darkness Ablaze (SWSH03) is the third Sword & Shield era expansion, released August 14, 2020. The set centers on VMAX Gigantamax forms and draws directly from the SwSh main story, making Rose's Supporter inclusion a narrative tie-in to the game's central antagonist. `[PokemonTCG.io API; Bulbapedia via WebSearch]`
- **Character lore** — Chairman Rose is founder and president of Macro Cosmos, the largest conglomerate in Galar, and simultaneous chairman of the Galar Pokemon League. He personally sponsored Leon's championship run and Bede's Gym Challenge entry. His plan to summon Eternatus — an alien Pokemon whose energy catastrophe (the Darkest Day) nearly destroyed Galar 3,000 years prior — constitutes the SwSh main-story climax. He surrenders himself to authorities after the player and Leon capture Eternatus. `[Bulbapedia/Rose via WebSearch; Villains Wiki/Chairman Rose via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical flavor** — Oracle text: "Attach up to 2 basic Energy cards from your discard pile to 1 of your Pokemon VMAX. If you attached any Energy cards in this way, discard your hand." The effect mirrors Rose's in-game role as an energy-industry magnate willing to sacrifice his political standing to fuel his plan. No flavor text is printed on this card. `[PokemonTCG.io API]`
- **Supporting cast** — Oleana is Rose's Vice President and personal secretary. She recruits Macro Cosmos league staff to block the player from reaching Rose Tower's summit, and continues voluntary service on Rose's behalf after his arrest. `[Bulbapedia/Oleana via WebSearch; Pokemon Fandom Wiki/Oleana via WebSearch]`

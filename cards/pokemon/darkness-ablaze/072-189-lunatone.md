## Trivia

- **Set context** — Darkness Ablaze (Sword & Shield series, released August 14, 2020) is the third S&S expansion, headlined by Charizard VMAX and Eternatus VMAX. Lunatone sits at card 072/189 in the printed set of 189 (201 total with secret rares). `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-72]`
- **Design — print comparison** — The prior Lunatone print (BUS-68, artist Shin Nagasawa) was a Fighting-type with the Heal Block ability requiring Solrock in play — leaning into the sun/moon pair mechanic. This DAA print (artist Miki Tanaka) is Psychic-type with standalone Future Sight and Psychic attacks, requiring no partner. Different artist, different typing, different design philosophy across two Sun & Moon-era prints. `[PokemonTCG.io API: sm3-68 vs swsh3-72]`
- **Flavor text** — "The phase of the moon apparently has some effect on its power. It's active on the night of a full moon." `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-72]`
- **Lore** — Lunatone (National Pokedex no. 337) is classified as the Meteorite Pokemon. Lore holds it was first discovered at the site of a meteor crash and may have originated from outer space. Its counterpart Solrock (no. 338) shares this meteorite origin — both are Rock/Psychic types appearing together in Hoenn. `[WebSearch snippet: Lunatone Bulbapedia via WebSearch]`

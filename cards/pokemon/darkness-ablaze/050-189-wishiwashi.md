## Trivia

- **Design / stat gimmick** — Wishiwashi (National Pokedex no. 746) holds the record for the lowest base stat total of any Pokemon in Solo Form at 175, lower even than Sunkern's previous record. Its School Form — activated by the Schooling ability when HP is above 25% and the Pokemon is at least level 20 — has a base stat total of 620, exceeding most Legendary Pokemon. This is the single largest stat-swing between forms in the franchise. `[PokemonDB: Wishiwashi; Bulbapedia: Wishiwashi, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **TCG card vs. game mechanic** — This DAA-050 card depicts and plays as Solo Form only — there is no in-card Schooling transformation ability. The TCG has separately printed Wishiwashi-GX (Burning Shadows) which does represent the School Form at GX power level. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-50; PokemonDB: Wishiwashi]`
- **Flavor text** — "Individually, they're incredibly weak. It's by gathering up into schools that they're able to confront opponents." — printed on DAA-050. The flavor text encodes the entire design thesis in one sentence: the solo fish is a known pushover; collective action is the power source. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-50]`
- **Art** — Art by sui (single lowercase name, a recurring TCG illustrator). The close-up portrait of a tiny fish alone in wide open water is a deliberate visual expression of the Solo Form's vulnerability; the large teardrop-shaped eyes amplify the sense of fragility before the transformation. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-50 artist field]`

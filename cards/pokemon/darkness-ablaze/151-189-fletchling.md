## Trivia

- **Species** — Fletchling (National Pokedex no. 661) is the Tiny Robin Pokemon, a Normal/Flying type introduced in Generation VI (Kalos). It evolves into Fletchinder at level 17, then Talonflame at level 35. The line is notable for a type shift on first evolution: Fletchling alone is Normal/Flying; Fletchinder and Talonflame are Fire/Flying. `[Pokemondb.net: Fletchling, via WebSearch; Bulbapedia: Fletchling, via WebSearch]`
- **Flavor text** — "Its body is always warm. Trainers who live in cold areas apparently sleep with it in their bed." `[PokemonTCG.io / frontmatter]`
- **Design** — Fletchling is based on the robin, a small passerine bird with a distinctive orange-red breast. The robin's cultural association with warmth in cold climates (prominent in UK winter/Christmas iconography) directly informs its flavor-text characterization as a living heat source. `[Pokemondb.net: Fletchling, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Competitive lineage** — Talonflame dominated early Generation VI competitive play via Gale Wings, which granted priority to all Flying-type moves. Its presence was so pervasive that Game Freak nerfed the ability in Generation VII, restricting priority to full-HP situations only. Fletchling is the entry point to one of the more documented balance-intervention arcs in competitive Pokemon history. `[Bulbapedia: Talonflame, via WebSearch; Pokemaniacal: Fletchling line, via WebSearch]`

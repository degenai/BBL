## Trivia

- **Set context** — Rose Tower is the headquarters of Macro Cosmos, located in Wyndon, the capital city of the Galar region. The tower stands nearly a thousand feet tall and was built on a Power Spot, which allows Pokemon to Dynamax on its roof — a structural detail that doubles as a late-game narrative device when Eternatus is summoned at the tower summit. `[Bulbapedia/Rose Tower via WebSearch; Serebii/Macro Cosmos via WebSearch]`
- **Plot role** — The tower is the final destination in SwSh's main story. The player fights through Macro Cosmos league staff recruited by Oleana before confronting Chairman Rose at the summit. The tower's elevator system connects to Hammerlocke Stadium's underground Energy Plant — the site of Eternatus's summoning. `[Bulbapedia/Rose Tower via WebSearch; Millenium.gg walkthrough via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical design** — As a Stadium card, Rose Tower lets each player draw up to 3 cards once per turn — a hand-refill effect supporting aggressive VMAX strategies. Legal in Unlimited and Expanded formats. Art by 5ban Graphics, the studio responsible for most Pokemon V/VMAX CGI-style trainer card art in the SwSh era. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-169]`

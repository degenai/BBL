## Trivia

- **Species / Pokedex** — Combusken is National Pokedex no. 256, a Fire/Fighting-type Stage 1 introduced in Generation III (Pokemon Ruby and Sapphire, 2002). It evolves from Torchic at level 16 — the point at which Fighting secondary type emerges — and into Blaziken at level 36. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-23]`
- **Design pattern** — Combusken began a three-generation streak of Fire/Fighting starter middle evolutions. Torchic (Gen III), Chimchar (Gen IV), and Tepig (Gen V) all gain Fighting secondary type at first evolution. The streak ran Generations III through V and was broken by Fennekin (Gen VI, Kalos), whose Braixen gained Psychic instead — a design departure publicly noted by the community. `[Bulbapedia: Combusken / Blaziken / Emboar, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "During a battle, the hot flame in its body increases. Its kicks have outstanding destructive power." — printed on DAA-023. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-23]`
- **Art** — Art by kodama (single lowercase name). The mid-air kick pose surrounded by swirling flame is a direct visual encoding of the flavor text's "kicks have outstanding destructive power" framing and the Fighting-type emergence at this evolutionary stage. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-23 artist field]`

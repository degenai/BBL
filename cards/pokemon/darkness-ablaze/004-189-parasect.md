## Trivia

- **Design — Cordyceps zombie** — Parasect is based on Cordyceps sinensis (tochukaso in Japanese), a real parasitic fungus that takes over insect hosts. The Pokedex states the mushroom has consumed the host entirely. "The bug is mostly dead" is verbatim flavor text on this card. The host's white blank eyes are a visual signal of zero independent will remaining. This is one of the earliest explicit body-horror designs in the franchise, introduced in Generation I (1996). `[Bulbapedia: Paras/Parasect via WebSearch; gamerant.com via WebSearch]`
- **Cultural moment** — The Last of Us (2013 game, 2023 HBO series) brought Cordyceps-zombie fungus into mainstream horror. Multiple gaming outlets noted the design parallel to Parasect after the show aired. Parasect predates that cultural moment by over 25 years. `[gamerant.com Pokemon/The Last of Us article via WebSearch]`
- **Flavor text** — "The bug is mostly dead, with the mushroom on its back having become the main body. If the mushroom comes off, the bug stops moving." `[PokemonTCG.io API flavor_text field (swsh3-4)]`
- **Set note** — Darkness Ablaze (2020), Sword and Shield series. Card no. 004/189 (Uncommon Stage 1). Art by Miki Tanaka. `[PokemonTCG.io API (swsh3-4)]`

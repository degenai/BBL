## Trivia

- **Design origin** — Mr. Rime (National Pokedex no. 866) is a Galar-exclusive Ice/Psychic-type with no Kantonian counterpart. Its design is widely cited as based on Charlie Chaplin's Tramp character: bowler hat-like upper body, dark coat, baggy silhouette, ice cane, and mustache-like marking. Galar is modeled on Great Britain; Chaplin was British-born. `[Bulbapedia: Mr. Rime, via WebSearch snippet; Pokejungle.net: Origin of Species, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "It's highly skilled at tap-dancing. It waves its cane of ice in time with its graceful movements." — printed on DAA-036. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-36]`
- **Mechanical** — The Shuffle Dance ability (swap a face-down opponent's Prize card with the top card of their deck) is a prize-manipulation mechanic unusual in the TCG. The Mad Party attack (20x damage per discard-pile Pokemon with Mad Party) ties Mr. Rime into the Mad Party archetype alongside Bunnelby and others from the Chilling Reign and Battle Styles eras. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-36 abilities + attacks]`
- **Art** — Art by Naoki Saito. The dark navy coat against icy white background is the visual inverse of the adjacent Galarian Mr. Mime card (DAA-035, Misa Tsutsui) — encoding the evolution-line's shift from defensive barrier-dance to theatrical full-performer. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-36 artist field]`

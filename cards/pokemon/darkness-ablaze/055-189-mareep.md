## Trivia

- **Second inventory print** — This is a second Mareep print in inventory, distinct from CRE-047 (Chilling Reign, trivia'd in wave 20). DAA-055 (Darkness Ablaze, August 14, 2020) predates CRE-047 (Chilling Reign, June 18, 2021) by approximately ten months — the earlier of the two SwSh-block Mareep prints in this collection. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-55; swsh6-47]`
- **Different artist, different art** — DAA-055 is illustrated by sowsow; CRE-047 is illustrated by Miki Tanaka. The two prints use meaningfully different registers for the same species: sowsow's DAA version places Mareep in a forest/day setting with warm golden and green tones; Miki Tanaka's CRE version is a spring meadow with scattered white wildflowers and a clear sky horizon. Different illustrators, different seasons. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-55 artist; swsh6-47 artist]`
- **Different attack layout** — DAA-055 has a single attack: Static Shock (Lightning) 20. CRE-047 has two attacks: Growl (reduces opponent's damage 20) plus Static Shock (LightningColorless) 20. The CRE version's extra Growl gives it more tactical utility as a stall piece in evolution-line builds, despite sharing the same species name and flavor text verbatim. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-55; swsh6-47 attacks]`
- **Flavor text** — "Rubbing its fleece generates electricity. You'll want to pet it because it's cute, but if you use your bare hand, you'll get a painful shock." — printed on DAA-055. Same text appears on CRE-047, a consistent TCG practice of cycling established Pokedex-derived flavor text across reprints of common species. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-55]`

## Trivia

- **Trainer class history** — Bird Keeper is a recurring Trainer class that debuted in Generation I (Red & Blue, 1996), making it one of the oldest NPC classes in the franchise. The class specializes in Flying-type bird-based Pokemon. Outside of Diamond, Pearl, Platinum, and their remakes (where they appear as female), Bird Keepers are consistently depicted as young men carrying bird cages — matching this card's art. `[Bulbapedia/Bird Keeper Trainer class via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical design** — Supporter: switch the player's Active Pokemon with a Benched Pokemon; if the switch occurs, draw 3 cards. Regulation mark D places it in the Sword & Shield era Standard rotation window. Illustrated by Hideki Ishikawa. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-159]`
- **Anime appearances** — Notable Bird Keeper NPCs in the anime include Wilbur (Fly Me to the Moon episode, who raises Spearow for a mail delivery service) and Wings Alexander (Throwing in the Noctowl). In Pokemon GO, "Bird Keeper" is also an achievement medal for catching at least 10 Flying-type Pokemon. `[Bulbapedia/Bird Keeper Trainer class via WebSearch]`

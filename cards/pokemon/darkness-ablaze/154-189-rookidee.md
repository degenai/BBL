## Trivia

- **Species context** — Rookidee is National Pokedex no. 821, a pure Flying-type introduced in Generation VIII (Sword & Shield, 2019), classified as the Tiny Bird Pokemon. It evolves into Corvisquire at level 18, then Corviknight at level 38. Corviknight, the final form, is a dual Flying/Steel type that operates as Galar's official aerial taxi service — a civilian utility role unique among Pokemon final evolutions. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-154; Bulbapedia via WebSearch; PokemonDB via WebSearch]`
- **Flavor text** — "It will bravely challenge any opponent, no matter how powerful. This Pokemon benefits from every battle — even a defeat increases its strength a bit." The detail about growing stronger through defeat is a recurring Pokedex-entry theme for Rookidee across the video games. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-154]`
- **Design lineage** — Rookidee's design appears to draw inspiration from birds in the Paridae family (chickadees and tits) rather than corvids directly, though it shares the Passeriformes order with its corvid-inspired evolutions Corvisquire and Corviknight. The evolution line shifts from Paridae aesthetics to armored raven aesthetics across its three stages. Illustrated by Hitoshi Ariga, a manga artist known for the Mega Man Megamix series who also illustrates extensively for the Pokemon TCG. `[WebSearch: Bulbapedia/Rookidee design; PokemonTCG.io API]`

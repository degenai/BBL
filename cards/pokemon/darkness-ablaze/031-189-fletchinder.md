## Trivia

- **Species / Pokedex** — Fletchinder is National Pokedex no. 662, a Fire/Flying-type Stage 1 introduced in Generation VI (Pokemon X and Y, 2013). Evolves from Fletchling at level 17, and into Talonflame at level 35. All three stages are Fire/Flying — one of the few lines where the secondary type is present from the first stage. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-31; PokemonDB: Fletchinder]`
- **Competitive legacy** — The line's final evolution Talonflame dominated the Generation VI OU metagame via its Gale Wings ability, which in Gen 6 gave +1 priority to ALL Flying-type moves unconditionally. Priority STAB Brave Bird (base power 120) made Talonflame the premier revenge killer of XY OU, described by Smogon's OU Spotlight as one of the most effective revenge-killing tools in the tier. The ability was nerfed in Gen 7 (Gale Wings now requires full HP), but Talonflame's Gen 6 dominance is a well-documented piece of competitive history. `[Smogon: OU Spotlight Talonflame, via WebSearch snippet; Kotaku: The Rise and Fall of Competitive Pokemon's Most Hated Bird, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "Its speed right after takeoff already puts it in the top speed class of all bird Pokemon." — printed on DAA-031. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-31]`
- **Art** — Art by Mina Nakai. The full-dive pose through a wall of embers is a direct visual expression of the flavor text's speed emphasis and the line's fire-acceleration design motif. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-31 artist field]`

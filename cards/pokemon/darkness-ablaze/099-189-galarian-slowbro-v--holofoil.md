## Trivia

- **Set context — Galarian form origin** — Galarian Slowbro is a regional variant introduced in the Isle of Armor DLC (June 2020). Galarian Slowpoke had been eating Galarica spices, and when a Shellder clamps onto its arm (rather than its tail as in Kanto), the spice residue causes a chemical reaction producing a potent poison. The result is Poison/Psychic typing rather than Kanto Slowbro's Water/Psychic. Evolution from Galarian Slowpoke requires a Galarica Cuff, crafted from eight Galarica Twigs found on the Isle of Armor. `[WebSearch snippet: GameSpot Galarian Slowbro evolution guide]`
- **Design — the shell-cannon mechanic** — Galarian Slowbro fires poisonous liquid by grabbing the Shellder's tongue, triggering it to shoot at speeds described as rivaling the fastest known reflexes — a stark contrast to canonical Slowbro's reputation for obliviousness and slowness. The TCG attack Tripping Shot (130 damage, locks the Defending Pokemon from retreating) and Rapid-Fire Poison ability both encode this "gun-arm" combat concept. `[WebSearch snippet: pokejungle.net Galarian Slowpoke origin; PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-99]`
- **Mechanical** — As a Pokemon V (Basic V, 210 HP), Galarian Slowbro V yields 2 Prize cards when knocked out. Rapid-Fire Poison provides free passive poisoning every turn it is Active, creating ongoing pressure even when the opponent stalls retreat. Market price $1.21 as of the 2026-05-13 CSV — the highest-valued card in this trivia wave. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-99]`

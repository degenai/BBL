## Trivia

- **Species / Pokedex** — Heatran is National Pokedex no. 485, the only Fire/Steel-type Pokemon in the franchise, and a Legendary introduced in Generation IV (Pokemon Diamond and Pearl, 2006). It is also notable for being the only Legendary Pokemon with a 50/50 gender ratio — it can be male or female. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-25; Bulbapedia: Heatran, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Sinnoh lore** — Heatran is a post-game encounter at Stark Mountain — accessible only after defeating the Pokemon League. The encounter is plot-gated: the NPC Buck removes the Magma Stone from the mountain's deepest chamber, disturbing Heatran and triggering volcanic unrest. After Buck returns the stone to its proper place (following an event at the Survival Area), Heatran appears as a catchable level 70 Legendary. Without the stone, Heatran would cause the volcanic crater to erupt. `[Bulbapedia: Stark Mountain; Bulbapedia: Heatran, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "Boiling blood, like magma, circulates through its body. It makes its dwelling place in volcanic caves." — printed on DAA-025. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-25]`
- **Design** — Heatran's Steel typing is physically expressed: its body is described as iron-like metal partially melted by its own internal heat, with cross-shaped claws for digging into walls and ceilings. The cross-shaped face marking visible in the card art is a consistent canonical visual identifier across all official Heatran depictions. Art by Anesaki Dynamic (a studio/artist known for dramatic legendary Pokemon compositions). `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-25 artist field; Bulbapedia: Heatran, via WebSearch snippet]`

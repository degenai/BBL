## Trivia

- **Design — the dandy** — Dartrix is explicitly called a "dandy" in its official Sun Pokedex entry. It will pause or abandon a battle to groom itself if its feathers get dirty, and it dislikes its bangs being touched. The leaf structures framing its face and the bowtie-like leaf at its chin are visual reinforcements of this grooming-obsessed character design. This vanity is deliberately temporary: it evolves out of it upon becoming Decidueye. `[Bulbapedia: Dartrix via WebSearch; tvtropes.org Gen VII characters via WebSearch]`
- **Artist** — Illustrated by Mitsuhiro Arita — the artist who drew Base Set Charizard and Pikachu, designed the original seven energy type symbols, and has illustrated over 800 TCG cards since the prototype stage in 1996. His continued work on modern common cards (like this Sword and Shield era Dartrix) demonstrates his ongoing active role in the TCG well past the vintage era. `[billsarchive.com Arita profile via WebSearch; pokemon.com creator profile via WebSearch]`
- **Flavor text** — "It throws one knifelike feather after another at its enemies, and each one precisely strikes a weak point. These feathers are known as blade quills." `[PokemonTCG.io API flavor_text field (swsh3-12)]`
- **Set note** — Reverse Holofoil variance. Darkness Ablaze (2020) is the third Sword and Shield series expansion. Card no. 012/189 (Uncommon Stage 1). `[PokemonTCG.io API (swsh3-12)]`

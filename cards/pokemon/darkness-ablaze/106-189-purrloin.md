## Trivia

- **Set context** — Sword & Shield: Darkness Ablaze (SWSH3) is the third main Sword & Shield expansion (released 2020-08-14), centered on Generation 8 Galar-region Pokémon with Charizard VMAX as the chase card; 201 cards total, printed total 189. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3 set object]` `[Bulbapedia: Darkness Ablaze (TCG), via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Design** — Illustrated by sowsow (b. 1988), known for environmental-storytelling Pokémon art that depicts species "in their own unique environments" — debuted with Avalugg, Inkay, and Cubone in Sun & Moon: Forbidden Light; ~150 cards illustrated, including the Gengar VMAX Alt Art (Fusion Strike) and Espeon V Alt Art (Evolving Skies). The dust-shaft interior here is recognizably his "day in the life" framing. `[The Art of Pokémon: sowsow page, via WebSearch snippet]` `[Bleeding Cool: sowsow Artist Spotlight, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — *"It steals things from people just to amuse itself with their frustration. A rivalry exists between this Pokémon and Nickit."* `[PokemonTCG.io API: flavorText field]` — The Nickit framing is Galar-specific: both are Dark-type theft-archetype species; Nickit is Galar Pokédex no. 29, Purrloin is Galar no. 44 (National Pokédex no. 509). `[Serebii Galar Pokédex, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Design lineage** — Purrloin is a Generation V Dark-type that evolves into Liepard at level 20; Bulbapedia design analysis frames both as cat-burglar archetypes, with Purrloin drawing on tuxedo-cat patterning and Liepard pulling from leopards, ocelots, servals, and Savannah cats. Notable for BBL purposes as a cross-game cat-tribal bridge — strong anchor for any Discrete Lair that wants to span Pokémon feline cards into MTG cat cards. `[Bulbapedia: Purrloin (Pokémon), via WebSearch snippet]` `[Bulbapedia: Liepard (Pokémon), via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Mechanical** — Basic Darkness-type, 70 HP, Grass weakness ×2, 1-Colorless retreat, regulation mark D; legal in Unlimited and Expanded. Attacks: Dig Claws (1 Darkness for 10) and Fake Out (2 Colorless for 20, coin-flip Paralysis). TCGplayer market price ~$0.08 normal / ~$0.25 reverse holo as of 2026-05-11, confirming common-bulk status. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-106]`

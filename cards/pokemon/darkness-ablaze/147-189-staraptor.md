## Trivia

- **Species context** — Staraptor is National Pokedex no. 398, a Normal/Flying-type introduced in Generation IV (Diamond & Pearl, 2006). Final evolution of the Starly line: Starly → Staravia (level 14) → Staraptor (level 34). `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-147; Bulbapedia via WebSearch]`
- **Flavor text** — "When Staravia evolve into Staraptor, they leave the flock to live alone. They have sturdy wings." The lore of solitary post-evolution departure is consistent across the Pokedex entries for Staraptor in the video games. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-147]`
- **Competitive reputation** — Staraptor has two abilities in the video games: Intimidate (lowers opposing Attack on entry) and hidden ability Reckless (boosts recoil moves by 20%). Smogon OU analysis consistently rates Reckless as the preferred competitive ability, powering Brave Bird and Double-Edge at the cost of accumulated self-damage — directly mirrored by this card's Brave Bird attack (170 damage, 30 recoil to itself). Stealth Rock weakness and recoil accumulation are Staraptor's primary longevity constraints in competitive play. `[WebSearch: Smogon/Staraptor OU; PokemonDB via WebSearch]`

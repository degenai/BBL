## Trivia

- **Set context** — Darkness Ablaze is the 3rd Sword & Shield expansion, released August 14, 2020; 189 base-set cards (201 with secret rares), anchored by Charizard VMAX and Eternatus VMAX as the primary chase cards. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3 set metadata]`
- **Species / Pokedex** — Simipour is National Pokedex no. 516, a Water-type Stage 1 introduced in Generation V (Pokemon Black and White, 2010), evolving from Panpour via Water Stone. Stone evolution means Simipour learns no moves by level-up — move selection at the time of evolution is permanent. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-42; PokemonDB: Simipour]`
- **Design origin** — The elemental monkey trio (Pansage / Pansear / Panpour and evolutions) is widely understood to reference the Three Wise Monkeys. Panpour / Simipour maps to Mizaru (see-no-evil): Panpour's closed-eye design encodes the covered-eyes gesture. Pansear = Kikazaru (hear-no-evil), Pansage = Iwazaru (speak-no-evil). `[Bulbapedia: Elemental monkeys, via WebSearch snippet; name-rater.tumblr.com: Mizaru/Kikazaru/Iwazaru mapping, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "It prefers places with clean water. When its tuft runs low, it replenishes it by siphoning up water with its tail." — printed on DAA-042. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-42]`
- **Art** — Art by Shigenori Negishi. The seated tide-pool pose is a direct visual expression of the flavor text's clean-water habitat preference and the Mizaru see-no-evil serenity register. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-42 artist field]`

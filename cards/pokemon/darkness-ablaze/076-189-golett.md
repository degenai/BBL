## Trivia

- **Set context** — Darkness Ablaze (SWSH3) released August 14, 2020. Golett (National Pokedex no. 622) appears as a Psychic-type common at no. 76/189. Ghost-types in the mainline games are classified as Psychic-type in the TCG `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-76]`.
- **Design origin** — Golett (Ghost/Ground-type, introduced Gen 5, Unova, 2010) draws from multiple ancient-construct traditions: primarily the Golem of Jewish folklore — the "shem" parchment that animates and controls the golem maps directly to Golurk's chest seal (remove the seal, the construct rages). Secondary inspirations include Haniwa (Japanese terracotta funerary objects from the Kofun period) and the Terracotta Army of China (circa 210 BCE, clay soldiers built to protect Emperor Qin Shi Huang) `[WebSearch: bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Golett_(Pokemon); WebSearch: bogleech.com/pokemon/p622golett]`.
- **Unova connection** — Golett and Golurk's home region Unova is modeled on New York City; their Golem-of-Prague design lineage may reference that New York has the largest Jewish population of any city in the world — grounding a piece of Eastern European Jewish folklore inside a New York-mapped region `[WebSearch: bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Golurk_(Pokemon)]`.
- **Flavor text** — "They were sculpted from clay in ancient times. No one knows why, but some of them are driven to continually line up boulders." The compulsive boulder-arrangement with no stated purpose encodes the design's intentional ambiguity: the civilization that built these constructs is gone; only autonomous behavioral drives remain, now unmoored from any purpose `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh3-76]`.
- **Evolution mechanic** — Golurk runs on a mysterious internal energy (possibly perpetual motion). Removing the seal on its chest causes it to rage and level entire towns — a direct structural echo of the Golem of Prague's berserk state when the shem is removed `[WebSearch: bogleech.com/pokemon/p622golett; WebSearch: facts.net/characters/pokemon/18-facts-about-golurk]`.

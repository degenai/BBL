## Trivia

- **Location lore** — Granite Cave is a Hoenn location northwest of Dewford Town, accessible via Route 106. In Pokemon Ruby, Sapphire, and Emerald (2002/2004), Steven Stone's room is in the cave's deepest level. The player's first encounter with Steven occurs here: he receives a letter from his father (Mr. Stone, Devon Corporation president) and rewards the player with TM47 Steel Wing. `[Bulbapedia/Granite Cave via WebSearch; Thonky walkthrough via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical design** — As a Stadium card in Destined Rivals, Granite Cave reduces damage dealt to "Steven's Pokemon" (both players') by 30. The oracle text explicitly names Steven, tying the mechanical effect directly to the Hoenn Champion and his Steel-type specialty. Art by AYUMI ODASHIMA. `[PokemonTCG.io API: sv10-166]`
- **Set context** — Destined Rivals (SV10) draws from multiple Japanese source sets including Marnie and Steven starter decks, placing Granite Cave inside a Steven Stone mini-theme within the set alongside his Trainer card. Wild Pokemon found in Granite Cave in-game include Makuhita, Geodude, Zubat, Abra, and version-exclusive Sableye (Sapphire) or Mawile (Ruby). `[Bulbapedia/Granite Cave via WebSearch; PokeBeach/Destined Rivals via WebSearch]`

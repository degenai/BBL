## Trivia

- **Set context** — Destined Rivals (SV10) is the tenth Scarlet & Violet era expansion, released May 30, 2025. The English set compiles two Japanese source sets: "Glory of Team Rocket" (released in Japan April 18, 2025) and "Heat Wave Arena," along with Marnie and Steven starter deck cards. It is the first major Team Rocket-centric set since the original Team Rocket expansion (2000), featuring over 45 Team Rocket's Pokemon cards and admins Giovanni, Archer, Ariana, Petrel, and Proton. `[WebSearch: PokeBeach/Destined Rivals; JustInBasil sv10 setlist; tcgstacked.com DRT guide]`
- **Set theme** — The Japanese source set "Glory of Team Rocket" (SV10) prominently features Giovanni and Mewtwo on its booster pack artwork and contains 26+ Team Rocket-focused cards. Team Rocket's Mewtwo ex is the set's flagship chase card. `[WebSearch: JustInBasil sv10; WebSearch: Glory of Team Rocket card list]`
- **Mechanical design** — Item card with a coin-flip mechanic: heads places 2 damage counters on an opponent's Pokemon; tails places 2 on the player's own Active Pokemon. The gamble-or-self-damage design mirrors the chaotic recklessness associated with Team Rocket grunt-level operations. Illustrated by Toyste Beach. `[PokemonTCG.io API: sv10-179]`

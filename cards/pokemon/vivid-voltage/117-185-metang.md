## Trivia

- **Set context** — Vivid Voltage (SWSH4), released November 13, 2020. Metang prints as a Stage 1 Metal-type Uncommon (117/185), sitting in the middle of the Beldum-Metang-Metagross line. Regulation Mark D. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh4]`

- **Species design** — Metang is National Pokedex no. 375, introduced in Generation III (Hoenn). It forms when two Beldum fuse: a magnetic nervous system joins their brains, doubling psychokinetic output. Its design appears based on flying saucers and crabs. The name likely combines "metal" with "tang" (onomatopoeia for struck metal) or the French "etang" (pond blue, matching the teal palette). Art on this printing by Kazuma Koda. `[WebSearch snippet: Bulbapedia/Metang; pokemondb.net/pokedex/metang]`

- **Flavor text** — "It flies at high speeds around the skies. When it finds its prey, Metang takes a firm grip with its sharp claws and never lets go." `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh4-117]`

- **Champion connection** — Metagross, the final evolution, is the signature Pokemon of Steven Stone, Hoenn Pokemon League Champion across Ruby, Sapphire, Omega Ruby, and Alpha Sapphire. Steven owned multiple Metang as well (first seen stopping a group of Mawile in Granite Cave). The full line (Beldum-Metang-Metagross) is Steel/Psychic dual-type and qualifies as a pseudo-legendary with a 600 base stat total at final stage. `[WebSearch snippet: Bulbapedia/Steven Stone; Bulbapedia/Pseudo-legendary Pokemon]`

- **Mechanical note** — Stage 1 Metal-type, 100 HP, weak to Fire (x2), resistant to Grass (-30). Metal Claw (2 energy, 30 damage) and Magnetic Blast (3 energy, 60 damage). Retreat cost of 3 is above average for an Uncommon-slot Stage 1. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh4-117]`

## Trivia

- **IP note** — League Staff is a named Trainer class in Pokemon Sword & Shield per Bulbapedia. Members staff the Galar Pokemon League at Wyndon Stadium and other venues; a male and female League Staff pair is also depicted apprehending Bede in Stow-on-Side in the main game. The two figures on this card are generic representatives of the class in their canonical black-and-red livery — no specific named individuals. `[Bulbapedia: League Staff (Trainer class), via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Mechanical design** — Oracle text: "Draw 2 cards. If Wyndon Stadium is in play, draw 2 more cards." The conditional draw-4 requires the co-printed Stadium card (VIV-161) to be in play. This is the only Supporter in Vivid Voltage whose full effect is gated behind a specific Stadium, making it a deliberate within-set 2-card combo. `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-153 oracle text]`
- **Design note** — Illustrated by Emi Ando. The card sits at no. 153/185, eight positions below Wyndon Stadium at no. 161/185 in the set's Trainer block — the within-set numeric proximity is consistent with intentional thematic grouping of league-related cards. `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-153 metadata]`

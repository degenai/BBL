## Trivia

- **Set context** — Vivid Voltage is the fourth Sword & Shield series expansion, released November 13, 2020. Its headline feature was Pikachu VMAX (Gigantamax form) and the introduction of Amazing Rare cards for Legendary and Mythical Pokemon. Telescopic Sight is an uncommon trainer item in that set. `[Bulbapedia: Vivid Voltage TCG, via WebSearch; Pokemon.com: Vivid Voltage announcement, via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical note** — As a Pokemon Tool, Telescopic Sight grants +30 damage to attacks against Benched Pokemon V and Benched Pokemon-GX specifically. It rewards snipe-damage strategies that chip down high-HP V cards before they rotate to the active position — a niche but deliberate counter to the V-heavy Sword & Shield meta. `[Pojo.com: Telescopic Sight review, via WebSearch; Limitless TCG: VIV 160, via WebSearch]`
- **Design** — Illustrated by Toyste Beach, who also illustrated Urn of Vitality from Battle Styles (also in this wave). The concentric red-green-yellow rings render the scope as a colorful toy-like gadget, consistent with Vivid Voltage's high-saturation visual identity. `[Frontmatter artist field]`

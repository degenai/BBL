## Trivia

- **Set context** — Pikipek (National Pokedex no. 731) is a Normal/Flying type introduced in Generation VII (Pokemon Sun & Moon, 2016). It is Alola's early-route bird — the regional equivalent of Pidgey, Taillow, or Starly. Its evolution line is Pikipek -> Trumbeak (level 14) -> Toucannon (level 28). `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-143; Bulbapedia: Pikipek, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Design: phylogenetics reference** — Though Pikipek and Trumbeak are based on woodpeckers, the line's final evolution Toucannon is based on a Toco toucan. This reflects real-world phylogenetics: woodpeckers and toucans are both members of the order Piciformes, making the design progression a nod to actual evolutionary relationships between the two bird groups. `[Bulbapedia: Pikipek, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "It may look spindly, but its neck muscles are heavy-duty. It can peck at a tree 16 times per second!" `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-143]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — This card is from Sword & Shield — Vivid Voltage (set code swsh4), released 2020-11-13. Vivid Voltage was the fourth main Sword & Shield expansion and introduced the Amazing Rare rarity tier, featuring Legendary Pokemon (Jirachi, Raikou, Rayquaza, Zamazenta) with rainbow-foil treatments. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh4-113]`

- **Flavor text** — "What appears to be an iron helmet is actually hardened hair. This Pokemon lives for the thrill of battle." Illustrated by Shin Nagasawa. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh4-113]`

- **Design** — Galarian Perrserker (National Pokedex no. 863) is a Galar-exclusive regional evolution of Galarian Meowth, introduced in Pokemon Sword and Shield. Standard Kantonian Meowth evolves into Persian; Galarian Meowth diverges and evolves into Perrserker at level 28 — the only split evolution in the Meowth line. The design is explicitly modeled on the stereotypical image of Viking warriors: the hardened hair forms a horned helmet shape, claws extend sword-like, and the Pokedex notes Galarian Meowth's body toughened from living with "a savage, seafaring people." The name Perrserker is likely a portmanteau of "purr," "Persian," and "berserker." Pokedex lore also notes that Perrserker and Persian share "mutual disdain." `[WebSearch: Bulbapedia Perrserker entry via snippet; Newsweek Galarian forms guide]`

- **Mechanical** — Stage 1 Metal-type, 120 HP. Stealy Claws (1 Metal energy, 20 damage) flips 3 coins: on any heads, the opponent reveals their hand; each heads discards a Trainer card — a hand-disruption effect at unusually low energy cost. Claw Slash (3 energy: Metal + 2 Colorless) hits for 90 flat. Fire weakness x2; Grass resistance -30. Regulation Mark D (Sword & Shield Standard era). `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh4-113]`

## Connections

- [[galarian-regional-forms]] — Cohort member. Galarian Perrserker is the Steel-type SwSh-introduced evolution of Galarian Meowth and one of the cohort's Galar-exclusive evolved endpoints (no Kantonian counterpart; the Kantonian Meowth line evolves into Persian instead). The Viking-warrior design — hardened-hair iron helmet, claw-tipped limbs, battle-stance — is Pokedex-attested as the species' adaptation to living among Galar's "savage, seafaring people," carrying the cohort's regional-cultural-adaptation thesis in the same register as Galarian Farfetch'd's leek-warrior framing. `[Bulbapedia: Perrserker; PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-113; Pokemon Sword & Shield 2019 primary canon]`

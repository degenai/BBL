## Trivia

- **Set context** — Vivid Voltage (SWSH4), released November 13, 2020. Mightyena prints as a Stage 1 Darkness-type Uncommon (104/185), evolving from Poochyena. Regulation Mark D. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh4]`

- **Species design** — Mightyena is National Pokedex no. 262, introduced in Generation III (Hoenn). Its design is based on a hyena — possibly the brown or striped hyena, with the species' thick dorsal mane influencing the fur collar — and may also draw from breeds like the German Shepherd and Australian Kelpie. Name combines "mighty" and "hyena." Evolves from Poochyena at level 18. Art on this printing by Aya Kusube. `[WebSearch snippet: Bulbapedia/Mightyena]`

- **Flavor text** — "It chases down prey in a pack of around ten. They defeat foes with perfectly coordinated teamwork." `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh4-104]`

- **Hoenn lore** — Mightyena was the signature rank-and-file Dark-type for both Team Magma and Team Aqua throughout the Gen 3 games and the Ruby/Sapphire anime arc. Both villainous organizations fielded Poochyena/Mightyena as their standard grunt Pokemon, making the species one of the most visually associated Hoenn antagonist markers across the era. `[WebSearch snippet: Bulbapedia/Team Magma Grunt; TV Tropes/Characters/PokemonRubyAndSapphire]`

- **Mechanical note** — Stage 1 Darkness-type, 110 HP, weak to Grass (x2), 1 retreat cost. Ferocious Bellow (1 Darkness, 20 damage, -50 to opponent's next attack) and Pitch-Black Fangs (3 energy, 100 damage). Modest Uncommon role in the SWSH format. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh4-104]`

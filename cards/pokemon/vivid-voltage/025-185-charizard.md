## Trivia

- **Set context** — Charizard (no. 025/185) is a Stage 2 Rare from Vivid Voltage (SWSH4), the fourth Sword & Shield expansion, released November 13, 2020. The set contains 203 cards total (185 standard plus 18 secret rares) and introduced the first "Amazing Rare" card type. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh4-25; WebSearch: Vivid Voltage set details]`
- **Design** — Illustrated by Ryuta Fuse, who debuted in the TCG with Sun & Moon: Cosmic Eclipse. Fuse is also credited on Nessa Full Art (Vivid Voltage) and Rayquaza V Alternate Art (Evolving Skies), the latter considered one of the Sword & Shield era's landmark illustrations. Fuse's style shifted from airbrush coloring toward high-rendered cinematic output across the era. `[WebSearch: bleedingcool.com Ryuta Fuse artist spotlight; artofpkm.com/illustrators/163]`
- **Mechanical** — The Royal Blaze attack (FireFire, 100+) gains 50 additional damage per Leon card in the discard pile. With four Leon cards discarded (the maximum allowed per deck-rule), the attack hits for 300 damage from a two-energy cost. This mechanically cites the canonical pairing of Galar Champion Leon and his Gigantamax Charizard in Pokemon Sword & Shield (Game Freak, 2019) — Gigantamax Charizard is the only Gigantamax form canonically belonging to a named Champion trainer. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh4-25 oracle_text; WebSearch: pokemon.com strategy "Three Approaches to Building a Charizard Deck"; [[charizard-line]]]`
- **Flavor text** — "It spits fire that is hot enough to melt boulders. It may cause forest fires by blowing flames." Drawn from the Charizard Pokedex entry, framing the Stage 2 apex as a destructive force at landscape scale rather than as a trainer's companion. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh4-25 flavorText]`
- **Set cohort** — Within the corpus, this card completes the within-set Vivid Voltage evolutionary triptych: Charmander (no. 023/185), Charmeleon (no. 024/185), and Charizard (no. 025/185) occupy three consecutive collector numbers in SWSH4, the corpus's strongest single-set evolutionary-arc cohesion stack on the Pokemon side. `[PokemonTCG.io API set metadata; [[charizard-line]]]`

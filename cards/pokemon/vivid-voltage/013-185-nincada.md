## Trivia

- **Set context** — Nincada appears in Sword & Shield — Vivid Voltage (2020) as card no. 013/185, a Common. It is National Pokedex no. 290, a Bug/Ground-type introduced in Generation III (Hoenn). `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-13]`
- **Unique branching evolution** — Nincada is the only Pokemon in the franchise whose evolution produces two Pokemon simultaneously. At level 20, Nincada evolves into Ninjask. If the player also has an empty party slot AND a Poke Ball in their bag, the empty exoskeleton left behind becomes Shedinja — the shed husk is placed into the empty Poke Ball automatically. Shedinja IS the cocoon Nincada left. This mechanic has been in the game since Generation III and remains unique to this line. `[WebSearch: Bulbapedia: Nincada]` `[WebSearch: pokemondb.net: Nincada evolution mechanic]`
- **Shedinja's design** — Shedinja has exactly 1 HP and the ability Wonder Guard, which makes it immune to all damage that is not super effective. It can only be damaged by Fire, Flying, Rock, Ghost, or Dark-type moves. The 1 HP is lore-consistent: it is a hollow shell with no life force of its own — just a spirit peeking through. `[WebSearch: Bulbapedia: Shedinja snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — `"Because it lived almost entirely underground, it is nearly blind. It uses its antennae instead."` `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-13]`

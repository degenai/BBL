## Trivia

- **Set context** — Charmeleon appears in Sword & Shield — Vivid Voltage (2020) as card no. 024/185, an Uncommon. It is National Pokedex no. 5, mid-stage of the Charmander line (Charmander → Charmeleon → Charizard), one of the original three Kanto starter evolution lines. `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-24]`
- **Corpus context: 3rd print** — This is the third Charmeleon in the corpus (after BUS-19 and at least one other). The VIV-024 art by SATOSHI NAKAI presents a close mid-shot framing with dark fiery background and strong rim lighting — a more aggressive, close-action register compared to other prints. The artist credential is rendered in all-caps in Scryfall/PokemonTCG.io data, which is how this artist's name appears on printed cards. `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-24]`
- **Flavor text** — `"It has a barbaric nature. In battle, it whips its fiery tail around and slashes away with sharp claws."` `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-24]`
- **Card mechanic: Raging Flames** — The oracle text includes Raging Flames (FireFire, 60 damage) with the cost of discarding the top 3 cards of your deck. This self-destructive attack mechanic is consistent with Charmeleon's lore of barely-controlled aggression — it hits hard but costs resources unpredictably. `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-24]`
- **Charizard-line character node** — This card is wired to the charizard-line character node (established in wave 32). Charmeleon's defining lore note: its Pokedex entries across generations describe it as difficult to control, attacking anyone who provokes it regardless of trainer bond — behavior that carries into Charizard's famous disobedience arc in the anime. `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-24]`

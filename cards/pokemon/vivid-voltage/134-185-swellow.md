## Trivia

- **Set context** — Swellow (National Pokedex no. 277) is a Normal/Flying type introduced in Generation III (Pokemon Ruby & Sapphire, 2002). It evolves from Taillow at level 22. Taillow appears on Routes 104, 115, 116, and in Petalburg Woods in the Hoenn region. `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-134; Bulbapedia: Swellow, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Competitive note** — In mainline competitive Pokemon, Swellow historically sits in the NU (Never Used) tier but is considered one of that tier's stronger offensive threats, primarily due to base 125 Speed. Its Guts ability enables powerful Facade usage under status conditions; Scrappy + STAB Boomburst makes it a capable Choice Specs cleaner. This competitive history is not reflected in the TCG card's simpler Quick Attack / Energy Assist kit. `[Bulbapedia: Swellow competitive, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "It dives at a steep angle as soon as it spots its prey. It catches its prey with sharp claws." `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-134]`

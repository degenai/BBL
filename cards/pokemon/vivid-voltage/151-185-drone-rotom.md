## Trivia

- **Set context** — Drone Rotom (no. 151/185) is an Item Trainer card from Vivid Voltage (SWSH4), released November 13, 2020 in English (first appeared in the Japanese Amazing Volt Tackle expansion). The base species, Rotom, is National Pokedex no. 479, an Electric/Ghost-type introduced in Generation IV (Pokemon Diamond and Pearl, Game Freak, 2006). `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh4-151; WebSearch: Bulbapedia Drone Rotom (Vivid Voltage 151) via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Species lore** — Rotom's signature mechanic across the mainline games is possessing household appliances to take five alternate battling forms: Heat Rotom (oven, Electric/Fire), Wash Rotom (washing machine, Electric/Water), Frost Rotom (refrigerator, Electric/Ice), Fan Rotom (fan, Electric/Flying), and Mow Rotom (lawnmower, Electric/Grass). The Drone form in Sword & Shield is a sixth, non-battling role: Rotom inhabit drones to film live Gym Challenge and League matches at Wyndon Stadium. Five Drone Rotom also expelled colored smoke to form the World Coronation Series logo in the sky above Wyndon in the Pokemon Journeys anime. `[WebSearch: pokemondb.net/pokedex/rotom; WebSearch: Bulbapedia Rotom Drone via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Mechanical** — Effect: force the opponent to reveal their hand; if they do, look at the top card of their opponent's deck. Multiple Item cards may be played per turn. The opponent chooses whether to accept the hand-reveal; if they decline, the top-deck-peek is forfeited. This gives the card a soft information-bluff angle. No flavor text printed on this card. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh4-151 rules]`
- **Design pattern** — Rotom's TCG history includes multiple non-Pokemon Trainer-card appearances (Rotom Phone, Rotom Dex, Drone Rotom) that use the species as a technology-interface framing device rather than as a battling Pokemon. This pattern — a Ghost-type species that possesses machines, itself instrumentalized as an in-game utility Item or Supporter — is a consistent design choice across the Sun & Moon and Sword & Shield TCG eras. `[WebSearch: Bulbapedia Rotom Pokedex; WebSearch: Bulbapedia Drone Rotom (Vivid Voltage 151) via WebSearch snippet]`

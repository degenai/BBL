## Trivia

- **Set context** — Regirock appears in Sword & Shield — Vivid Voltage (2020) as card no. 089/185, a Holo Rare. It is National Pokedex no. 377, a Legendary Rock-type introduced in Generation III (Hoenn). `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-89]`
- **Regi cycle: 3+2 structure** — The Legendary Giants form a structured cycle across three generations. The original trio — Regirock, Regice, and Registeel — were introduced in Gen III (Hoenn) as constructs said to have been created by Regigigas (their dormant master, introduced in Gen IV Sinnoh). In Gen VIII's Crown Tundra DLC, two additions joined the cycle: Regieleki (Electric) and Regidrago (Dragon), each locked behind a split-decision ruins where players choose one. Collecting all five in the party activates a Max Raid Battle against Regigigas. `[WebSearch: Regi cycle, Bulbapedia: Legendary giants snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — `"The same rocks that form its body have been found in ground layers around the world."` `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-89]`
- **Design signature** — The seven-dot cross/H-pattern on Regirock's torso face is shared across all Regi-family members (each with a unique dot arrangement), functioning as a canonical visual identifier for the family. This card's Vision section correctly identifies the dots as "a defining visual marker of the Regi-family." `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-89]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Bea is one of five Galar Gym Leader Supporters printed in Vivid Voltage (SWSH4), alongside Allister (no. 146), Leon (no. 154), Nessa (no. 157), and Opal (no. 158). `[Bulbapedia: Vivid Voltage, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Set context** — The Vivid Voltage 147 print art specifically depicts the interior of **Stow-on-Side Stadium** as it appears in Pokemon Sword — Bea's home gym in the Galar league. `[Bulbapedia: Bea (Vivid Voltage 147), via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Version asymmetry** — Bea is Sword-exclusive: in Pokemon Shield, the Stow-on-Side slot is held by Allister (Ghost-type) instead. Stow-on-Side is the headline version-asymmetric gym pair in the Galar league. `[Bulbapedia: Bea / Stow-on-Side Stadium]`
- **Character** — In Sword/Shield game canon, Bea is an expert of Galar karate, characterized by a stoic, controlled demeanor; her gym team is headed by a Gigantamax Machamp. `[Bulbapedia: Bea]`
- **Design** — Regular print art by **take**; the Full Art and Rainbow Rare prints in the same set are by Hideki Ishikawa, a common SWSH-era Trainer card split. `[Bulbapedia: Bea (Vivid Voltage 147)]`
- **Mechanical** — Oracle text: "Discard the top 5 cards of your deck, and attach any Energy cards you discarded in this way to your Benched Fighting Pokemon in any way you like." A milling-payoff Supporter built around bench-acceleration Fighting decks. `[pokemon.com TCG database]`
- **Flavor text** — None printed. SWSH-era Pokemon TCG Trainer Supporter cards carry mechanical text only. `[pokemon.com TCG database]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Vivid Voltage (SWSH4), released November 13, 2020. Shuppet prints as a Basic Psychic-type Common (067/185), Regulation Mark D. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh4]`

- **Species design** — Shuppet is National Pokedex no. 353, introduced in Generation III (Hoenn). Its design draws from the teru teru bozu — a traditional Japanese paper ghost-doll hung to pray for sunny weather — and from tsukumogami mythology (inanimate objects gaining spirit). Its Japanese name Kagebouzu combines kage (shadow) and teru teru bozu. Art on this printing by Miki Tanaka. `[WebSearch snippet: Bulbapedia/Shuppet; Bulbanews "On the Origin of Species: Shuppet and Banette"]`

- **Flavor text** — "There's a proverb that says, 'Shun the house where Shuppet gather in the growing dusk.'" `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh4-67]`

- **Lore — negative emotion feeder** — Canonically Shuppet feeds on negative emotions: grudges, jealousy, sorrow. It gathers under the eaves of houses where people harbor long resentments. Its evolution Banette originates from a discarded plush doll filled with hatred, animated by a spirit seeking revenge on the child who threw it away — making the Shuppet-to-Banette pipeline one of the more coherent two-stage lore progressions in Gen 3. `[WebSearch snippet: Bulbapedia/Banette; bogleech.com/pokemon/p353shuppet]`

- **Mechanical note** — Basic Psychic-type, 60 HP, weak to Darkness (x2), resistant to Fighting (-30). Single attack: Haunt (1 Psychic Energy) — places 1 damage counter on the opponent's Active Pokemon. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh4-67]`

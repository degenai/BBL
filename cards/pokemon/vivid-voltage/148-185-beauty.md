## Trivia

- **Set context** — Beauty (no. 148/185) is a Supporter Trainer card from Vivid Voltage (SWSH4), released November 13, 2020. The Beauty Trainer class debuted in Generation I (Pokemon Red and Blue, 1998), making it one of the original NPC trainer archetypes that has carried through every mainline generation since the franchise's launch. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh4-148; WebSearch: Bulbapedia Beauty (Trainer class) via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Design** — Illustrated by kirisAki, a freelance illustrator who has contributed to the Pokemon TCG since 2013 after studying at Tokyo Designer Gakuin. kirisAki has illustrated multiple Supporter and Trainer cards across the Sun & Moon and Sword & Shield eras. `[WebSearch: artofpkm.com/illustrators/158 kirisAki; WebSearch: Bulbapedia kirisAki via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Mechanical** — Effect: draw 2 cards. This card may be played on the first player's first turn, explicitly overriding the standard Supporter restriction. This first-turn exception gives Beauty mild tempo value in decks that need early draw access. No flavor text printed on this card. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh4-148 rules]`
- **Trainer class lore** — In Pokemon Sword and Shield, Beauties can team with a Rail Staff NPC to form the "Daring Couple" double-battle Trainer class. The Beauty class is canonically described as favoring Pokemon associated with beauty and cuteness, and is noted for paying out a high prize amount when defeated — a design convention carried from Generation I. `[WebSearch: Bulbapedia Beauty (Trainer class) via WebSearch snippet; WebSearch: serebii.net Beauty NPC Sword Shield]`

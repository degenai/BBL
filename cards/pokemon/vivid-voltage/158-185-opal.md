## Trivia

- **Set context** — Opal is one of five Galar Gym Leader Supporters printed in Vivid Voltage (SWSH4): Allister (no. 146), Bea (no. 147), Leon (no. 154), Nessa (no. 157), Opal (no. 158). `[Bulbapedia: Vivid Voltage, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Character** — Opal is the Fairy-type Gym Leader of Ballonlea Stadium in both Sword and Shield (no version split). `[Bulbapedia: Opal, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Character** — Game canon: Opal is 88 years old, the oldest Gym Leader in the series, and inherited Ballonlea Gym from her mother roughly 70 years before the events of Sword/Shield. `[Bulbapedia: Opal]`
- **Worldbuilding** — Opal's uniform carries the logo of **HIJIKI OKA**, a Galar textile company that sponsors Ballonlea Gym (and her successor Bede); her Japanese name *Popura* derives from poplin fabric, consistent with the textile-brand connection. `[Bulbapedia: Opal / List of clothing in Pokemon Sword and Shield]`
- **Character** — In-game, Opal is nicknamed "the wizard" for her ability to read and predict opponents' moves; she ultimately passes Ballonlea Gym leadership to Bede. `[Bulbapedia: Opal]`
- **Design** — Regular print art by **take**, the same artist credited on the rest of the Vivid Voltage Galar Gym Leader Supporters. `[Bulbapedia: Opal (Vivid Voltage 158)]`
- **Mechanical** — Oracle text: "Flip 2 coins. Search your deck for a number of cards up to the number of heads, put them into your hand, and shuffle your deck." A coin-flip tutor Supporter that returns 0-2 cards on average. `[pokemon.com TCG database]`
- **Flavor text** — None printed. `[pokemon.com TCG database]`

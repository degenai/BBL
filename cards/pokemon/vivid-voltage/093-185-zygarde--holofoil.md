## Trivia

- **Set context** — Vivid Voltage (swsh4) is the fourth Sword & Shield series expansion, released November 13, 2020. Its marquee card is Pikachu VMAX (no. 044/185). `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4 set metadata]`
- **Lore: Aura Trio** — Zygarde (National Pokedex no. 718, Generation VI) is the third member of the Aura Trio alongside Xerneas (Life) and Yveltal (Death). The trio is inspired by Norse Yggdrasil mythology: Xerneas the deer guards the soil, Yveltal the bird guards the upper tree, Zygarde the serpent guards the underground. Zygarde's role is Order — capable of reversing the excesses of both Life and Death auras to restore balance. `[Bulbapedia: Aura trio, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Design: three formes** — Zygarde has three formes: 10% Forme (dog-like, ~10% of cells gathered), 50% Forme (serpentine, depicted on this card), and Complete Forme (triggered when HP drops below half with Power Construct ability — stated to be more powerful than either Xerneas or Yveltal). `[Bulbapedia: Zygarde, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "Born when all of Zygarde's cells have been gathered together, it uses force to neutralize those who harm the ecosystem." `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-93]`
- **Mechanical note** — Core Avenger does 80 base + 80 bonus damage when the player has more Prize cards remaining than the opponent — a come-from-behind mechanic that thematically mirrors Zygarde's lore function of intervening when the ecosystem's balance has been disrupted. `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-93 oracle text]`

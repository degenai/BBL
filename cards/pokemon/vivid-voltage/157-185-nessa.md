## Trivia

- **Set context** — Nessa is one of five Galar Gym Leader Supporters in Vivid Voltage (SWSH4): Allister (no. 146), Bea (no. 147), Leon (no. 154), Nessa (no. 157), Opal (no. 158). `[Bulbapedia: Vivid Voltage, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Character** — Nessa is the Water-type Gym Leader of Hulbury Stadium in both Sword and Shield (no version split). `[Bulbapedia: Nessa, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Character** — In Galar canon she works a dual career: competitive Gym Leader and professional fashion model — popular enough in both that Bulbapedia notes many fans assume the "Gym Leader Nessa" and "model Nessa" are different people. `[Bulbapedia: Nessa]`
- **Worldbuilding** — Her father is a Hulbury fisher and her mother works the town marketplace, framing the Water-type specialty as inherited maritime trade context; she met Sonia (Magnolia's granddaughter) during their Gym Challenge run. `[Bulbapedia: Nessa]`
- **Design** — Regular print art by **take**, matching the artist on the rest of the Vivid Voltage Galar Gym Leader Supporters. `[Bulbapedia: Nessa (Vivid Voltage 157)]`
- **Mechanical** — Oracle text: "Put up to 4 in any combination of Water Pokemon and Water Energy cards from your discard pile into your hand." A heavy-duty Water-recursion Supporter, well regarded in SWSH-era single-Prize Water archetypes. `[pokemon.com TCG database]`
- **Flavor text** — None printed. `[pokemon.com TCG database]`

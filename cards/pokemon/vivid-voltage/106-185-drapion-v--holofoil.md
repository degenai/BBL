## Trivia

- **Set context** — Drapion (National Pokedex no. 452) is a dual Poison/Dark type introduced in Generation IV (Diamond, Pearl, Platinum, 2006). It evolves from Skorupi at level 40. Notably, Skorupi is Poison/Bug type and Drapion loses the Bug typing on evolution — a rare type-loss upon evolving. `[Bulbapedia: Drapion, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Mechanical note** — Hazardous Claws (4 energy, 130 damage) inflicts both Paralyzed and Poisoned on the opponent's Active Pokemon while discarding 2 Energy from Drapion V. Dual-status application in a single attack is mechanically notable; the energy discard is the cost of the status-stack. The V-rule raises the stakes — an opponent takes 2 Prize cards when Drapion V is knocked out. `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-106 oracle text]`
- **Design note** — Illustrated by Eske Yoshinob, a frequent contributor to V-rank cards in the Sword & Shield era. Drapion V has no VMAX or VSTAR counterpart in Vivid Voltage — it is a standalone V, without an evolution within the V-rank framework. `[PokemonTCG.io: swsh4-106 metadata]`

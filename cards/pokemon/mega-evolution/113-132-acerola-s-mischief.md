## Trivia

- **Set context** — Acerola's Mischief (113/132) is the regular Uncommon print from the English Mega Evolution set (ME01), which released September 26, 2025. It originated in the Japanese Mega Symphonia expansion (August 2025). Three print variants exist in this set: the regular Uncommon (113/132, art by Yuu Nishida), a Full Art ultra rare (165/132), and a Special Illustration Rare (183/132, art by sui). `[TCGPlayer: ME01 Mega Evolution, PokeBeach: Mega Brave/Mega Symphonia announcement]`
- **Lore context** — Acerola is the Trial Captain of the abandoned Thrifty Megamart on Ula'ula Island in Pokemon Sun and Moon, specializing in Ghost-type Pokemon. Her trial challenges players to photograph Gastly, Haunter, Gengar, and Totem Mimikyu in the decrepit supermarket. She later becomes an Elite Four member, where she describes wanting to bring "the old royal touch" to the Pokemon League. `[Bulbapedia: Acerola, WebSearch snippet]`
- **Mechanical** — The Supporter effect is usable only when the opponent has 2 or fewer Prize cards remaining — a last-ditch protection move that prevents all damage and effects to one Pokemon for a turn. The late-game restriction is thematically consistent with the 'Mischief' framing: a sudden reversal deployed when cornered. No flavor text on this card. `[PokemonTCG.io API]`

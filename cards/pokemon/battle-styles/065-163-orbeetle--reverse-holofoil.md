## Trivia

- **Species** — Orbeetle (National Pokedex no. 826) is a dual Bug/Psychic type introduced in Generation VIII (Galar). It is the Stage 2 final evolution of Blipbug > Dottler > Orbeetle, reaching final form at level 30 from Dottler. `[Pokemondb.net: Orbeetle, via WebSearch; Bulbapedia: Orbeetle, via WebSearch]`
- **Flavor text** — "It emits psychic energy to observe and study what's around it — and what's around it can include things over six miles away." `[PokemonTCG.io / frontmatter]`
- **Design lineage** — The spotted red shell draws from the ladybug (Coccinella septempunctata); the oversized orb and mind-control abilities reference UFO/alien sci-fi archetypes. Orbeetle's Gigantamax form enlarges the orb into an explicit flying-saucer silhouette — the alien-ship reference is present in official Pokedex text, not just fan interpretation. `[Pokemon GO Hub: Orbeetle Lore and Design, via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical note** — Evomancy (one Energy = one Stage 2 directly to Bench) is among the most strategically irregular attacks printed in the Sword & Shield era. It bypasses evolution chains entirely, enabling combo lines that wouldn't otherwise be legal. Pokemon.com featured it at Battle Styles launch in a Triple Play deckbuilding showcase. Illustrated by Mizue. `[Pokemon.com: Triple Play featuring Orbeetle BST, via WebSearch]`

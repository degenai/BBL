## Trivia

- **Set context** — Urn of Vitality is a Single Strike Item card from Battle Styles (March 19, 2021). Its effect — shuffle up to 2 Single Strike Energy from discard pile back into deck — directly addresses the resource-drain problem Single Strike decks face when their special energy gets discarded. `[Frontmatter oracle_text; Bulbapedia: Battle Styles TCG, via WebSearch]`
- **Ecosystem role** — Alongside Single Strike Scroll of Scorn (which grants a damage-scaling attack) and Single Strike Urshifu VMAX (the set's flagship attacker), Urn of Vitality forms part of a co-designed three-piece support suite. Scroll is the offensive tool; Urn is the recovery engine. `[Pokemon.com: Battle Styles Single Strike announcement, via WebSearch]`
- **Design** — Illustrated by Toyste Beach, who also illustrated Telescopic Sight from Vivid Voltage (also in this inventory wave). The urn's dark clay body, chiaroscuro rim-lighting, and Single Strike emblem badge blend ceremonial vessel aesthetics with the battle style's red-black color language. `[Frontmatter artist field]`

## Connections

- [[133-163-single-strike-scroll-of-scorn]] — Designer-coordinated Single Strike support pair. Both cards are Battle Styles items printed within the same expansion (March 19, 2021) and stamped with the Single Strike emblem; together with Single Strike Urshifu VMAX they form a co-designed three-piece support suite. Urn of Vitality is the recovery engine (shuffles up to 2 Single Strike Energy from the discard pile back into the deck); Scroll of Scorn is the offensive tool (grants the Furious Anger attack, scaling with damage taken on the user). The complementary mechanical roles — energy-attrition recovery vs punishment-scaling aggression — are the Single Strike battle style's full thesis distilled into two item cards. The pair is also iconographically twinned: both depict a single ceremonial object (urn, scroll) glowing with internal energy against a moody warm-and-dark background, no figure, both with the red-and-black Single Strike logo as the badge on the artifact. `[Pokemon.com: Battle Styles Single Strike and Rapid Strike announcement, via WebSearch]` `[Bulbapedia: Battle Styles TCG, via WebSearch]`

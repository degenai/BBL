## Trivia

- **Set context** — Cubone (069/163) is a Common card from Sword & Shield Battle Styles (set ID: swsh5), released March 19, 2021. Battle Styles introduced the Single Strike and Rapid Strike mechanics, themed around two contrasting fighting philosophies. Cubone is printed at 069/163 in the main set of 163 cards (total 183 with secret rares), with regulation mark E `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh5-69]`
- **IP verified** — National Pokedex no. 104. Cubone is a Basic Fighting-type that evolves into Marowak. This card's attacks are Beat (Fighting, 10 damage) and Headbutt (Fighting + Colorless, 30 damage). Artist: You Iribi `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh5-69]`
- **Flavor text and lore** — `"It wears its mother's skull on its head, so no one knows what its bare face looks like. However, it's clear that it's always crying."` The Pokedex consistently frames Cubone as "the Lonely Pokemon" — it is said to have lost its mother and mourns through nightly cries; on full moon nights the skull vibrates and emits a mournful sound. Cubone is said to evolve into Marowak when it has come to terms with its grief, at which point the skull fuses to its head `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh5-69 (flavor text); Bulbapedia: Cubone, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Lore paradox** — Despite Pokedex entries stating Cubone wears its dead mother's skull, Cubone eggs hatch with the skull already in place, creating an unresolved continuity loop in official lore that has persisted across generations `[Bulbapedia: Cubone; Quora discussion, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Competitive profile** — Cubone is a Basic Pokemon with 70 HP, Grass weakness, single Colorless retreat. It functions as an evolution setup piece for Marowak lines, not as a standalone attacker. Unremarkable by itself; its emotional resonance in lore far outweighs its competitive utility `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh5-69]`

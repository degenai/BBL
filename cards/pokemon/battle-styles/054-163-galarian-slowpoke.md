## Trivia

- **Set context** — Battle Styles (SWSH05) was released March 19, 2021, the fifth Sword & Shield TCG expansion. It was the first set to introduce Single Strike and Rapid Strike card mechanics — a new design layer distinguishing Pokemon by fighting style within the same set. The base set contains 163 cards. `[Pokellector Battle Styles set list, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Regional variant lore** — Galarian Slowpoke is a pure Psychic-type, unlike standard Slowpoke (Water/Psychic). The type difference traces directly to its diet: Galarian Slowpoke eat Galarica seeds from a plant native only to Galar, and over generations built up spice particles in their bodies. This changed their appearance and behavior without requiring the Shellder-bite that gives standard Slowpoke its Water typing. `[Official Pokemon Sword & Shield expansion pass site, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "Because Galarian Slowpoke eat the seeds of a plant that grows only in Galar, their tails have developed a spicy flavor." (Pokemon Shield Pokedex entry.) The Galarica plant and its seeds are also an in-game Galar culinary staple; Galarica Twigs (harvested on the Isle of Armor DLC map) are the evolution item material for both Galarian Slowbro (Galarica Cuff, 8 twigs) and Galarian Slowking (Galarica Wreath, 15 twigs). `[Card frontmatter flavor_text; Isle of Armor guides, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Attack name** — The attack "Tantailizing" is a pun on "tantalizing" and "tail," a direct nod to the spicy-tail flavor lore. Common rarity, illustrated by Ryota Murayama. `[Card frontmatter oracle_text / artist]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Oleana serves as Vice President of Macro Cosmos and personal secretary to Chairman Rose in Pokemon Sword and Shield. She is the Phase 1 gate-boss: the player must defeat her in Rose Tower before confronting Rose, making her the mechanical and narrative obstacle between player and the corporation's head [Bulbapedia: Oleana, via WebSearch snippet].
- **Design** — Rebel Clash (swsh2) was released as the second Sword and Shield Series set with Regulation Mark D. Artist Yusuke Ohmura also illustrated the Sonia Supporter in the same set (no. 167/192) — both are Galar-region Supporting figures rendered by the same hand [PokemonTCG.io swsh2-163, swsh2-167].
- **Mechanical** — Oleana's Supporter effect (discard 2 cards, put a Trainer from the opponent's hand on the bottom of their deck) mechanically enacts her narrative role: she buries threats before they surface. The discard cost mirrors her in-game willingness to expend resources in service of Rose's agenda [PokemonTCG.io swsh2-163 oracle text].
- **Lore depth** — Before joining Rose, Oleana was a researcher who invented the Dynamax Band. Rose recruited her after discovering her talent. Her Gigantamax Garbodor during the Rose Tower battle is the game's signal that loyalty to Macro Cosmos produces monstrous results [Bulbapedia: Oleana, via WebSearch snippet].

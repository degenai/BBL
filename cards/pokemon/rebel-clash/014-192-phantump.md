## Trivia

- **Art distinction vs CSE-93** — The corpus holds two Phantump prints: this one (RBC-014, artist sui, Sword & Shield era 2020) and the Cosmic Eclipse print (CSE-93, artist miki kudo, Sun & Moon era 2019). The CSE print frames the species in a dim, orb-lit forest with a lonely-spirit atmosphere — painterly and dark. This RBC print is distinguished by a vivid teal aura emanating from and around the creature, a glowing-energy register that reads as supernatural power rather than loneliness. Same species, same setting archetype, divergent emotional key: abandoned child-spirit vs. eerie power-source. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh2-14, sm12-93]`
- **Flavor text** — "After a lost child perished in the forest, their spirit possessed a tree stump, causing the spirit's rebirth as this Pokémon." This is the darkest origin statement among Grass-type species in the corpus — a child death is the canonical mechanism of the species' existence. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh2-14]`
- **Mechanical note** — Dark Guidance (put a Basic from discard to Bench) is a recursion ability on a 60HP 1-retreat basic. The Trevenant trade-evolution line is sometimes used in Mill and disruption strategies; Dark Guidance makes Phantump a recycling engine in those builds. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh2-14]`

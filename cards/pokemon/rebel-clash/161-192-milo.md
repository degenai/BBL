## Trivia

- **Set context** — Milo is the Grass-type Gym Leader of Turffield Stadium in both Sword and Shield, and Turffield is the first stop on Galar's league progression — the introductory gym. `[Bulbapedia: Milo, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Character** — Born in Turffield to a farmer couple; works the family farm and is shown in-game wrangling stray Wooloo with a herding Yamper. `[Bulbapedia: Milo]`
- **Character** — His battle team uses two pure-Grass Pokemon (Gossifleur, Eldegoss); his Eldegoss enters at Level 20, the highest-level ace of any first-gym Leader in the Pokemon series to that point. `[Bulbapedia: Milo / Game8 walkthrough]`
- **Design** — Regular print art by **take**; the Rebel Clash Full Art (no. 190/192) and Secret Rare versions are by Hitoshi Ariga. `[Bulbapedia: Milo (Rebel Clash 161)]`
- **Reprints** — Regular print reprinted in Champion's Path outside Asia; in Japan, reprinted in the Charizard VMAX Starter Set and as a Mirror Holofoil S-P promotional card. `[Bulbapedia: Milo (Rebel Clash 161)]`
- **Mechanical** — Oracle text: "Discard up to 2 cards from your hand, and draw 2 cards for each card you discarded in this way." A rummage / loot Supporter (quality-for-quantity); saw competitive play in single-Prize and toolbox decks during SWSH-era Standard. `[pokemon.com TCG database]`
- **Flavor text** — None printed. `[pokemon.com TCG database]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Arcanine (no. 028/192) is a Stage 1 Fire-type Rare in Rebel Clash (SWSH2), released May 1, 2020. The set features Galar-region Pokemon and returning Kanto/Johto/etc. species alongside Pokemon V and VMAX mechanics. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh2-28]`
- **"Legendary Pokemon" classification** — Arcanine's official Pokedex species designation is "Legendary Pokemon" — printed since Generation I — despite it not being a game-mechanical Legendary. The classification references Chinese guardian lion statues (known as shishi or "foo dogs"), which were said in legend to ward off evil spirits and possessed extraordinary speed and power. Arcanine's design is more specifically linked to the Shisa, a Ryukyuan guardian figure from Okinawa resembling a lion-dog hybrid. `[Bulbapedia: Arcanine (Pokemon) via WebSearch snippet; Screen Rant: Pokemon Theory Arcanine Legendary via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Legendary bird engraving** — In the original Pokemon anime, Arcanine appeared in an engraving alongside Articuno, Zapdos, and Moltres — the three canonical Legendary birds — suggesting it may have been considered for the Legendary trio before the final design was locked. This placement is a persistent piece of Gen 1 production history. `[Screen Rant: Pokemon Theory Arcanine Legendary via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "The sight of it running over 6,200 miles in a single day and night has captivated many people." The 6,200-mile distance approximates the straight-line distance from Tokyo to London. The physically impossible endurance figure is a recurring Pokedex motif for Arcanine across generations, grounding its "Legendary" classification in superlative but unverifiable claims. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh2-28]`
- **Mechanical note** — The Warming Up Ability grants +100 HP when Burning Scarf is attached, pushing Arcanine from 130 to 230 HP as a Stage 1 — a substantial total. The Item-dependency mechanic rewards mono-Fire or Arcanine-focused builds willing to commit a tool slot. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh2-28]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Sonia is the granddaughter and former assistant of Professor Magnolia, and a childhood friend of Champion Leon. She investigates the ancient Darkest Day event alongside the player throughout Sword and Shield, eventually being named Galar's new Pokemon Professor by Magnolia at the game's conclusion — the first character in the main series to earn the Professor title on-screen [Bulbapedia: Sonia, via WebSearch snippet].
- **Design** — Rebel Clash (swsh2) is the second Sword and Shield Series set, Regulation Mark D. Artist Yusuke Ohmura also illustrated Oleana (no. 163/192) in the same set — both are Galar Supporter cards from the same Sword and Shield narrative block [PokemonTCG.io swsh2-167, swsh2-163].
- **Mechanical** — Sonia's Supporter effect (search deck for up to 2 Basic Pokemon or up to 2 basic Energy) is a flexible search with no discard cost — a research tool that delivers without conditions, matching her role as the player's collaborating researcher [PokemonTCG.io swsh2-167 oracle text].
- **Lore depth** — Sonia's investigation of the Darkest Day places her in direct collision with Macro Cosmos: Rose's scheme to reactivate the Darkest Day for energy production is the secret her research uncovers. She is the daughter of Magnolia's legacy confronting the machinery Rose built from that same academic tradition [Bulbapedia: Sonia, Macro Cosmos, via WebSearch snippet].

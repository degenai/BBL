## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Rolycoly (National Pokedex no. 837) is a pure Rock-type Pokemon introduced in Generation VIII (Pokemon Sword & Shield, 2019), set in the UK-modeled Galar region. The Galar region's design draws heavily from Great Britain's industrial period, including its coal-mining economy `[WebSearch: PokéJungle "Rolycoly, Carkol, Coalossal — Origin of Species"; WebSearch: Bulbapedia Rolycoly snippet]`.
- **Flavor text** — "Most of its body has the same composition as coal. Fittingly, this Pokémon was first discovered in coal mines about 400 years ago." `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh2-105 flavor_text]` The 400-year timeframe aligns with the early stages of the British Industrial Revolution and coal-mining boom, grounding the species in Galar's period setting.
- **Design** — Rolycoly's Carkol evolution is designed to evoke a minecart: a dark-bodied cart-like form that rolls along mine tracks. Coalossal (the final evolution, National Pokedex no. 839) is a towering coal furnace-form, completing the mine-to-furnace arc of the line's industrial metaphor `[WebSearch: PokéJungle "Rolycoly, Carkol, Coalossal — Origin of Species"; retbit.com "Rolycoly evolution redesign"]`.
- **Mechanical note** — Rolycoly's hidden ability is Flash Fire (activated on Carkol and Coalossal), making the line relevant in competitive doubles formats as a Fire absorber for its partner `[WebSearch: Bulbapedia Carkol snippet]`.

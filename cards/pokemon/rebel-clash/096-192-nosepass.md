## Trivia

- **Species** — Nosepass (National Pokedex no. 299) is the Compass Pokemon, a pure Rock-type introduced in Generation III (Hoenn). It evolves into Probopass when leveled up in a special magnetic field. `[Pokemondb.net: Nosepass, via WebSearch; Bulbapedia: Nosepass, via WebSearch]`
- **Flavor text** — "It hunts without twitching a muscle by pulling in its prey with powerful magnetism. But sometimes it pulls natural enemies in close." `[PokemonTCG.io / frontmatter]`
- **Design** — Nosepass is modeled on two sources: the Moai statues of Easter Island (the block-body silhouette and stoic forward posture) and a compass (the oversized red nose always points magnetic north). Its name combines "nose" and "compass" (or "north"). `[Bulbapedia: Nosepass, via WebSearch snippet; Pokemondb.net: Nosepass, via WebSearch]`
- **Evolution lore** — Probopass, Nosepass's evolution, completes the compass metaphor: it grows three detachable Mini-Noses that point East, South, and West, forming a full compass rose with the main nose pointing North. Probopass also gains the Steel type, making the pair unusual for having different type compositions across the evolutionary line. `[Pokemondb.net: Probopass, via WebSearch; Bulbapedia: Probopass, via WebSearch]`

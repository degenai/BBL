## Trivia

- **Set context** — Rebel Clash (SWSH2) released May 1, 2020, the second expansion of the Sword & Shield TCG series. The set introduced a wave of Galar regional Pokemon including several V and VMAX cards; Galarian Meowth appears as a common at no. 126/192 `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh2]`.
- **Species context** — Galarian Meowth shares National Pokedex no. 52 with Kanto Meowth but is a distinct regional form introduced in Pokemon Sword & Shield (2019). Where Kanto Meowth evolves into Persian, Galarian Meowth evolves into Perrserker — a Galar-exclusive Steel-type Viking Pokemon — beginning at level 28 `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh2-126; WebSearch: pokemondb.net/pokedex/perrserker]`.
- **Design origin** — The design lineage draws on the Norwegian Forest Cat, a breed historically carried aboard Viking longships for pest control. Perrserker's name combines "purr" and "berserker." The seafaring-hardship origin explains the Steel typing: repeated exposure to iron tools and weapons over generations `[WebSearch: pokejungle.net "Galarian Meowth and Perrserker - Origin of Species"]`.
- **Flavor text** — "Living with a savage, seafaring people has toughened this Pokemon's body so much that parts of it have turned to iron." The flavor text is the card's only in-game prose and directly encodes the Viking-cat origin myth `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh2-126]`.
- **Mechanical note** — The Ability "Evolution Roar" requires discarding 2 cards to fetch Galarian Perrserker from the deck — an unusually steep cost for a basic common, emphasizing the toughness-through-sacrifice theme the design line runs on `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh2-126]`.

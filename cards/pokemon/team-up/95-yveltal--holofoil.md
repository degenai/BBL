## Trivia

- **Set context** — Yveltal is the version mascot of Pokemon Y and a member of the Kalos Aura Trio alongside Xerneas (Life) and Zygarde (Order). Its role is destruction and death: when its lifespan ends, it absorbs the life energy of all surrounding creatures before cocooning into dormancy. National Pokedex no. 717 [PokemonTCG.io sm9-95; Bulbapedia: Yveltal, via WebSearch snippet].
- **Design** — Yveltal's body silhouette — wings spread, tail fanned — forms a recognizable Y-shape, directly mapping to the letter of its version title. Its name also begins with Y. The design includes both avian and draconic traits: beak-like snout, blue eyes, horns, and broad wings over a dark red-and-black body [Bulbapedia: Yveltal, via WebSearch snippet].
- **Flavor text** — "When its life comes to an end, it absorbs the life energy of every living thing and turns into a cocoon once more." [PokemonTCG.io sm9-95]
- **Design** — Yveltal is a Dark/Flying dual-type — one of the few Legendary Pokemon with the Dark type. Artist Misa Tsutsui. This Team Up (sm9) printing is a Holo Rare; Yveltal also appears as an EX and GX across earlier sets [PokemonTCG.io sm9-95].

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Alolan Grimer is the regional form of National Pokedex no. 88 (Grimer), introduced in Pokemon Sun and Moon (2016). Unlike standard Kantonian Grimer, the Alolan form was brought to Alola specifically to manage the region's excess garbage — its solid-waste diet transformed its body chemistry, producing crystalline toxin formations instead of smooth sludge. `[Bulbapedia: Grimer, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "The crystals on Grimer's body are lumps of toxins. If one falls off, lethal poisons leak out." `[PokemonTCG.io API: sm9-83]`
- **Set context** — Team Up (SM9) released February 1, 2019. Alolan Grimer appears at no. 83/181 printed as a Common with Darkness typing — TCG-accurate to its Poison/Dark dual typing in the video games. The attack "Chemical Breath" scales damage with Special Conditions on the opponent's Active Pokemon (+50 per condition), rewarding status-stacking strategies. `[PokemonTCG.io API: sm9-83]`
- **Lore note** — In recent years, environmental improvements and reduced waste availability have caused Alolan Grimer populations to decline. Alolan Grimer also preys on Trubbish and Garbodor; its introduction to the Alola ecosystem contributed to those species' population decreases in the region. `[Bulbapedia: Grimer, via WebSearch snippet]`

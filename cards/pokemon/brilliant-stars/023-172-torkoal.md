## Trivia

- **Species** — Torkoal (National Pokedex no. 324) is the Coal Pokemon, a pure Fire-type introduced in Generation III (Hoenn). It does not evolve — one of few single-stage Fire-types in the main series. `[Pokemondb.net: Torkoal, via WebSearch]`
- **Flavor text** — "It burns coal inside its shell for energy. It blows out black soot if it is endangered." `[PokemonTCG.io / frontmatter]`
- **Lore** — Torkoal communicates via expelled smoke — varying pitch, duration, and volume. The hexagonal shell vents are speculated in Pokedex lore to double as communication organs. When coal reserves run dry, it becomes immobile. `[Bulbapedia: Torkoal, via WebSearch snippet; Neoseeker: Torkoal, via WebSearch]`
- **Competitive** — Despite its defensive shell design, Torkoal's main competitive niche is as a sun setter: its Drought ability activates harsh sunlight on entry, boosting allied Fire-type moves and enabling sun-reliant strategies. Its rock-bottom base Speed (20) makes it ideal for Trick Room sun teams in VGC doubles — Eruption under sun + Trick Room is a documented high-damage opening. `[Smogon: Torkoal SS, via WebSearch; Pikalytics VGC 2026, via WebSearch]`

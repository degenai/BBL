## Trivia

- **Species** — Cherubi (National Pokedex no. 420) is the Cherry Pokemon, a pure Grass-type introduced in Generation IV (Sinnoh). It evolves into Cherrim at level 25. `[Pokemondb.net: Cherubi, via WebSearch; Bulbapedia: Cherubi, via WebSearch]`
- **Flavor text** — "The deeper a Cherubi's red, the more nutrients it has stockpiled in its body. And the sweeter and tastier its small ball!" `[PokemonTCG.io / frontmatter]`
- **Lore** — The secondary sphere attached to Cherubi's body holds concentrated nutrients the main body absorbs before evolution. Because it is edible and sweet, Cherubi is actively hunted by other wild Pokemon, making it prey rather than predator. In Sinnoh it is found by Honey Trees, the region's special random-encounter mechanic. `[Bulbapedia: Cherubi, via WebSearch snippet; Pokemondex.com, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Mechanical note** — This Brilliant Stars print carries the Lively Fruit ability, preventing all attack effects (damage still applies) — a narrow but useful defensive keyword for bench-sitting roles in spread-effect metas. Artist: Naoyo Kimura. `[Frontmatter oracle_text, PokemonTCG.io]`

## Trivia

- **Species context** — Zarude (National Pokedex no. 893) is a Generation VIII Mythical Pokemon and the first Dark/Grass dual-type in the series. That type combination was designed to evoke nature's unforgiving qualities: the forest shelters and hides, but also conceals predators. Zarude was the central figure of "Pokemon the Movie: Secrets of the Jungle" (Japan release December 25, 2020), a film about a wild Zarude who raises a human infant named Koko in the Forest of Okoya. `[Wikipedia: Pokemon the Movie: Secrets of the Jungle]`
- **Distribution history** — As a Mythical, Zarude was distributed through limited event promotions. Players who preordered tickets for "Secrets of the Jungle" in their region received a Zarude in its "Dada" form (wearing a pink cloth memento from the film) plus a Shiny Celebi in Pokemon Sword and Shield. The standard form depicted on this card is the wild-roaming variant; the Dada form is distinguished by the pink cloth and is mechanically identical. `[CBR: Pokemon: Secrets of the Jungle — Who Is Zarude, via WebSearch]`
- **Mechanical note** — Zarude V (BRS no. 016/172) has 220 HP as a Grass-type Basic Pokemon V. Its Jungle Rage attack deals 120 base damage and doubles to 240 against an opponent's Pokemon V, making it an anti-V tech piece. Brilliant Stars was the first SWSH set to introduce VSTAR; Zarude V has no corresponding VSTAR print and is not upgraded in this set. `[PokemonTCG.io API: swsh9-16]`

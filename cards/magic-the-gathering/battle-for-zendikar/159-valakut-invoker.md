## Trivia

- **Design: Invoker pattern** — Valakut Invoker belongs to the Invoker design category: common creatures with a repeatable {8}-mana activated ability intended as a late-game mana sink. The pattern originated in Legions (2003), returned in Rise of the Eldrazi (2010), and appeared as only two cards in BFZ (Valakut Invoker red, and Oran-Rief Invoker green, 182) rather than a full 5-color cycle. `[MTG Wiki: Invoker, mtg.wiki/page/Invoker]`
- **Design: partial cycle** — BFZ fielded just two Invokers rather than a full cycle, a compression apparently driven by the set's Eldrazi-invasion design focus. The pattern returned to a full cycle in Commander Legends: Battle for Baldur's Gate (2022). `[MTG Wiki: Invoker, mtg.wiki/page/Invoker]`
- **Flavor text** — `"As long as we have walked this world, we have known it as a harsh and unforgiving place."` — *The Invokers' Tales* `[Scryfall]`. The source is a named in-world text rather than a named NPC — one of the few BFZ flavor credits citing a fictional document. The quote implies an oral/written tradition of invoker knowledge passed down through Zendikar's history.
- **Mechanical profile** — 2/3 for {2}{R}, {8}: deals 3 damage to any target. Legal in Pauper (common), Pioneer, Modern, Legacy, and Commander. EDHREC rank 15315. The {8} cost is intentional to the Invoker template — useful only in games that go long enough to accumulate that mana, functioning as a built-in late-game topdeck value mode on an otherwise vanilla-statted body. `[Scryfall]`

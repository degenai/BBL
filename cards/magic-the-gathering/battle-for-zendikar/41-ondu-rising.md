## Trivia

- **Set lore** — Ondu is one of the seven continents of Zendikar, located in the southwestern quadrant jutting into the Silundi Sea. Its geography is dominated by sweeping verticality — the Makindi Trenches, the skyscraping Turntimber forests, and the Crypt of Agadeem all define a world where travel is felt up and down rather than east and west. The ancient Kor empire's capital Makindi was located here before a civil war split it. `[MTG Wiki: Ondu, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Mechanic design** — The Awaken keyword was designed by Mark Rosewater's team to represent Zendikar's sentient land rising against the Eldrazi. A deliberate mechanical trick: animating a land produces a colorless creature, which could then be buffed by Eldrazi "colorlessness matters" cards — giving Awaken tokens cross-faction relevance in the same draft environment. `[Wizards of the Coast: Battle Plans design article, 2015-10-12, via WebFetch]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — Ondu Rising has only one printing (BFZ, 2015) and has never been reprinted. EDHREC rank: 23,510 — deep long-tail, minimal EDH presence. Legal in Pioneer, Modern, Legacy, Vintage, and Commander. `[Scryfall]`
- **Community sentiment** — MTGSalvation BFZ limited retrospectives note Awaken was "such a blowout mechanic that messed with planning — then you started planning for it and that led to grinder games." The broader critical view: Wizards attached Awaken to ordinary spells rather than designing around it, making many Awaken cards feel mechanically lazy. Ondu Rising fits that pattern — its base 1W mode is functional but unremarkable; the Awaken mode at 4W is where the card earns consideration. `[MTGSalvation: Battle for Zendikar Postmortem thread, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Design** — Illustrated by Igor Kieryluk, a Polish fantasy artist with sustained Zendikar ties — he also illustrated Anticognition for Zendikar Rising (2020). His MTG work is noted for pairing darkness with high-saturated color, fitting Zendikar's geological sublime. `[Art of Magic: the Gathering — Ondu Rising; Scent of a Gamer: Igor Kieryluk profile, via WebSearch snippets]`

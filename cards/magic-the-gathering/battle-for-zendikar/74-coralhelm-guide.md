## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Coralhelm is a named location in Zendikar lore: the merfolk capital built inside enormous conch shells suspended by nets between the walls of the Umara gorge in Tazeem, above Halimar. Founded by Noyan Dar (lullmage, scholar, and holder of the title Coralhelm Commander) after the Eldrazi overran Sea Gate and the original merfolk Enclave, it became the largest merfolk settlement on the plane. The unnamed scout on this card operates out of that refuge. `[mtglore.com: A Planeswalker's Guide to Zendikar — Tazeem and Merfolk]` `[bacejelerenvorthos.tumblr.com: Noyan Dar, Merfolk Leader]`

- **Flavor text** — "She knows every step of this coastline, both above and below the surface, and she has hideouts all along the way. She will get you there." — Jori En, expedition leader `[Scryfall]`. Jori En is a named merfolk from Bala Ged who appears in more BFZ block stories than almost any non-planeswalker NPC; she was instrumental in linking Zendikar's leylines to the hedron puzzle that eventually defeated the Eldrazi titans. She later received her own legendary card: Jori En, Ruin Diver (Oath of the Gatewatch, no. 155), a {1}{U}{R} Merfolk Wizard that draws a card whenever you cast your second spell each turn — mechanically the scholar who keeps feeding new knowledge to her allies. `[Scryfall: Jori En OGW 155]` `[coolstuffinc.com: Legends of Zendikar]`

- **Mechanical reputation** — Coralhelm Guide is a 2/1 Merfolk Scout Ally for {1}{U}. The Ally subtype triggers other BFZ Ally payoffs on entering the battlefield. Its activated ability ({4}{U}: target creature can't be blocked this turn) is expensive but guarantees evasion, making it a limited finisher-enabler. Legal in Pauper. EDHREC rank 22887, reflecting role-player rather than standalone Commander draw. `[Scryfall]`

- **Design / reprint history** — Coralhelm Guide has been reprinted four times beyond the original BFZ printing: Masters 25 (A25, 2018), Battlebond (BBD, 2018), The List (PLST, 2019), and Jumpstart: Historic Horizons (J21, 2021). Viktor Titov's original art was retained across all printings. The card's inclusion across a curated reprint set (Masters 25), a multiplayer-focused set (Battlebond), The List, and a digital-only set reflects sustained limited-format utility as a low-cost Merfolk Ally with guaranteed evasion text. `[Scryfall prints search]`

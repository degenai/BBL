## Trivia

- **Set context: Retreat cycle** — Retreat to Valakut is the red member of the five-card BFZ Retreat enchantment cycle, each named for a Zendikar location and carrying a landfall trigger. The full cycle: Retreat to Emeria (white, 44), Retreat to Coralhelm (blue, 82), Retreat to Hagra (black, 121), Retreat to Valakut (red, 153), Retreat to Kazandu (green, 186). `[Scryfall: BFZ set]`
- **Lore context: Valakut** — Valakut, the Molten Pinnacle is Zendikar's most famous volcanic landmark — the largest active peak of the Crown range — and has its own legendary land card from the original Zendikar set (2009), which deals 3 damage per Mountain entering when five or more Mountains are in play. That Valakut card became a Modern combo staple. `[Scryfall: Valakut the Molten Pinnacle ZEN-228; WebSearch snippet]`
- **Design note** — Retreat to Valakut carries no flavor text `[Scryfall]`; it is the only member of the Retreat cycle where the location-establishing work is done entirely through the art. Artist Kieran Yanner renders Valakut as pure volcanic landscape — no characters or figures, only fire columns rising through fractured rock.
- **Mechanical reputation** — EDHREC rank 15614 (moderate Commander inclusion). Its two landfall modes — +2/+0 or "can't block this turn" — serve as combat-efficiency tools in landfall-heavy decks. Sees fringe use in Pioneer Scapeshift-adjacent lists where the "can't block" mode enables the combo kill; not a centerpiece of any tier-1 deck. `[Scryfall: edhrec_rank; WebSearch: Valakut Pioneer combo]`

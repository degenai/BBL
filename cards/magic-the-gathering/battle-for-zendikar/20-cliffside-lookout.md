## Trivia

- **Set context + Ally type** — Cliffside Lookout is typed `Creature — Kor Scout Ally`, which is the canonical Wizards signal for Zendikari Resistance membership. The Kor are Zendikar's nomadic cliff-dwelling people, at home on canyon walls and cliff faces — the Lookout's name and setting literalize the species' vertical-terrain expertise. The Stone Havens referenced in the flavor text are fortified positions, some unearthed by the Eldrazi's geological devastation, where kor and other resistance groups held ground in Akoum. `[Scryfall type_line]` `[WebSearch: Wizards BFZ story summary kor resistance coalition]`
- **Flavor text** — "Though losses run high among the scouts of the Stone Havens, they never flinch from their duty." The "Stone Havens" are canonical Zendikari fortifications — rocky strongholds used as resistance outposts. The flavor frames the kor scouts as accepting of attrition: the duty is performed regardless of cost. This is the Zendikari resistance's stewardship register: not triumphalist, but persistent. `[Scryfall flavor_text]`
- **Mechanical note** — A 1/1 white Ally for one white mana with a pump ability costing five mana, Cliffside Lookout is a cheap Ally-count extender in BFZ limited rather than a powerful top-end. Its value came from the Rally mechanic: each additional Ally that enters the battlefield triggers any and all Rally abilities in play, so playing Cliffside Lookout as a one-drop Ally-count bump could chain into significant Rally triggers from other Allies on board. Pauper-legal. `[Scryfall oracle_text, legalities]` `[Wizards BFZ Mechanics article — magic.wizards.com/en/news/feature/battle-for-zendikar-mechanics]`
- **Zendikari Resistance roster** — This is the clearest roster candidate in the 6-card batch. Kor Scout + Ally type line + Stone Havens flavor text (the canonical resistance fortification) + active rallying gesture in the art together make the case without ambiguity. The Stone Havens are not a generic location — they are named specifically as kor-resistance-held positions in BFZ lore. `[Scryfall type_line, flavor_text]` `[WebSearch: Stone Havens kor BFZ lore]`

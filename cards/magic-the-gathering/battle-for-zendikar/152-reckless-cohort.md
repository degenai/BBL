## Trivia

- **Flavor text** — "You have a family. Mine died at Sea Gate. You go to yours, and I'll go to mine." — no named speaker `[Scryfall]`. Sea Gate was Zendikar's largest and most developed city, built atop a massive stone dam on Tazeem, and maintained the most organized resistance against the Eldrazi incursion before falling `[MTG Wiki: Sea Gate, mtg.wiki]`. The flavor text places this unnamed soldier as a Sea Gate survivor fighting not for hope but for grief.
- **Mechanical resonance** — Oracle text: "This creature attacks each combat if able unless you control another Ally." The self-attack compulsion encodes the flavor directly: the Cohort is reckless when alone — a solo survivor with nothing left to restrain them — but the drawback vanishes the moment another Ally is present, mechanically mirroring the flavor text's grief-without-community theme `[Scryfall: oracle_text]`.
- **Set context** — In BFZ, the Allies mechanic was reframed from Zendikar block's "adventuring party" into a unified resistance army. Reckless Cohort's type line — Human Warrior Ally — places it in that resistance force, though the flavor text reveals motivation as personal loss rather than collective cause `[Scryfall: type_line; WebSearch: BFZ Allies Rally mechanic]`.
- **Design** — Artist: Volkan Baga — same artist as Ondu Champion (149) in this set. Pauper-legal; EDHREC rank 28831 `[Scryfall]` — deep bulk tier at $0.04 market price `[Scryfall: prices]`, but flavor-text/mechanics alignment makes it a strong narrative object for bundling.

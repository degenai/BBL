## Trivia

- **Set context** — Battle for Zendikar (released October 2, 2015) depicts Zendikar under full Eldrazi invasion. The Eldrazi titans Ulamog and Kozilek had escaped their ancient hedron prison and were consuming the plane's mana and life energy — Zendikar as extraction victim at cosmological scale `[MTG Wiki: Battle for Zendikar, mtg.wiki]`.
- **Design** — Typed Eldrazi Drone, the servant-caste subtype spawned by one of the three titans. Devoid — introduced in BFZ — strips the card of color despite its red mana cost, mechanically representing the Eldrazi stripping mana identity from the plane as they move through it. Artist: Raymond Swanland `[Scryfall]`.
- **Flavor text** — "It moves through the tunnels as slowly and surely as the shifting stones." — no named speaker `[Scryfall]`. The line frames the drone not as a rushing attacker but as geological inevitability.
- **Mechanical** — Pauper-legal; EDHREC rank 26929 `[Scryfall]` — deep bulk tier, functional in niche Eldrazi-tribal Pauper builds where colorless-spell density rewards the pump ability.

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Razorkin are one of three named factions of Duskmourn alongside the Cult of Valgavoth and the Survivors; per Emily Teng's Planeswalker's Guide to Duskmourn they are "former survivors who now hunt the remaining survivors for their own twisted amusement," operating loosely under The Lord of Pain (the first-ever razorkin) and inhabiting elaborate death-trap rooms in the Boilerbilges called the razor mazes `[Wizards: Planeswalker's Guide to Duskmourn]` `[MTG Wiki: Duskmourn #Razorkin]`.
- **Set context** — Only three cards in DSK proper carry the Razorkin name: Razorkin Hordecaller (no. 152, U), Razorkin Needlehead (no. 153, R), and Ripchain Razorkin (no. 154, C). All three are red and collector-numbered consecutively as a flavor cycle. The full razorkin faction shows up on roughly 40 cards by art across the DSK block, but "Razorkin" is not a creature subtype on any card — the antagonist identity exists in lore and card-name flavor only `[Scryfall set search e:dsk]` `[MTG Wiki: Duskmourn #Razorkin]`.
- **Design** — Scryfall type_line is "Creature — Human Clown Berserker"; Hordecaller is the only Razorkin-named card with the Clown subtype (Needlehead is Human Assassin, Ripchain is Human Berserker). The Clown subtype is rare across DSK, appearing on just three cards in the set: Razorkin Hordecaller, Vicious Clown (no. 163), and The Jolly Balloon Man (no. 219) — so the pierrot read of this art is a per-card design choice, not a Razorkin-wide aesthetic `[Scryfall type-search t:clown e:dsk]`.
- **Design** — Previewed 2024-09-06 by Bandit on Twitter; magicspoiler.com archived the print under the URL slug "bloodslicer-horde-leader," suggesting the public name shifted from Bloodslicer Horde Leader to Razorkin Hordecaller late in the preview cycle to align with the named-faction nomenclature `[Scryfall preview field]` `[magicspoiler.com URL slug evidence, via WebSearch snippet]`.
- **Flavor text** — `"Know your place, pets! First blood is mine!"` — speaker unattributed on the print. Consistent with the canonical Razorkin disposition: pack-leadership by cruelty, with razorkin known to turn on each other or themselves when survivors aren't available to torture `[Scryfall]` `[Wizards: Planeswalker's Guide to Duskmourn]`.
- **Mechanical** — A 4/4 for `{4}{R}` with haste plus an attack-trigger 1/1 red Gremlin token-maker; Scryfall edhrec_rank 16256 puts it in the lower-popularity uncommon tier for commander, with a secondary-market price around $0.12 USD non-foil as of 2026-05-12. Gremlin is a niche subtype in DSK — only Irreverent Gremlin (no. 142) shares the type in the set, so the token stream is a callback rather than a tribal payoff `[Scryfall]`.

### Related cards
- The Lord of Pain (Duskmourn: House of Horror Commander, no. 3) — canonical leader figure the razorkin take directives from; the primary lore anchor for any Razorkin-faction bundle copy
- Razorkin Needlehead (Duskmourn: House of Horror, no. 153) — direct cycle partner; the rare-slot razorkin with the chant-flavor about stitching eyes and skin
- Ripchain Razorkin (Duskmourn: House of Horror, no. 154) — common-slot razorkin rounding out the three-card faction cycle
- Diversion Specialist (Duskmourn: House of Horror, no. 132) — the Survivor-side counterpart whose flavor "Hey! Fang-faces! Over here!" is shouted at razorkin antagonists like Hordecaller
- Irreverent Gremlin (Duskmourn: House of Horror, no. 142) — the only other Gremlin-typed creature in the set; natural pairing partner for the gremlin token stream
- Meathook Massacre II (Duskmourn: House of Horror) — per MTG Wiki, the razorkin recreated this on-plane after hearing the original Innistrad Meathook Massacre story

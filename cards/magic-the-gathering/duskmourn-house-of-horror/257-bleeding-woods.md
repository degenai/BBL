A dense, shadowed forest interior rendered in saturated reds, oranges, and deep blacks. Massive twisted tree trunks dominate the scene; embedded into their bark are ornate arched window frames and grave-marker-style stone surrounds, several glowing with cold pale-white light that cuts against the surrounding fire-hued ambience. Smaller upright slab shapes are visible mid-ground, reading as gravestones or tombstone fragments scattered through the underbrush. The sky between the trunks burns in lurid orange tones with no visible sun, giving the impression of perpetual dusk or distant fire. No figures are depicted.

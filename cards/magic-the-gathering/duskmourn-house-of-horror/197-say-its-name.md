## Trivia

- **Set context** — Duskmourn: House of Horror is the 102nd MTG expansion (released 2024-09-27), a top-down horror plane that consists of a single plane-spanning haunted house ruled by the demon Valgavoth, whose wings condemn everyone inside to feed his appetite for terror `[Wizards: Planeswalker's Guide to Duskmourn]` `[MTG Wiki: Duskmourn, via WebSearch snippet]`.
- **Design — ritual-name trope** — The card's mechanic — exile three copies of Say Its Name from your graveyard to fetch a card named Altanak, the Thrice-Called (no. 166) — reads as a deliberate nod to the speak-the-monster's-name horror trope (Bloody Mary, Candyman, Beetlejuice). The cracked-mirror art leans toward Bloody Mary / Candyman; the giant-insect payoff (Altanak is an Insect Beast) makes the Beetlejuice pun the most pointed. Outlets generally read it as a composite reference to the trope itself rather than any single example `[Wargamer: "MTG Duskmourn's latest horror reference is a Beetlejuice Beetle"]` `[CoolStuffInc: Travis Hall, "Altanak, Altanak, Altanak"]`.
- **Design — artist** — Sam Wolfe Connelly has illustrated MTG cards since Avacyn Restored (2012), including the 2013 Judge Gift promo Swords to Plowshares, and contributed multiple Duskmourn pieces (Say Its Name, Altanak, Fear of the Dark, The Swarmweaver — the last two as Double Exposure showcases). Connelly works primarily in Conte (an oily chalk medium); Say Its Name began as a Conte drawing with significant digital additions on top `[Commander's Herald: "Double Exposed: A Chat with Sam Wolfe Connelly," Nick Wolf, 2024-09-05]`.
- **Mechanical reputation** — Common at rarity, EDHREC rank 16548 (low Commander adoption). Standard reviewer Travis Hall describes it as a card that "barely makes the line of playability on its own," redeemed entirely by its role as the three-piece key to the Altanak combo. Buildable in draft because Say Its Name is common and Altanak is only uncommon — an unusually low rarity floor for a 9/9 build-around payoff `[CoolStuffInc: Travis Hall]` `[Scryfall edhrec_rank]` `[Wargamer]`.
- **Flavor text** — Card has no printed flavor text; the flavor work is done by the title, the oracle-text ritual ("two other cards named Say Its Name"), and the infested-reflection art `[Scryfall]`.

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Fear of Abduction is one of 14 Enchantment Creature — Nightmare cards bearing the "Fear of" naming pattern in Duskmourn: House of Horror (DSK, September 2024), one of the set's defining cycles. Each personalizes a specific phobia as a supernatural creature native to The House, a sentient haunted manor that functions as its own horror-genre plane. `[Scryfall]`
- **Mechanical design** — The card's mechanic literalizes the abduction trope: an additional-cost creature exile to cast (the "taken" victim), an opponent's creature exiled on entry (the abductee), and that creature returned when the Nightmare leaves — the classic alien-abduction loop encoded as temporary conditional removal. Legal in Standard, Pioneer, Modern, Legacy, Vintage, and Commander. `[Scryfall]`
- **Design** — Art by Fernando Falcone. No flavor text. EDHREC rank 18025 at time of research — limited Commander adoption; the additional-cost exile requirement raises the bar compared to cycle members that only require mana. `[Scryfall]`

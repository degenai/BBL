## Trivia

- **Set context** — Duskmourn: House of Horror released September 27, 2024. Norin, Swift Survivalist (DSK 145; this copy is the borderless 0297 variant) is one of the set's uncommons and the first new Norin card since Time Spiral (2006). The character is one of Magic's oldest named figures, first appearing in flavor text on Jade Statue in Limited Edition Alpha (1993). `[Scryfall: DSK #145 and TSP #171; WebSearch: magicspoiler.com]`
- **Lore connection** — Norin the Wary is a Dominarian human warrior infamous for cowardice. Per Wizards lore, he stumbled through an Omenpath door and found himself trapped inside Duskmourn. His creature type changed from Human Warrior (Time Spiral) to Human Coward in this printing — a deliberate callback encoding his defining characteristic directly in the type line. `[Scryfall: type_line fields; WebSearch: magicspoiler.com lore summary]`
- **Design** — The original Norin the Wary exiles himself whenever any player casts a spell or a creature attacks, making him nearly impossible to interact with (EDHREC rank ~4,956 on the TSP version, with a sustained niche Commander fan base). The Duskmourn version inverts his cowardice into utility: he attacks, and when blocked, you may exile the blocked creature to replay it this turn — dodge-and-redeploy as survival strategy. His flavor text, "I don't know what it was! Why would I stick around to find out?" echoes TSP's "I have a bad feeling about this." — the same deadpan-coward register maintained across 18 years. `[Scryfall: oracle_text, flavor_text, edhrec_rank on Norin the Wary]`
- **Verified IP** — Norin the Wary is a Wizards/MTG-internal character (Dominaria-born). No external IP risk. `[Scryfall type_line; Wizards lore]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Duskmourn: House of Horror (DSK) released 2024-09-27 as a top-down Modern Horror set built around the Haunted House trope. Fear of Failed Tests is part of a cycle of Enchantment Creature — Nightmare cards spanning 20 cards across DSK and its Commander product (DSC), each representing a distinct human fear. Per Mark Rosewater's design retrospective: "Set Design, working with Creative, made a series of creatures that represent different types of fears, all with the creature type Nightmare." [Wizards of the Coast, "Top of the Duskmourning, Part 1"]
- **Flavor text** — `"W-wait! What do you mean this quiz determines half our grade?"` — Zimone, muttering in her sleep. [Scryfall] Zimone Wola is a Quandrix College graduate student from Strixhaven on Arcavios, a mathematics prodigy whose mastery of fractal theory gives her insight into the structure of the Multiverse. In Duskmourn's story she joins the mission to rescue the planeswalker Nashi and becomes trapped with Tyvar in a vast shifting library inside the House. The flavor text positions her academic anxiety as the nightmare feeding this creature — her literal worst fear is a pop quiz she is not prepared for. [MTG Wiki: Zimone Wola, via WebSearch snippet]
- **Mechanical profile** — Costs {4}{U} for a 2/7 Enchantment Creature. The 7 toughness makes it an extremely durable blocker; the "draw that many cards on combat damage" trigger rewards connecting through it offensively. EDHREC rank 12,076 as of 2024 release data suggests modest Commander adoption. [Scryfall]
- **Design** — Artist Jana Heidersdorf. Hipsters of the Coast's 2022 artist wishlist described Heidersdorf's work as "enchanting and ethereal." [Hipstersofthecoast.com, "My 2022 Magic Artist Wishlist, Part I"] This is one of her DSK contributions, with the psychological fragmentation of Zimone's screaming faces consistent with Heidersdorf's style in recurring Strixhaven Mystical Archive work.

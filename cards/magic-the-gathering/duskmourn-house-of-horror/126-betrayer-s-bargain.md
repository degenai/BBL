## Trivia

- **Flavor text** — `"This is my way out. You'll have to find your own." —Winter, to Niko` `[Scryfall]`. Winter is the human survivor-guide who agreed to deliver four lives to the demon Valgavoth in exchange for an escape door out of Duskmourn; this card freezes the moment he abandons Niko Aris, the Wanderer, and Nashi to the cultists `[MTG Wiki: Winter, via WebSearch snippet]`.
- **Set context** — Card is flagged `story_spotlight: true` on Scryfall, sitting in a DSK story cycle with Winter's Intervention (no. 123, the first meeting beat) and the legendary commander Winter, Misanthropic Guide (no. 240, the named character) `[Scryfall API; Scryfall set:dsk is:story]`.
- **Lore continuation** — Per Wizards story summary, Winter came to Duskmourn as a teenager through one of its doors; the four sacrifices he owed Valgavoth began with the person he loved most. After delivering his final victims, Valgavoth opens a door to Innistrad for him, then drags Winter into Duskmourn's bowels — betraying the betrayer `[Star City Games: "Valgavoth's True Form Is Revealed In Duskmourn Episode 6"; MTG Wiki: Winter]`.
- **Design** — Art by Billy Christian, released 2024-09-27 as an uncommon (no. 126). The walkman, headphones, and cassette anchor Duskmourn's 1980s-slasher / VHS-era horror styling, the set's stated genre register `[Scryfall artist + released_at + collector_number]`.
- **Mechanical** — `{1}{R}` instant with additional cost: sacrifice a creature or enchantment or pay `{2}`; deals 5 to target creature and exiles instead of letting it die this turn. EDHREC rank 17543 marks it as niche/sleeper rather than staple. The sac-or-pay-`{2}` clause rhymes with the flavor: a price someone else pays so you walk away `[Scryfall oracle_text + edhrec_rank]`.

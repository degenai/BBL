## Trivia

- **Set context** — Feed the Clan was released September 26, 2014 as part of Khans of Tarkir (KTK), the first set of the Tarkir block and the last three-set block in Magic's history. KTK is structured around five wedge-aligned clans; Feed the Clan belongs to the Temur Frontier (green-blue-red), the isolationist mountain clan that values the strength of living things over politics or wealth. [Scryfall], [Wizards: Mechanics of Khans of Tarkir](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/feature/mechanics-khans-tarkir)
- **Mechanic** — The card introduces the Ferocious ability word, the Temur's signature mechanic: a conditional bonus that activates when you control a creature with power 4 or greater. At {1}{G}, Feed the Clan gains 5 life baseline or 10 life with Ferocious active — a 2-for-1 value swing that makes it a consistent sideboard staple in Pauper Mono-Green Stompy, where it is run to shut down Burn matchups. [Scryfall], [Wizards: Mechanics of Khans of Tarkir](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/feature/mechanics-khans-tarkir), [Pauper Stompy guide via Team Hold Priority](https://teamhp555130435.wordpress.com/2018/10/26/mono-green-stompy-pauper-guide/)
- **Flavor text** — "The Temur believe three things only are needed in life: a hot fire, a full belly, and a strong companion." [Scryfall] — The speaker is unnamed but the line encapsulates the Temur's anti-civilization ethos: survival, community, and raw strength, without hierarchy or property. The flavor text mirrors the card's mechanical identity (feeding your clan = restoring life; Ferocious = strong companion).
- **Artist** — Illustrated by Winona Nelson, a prolific MTG artist with over 140 cards in the official database. Nelson was interviewed by Hipsters of the Coast's "Arting Around" column in July 2015; she describes connecting emotionally to each assignment rather than approaching illustration purely technically. Feed the Clan's intimate, backlit campfire scene is a strong example of her figurative warmth. [Hipsters of the Coast: Arting Around — Winona Nelson Interview, 2015](https://www.hipstersofthecoast.com/2015/07/arting-around-winona-nelson-interview/)
- **Format reach** — Legal in Pauper, Pioneer, Modern, Legacy, Vintage, Commander, and Historic as of 2026. EDHREC reports the card in 5,722 Commander decks, paired most often with Heroes' Reunion and Rest for the Weary in lifegain-themed builds. [EDHREC: Feed the Clan](https://edhrec.com/cards/feed-the-clan), [Scryfall legalities]
- **Reprint note** — Feed the Clan received one reprint: The List (PLST), released November 7, 2019, as collector number KTK-132. It has not appeared in any draftable reprint set. [Scryfall prints search]

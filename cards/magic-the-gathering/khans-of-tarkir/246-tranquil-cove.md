## Trivia

- **Set context / cycle** — Tranquil Cove (KTK no. 246, released 2014-09-26) is the originating print of this card. It belongs to the KTK cycle of 10 dual gain-lands, one for each two-color pair: Bloodfell Caves (BR), Blossoming Sands (GW), Dismal Backwater (UB), Jungle Hollow (BG), Rugged Highlands (GR), Scoured Barrens (WB), Swiftwater Cliffs (UR), Thornwood Falls (GU), Tranquil Cove (WU), and Wind-Scarred Crag (RW). All 10 share the same oracle template: enters tapped, gain 1 life on entry, tap for one of two colors `[Scryfall: KTK tapped-land oracle search]`
- **Cycle clarification (ktk-gain-lands flag)** — Tranquil Cove KTK is NOT a member of the Zendikar-block "Refuges" cycle (Akoum Refuge, Graypelt Refuge, etc., 2009). The corpus has a `ktk-gain-lands` node and this card carries a `characters: ktk-gain-lands` edge. Scryfall confirms Fate Reforged (FRF, 2015-01-23) reprinted the same 10 KTK gain-lands under the same oracle IDs; those FRF copies are reprints of KTK originals, not a separate FRF-originating cycle. **The ktk-gain-lands node and this edge should be audited**: if the node was seeded to track the KTK/FRF gain-lands collectively, the naming is misleading; the Refuge name belongs to the distinct Zendikar cycle `[Scryfall: oracle_id 5d641bf6 prints search]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — EDHREC rank 1,072 and Pauper-legal status across 19 printings through 2025 (Tarkir: Dragonstorm) make this one of the most durably reprinted gain-lands in Magic. Pauper staple for white-blue mana-fixing; budget Commander go-to `[Scryfall; EDHREC]`
- **Design** — Illustrated by John Avon, one of MTG's most prolific land artists. No flavor text in any printing — Scryfall flavor_text field is null for KTK `[Scryfall]`

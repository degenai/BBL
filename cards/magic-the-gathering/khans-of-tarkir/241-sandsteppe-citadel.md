## Trivia

- **Set context / cycle** — Sandsteppe Citadel is one of five wedge tri-lands in Khans of Tarkir (KTK, 2014-09-26), one per clan: Frontier Bivouac (Temur, GUR), Mystic Monastery (Jeskai, URW), Nomad Outpost (Mardu, RWB), Opulent Palace (Sultai, BGU), and Sandsteppe Citadel (Abzan, WBG). Each enters tapped and taps for any of its clan's three colors with no life-gain trigger, distinguishing this cycle from the 10 dual gain-lands also in the set `[Scryfall: KTK land oracle search]`
- **Flavor text** — "That which endures, survives." — no speaker attributed. The phrase is a pure Abzan creed: the clan's identity is organized around multi-generational family bonds and the philosophy that survival through hardship is itself the highest value. The Abzan watermark is confirmed in card metadata `[Scryfall]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — EDHREC rank 496 places this among the more broadly-played lands in Commander. Budget Abzan (white-black-green) Commander builds consistently include it as a no-frills three-color land when premium dual lands are out of range `[EDHREC: Sandsteppe Citadel card page]`
- **Design** — Illustrated by Sam Burley. The art depicts the Abzan stronghold on the Sandsteppe, with Central Asian steppe-fortress aesthetics consistent with the Abzan clan's world-building sources `[Scryfall]`

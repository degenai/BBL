A purplish-grey artifact creature crouches against a dark, smoky background. Its silhouette is unmistakably sliver-shaped — a single hooked, eyeless head curving forward from a hunched body, with segmented plates running across its back and long sickle-like claws extended in front. The construct is built of riveted, beaten metal rather than living chitin, giving it a salvaged, machine-cult quality. Light catches the curved blade of its head and the leading edge of its claws, picking the figure out of an almost monochromatic charcoal-and-violet haze. There is no setting beyond ambient smoke and gloom; the figure is the entire composition.

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Sliver Construct is unique in the Sliver creature type's history: it is an Artifact Creature — Sliver Construct, making it colorless and typed as both artifact and sliver simultaneously. It has no oracle text (a 2/2 for three mana with no abilities). The M14 sliver cycle represents Slivers as found on Shandalar, a mana-rich plane that "floats freely through the Blind Eternities" — the only plane outside Dominaria where Slivers have appeared in core sets. `[Scryfall]` `[WebSearch: Shandalar Slivers — Fireside Magic]`
- **Lore: the Skep** — The flavor text references "the Skep," the central hive of Shandalar's slivers, first named in M14 flavor text. A Thunian Scout named Hastric is identified in Shandalar lore as the person who named both the creatures and their hive. The Skep is ruled by an insectile hivelord described as roughly 20 times the size of a human. `[WebSearch: M14 Slivers Shandalar — search snippet citing sliver lore sources]`
- **Design context** — The M14 Creative team redesigned the sliver silhouette from the classic one-claw Tempest-era form specifically because, according to Doug Beyer, "the range of different body morphologies of the one-claw sliver was already in danger of being played out." Sliver Construct's metal-riveted aesthetic fits this redesign's broader range of morphologies. `[dougbeyermtg.tumblr.com: "Slivers Evolved," 2013]`
- **Mechanical note** — Sliver Construct has no enters-the-battlefield ability and grants no bonus to other slivers, consistent with M14's revision to sliver wording ("All slivers you control" rather than "All slivers"). Its value is purely as a colorless 2/2 body — playable in non-green/non-white sliver shells that want a cheap artifact synergy. `[Scryfall]`
- **Format legality** — Sliver Construct is Pauper-legal (common rarity), and one of the few Sliver-type cards that can slot into Pauper Slivers lists without needing colored mana. `[Scryfall: legalities field]`

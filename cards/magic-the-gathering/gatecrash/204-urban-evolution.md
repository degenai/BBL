# Urban Evolution (Gatecrash)

## Vision

A towering biomechanical-organic hybrid creature rises from a tangle of vines and twisted vegetation, its form a fusion of plant matter, fleshy tendrils, and crustacean-like plates. The thicket dominates the foreground in saturated golden-greens lit harshly from above. Behind the creature, sun-baked stone ruins and broken urban architecture peek through the encroaching jungle, with shafts of bright sunlight breaking through the canopy. The palette is dominated by acid greens, ochre yellows, and warm umber browns, evoking a hothouse atmosphere where civilization is being eaten by accelerated nature.

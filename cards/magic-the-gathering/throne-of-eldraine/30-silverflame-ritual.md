## Trivia

- **Set-lore** — Ardenvale's defining ritual space is the Circle of Loyalty — an eternally burning silver fire at the center of the court. Aspiring knights must step into the silver flame to prove their loyalty; those who pass through unscathed are knighted at the center of the ring. The art's overhead radial formation of five armored figures around a glowing central sphere appears to depict this ceremony directly. `[magic.wizards.com: Planeswalker's Guide to Eldraine]` `[WebSearch: Ardenvale MTG Wiki]`
- **Flavor text** — "Tempered by fire and united in virtue, the knights of Ardenvale fight with a single, unbreakable will." The phrase 'tempered by fire' directly references the silver-fire ordeal of the Circle of Loyalty; 'single, unbreakable will' is a mechanical echo of the vigilance keyword granted by the card's Adamant bonus. `[Scryfall]`
- **Design** — Adamant is a keyword mechanic introduced in Throne of Eldraine: spend at least three mana of a specified color when casting the spell, and an additional effect triggers. Silverflame Ritual's Adamant bonus grants vigilance — mechanically appropriate for Ardenvale, the defensive-unity court, where holding ground and not tapping out is a virtue. The mechanic was previewed by Wizards on 2019-09-04. `[Scryfall: keywords: Adamant]` `[magic.wizards.com: Throne of Eldraine mechanics, 2019-09-04]`
- **Mechanical** — Puts a +1/+1 counter on each creature you control at 4 mana, making it a go-wide anthem in creature-token and weenie strategies. EDHREC rank 19,269 — steady commander inclusion. No longer Standard-legal after the 2021 rotation. `[Scryfall]`

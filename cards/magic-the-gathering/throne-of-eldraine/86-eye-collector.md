## Trivia

- **Flavor text** — `"Lord Rankle will see you now—all he asks is a small token of tribute."` Eye Collector is explicitly one of Rankle's delivery agents, collecting the "tribute" Rankle extracts from those who attract his attention. The tribute framing is the card's sole narrative function: this creature exists to carry things to its master. `[Scryfall]`

- **Rankle's design genealogy** — In the Throne of Eldraine vision design handoff document (published November 2019), Mark Rosewater describes the prankster-fae archetype as both a "Deal-Making Imp — a nod to Rumpelstiltskin" and the plane's Puck figure from Shakespeare's *A Midsummer Night's Dream*. Rankle synthesizes both: the deal-maker who extracts impossible prices, and the imp who delights in the chaos he sows. Eye Collector's tribute mechanic sits squarely in the Rumpelstiltskin vein — the fae demands a thing of value, and the imp delivers. `[Wizards.com: "Throne of Eldraine Vision Design Handoff, Part 2," Nov 2019]`

- **Rankle's extended lore: WOE and MOM** — Rankle does not appear as a creature card in Wilds of Eldraine (September 2023); his presence is carried by a spell, "Rankle's Prank" (WOE no. 102, sorcery), whose flavor text reads "The louder they scream, the harder he laughs." His creature-card return came earlier, in March of the Machine (April 2023), where he appears as "Rankle and Torbran" (MOM no. 252, Legendary Creature — Faerie Dwarf, rare), paired with Torbran of Locthwain after a comedic adventure involving three wishes, a love potion, and the Phyrexian invasion. The Wizards story "The Adventures of Rankle, Master of Love" (magic.wizards.com, 2023) covers this arc. Eye Collector's "lord" predates all of this by four years. `[Scryfall: Rankle's Prank WOE no. 102; Scryfall: Rankle and Torbran MOM no. 252; Wizards.com: "The Adventures of Rankle, Master of Love"]`

- **Mechanical profile: Pauper and PDH** — Eye Collector is legal in both Pauper and Pauper Commander formats. A one-mana 1/1 flyer with an on-hit mill trigger fills a niche in Pauper UB Faeries shells (the format runs creatures like Faerie Miscreant and Spellstutter Sprite at the same mana range), and in PDH mill lists where it can serve as the commander. Its EDHREC rank of 9,967 across all formats reflects its narrow role: it is not a staple, it is a role-player in tribal or mill-specific brews. `[Scryfall legalities; EDHREC rank via Scryfall API]`

- **Reprint history** — Eye Collector received its first reprint in Foundations Jumpstart (J25, 2025), card no. 439. The original ELD printing has not been significantly reprinted elsewhere. Its employer, Rankle, Master of Pranks (ELD no. 101, mythic), has a busier reprint history: Commander Masters (CMM), Outlaws of Thunder Junction Commander (OTC), and a digital-only Arena Alchemy variant ("Rankle, Pitiless Trickster"). By EDHREC's count, over 1,000 Commander decks list Rankle as their general (ranked no. 1,294 among commanders), a Commander demand footprint that ultimately drove repeated reprints of the mythic while the common Eye Collector went unreprinted for six years. `[Scryfall: J25 no. 439; Scryfall: Rankle reprint list; EDHREC via Scryfall API]`

- **Artist: Uriah Voth** — Uriah Voth is credited with 67 cards in Gatherer as of 2026. Within Throne of Eldraine, Voth illustrated two cards: Eye Collector and Bramblefort Fink (ELD no. 311). No Hipsters of the Coast artist profile or published interview with Voth on this card was found. `[Gatherer: Uriah Voth artist search; Scryfall: a:"Uriah Voth" set:eld]`

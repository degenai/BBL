## Trivia

- **Set context** — Turn into a Pumpkin is one of four cards forming the Cinderella thematic cycle in Throne of Eldraine (ELD, released 2019-10-04). The cycle encodes the key beats of the Cinderella fairy tale: Faerie Guidemother (no. 11, the fairy godmother), Midnight Clock (no. 54, the stroke of midnight), Enchanted Carriage (no. 218, the pumpkin-coach), and this card (the reversal — the coach turning back at the deadline). Head designer Mark Rosewater confirmed ELD was designed top-down from fairy tales first in the "Eldraine or Shine" Making Magic column. `[Wizards of the Coast: Mark Rosewater, "Eldraine or Shine," 2019-09-09]` `[dotesports.com: "Mark Rosewater shares the Throne of Eldraine tale"]`
- **Design** — ELD no. 69, illustrated by Vincent Proce, released 2019-10-04. No flavor text is printed on this card. The art renders the Cinderella reversal ambiguously: the hunched figure in the pumpkin patch reads simultaneously as the creature becoming a pumpkin AND as the coach mid-reversion. The Adamant ability (three-blue-mana requirement for the Food token bonus) grounds the card in ELD's courtly blue-magic color identity. `[Scryfall: eld/69]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — Oracle text: "Return target nonland permanent to its owner's hand. Draw a card. Adamant — If at least three blue mana was spent to cast this spell, create a Food token." The base bounce-plus-draw makes this a functional tempo piece in blue. The Food token links to ELD's Food-token subtheme shared by Enchanted Carriage (Mouse tokens) and the set's Witch's Oven / Baked treats cluster. Mid-niche by Scryfall edhrec_rank; sees play in ELD-tribal Commander brews rather than competitive blue shells. `[Scryfall: eld/69]`
- **Corpus cycle coverage** — Enchanted Carriage (ELD no. 218) is trivia-enriched in corpus. Turn into a Pumpkin (this card) is now the second of four Cinderella cycle members enriched. Remaining: Faerie Guidemother (no. 11) and Midnight Clock (no. 54).

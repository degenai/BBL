## Trivia

- **IP verification** — Verified at card level. Title names Oko, rules text tutors specifically for "Oko, the Trickster," and the flavor text is signed "—Oko," all of which lock the card's identity to the Oko planeswalker. The depicted antlered figure is best read as Oko in stag/antler shapeshift form (canonically attested) rather than asserted as a strict identity: MTG Wiki documents that during the Eldraine escape Oko "shifted form into a drake once more, then a raven, a crested eagle, then an antler before dissolving into golden sparks," and the Wildered Quest novella has him escaping Garruk "in the form of a stag." `[MTG Wiki: Oko]` `[MTG Wiki: Throne of Eldraine: The Wildered Quest]`
- **Set context** — Card is a Throne of Eldraine Planeswalker Deck exclusive (Oko, the Trickster precon, green-blue), not a main-set rare and not a showcase/storybook variant; collector range 309-313 covers the five Oko deck exclusives, with Hospitality at no. 312. `[magic.wizards.com: Throne of Eldraine Planeswalker Decks, 2019-09-18]` `[Card Kingdom: oko-the-trickster-planeswalker-deck listing]`
- **Set context** — Sits inside the Wildered Quest plot, in which Oko has transformed High King Algenus Kenrith into a stag to be hunted at the midwinter Wild Hunt; the stag-and-forest imagery on this card is the visual shorthand for that scheme, not generic druidic decoration. `[MTG Wiki: Throne of Eldraine: The Wildered Quest]`
- **Design** — Artist is Deruchenko Alexander; he has 94 official Magic cards on Gatherer (210 across all printings) and other Throne of Eldraine work includes Bartered Cow (no. 6) and Rowan's Stalwarts (no. 307). `[Gatherer artist search: Deruchenko Alexander]` `[artofmtg.com/artist/deruchenko-alexander]`
- **Flavor text** — `"My advisors recommend I let them eat you."` — Oko, `[Gatherer: Oko's Hospitality ELD no. 312]`
- **Mechanical** — This card itself is NOT banned in any format. The notorious 2019 Oko bans (Standard 2019-11-18, Brawl 2019-11-05, Pioneer 2019-12-12, Modern 2020-01) all hit Oko, Thief of Crowns (ELD no. 197) — a different card from the precon's Oko, the Trickster (ELD no. 309) that this Hospitality tutors for. `[Card Kingdom Blog: Oko Banned in Standard]` `[MTG Goldfish: Banned and Restricted Update 2019-12-12]`
- **Mechanical** — Low EDH traffic: roughly 258 deck listings on EDHREC at ~0.01% inclusion across 1.80 million decks. Top homes are niche fae/spell shells: Maralen, Fae Ascendant; Mr. Foxglove; Adrix and Nev, Twincasters; Kellan, the Kid — all decks where having an in-pile Oko tutor pulls some weight. `[EDHREC: okos-hospitality (via search summary)]`
- **Community resonance** — Sentiment, not fact: "Oko" as a character carries 2019 community baggage as shorthand for format-warping design even though THIS card (the precon Hospitality) is harmless; the broader Oko-the-meme is documented in its own Know Your Meme entry. `[knowyourmeme.com: Oko, Thief of Crowns]`

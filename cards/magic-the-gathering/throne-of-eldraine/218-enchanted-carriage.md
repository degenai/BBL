## Trivia

- **Set context** — Throne of Eldraine (released 2019-10-04) is Wizards' top-down designed set fusing Grimms' Fairy Tales with Arthurian legend; lead designer Mark Rosewater said he had been "waiting 10 years to do a Grimm's world" `[CBR: "Grimms' Fairy Tales Meet Arthurian Legend in New Magic: The Gathering Set"]` `[Wikipedia: Throne of Eldraine]`.
- **Design / Cinderella confirmation** — Rosewater explicitly groups "the pumpkin carriage" among the fairy-tale objects driving the set's artifact design: *"those had a lot of objects in them (Excalibur, the Holy Grail, the Round Table, the glass slipper, the pumpkin carriage, the glass casket, Pinocchio, the Gingerbread Man, the Magic Mirror, etc.)"* `[Wizards: "More Odds & Ends: Throne of Eldraine" 2019-10-21]`.
- **Design / Mouse tokens** — The Mouse creature type was invented *for this card*. Rosewater writes verbatim: *"This creature type exists only because the Enchanted Carriage needed white Mice for flavor."* The two 1/1 white Mouse tokens directly reference the mice-turned-coachmen of the Cinderella tale `[Wizards: "More Odds & Ends: Throne of Eldraine" 2019-10-21]`.
- **Design / artist** — Illustrated by Zoltan Boros `[Scryfall card page metadata]`. Rosewater also surfaces the card in his earlier design diary as a modular-flavor example: *"my Gingerbrute and my Flaxen Intruder crew my Enchanted Carriage"* `[Wizards: "Odds & Ends: Throne of Eldraine" 2019-10-14]`.
- **Flavor text** — No printed flavor text on this card; Throne of Eldraine artifacts in the "iconic object" cycle typically let the art and the oracle text carry the storybook beat `[Scryfall card data via search-result summaries]`.
- **Mechanical reputation** — 5-mana 4/4 Artifact - Vehicle, Crew 2, enters with two 1/1 white Mouse tokens. EDHREC: included in ~2,640 of 8.78M decks (~0.03% overall). Strongest commander synergy is Mabel, Heir to Cragflame (~10% inclusion, Mouse tribal from Bloomburrow); second is Greasefang, Okiba Boss (~5.1%, vehicles reanimator) `[EDHREC: enchanted-carriage]`.
- **Community resonance** — Community-deck sentiment treats Enchanted Carriage as the foundation of post-Bloomburrow Mouse tribal — repeatedly referenced as "the first mouse card ever made" in Mabel tribal deck descriptions on TappedOut, Archidekt and Moxfield. Sentiment, not Wizards fact; sourced from deck-build pages rather than r/EDH threads (direct Reddit threads were sparse for this query) `[tappedout.net: Mabel Mouse Tribal and Equipment]` `[moxfield.com: Mouse Tribal (I gave it a knife)]` `[archidekt.com: Mabel Mouse tribal]`.

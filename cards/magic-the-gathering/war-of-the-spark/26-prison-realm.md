## Trivia

- **Set context** — Story-spotlight card in War of the Spark (Act 3, the post-defeat coda) depicting Nicol Bolas imprisoned within the Meditation Plane. Per MTG Wiki, the Meditation Plane is "also known as Bolas's Meditation Realm and after the War of the Spark as the Prison Realm"; after the WAR climax, "Ugin expanded himself over the Meditation Realm. The Realm thus became an eternal prison for Bolas and Ugin alike. Neither could leave." `[Scryfall story_spotlight: true]` `[MTG Wiki: Meditation Plane]`
- **Flavor text** — *"After millennia to craft victory, Bolas had eternity to contemplate defeat."* — narratorial (not character-attributed); explicitly names Bolas and frames the card as the bookend to the elder dragon's millennia-long planeswalker-harvest scheme. `[Scryfall]`
- **Cluster** — Third member of the WAR Bolas-Falls story-spotlight cluster alongside Topple the Statue (no. 035, Vitu-Ghazi toppling Bolas's Ravnican statue) and Despark (no. 190, Liliana commanding the God-Eternals against him). Scryfall's `is:storyspotlight` search confirms 27 WAR story-spotlights; Prison Realm is the narrative terminus, depicting Bolas's final containment rather than the battle itself. `[Scryfall is:storyspotlight set:war]`
- **Design** — Art by Daarken; previewed 2019-04-18 by The Vorthos Cast, the Magic story-and-art podcast — consistent with WAR's pattern of routing story-spotlight previews to flavor-focused outlets. Uncommon, never reprinted as of the 2026-05-11 prints index. `[Scryfall: artist + preview fields]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — Three-mana white Banishing Light variant with a scry-1 rider on enters-the-battlefield. EDHREC tracks Prison Realm in roughly 3,175 commander decks (rank ~16,710) — niche relative to Banishing Light, but the scry trigger gives it a home in blink and library-manipulation shells; EDHREC's WAR set review noted the card "won't be kicking Grasp of Fate out" of removal lineups but found a place in Aminatou-style filter decks. `[EDHREC: Prison Realm]` `[EDHREC: War of the Spark Set Review - White]`

### Related cards

- Despark (War of the Spark, no. 190) — already-verified Bolas-Falls cluster sibling; Liliana, freed from Bolas's contract, commanding the God-Eternals against him.
- The Elderspell (War of the Spark) — Bolas's planeswalker-harvest macguffin; the apparatus Prison Realm's containment is the consequence of.
- Ugin, the Ineffable (War of the Spark) — Bolas's twin and his eventual jailer; per MTG Wiki, Ugin expanded himself over the Meditation Realm to make it the prison depicted here.
- Nicol Bolas, Dragon-God (War of the Spark) — Bolas at the height of his Elderspell-fueled apotheosis; the inverse pole of Prison Realm's depiction of his defeat.

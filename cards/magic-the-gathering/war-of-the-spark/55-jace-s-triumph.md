## Trivia

- **Set context** — One of 5 "Triumph" cards in War of the Spark (Gideon WAR no. 15, Jace no. 55, Liliana no. 98, Chandra no. 121, Nissa no. 170): one per Gatewatch founder, one per color, all uncommon, all by Kieran Yanner. The cycle is the in-set callback to the Hour of Devastation "Defeat" cycle (Jace's Defeat HOU no. 38 and its four siblings) — together they bookend the Gatewatch's arc against Bolas `[Scryfall set:war name:Triumph; Scryfall set:hou name:Defeat]`.
- **Design** — Uncommon sorcery, `{2}{U}`, illustrated by Kieran Yanner, released 2019-05-03. Also shipped as a 1-of in the WAR Jace, Arcane Strategist Planeswalker Deck `[Scryfall; Wizards: "War of the Spark Planeswalker Deck Lists", 2019-04-18]`.
- **Flavor text** — `"His triumph was not in outsmarting Bolas's plan, but in understanding why ultimate power is self-defeating."` All five Triumph cards use the same templated `"X's triumph came not from Y, but from Z"` construction — a flavor cycle as deliberate as the mechanical one `[Scryfall flavor_text fields, all five WAR Triumph cards]`.
- **Mechanical reputation** — `"Draw two cards. If you control a Jace planeswalker, draw three cards instead."` Functions as a flexible card-draw spell that upgrades to a {2}{U} three-card-draw only when paired with a Jace walker. Scryfall edhrec_rank ~18,213 (mid-niche); EDHREC reports inclusion in roughly 78% of dedicated Jace-tribal lists `[Scryfall; WebSearch snippet of EDHREC Jace's Triumph page]`. Outside Jace-themed Commander/Brawl builds the conditional is too narrow to make it a generic blue draw spell.
- **Set lore** — In-fiction context: during the War of the Spark, Jace was trapped on Ravnica by Bolas's Immortal Sun and lost his Living Guildpact powers when the Planar Bridge disrupted the leylines feeding the Guildpact's magic. This card is a story-adjacent moment of mind-magic during the defense, **not** a Story Spotlight — Scryfall returns `story_spotlight: false`, and the card is absent from the MTG Wiki Story Spotlight list for the set `[MTG Wiki: Jace Beleren, War of the Spark section; MTG Wiki: War of the Spark, Story Spotlights list; Scryfall]`.

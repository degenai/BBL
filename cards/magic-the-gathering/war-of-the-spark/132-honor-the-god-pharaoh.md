## Trivia

- **Set context** — War of the Spark (WAR, May 2019) is the climax of Nicol Bolas's long-running plan: using the Amonkhet block's trials to build an undead elite army (the Dreadhorde, also called the Eternals), then opening a Planar Bridge to unleash them on Ravnica. Honor the God-Pharaoh depicts the Eternals performing their programmed devotional salute — arms wide, heads raised — while Ravnica burns around them. The flavor text makes the theological inversion explicit: these warriors once had a diverse pantheon; Bolas erased that and replaced it with himself. `[Scryfall]` `[Wizards: "War of the Spark Mechanics," 2019-03-31]`
- **Flavor text** — *"In life, they revered a diverse pantheon of gods. Now they revere only the God-Pharaoh."* The God-Pharaoh is Nicol Bolas's Amonkhet title, adopted to present himself as the supreme deity to Amonkhet's populace. The flavor text is a two-sentence theological dispossession: pre-existing faith stripped and replaced with singular worship of Bolas. `[Scryfall]` `[Wizards: "War of the Spark Mechanics," 2019-03-31]`
- **Mechanical design** — Amass was the one new keyword in WAR, mechanically representing Bolas's ever-growing zombie horde: "Amass Zombies 1" either creates a 0/0 black Zombie Army token or grows an existing one with a +1/+1 counter. Honor the God-Pharaoh pairs Amass 1 with looting (discard + draw 2) at {2}{R} — a red-aligned card-selection effect wearing the Dreadhorde's flavor. The Wizards mechanics article describes Amass as being "on the side of evil" — it is mechanically color-coded to Bolas's Grixis palette (blue, black, red). `[Wizards: "War of the Spark Mechanics," 2019-03-31]`
- **IP note** — Nicol Bolas is confirmed as the God-Pharaoh depicted in this card's lore context. The card is directly linked to the `nicol-bolas` character node in this archive. The Eternals performing the salute are not named individual characters; they are the Dreadhorde — a mass undead army created from Amonkhet's dead champions. `[cards/_characters/nicol-bolas.md]` `[Scryfall: type_line Sorcery, WAR]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — EDHREC rank 14,180; sees play in Zombie Army / Amass tribal Commander lists and Pauper red-black looting builds. The discard-to-draw cost makes it card-neutral (minus the Amass token generated), giving it marginal efficiency at common. Legal in Pioneer, Modern, Legacy, Vintage, Pauper, and Commander. `[Scryfall]`

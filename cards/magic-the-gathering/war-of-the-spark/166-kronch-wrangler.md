The beast charges through churned earth and broken stone, kicking up plumes of brown dust that fill the lower half of the frame. The wrangler's pale, dust-streaked body is rendered with thick painterly brushwork, his mouth open in a laugh or shout. The composition is dynamic and forward-charging, conveying brute force and reckless glee.

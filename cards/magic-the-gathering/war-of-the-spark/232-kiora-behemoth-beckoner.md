## Trivia

- **Set context** — One of 36 planeswalkers printed in War of the Spark (May 2019), the first MTG set to include a designed cycle of uncommon planeswalkers (3 mythic / 13 rare / 20 uncommon) `[MTG Wiki: War of the Spark]`.
- **Design** — Kiora is one of the 20 uncommon walkers from the WAR cycle. The uncommon-walker template that Mark Rosewater walked through in his preview article gives each uncommon walker exactly one static or triggered ability plus a single loyalty ability so they slot into Limited without warping it. Kiora's draw-trigger on power-4-or-greater ETB plus a single `-1: untap target permanent` follows that template exactly `[Wizards: "Waging War of the Spark, Part 1", Mark Rosewater, 2019-04-01]`.
- **Design** — Hybrid `{2}{G/U}` cost — the Simic-coded hybrid mana lets her run in either mono-blue or mono-green decks, while signalling her continued Simic-leaning tie to ocean and leviathan magic established on her earlier Born of the Gods and Battle for Zendikar printings `[Scryfall]`. Art by Jaime Jones `[Scryfall]`.
- **Mechanical** — Strong uncommon walker for the cycle: 7 starting loyalty (high for the slot) and `-1` as her only loyalty cost means she's hard to kill and can untap turn after turn. EDHREC rank around 2258 `[Scryfall]`. Community write-ups frame her as an under-discussed role-player whose untap is *not* restricted to lands — it untaps any permanent, enabling tap-activated creature and artifact combos `[MTGGoldfish: "The Beauty of Uncommon Planeswalkers"; Star City Games: "Kickin' It With Kiora, Behemoth Beckoner"]`.
- **Set lore** — Kiora is a merfolk planeswalker born on Zendikar who venerates leviathans, krakens, and the deep, and wields a bident she stole from the god Thassa `[MTG Wiki: Kiora]`. The WAR art reads her on Ravnica during Bolas's assembly of planeswalkers, summoning a leviathan to the battlefield, consistent with WAR's premise that every walker arrives on Ravnica for the war `[MTG Wiki: Kiora; MTG Wiki: War of the Spark]`.

### Related cards

- Kiora, the Crashing Wave (Born of the Gods, no. 149) — Kiora's first planeswalker printing; established the leviathan-summoner identity and the Thassa-stolen bident.
- Kiora, Master of the Depths (Oath of the Gatewatch, no. 41) — Battle for Zendikar block Kiora; same character, same theme, mid-Eldrazi-crisis framing.
- Kiora's Follower (Born of the Gods, no. 167) — original Kiora-flavored creature; namesake-character tie-in.
- Kasmina, Enigmatic Mentor (War of the Spark, no. 196) — sibling uncommon planeswalker on the same WAR design template (hybrid cost, one ability, high loyalty for slot).
- Vivien, Champion of the Wilds (War of the Spark, no. 175) — sibling WAR planeswalker, green half of Kiora's color identity, same overarching cycle.

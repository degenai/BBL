## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Lazotep is a blue mineral native to the plane of Amonkhet, used by Nicol Bolas to produce the Dreadhorde: corpses coated in layers of the mineral whose lifeforce and deeds are preserved inside the shell. This Behemoth is a lazotep-coated zombie hippopotamus, part of the Dreadhorde army Bolas transferred to Ravnica via the Planar Bridge for the War of the Spark. `[MTG Salvation: The World of Amonkhet, via WebSearch snippet]` `[Scryfall type_line: Creature — Zombie Hippo]`
- **Flavor text** — "\"I know I should be more concerned. But a big, blue zombie-potamus from beyond the stars? This is what they're invading us with?\" —Mileva, Boros legionnaire." Mileva is a named Boros soldier; her wry disbelief in the face of an otherworldly zombified hippo functions as black comedy against the card's threatening 5/4 stat line. `[Scryfall flavor_text]`
- **Creature type** — Zombie Hippo is one of the odder tribal combinations in Magic. Hippos are natively a green creature type (River Hippo, Pygmy Hippo), but this card leads with Zombie, signaling that lazotep zombification overrides the animal's original color identity. The cobalt-blue armored visual erases any green-plains origin. `[Scryfall type_line]`
- **Mechanical profile** — Vanilla 5/4 for {4}{B} with no abilities. EDHREC rank 27582 — sees essentially no Commander play. In WAR limited it was a passable curve-topper for black midrange but offered nothing beyond its stats. Art by Zezhou Chen. `[Scryfall]` `[EDHREC rank]`

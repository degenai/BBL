## Trivia

- **Set context** — Despark is a designated DailyMTG Story Spotlight card from War of the Spark; the art depicts Liliana Vess, freed from Nicol Bolas' contract, commanding the corrupted God-Eternals Oketra and Bontu (the two cracked god-statue heads behind her) to turn on Bolas himself. The exile-permanent effect mirrors the narrative beat of unmaking the Elder Dragon's apparatus of control. `[Scryfall: story_spotlight]`, `[MTG Wiki: War of the Spark/Event]`, `[Wargamer: WAR story guide]`
- **Flavor text** — `"Liliana whispered to whatever consciousness Oketra and Bontu had left. 'You are the gods. He is the usurper. You know what to do.'"` — Liliana Vess, `[Scryfall]`
- **Design** — Art by Slawomir Maniak; the card was previewed by The Vorthos Cast in April 2019, a podcast focused on Magic's story and art, consistent with the card's story-spotlight status. `[Scryfall: artist + preview source]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — Played in roughly 30% of Orzhov (W/B) Commander decks on EDHREC (~99K of 327K decks tracked); a two-mana instant-speed exile that ignores indestructible is the appeal, with the mana-value-4-or-greater clause as the cost. `[EDHREC: Despark]`, `[EDHREC: Orzhov staples]`
- **Set lore** — War of the Spark's design conceit was that every rare and mythic included a planeswalker, which made story-spotlight uncommons like Despark the primary vehicle for non-planeswalker plot beats. `[MTG Wiki: War of the Spark]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — One of 5 "Triumph" cards in War of the Spark (Gideon WAR no. 015, Jace no. 055, Liliana no. 098, Chandra no. 121, Nissa no. 170): one per Gatewatch founder, one per color, all uncommon, all by Kieran Yanner. The MTG Wiki War of the Spark page explicitly frames the cycle as "a direct reflection of the Defeats from Hour of Devastation" (2017) — together they bookend the Gatewatch's arc against Bolas, with the Defeats showing each member at their lowest and the Triumphs showing each member's resolution `[MTG Wiki: War of the Spark, Cycles section]`. Nissa's specific bookend partner is Nissa's Defeat (HOU no. 123), also by Yanner, also `{2}{G}`, flavored with a Nicol Bolas quote `[Scryfall: Nissa's Defeat HOU 123]`.
- **Design** — Uncommon sorcery, `{G}{G}`, illustrated by Kieran Yanner (who also drew the matching HOU Defeats, unifying the two-set visual cycle), released 2019-05-03. No reprints — Scryfall's prints search returns a single printing for this oracle_id `[Scryfall]`. Preview was published 2019-04-10 via the community Twitter account @NissaCosplay, an unusual cosplayer-as-preview-source choice `[Scryfall preview field]`.
- **Flavor text** — `"Her triumph came not from destroying the unnatural, but from fostering life."` — no speaker attributed `[Scryfall flavor_text]`. All five Triumph cards share the templated `"X's triumph came not from Y, but from Z"` construction; Nissa's is the most pacifist of the five (Gideon and Chandra still frame triumph in opposition; Nissa reframes it as cultivation) `[Scryfall flavor_text fields, all five WAR Triumph cards]`.
- **Mechanical reputation** — `"Search your library for up to two basic Forest cards. If you control a Nissa planeswalker, instead search your library for up to three land cards. Reveal those cards, put them into your hand, then shuffle."` Without a Nissa walker it's a strictly-worse Cultivate (no land enters); WITH a Nissa walker it becomes a 3-of-any-land tutor at sorcery speed for `{G}{G}` — strong in dedicated Nissa-tribal Commander or Oathbreaker. Scryfall edhrec_rank 13,603 (mid-niche); legal in Commander / Modern / Legacy / Vintage / Pioneer, not legal in Standard or Pauper `[Scryfall oracle_text, edhrec_rank, legalities]`.

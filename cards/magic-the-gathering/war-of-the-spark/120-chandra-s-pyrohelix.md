## Trivia

- **Set context** — Released in *War of the Spark* (WAR) on 2019-05-03; the set centers on Nicol Bolas's Eternal army invading Ravnica with all 36 planeswalkers featured. Chandra was a frontline Gatewatch combatant in the conflict `[Scryfall API: set page]`.
- **Design** — Art by **Aleksi Briclot**, one of Chandra's signature artists — Briclot also painted the original 2007 *Chandra Nalaar* planeswalker (Lorwyn) and *Chandra's Phoenix* (M12), giving this common-slot spell unusual portrait-grade pedigree `[Scryfall API: artist credit]`.
- **Mechanical reputation** — Tracked in **6,569 Commander decks** on EDHREC, with top synergy partners including *Renegade Firebrand*, *Chandra's Revolution*, and *Furious Reprisal* — a budget red split-damage instant whose appeal is target flexibility (2-to-one or 1-and-1) at common rarity `[EDHREC: Chandra's Pyrohelix card page]`.
- **Flavor text** — *"Now that I've dealt with your little toys, I can focus on the real problem: you."* — Chandra Nalaar, to Dovin Baan `[Scryfall flavor_text]`. Dovin Baan is the Kaladesh-born planeswalker who defected to Bolas and engineered the Thopter constructs — the "little toys" the quote dismisses `[MTG Wiki: Chandra Nalaar; War of the Spark set narrative context]`.
- **Cycle context** — Part of the *Chandra's <Spell>* flavor cycle, currently spanning **17 cards** on Scryfall (incl. *Chandra's Outrage, Ignition, Phoenix, Embercat, Firemaw, Defeat, Spitfire, Triumph, Revolution, Magmutt, Pyreling, Regulator, Flame Wave, Fury, Incinerator*), all canonically depicting or attributed to Chandra Nalaar's pyromancy `[Scryfall search: name:"Chandra's"]`.

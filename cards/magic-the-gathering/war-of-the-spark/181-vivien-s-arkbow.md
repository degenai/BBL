## Trivia

- **Set context** — War of the Spark (released 2019-05-03) is the climactic set of the Ravnica story arc, depicting Nicol Bolas's attempt to harvest planeswalker sparks on Ravnica. Vivien Reid is among the planeswalkers who chose to remain on Ravnica after Chandra disabled the Immortal Sun, fighting alongside the Gatewatch. `[Scryfall: WAR release date]` `[MTG Wiki: Vivien Reid, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Lore — the Arkbow's origin** — The Arkbow survived the destruction of Vivien's home plane, Skalla, when Nicol Bolas attacked it. The Smaragdi (Vivien's people, forest-dwelling rangers and druids) and the Nura civilization combined their collective magic into the artifact, encoding an invocation of every creature that ever lived on Skalla. As the plane burned, Vivien's spark ignited and she planeswalked to safety with the bow. The glowing medallion-discs visible in the card art each represent a preserved species-spirit inside the weapon. `[MTG Wiki: Vivien Reid / Arkbow, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Design** — No flavor text is printed on this card `[Scryfall]`. It is typed as a Legendary Artifact — not an Equipment — mechanically framing the Arkbow as a standalone summoning engine rather than a weapon you attach to a creature. Art is by Zack Stella; the card was previewed on 2019-04-01 by Magic Pro Tour player Alexander Hayne via Twitter. `[Scryfall preview metadata]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — EDHREC rank 16090 `[Scryfall EDHREC link]`, placing the Arkbow in the long tail of Commander play. The X-cost creature-cheat effect is mechanically novel but requires discarding a card and tapping, which limits its competitive reach relative to more reliable green tutors. Legal in Pioneer, Modern, Historic, Commander, and Legacy. `[Scryfall legalities]`

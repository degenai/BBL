## Trivia

- **Set context** — Story-spotlight card in War of the Spark (Act 2), commemorating the moment Nissa animates Vitu-Ghazi and the colossal tree topples Bolas's statue on Ravnica — a brief defender rally before the God-Eternals enter the fight and turn the tide. `[Scryfall: story_spotlight: true]` `[MTG Wiki: Vitu-Ghazi]` `[MTG Wiki: War of the Spark]`
- **Flavor text** — `"Bolas cared little when his statue fell, thinking it a useful distraction, underestimating the surge of resolve it gave his opponents."` — narratorial (no in-fiction speaker); frames the toppling as a morale beat Bolas tactically dismissed. `[Scryfall flavor_text]`
- **Design** — Art by Sidharth Chaturvedi. Common in the WAR printing; reprinted at uncommon in Double Masters (2XM, 2020) with the same art and flavor text, though only the WAR printing keeps the story_spotlight flag. `[Scryfall prints index]`
- **Mechanical** — Three-mana white instant: tap any permanent, destroy it if it's an artifact, then cantrip. Legal in Modern, Pioneer, Pauper, Legacy, Vintage, and Commander; EDHREC tracks ~1,646 decks running it, ranking it well outside staple tier (~20,132) — a budget/flavor pick rather than a power-level inclusion. `[Scryfall legalities]` `[EDHREC]`
- **Story cluster** — Sits inside WAR's Bolas-Falls narrative cluster alongside Awakening of Vitu-Ghazi (the cause), Enter the God-Eternals (Bolas's response), The Elderspell (the macguffin), and Despark (Act 3 story-spotlight, same Bolas-defeat arc). The Act 2 story-spotlight cycle is: Pledge of Unity, Rally of Wings, Awakening of Vitu-Ghazi, Topple the Statue, Enter the God-Eternals, Spark Harvest, The Elderspell, Deliver Unto Evil. `[MTG Wiki: Story Spotlight]` `[epicstream: WAR Act 2 Story Spotlight]`

## Trivia

- **Flavor text** — `"To Liliana's surprise, the cost of betraying Bolas did not fall on her."` This line, sourced directly from the card, names both key figures and captures the WAR climax: Liliana broke her demonic contract with Nicol Bolas by turning his Dreadhorde armies against him — an act that should have killed her. Gideon stepped in and transferred the contract's death-curse onto himself using his hieromancy, dying in her place. `[Scryfall API: flavor_text]`

- **Set context** — Gideon's Sacrifice is marked as a Story Spotlight card in War of the Spark (released May 3, 2019), a designation Wizards reserves for cards depicting pivotal narrative moments in the set's story arc. War of the Spark was the culmination of Nicol Bolas's multi-set plan to harvest planeswalker sparks on Ravnica using the Immortal Sun. Gideon Jura's death is the emotional climax of that arc. `[Scryfall API: story_spotlight:true]` `[Wargamer: War of the Spark set guide]`

- **Design** — Art by Chris Rallis. The card's oracle text maps unusually literally onto the story moment: redirect all damage dealt to you and your permanents onto one chosen permanent — exactly the mechanical translation of Gideon absorbing the contract's killing blow. `[Scryfall API: artist, oracle_text]`

- **Mechanical reputation** — A {W} Common with EDHREC rank 5679, indicating modest Commander play. Sees broader use in Pauper and Pioneer formats as a damage-redirect tool. Received a reprint in the Murders at Karlov Manor Commander decks (MKC no. 68, 2024), confirming continued mechanical relevance in white's toolkit. `[Scryfall API: edhrec_rank, legalities]` `[Scryfall: MKC reprint]`

- **Lore context** — Gideon Jura (born Kytheon Iora of Akros) was one of the five founding members of the Gatewatch. Liliana Vess is one of the longest-running MTG planeswalker characters, first printed in Lorwyn (2007). Their dynamic in WAR — antagonist-turned-reluctant-ally redeemed by another's sacrifice — is a rare character beat for a Common card. `[Hipsters of the Coast: Gideon, Liliana and War of the Spark]` `[DailyEsports: WAR Story Recap Act III]`

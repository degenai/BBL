## Trivia

- **Set context** — War of the Spark (released 2019-05-03) is the climax set of the Nicol Bolas arc, set entirely on Ravnica as Bolas unleashes the Amonkhet Dreadhorde and harvests planeswalkers' sparks. Tamiyo arrived to observe and record events but was drawn into combat when she encountered God-Eternal Kefnet — the moment this card's art and flavor text depict. `[Scryfall: WAR release date]` `[Card Kingdom Blog: MTG Character Profile Tamiyo]`
- **Flavor text** — "Tamiyo wished only to observe the war, but she soon realized neutrality was not an option." Tamiyo is a Soratami scholar-planeswalker from Kamigawa whose magical practice is rooted in storytelling and scroll-reading. The Dreadhorde invasion ended that neutrality. `[Scryfall flavor_text]`
- **Art identification** — The massive divine figure with the elongated ceremonial head, spread wings, and staff is consistent with God-Eternal Kefnet, one of Bolas's reanimated Amonkhet gods. The WAR lore specifically identifies a confrontation with Kefnet as the event that broke Tamiyo's neutrality. The small moonfolk-scale figure floating in clouds below is Tamiyo herself. `[Card Kingdom Blog: MTG Character Profile Tamiyo]`
- **Mechanical profile** — Common Sorcery: Scry 4, then draw two cards ({3}{U}). Legal in Pauper, Modern, Pioneer, Legacy, and Commander. EDHREC rank 10951 — solid Pauper-tier card selection but below the staple threshold for Commander. Art by Lake Hurwitz. `[Scryfall]` `[EDHREC rank]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Released in *War of the Spark* (WAR) on 2019-05-03; the set centers on Nicol Bolas's invasion of Ravnica with his Eternal army of lazotep-encased zombies. Ral defects from Bolas's payroll and turns on the Eternals during the battle, which is the in-fiction setup for this card's flavor line `[Scryfall API: set page]` `[dotesports.com: Legendary Planeswalker Ral Zarek]` `[Card Kingdom: MTG Character Profile: Ral Zarek]`.
- **Design** — Art by **Joseph Meehan**, depicting Ral mid-cast unleashing forked blue-white lightning. The character's storm-mage identity is canon: per MTG Wiki, Ral's planeswalk is heralded by a crack of lightning, and he controls electrical storms based on Niv-Mizzet's equations `[Scryfall API: artist credit]` `[MTG Wiki: Ral Zarek, via WebSearch snippet]`.
- **Mechanical reputation** — Uncommon UR instant for {2}{U}{R}: 3 damage to any target plus a top-two impulse-style card-select (one to hand, one to graveyard). Rated **tier A+** in War of the Spark Limited draft tier lists as a strong removal-plus-card-advantage two-for-one in the Izzet color pair `[MTG Arena Zone: WAR draft guide]` `[Untapped.gg: WAR Limited draft pick order]`.
- **Flavor text** — *"Time to find the melting point of lazotep."* — Ral Zarek `[Scryfall flavor_text]`. Lazotep is the alchemical material Bolas used to encase his Eternal army; Ral, an Izzet pyrothermal experimenter, is framing the battle as a materials-science problem — a characteristically Izzet way to declare a betrayal `[bacejelerenvorthos Tumblr: War of the Spark Flavor Spotlight on Ral's Outburst, via WebSearch snippet]`.
- **Cycle context** — Part of the *Ral's <Spell>* attribution cycle alongside *Ral's Dispersal* (RNA), *Ral's Staticaster* (RNA), and *Ral's Vanguard* — all spells canonically attributed to the Izzet planeswalker. *Ral, Storm Conduit* in the same WAR set is his named planeswalker card; lore-wise, Ral, Jace, and Vraska resurrect Niv-Mizzet as Ravnica's protector by the storyline's end `[Scryfall search: name:"Ral's"]` `[MTG Wiki: Ral Zarek, via WebSearch snippet]`.

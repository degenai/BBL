## Trivia

- **Set context** — Anointer of Valor is a CMR-original card (Scryfall: reprint: false) — its first and only printing. It is an unnamed angel support piece designed specifically for CMR's draft environment, which reserved named character identity for legendary slots while the commons cohort filled generic fantastical archetypes. `[Scryfall]`
- **Flavor text** — "Light streaked down from the heavens, and a voice whispered in the soldier's ear: 'You shall not fall today.'" The speaker is the angel itself but unnamed — the line is written from the soldier's perspective, reinforcing the card's mechanical role as a support piece that blesses whoever attacks. `[Scryfall]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — A 3/5 flying Angel for six mana. The activated trigger (pay three mana when any creature attacks to put a +1/+1 counter on it) is expensive for the effect. EDHREC rank 20578 — almost no competitive Commander play. Functions primarily as a draft-environment filler in CMR limited. `[Scryfall]`
- **Design** — Artist Antonio Jose Manzanedo's composition — angel in descent, eyes downcast, staff vertical, light pouring from above — closely follows classical devotional iconography, specifically the annunciation pose of Western religious painting. The figure is anonymous by design. `[Scryfall]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Dragon Trainer is a generic unnamed Human from Magic: The Gathering Foundations (FDN, November 2024). The Foundations set was designed as a long-print-run entry-point set with a five-year Standard legality window (2024-2029), replacing the Core Set format discontinued after M21. The card has no named character associated — the depicted figure is anonymous. `[Scryfall]`
- **Flavor text** — "I was hardly a month old when he hatched. We grew up together. Even shared a crib!" `[Scryfall]`. The first-person voice frames the bond as familial rather than professional — the trainer raised the dragon from hatching, placing the relationship closer to siblings than handler and beast.
- **Mechanical** — Mana cost `{3}{R}{R}`, a five-mana red creature with 1/1 body; on entry creates a 4/4 red Dragon token with flying — the token doing the combat work the trainer's stats cannot. EDHREC rank 16564. Legal in Standard, Pioneer, Modern, Legacy, Vintage, and Commander. `[Scryfall]`
- **Design** — Art by Steve Prescott, who has illustrated 234 MTG cards since 2006, with work spanning core sets, commander products, and multiple expansions. Prescott's background includes Dungeons & Dragons interior art, Paizo Pathfinder, and World of Warcraft TCG; Dragon Trainer sits in a long career of humanoid-companion-beast portraits. `[Wikipedia: Steve Prescott (artist)]` `[Gatherer: artist search Steve Prescott]`
- **Design** — The 4/4 red Dragon creature token generated by Dragon Trainer is illustrated by Alexander Ostrowski on the Foundations Tokens sheet (TFDN no. 16) — a distinct commission from the main card art. `[Scryfall: TFDN 16]` `[WebSearch snippet]`

## Trivia

- **Design** — Frenzied Goblin was first printed in Ravnica: City of Guilds (RAV, October 2005) and has been reprinted across eight distinct sets through 2024: Magic 2015 (M15), Duel Decks: Speed vs. Cunning, Masters 25 (A25), GRN Guild Kit, Jumpstart 2022, Ravnica: Clue Edition, and Foundations (FDN). Nineteen years of continuous reprint is a mark of mechanical staying power for a one-drop. `[Scryfall: print history]`
- **Flavor text** — "The upside to not thinking about the consequences is that you'll always surprise those who do." `[Scryfall]`. Reads as a design philosophy statement for red's impulsive aggression identity — low deliberation, maximum disruption.
- **Mechanical** — Pauper-legal despite the FDN uncommon printing: the card was downshifted from uncommon to common in Masters 25 (A25, 2018, no. 131), and Pauper legality requires only one common printing anywhere in the card's history. The `{R}`-to-prevent-blocking ability is a soft evasion effect that remains a core tool in Pauper Goblins' tempo game plan. `[Scryfall: A25 printing]` `[WebSearch: Card Kingdom Blog — Pauper Goblins deck week]`
- **Commander inclusion** — EDHREC tracks Frenzied Goblin across approximately 10,507 Commander decks (0.25% inclusion). Its single highest-synergy home is **Krenko, Mob Boss** (EDH mono-red goblins), where it appears in roughly 15% of Krenko lists — the tap-a-blocker ability pairs cleanly with Krenko's mass-goblin-token swarm. `[EDHREC: Frenzied Goblin, via WebSearch snippet]`

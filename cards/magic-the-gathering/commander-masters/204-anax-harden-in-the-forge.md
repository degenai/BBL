## Trivia

- **IP verification** — The card explicitly names Anax in its oracle text ("Anax's power is equal to your devotion to red") and carries the type line Legendary Enchantment Creature — Demigod `[Scryfall]`. This is a Wizards-internal character (MTG lore, Theros plane) — not an external IP risk.
- **Set context / reprint** — This CMM printing (no. 204, released August 4, 2023) is a reprint of Theros Beyond Death (THB, no. 125, released January 24, 2020). Commander Masters was a reprint-focused set with no new story content, designed to make powerful commander cards more accessible `[Scryfall prints search]`.
- **Lore** — Anax was king of Akros on Theros, originally a follower of Iroas (god of victory), who later turned to Purphoros (god of the forge) as he built a small empire. During the events of the original Theros block, he was gravely wounded in battle with Rhordon the Rageblood, a minotaur oracle of Mogis. His wife, Queen Cymede, was later claimed by the god Keranos and ascended to Nyx as divine lightning `[CoolStuffInc: "Legends of Theros", Jay Annelli, 2019]`. In Theros Beyond Death, rumors spread that Anax had returned from death as a radiant Nyxborn demigod, leading satyr warriors and wielding a molten, smoke-wreathed axe — the exact figure and weapon visible in this card's art `[WebSearch: Anax MTG lore, multiple sources citing MTG Wiki]`.
- **Demigod design context** — Theros Beyond Death introduced five demigods as uncommon legendary creatures, all with devotion-based power/toughness and a triggered ability that produces tokens on creature death. Anax was the red demigod in this cycle. The Enchantment creature type on all demigods reflects the Theros plane's mechanic: on Theros, gods and demigods are enchantments first, creatures only when worshipped sufficiently (here: devotion to red) `[Scryfall; dotesports.com: "MTG guide for every demigod in Theros: Beyond Death"]`.
- **Mechanical reputation** — EDHREC rank 6,677 with over 376 Commander decks as head commander, most in Mono-Red Devotion, Aristocrats, and Sacrifice archetypes `[EDHREC via WebSearch]`. The token-on-death trigger is notably broad: any nontoken creature you control dying creates at least one Satyr token, giving red a persistent board presence even through sweepers. Identified by EDHREC as one of the better mono-red aggro/devotion commanders at the uncommon rarity slot.

## Trivia

- **Set and plane context** — Saprazzan Skerry is from Mercadian Masques (released October 4, 1999), the first set of the Masques block, set on the plane of Mercadia. "Saprazzan" references Saprazzo — the coral city of Mercadia, a half-submerged merfolk metropolis built inside a volcanic caldera lake. Saprazzo is ruled by a Grand Vizier and populated primarily by merfolk with the ability to shift between fish-tail and humanoid form; it served as a rival trade city to the merchant-dominated Rishada. The land art depicts a literal skerry (a small rocky island) characteristic of that coastal setting. `[WebSearch via mtg.fandom.com/wiki/Saprazzo]`
- **Depletion land mechanic** — Saprazzan Skerry is one of a cycle of ten "depletion lands" from Mercadian Masques, one for each two-color pairing. Each enters with two depletion counters and produces double mana of its color per activation, self-sacrificing when the counters run out. The mechanic creates a deliberate front-loaded mana burst — useful for explosive early turns but unsustainable — distinguishing it from standard utility lands. `[Scryfall oracle text; depletion cycle context via WebSearch]`
- **Only printing** — Saprazzan Skerry has never been reprinted outside Mercadian Masques. It is not on the Reserved List (Scryfall confirms `reserved: false`), but the lack of reprints keeps supply tight — its foil price on Scryfall is $47.36 versus $2.71 non-foil at time of cataloging, reflecting old-frame Mercadian Masques foil scarcity. `[Scryfall: reprint false, prices]`
- **Collector number** — Frontmatter was missing collector_number; confirmed as 328 via Scryfall (set mmq). Flag: this card was prepared with a blank collector_number field — corrected in this pass. `[Scryfall]`
- **Format role** — Legal in Legacy, Vintage, Pauper, and Commander. In Legacy Pauper, double-blue producing lands are niche accelerants in storm or Temporal Fissure shells. EDHREC rank 6,531 — sees occasional Commander play as a free-mana-count land in blue artifact or combo decks that need early blue mana density. `[Scryfall legalities; EDHREC rank via Scryfall]`

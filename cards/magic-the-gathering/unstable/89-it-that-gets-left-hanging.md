# It That Gets Left Hanging (Unstable)

## Vision

![89-it-that-gets-left-hanging](../../_images/magic-the-gathering/unstable/89-it-that-gets-left-hanging.png)

A lumpy, pale fleshy creature dominates the frame. From its swirled, featureless face protrude multiple glass lightbulbs — some lit, some dark — held in place by metal clamps. It extends a right arm that terminates in a burning, fiery claw reaching toward what appears to be a second creature's glowing hand on the far right. The creature's body has exposed rib-like structures and a segmented texture. The background glows in deep reds and oranges, giving a volcanic or infernal ambiance. The concept is the card's joke premise: a high-five left hanging — hence the reaching gesture.

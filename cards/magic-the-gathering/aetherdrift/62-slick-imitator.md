## Trivia

- **Set context** — Aetherdrift (DFT) is the 103rd MTG expansion, released 2025-02-14, themed as the Ghirapur Grand Prix: a multiplanar race across Avishkar (post-revolution Kaladesh), Amonkhet, and Muraganda, built around the new keywords Start your engines!, Max speed, and Exhaust. The set's ten Limited archetypes map to ten in-fiction racing teams; the shark-folk pictured here race for The Chordatan Keelhaulers (UR), an interplanar shark-pirate crew sponsored by Kari Zev and captained by Captain Howler, Sea Scourge. `[MTG Wiki: Aetherdrift]` `[mtgrocks.com: Captain Howler, Sea Scourge writeup]` `[butwhytho.net: Aetherdrift's Grand Prix Teams]`
- **Set lore** — Aetherdrift gives an official name to MTG's previously unnamed humanoid-shark race: **Chordatans**, per the Planeswalker's Guide. Chordatans are endemic to Avishkar's coastal regions, arrived via an instanced Omenpath, and have a strong affinity for aether (which they use as fuel for their aethercycles). Wizards' guide explicitly notes hammerhead variants among the new race — which is what the imitator here is impersonating. `[Commander's Herald: Maro's Aetherdrift Teaser Confirms New Creature Type]` `[mtgrocks.com: Aetherdrift Gives Official Name To MTG Shark People]`
- **Design** — Despite the name and the obvious mimicry premise, Slick Imitator's printed creature type is **Creature — Ooze**, NOT Shapeshifter. The only flavor signal that this figure is the imitator (not the imitated) is its translucent gelatinous body. Scryfall's API returns no flavor_text — the art is the whole story. `[Scryfall: Slick Imitator]`
- **Artist** — Art by Xabi Gaztelua, a Bilbao-based fantasy illustrator working for Wizards of the Coast, Wise Wizard Games, Brotherwise, and Arcana Games across TCG/TTRPG/board-game projects. Roughly 43 MTG illustrations to his credit; Slick Imitator is one of his Aetherdrift contributions alongside Diversion Unit (no. 41). `[Scryfall artist search a:'Xabi Gaztelua']` `[xabigaztelua.com]`
- **Mechanical** — `{1}{U}` 1/3 Creature — Ooze with Start your engines! and `Max speed — {1}, Sacrifice this creature: Copy target spell you control` (new targets allowed). EDHREC tracks 2,775 Commander decks running it at rank no. 17419; its top home is **Vnwxt, Verbose Host** at 917 of 5,264 decks (17.42% inclusion) — Aetherdrift's mono-blue Octopus commander. Secondary homes: Mendicant Core, Guidelight (2.88%) and Captain Howler, Sea Scourge (1.59%, the Keelhaulers' own captain). `[EDHREC: Slick Imitator]` `[Scryfall: edhrec_rank]`
- **Rulings** — WotC clarifications worth noting for play: speed is tracked per-player (one player at max speed doesn't push another); speed only changes when a spell or ability says so; and Start your engines! is a state-based action, not a triggered ability — so the moment Slick Imitator enters, your speed snaps to at least 1 automatically. Max speed is 4. `[Scryfall rulings: WotC, 2025-02-07]`

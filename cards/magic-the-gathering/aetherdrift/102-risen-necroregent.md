## Trivia

- **Set context** — Aetherdrift (DFT) is MTG's 103rd expansion, released February 14, 2025. The set spans three planes — Avishkar (formerly Kaladesh), Amonkhet, and Muraganda — during the Ghirapur Grand Prix, a cross-planar racing event organized by Avishkar's night minister Gonti. `[Scryfall: released_at 2025-02-14; Wizards Planeswalker's Guide to Aetherdrift Part 1, via WebSearch]`
- **Flavor text** — "In life, she commanded the respect of her army. In death, she demands it." — No named speaker. The line foregrounds the type line (Zombie Cat Knight): a figure who crossed death and retained command authority, now enforcing obedience rather than earning it. `[Scryfall]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — Risen Necroregent is a 5/4 for 5 mana (4B) that triggers Start Your Engines! and generates 2/2 Zombie tokens at max speed. EDHREC rank 17,372 — low Commander inclusion; its power lives in Standard and Pioneer race-to-max-speed strategies rather than the singleton format. `[Scryfall: edhrec_rank 17372]`

## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — "Greenbelt" is a direct Avishkar reference. The Ghirapur Grand Prix route tears through the greenbelt surrounding Ghirapur, and an Elf Ranger standing triumphant on wrecked racing vehicles reads as a saboteur defending the greenbelt from the race's destructive passage — a figure on the right side of an industrial war, just one with a vine-whip instead of a picket sign. `[Wizards Planeswalker's Guide to Aetherdrift Part 1 — Avishkar context, via WebSearch; Scryfall type_line Elf Ranger]`
- **Design** — Greenbelt Guardian has no prior printings and no flavor text. Wizards previewed it directly on January 28, 2025. Artist is Tianxing Xu. The Exhaust mechanic (new to Aetherdrift) allows the {3G} counter ability to fire once per permanent life — but if the Guardian leaves and re-enters the battlefield, it resets as a new object and Exhaust can trigger again. `[Scryfall: reprint: false, flavor_text absent, preview source Wizards of the Coast 2025-01-28, artist Tianxing Xu, oracle_text Exhaust ruling]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — A 2/2 Elf Ranger for 2 mana (1G) with a repeatable {G} trample-grant and a one-time {3G} to become a 5/5. EDHREC rank 16,345 — low Commander inclusion; the card lives in Standard green creature builds where the persistent trample-enabler ability earns its slot more than the Exhaust upgrade. `[Scryfall: edhrec_rank 16345]`

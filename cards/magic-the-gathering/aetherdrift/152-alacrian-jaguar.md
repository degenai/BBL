## Trivia

- **Set context** — "Alacrian" refers to Alacria, a plane linked to Avishkar by a stable, cyclically predictable Omenpath. The Alacrian Quickbeasts are one of Aetherdrift's ten racing teams: ancient, aether-fueled creatures of great intelligence that partner with chosen riders as embodiments of their home city districts' honor. The team's pairing — rider Caradora and Quickbeast Lagorin — was forced together by tragedy before the Grand Prix. Alacrian Jaguar is a rank-and-file Quickbeast foot soldier in that team's lore roster `[magic.wizards.com: Planeswalker's Guide to Aetherdrift Part 1; WebSearch snippet: Alacrian Quickbeasts MTG Wiki]`.
- **Mechanical** — A 4/4 Cat Mount for 5 mana (Green) with Vigilance and Saddle 1. While saddled and attacking it becomes a 6/6, and Vigilance means it doesn't tap to attack — making it both an attacker and a blocker on any given turn. Saddle 1 is the lowest threshold in the set; essentially any creature qualifies as a rider. Scryfall confirms Pauper legality, making this one of the few Aetherdrift Mounts playable at the Pauper floor. EDHREC rank 21,789. No flavor text `[Scryfall: card 2bd40bca]`.
- **Design** — Art by Andrey Kuzinskiy. The Saddle mechanic returned in Aetherdrift after its debut in Outlaws of Thunder Junction (2024). Key distinction from the older Vehicle/Crew mechanic: Mounts are already creatures that can attack and block unsaddled; Saddle activates at sorcery speed (Crew works at instant speed). The Cat creature subtype adds tribal upside in Commander and has historically been one of Green's most supported creature types `[Scryfall: card 2bd40bca — artist; magic.wizards.com: Aetherdrift Mechanics]`.

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Roadside Assistance is a new card (not a reprint) first printed in Aetherdrift (February 2025). As a white Aura, it sits in an uncommon slot in the set's Vehicle/Mount-focused design space [Scryfall].
- **Mechanical note** — The Aura creates a 1/1 Pilot token with inflated crew power (counts as though power were 2 greater), making it a rare example of an Aura that generates a separate token on entry alongside the +1/+1 and lifelink buffs it grants. The Pilot token synergizes specifically with Crew and Saddle mechanics introduced in Aetherdrift [Scryfall: oracle text].
- **Flavor text** — `"No road has to be traveled alone."` `[Scryfall]` The line functions as thematic branding for the Aetherdrift set's cooperative racing framing — multiple teams in the Grand Prix form temporary alliances against external threats.

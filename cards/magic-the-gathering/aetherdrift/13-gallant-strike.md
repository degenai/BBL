## Trivia

- **Flavor text** — "The ghost shrieked as Terrin's blade sank deep. Perhaps a second death would teach it not to mess with his dog." — Unnamed narrator. "Terrin" appears to be a new Aetherdrift-specific character; no prior MTG card names this figure. The line frames the mechanic (destroying high-toughness creatures) as loyalty — a warrior defending his dog, not chasing glory. `[Scryfall: flavor_text field]`
- **Set / lore context** — Gallant Strike's desert canyon setting and ghost enemies align with the Amonkhet race segment in Aetherdrift. After the Hour of Devastation shattered the Hekma barrier, the Shefet desert outside Naktamun is accessible to mummies, wraiths, and undead — exactly the threat Terrin and his dog face on the Grand Prix route. `[Wizards Planeswalker's Guide to Aetherdrift Part 1 — Amonkhet, via WebSearch; Scryfall set_name Aetherdrift]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — Gallant Strike costs 2 mana (1W) and destroys creatures with toughness 4 or greater — conditional removal that misses small utility creatures but catches most format-relevant threats. Cycling 2 provides the safety valve. EDHREC rank 16,003 — low Commander inclusion; primarily a Limited card and occasional sideboard piece. `[Scryfall: edhrec_rank 16003]`

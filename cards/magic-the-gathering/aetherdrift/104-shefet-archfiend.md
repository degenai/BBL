## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — "Shefet" is a geographic name from Amonkhet: the desert region outside the magical Hekma barrier where demons, mummies, and other terrors roam unchecked. Amonkhet is one of the three planes hosting the Ghirapur Grand Prix in Aetherdrift. After the Hour of Devastation, Naktamun has been rebuilding; the Shefet desert remains dangerous and demon-haunted. `[MTG Salvation: The World of Amonkhet, via WebSearch; Scryfall set_name Aetherdrift]`
- **Flavor text** — "It yields only to the gods themselves." — No named speaker. In post-Hour of Devastation Amonkhet lore, surviving gods include Hazoret, the Locust God, and the Scarab God, alongside new deities Ketramose and Sab-Sunen. The flavor text places this archfiend squarely beneath the divine tier — powerful enough to dominate everything else in the desert, but bound to the gods. `[Scryfall: flavor_text field; Wizards Planeswalker's Guide to Aetherdrift Part 1 — Amonkhet god context, via WebSearch]`
- **Design** — Artist is GodMachine (credited on Scryfall), known for bold, high-contrast graphic-art demon illustrations. The flat linework and warm red-orange palette visible in the Vision section are consistent with GodMachine's portfolio style. Shefet Archfiend has no prior printings — first print is DFT 104, February 2025. `[Scryfall: artist GodMachine, reprint: false]`

## Trivia

- **IP context** — The flavor text names both "Daretti" and "Pia" explicitly. Pia Nalaar is canonically present in Aetherdrift as co-founder and Chief Engineer of the Aether Rangers racing team; her named card "Pia Nalaar, Chief Mechanic" appears in the Aetherdrift Commander decks (DRC, card no. 2) [Scryfall]. Daretti also has a named card in the main set: "Daretti, Rocketeer Engineer" (DFT, no. 120), a Legendary Goblin Artificer who leads the Goblin Rocketeers [Scryfall].
- **Mechanical note** — Tune Up is a new card (not a reprint) first printed in Aetherdrift (released 2025-02-14). Its effect — returning an artifact from the graveyard and animating Vehicles as artifact creatures — dovetails directly with Daretti's signature graveyard-artifact recursion ability on "Daretti, Scrap Savant" [Scryfall], making the flavor text pairing mechanically resonant as well as narrative.
- **Flavor text** — `"For most people? The odds would be insurmountable," Daretti said. "But we're not most people," Pia replied.` — Daretti and Pia Nalaar. `[Scryfall]`
- **Set context** — Aetherdrift (set code DFT) is the 103rd Magic: The Gathering expansion, released February 14, 2025. The set is structured around ten racing teams from across the Multiverse competing in the Ghirapur Grand Prix; the Aether Rangers represent Avishkar's new post-revolutionary government, the Avishkar Assembly [MTG Wiki: Aetherdrift].

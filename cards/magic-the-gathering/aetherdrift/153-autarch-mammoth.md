## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Autarch Mammoth is a Muraganda card. Muraganda is a wild plane hosting massive fauna, dinosaurs, and giant flora — one of the three race stages in the Ghirapur Grand Prix, though it fields no team of its own. The "Autarch" in the name points to the Saurid Autocracy, the dominant faction on Muraganda that deploys mammoths in raids. `[Wizards Planeswalker's Guide to Aetherdrift Part 1 — Muraganda context, via WebSearch; WebSearch snippet citing GamesRadar preview: "The Mammoths of Muraganda are used in raids by the Saurid Autocracy"]`
- **Design** — Autarch Mammoth was previewed exclusively by GamesRadar on January 23, 2025 — two weeks before the February 14, 2025 release date. No prior printings. No flavor text. Artist is Leonardo Santanna. `[Scryfall: preview source gamesradar 2025-01-23, reprint: false, flavor_text absent, artist Leonardo Santanna]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — A 5/5 Elephant Mount for 6 mana (4GG) that generates a 3/3 Elephant token on entry and on each saddled attack — approximately 8/8 worth of stats on entry. Saddle 5 is a steep requirement. EDHREC rank 19,106 — lowest inclusion in this batch; outside dedicated creature-heavy green builds, the Saddle cost makes it hard to activate reliably. `[Scryfall: edhrec_rank 19106; Draftsim limited review via WebSearch: "a really strong uncommon that does a decent Titan impression"]`

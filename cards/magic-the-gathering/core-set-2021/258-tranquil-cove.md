## Trivia

- **Cycle origin and clan context** — Tranquil Cove is the White-Blue member of the ten-card gain-land cycle that debuted in Khans of Tarkir (September 2014). In that set, White-Blue is the color identity of the Ojutai clan — scholarly monks devoted to knowledge and discipline — a thematic alignment with the card's quiet, empty coastal composition. `[Scryfall; MTG Wiki: Khans of Tarkir, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Two John Avon artworks** — John Avon illustrated two distinct versions of Tranquil Cove. The M21 copy carries the original KTK coastal-bluff-and-cove composition (Scryfall illustration ID ff440765). Fate Reforged (2015) introduced a separate Avon piece (illustration ID 7fefa068). The KTK composition was carried forward only into M21; subsequent reprints in Ikoria, Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty, March of the Machine, and others commissioned new art from different artists entirely. `[Scryfall illustration_id data]`
- **Artist** — John Avon is one of Magic's most prolific long-term contributors. As of Throne of Eldraine (2019), he had illustrated 323 different Magic cards including more than 100 distinct basic lands across a continuous 23-year relationship with Wizards of the Coast. His original Lotus Field painting sold for $40,000 on the MTG Art Market (2019). He received a dedicated Secret Lair Artist Series release in October 2023 featuring new artworks: Emrakul, the Promised End; Progenitus; Brainstorm; and Serra Angel. `[Hipsters of the Coast: "Secret Lair Artist Series: John Avon," September 2023; Hipsters of the Coast: "Painting for Lotus Field by John Avon Sells for $40,000," 2019]`
- **Reprint history and format role** — 19 total printings as of Tarkir: Dragonstorm (2025) — the highest count among the five M21 gain lands in this batch — including two Warhammer 40,000 Commander printings, Tales of Middle-earth Commander, and Kaldheim Commander. Legal in Standard (M21 window), Pioneer, Modern, Legacy, Vintage, Pauper, Pauper Commander, and Commander. EDHREC rank: 1069. `[Scryfall prints search, oracle ID 5d641bf6; EDHREC rank 1069 via Scryfall API]`

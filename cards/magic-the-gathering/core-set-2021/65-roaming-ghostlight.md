## Trivia

- **Set context** — Core Set 2021 (M21, released July 3, 2020) centers on Teferi and reintroduced phasing to Standard for the first time since the Weatherlight era. Roaming Ghostlight is one of M21's blue common Spirits — a creature type that appears across blue and white in the set as restless, undirected souls `[Scryfall]`.
- **Printing history** — First printed in M21. A reprint appeared in Ravnica: Clue Edition (CLU, 2024), placing it among the handful of M21 commons chosen for that mystery-themed crossover product `[Scryfall; frontmatter manual_review_reason note]`.
- **Design** — Artist Zezhou Chen illustrated Roaming Ghostlight digitally. Per Hipsters of the Coast (April 2019), Chen's Wardscale Crocodile (War of the Spark) was his first-ever traditional painting for Magic — before that card, all 37+ of his MTG illustrations were digital `[Hipsters of the Coast — "Art for Wardscale Crocodile by Zezhou Chen Sells for $1,500," 2019]`.
- **Flavor text** — `"Some who die senseless deaths return as formless spirits, seeking answers where there are none."` — no attributed speaker; the line frames the spirit as grief-driven and unresolvable rather than malevolent `[Scryfall]`.
- **Mechanical reputation** — EDHREC rank 26,349. At {3}{U}{U} the 3/2 flier with a bounce trigger is costed for Limited rather than Constructed. The non-Spirit clause on the bounce makes it an opt-in Spirit-tribal enabler in Pauper blue-skies shells. Format legal in Pauper, Commander, Pioneer, and Modern `[Scryfall EDHREC rank field; Scryfall legalities]`.

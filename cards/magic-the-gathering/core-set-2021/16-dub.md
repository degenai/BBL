# Dub (Core Set 2021)

## Vision

![16-dub](../../_images/magic-the-gathering/core-set-2021/16-dub.png)

Two figures occupy a grand interior space, lit from behind by tall stained-glass windows. The standing figure wears a flowing white gown and holds a sword flat — an unmistakable dubbing gesture. The kneeling figure is in full plate armor with a red cape pooling on the stone floor, head bowed reverently. The stained-glass panels cast diamond-shaped patches of color across the hazy, golden atmosphere. The moment is formal, ceremonial, and charged with a sense of elevation and loyalty.

**Subject:** A knighting ceremony: a standing figure in a long white dress holds a sword horizontally, touching it toward a kneeling figure in red-caped plate armor, who bows their head in submission. Stained-glass windows backlight the scene in gold, teal, and amber.

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Dub was first printed in Dominaria (DOM, May 2018) before being reprinted in Core Set 2021 (M21, July 2020). Its flavor text — *"Rise, knight of New Benalia."* — roots it in the Dominaria setting, where New Benalia is the reconstituted Benalish nation that emerged after the Phyrexian Invasion, organized around seven knightly Houses and the Church of Serra. `[Scryfall]` `[MTG Wiki: Benalia, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Design** — Dub is an Aura that converts any creature into a Knight (in addition to its other types), giving it +2/+2 and first strike. This type-granting effect is the card's mechanical identity: it exists specifically to enable Knight synergies in Dominaria's Knight-heavy white. As of 2026, Dub has exactly two printings (Dominaria no. 15 and Core Set 2021 no. 16) with identical art by Bastien L. Deharme across both. `[Scryfall prints search]`
- **Flavor text** — *"Rise, knight of New Benalia."* The speaker is unnamed but the phrasing is that of a formal conferral of knighthood — the same ritual formulation that defines the card's art (sword on shoulder, kneeling figure). The New Benalia context names a specific post-Invasion MTG plane location: Benalia rebuilt on Dominaria as a military-mercantile power after the Phyrexian catastrophe. `[Scryfall]` `[MTG Wiki: Benalia, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Artist** — Bastien L. Deharme (full name: Bastien Lecouffe Deharme) is a French digital artist known for dark, atmospheric, painterly imagery. The Dub commission is notably quieter in register than his typical work; no dedicated artist feature on Hipsters of the Coast was found. `[ArtStation: Bastien Lecouffe Deharme]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — EDHREC rank 18,112 indicates low Commander uptake; the card finds its home primarily in budget Pauper and Pioneer Knight-tribal lists where the type-granting +2/+2 first-strike aura is an efficient enabler. Legal in Pauper, Pioneer, Modern, Legacy, Vintage, and Commander. `[Scryfall]`

A medium-sized canine elemental composed entirely of fire stands in profile against a dark, smoke-choked background. Its body is rendered as twisting orange and yellow flame, with a defined snout, ears, and bushy tail all flickering as though caught mid-motion. The palette is dominated by reds, oranges, and yellows against deep black and charred brown, giving the piece a strong ember-against-night silhouette. Chandra herself is not depicted in the artwork; only her elemental companion appears.

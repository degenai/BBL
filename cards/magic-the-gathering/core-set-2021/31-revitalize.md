## Trivia

- **Set context / reprint history** — Revitalize debuted in Core Set 2019 (July 2018), reprinted in Core Set 2021 (July 2020), again in Kaldheim (February 2021), and received two Strixhaven Mystical Archive printings (April 2021) — including a Japanese alternate-art version (card no. 072) available in Japanese collector boosters. It was reprinted again in Foundations Jumpstart (November 2024). The Mystical Archive treatment elevated a functional common to premium product, reflecting how low-rarity draw-plus-life cards stay in rotation. `[Scryfall]`
- **Flavor text** — *"A potion is no substitute for a skilled surgeon, but it will hold your bits in." — Torricks, battlefield medic*. Torricks is a named but otherwise unattested figure in Wizards lore — the name appears only in this flavor text across all Magic printings. The voice is pragmatic, anti-romantic about magic: the potion as damage-control, not salvation. `[Scryfall flavor_text]`
- **Artist** — Matt Stewart has created over 200 Magic: The Gathering illustrations. His work has been recognized with a Chesley Award nomination for "Master Trinketeer" (Kaladesh), and Hipsters of the Coast cited "Waste Not" (from the "You Make the Card" community contest) as a personal favorite of his. The M21 Revitalize art is one of his more intimate narrative pieces — a single figure in low light rather than his wider fantasy tableaux. `[Hipsters of the Coast: "The Best Magic The Gathering Artworks Since 2012", 2022-09; "A Look Inside Whimsy, Wonder & War", 2019-11]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — Revitalize holds an EDHREC rank of 4156, appearing in a substantial number of lifegain-payoff Commander builds. Its baseline ({1}{W}: gain 3 life, draw a card) is competitive enough that it has been a recurrent limited staple at common across four years of printings. `[Scryfall]` `[EDHREC rank, Scryfall]`

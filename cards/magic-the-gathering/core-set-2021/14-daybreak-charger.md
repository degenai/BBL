A unicorn with a dark body charges forward across a shallow reflective surface — water or wet ground — at dusk. Its mane and tail billow outward as flowing golden-luminous energy, trailing bright sparks and wisps of warm light. The single horn is clearly visible, pale and upright. The hooves emit glowing embers or sparks on contact with the ground. The background is a dusky twilight sky in muted purples and warm amber, with the silhouettes of distant cliffs or rock formations faintly visible on either side. The creature has a wild, untamed energy despite the absence of any rider or tack — it moves as a natural force of light rather than a domesticated mount.

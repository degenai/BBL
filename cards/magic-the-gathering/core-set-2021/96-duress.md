## Trivia

- **Print lineage** — Duress is one of the most reprinted cards in MTG history. First printed in Urza's Saga (October 1998, illustrated by Lawrence Snelly), the card has accumulated at least 39 distinct printings across 27 years as of 2025, spanning core sets, duel decks, event deck promos, Masters sets, Arena promos, Secret Lair, Strixhaven Mystical Archive, and Foundations `[Scryfall prints search]`. The Scryfall record marks this M21 copy as `reprint: true` `[Scryfall]`.
- **Artist dominance** — Steven Belledin illustrated Duress across at least 17 distinct printings from 2009 (Duel Decks: Divine vs. Demonic) through at least 2023 (Dominaria Remastered), making his version the single most-reprinted artwork on the card `[Scryfall prints search]`. Belledin offers signed artist proofs of this artwork through his official website `[stevenbelledin.com]`. The M21 copy carries Belledin's art and the flavor text "Resistance is unthinkable." `[Scryfall]`.
- **Competitive standing** — Duress is a staple discard spell across Legacy, Vintage, Pauper, Modern, Pioneer, and Standard formats; its M21 printing is currently Standard-legal via Foundations `[Scryfall: legalities]`. EDHREC rank 3,236 — among the highest-included black one-drops in Commander `[Scryfall: edhrec_rank field]`. Forum discussion on MTGSalvation's Legacy Staples thread lists Duress as a core Legacy black-sideboard staple particularly effective against combo and control `[mtgsalvation.com: A List of Legacy Staples, Legacy Archives]`. Its main limitation versus Thoughtseize and Inquisition of Kozilek is the miss rate against creature-heavy opponents (it can only take noncreature, nonland cards) `[tappedout.net: Inquisition or Duress, Modern forum]`.
- **Flavor text** — "Resistance is unthinkable." — no speaker attributed `[Scryfall]`. The three-word flavor line is among the more compressed in the game; the lack of attribution heightens the sense of omniscient coercive force rather than an individual speaker.
- **Art variety** — Other artists with Duress printings include Pete Venters (Seventh Edition, 2001), Jason Rainville (Dragons of Tarkir, 2015), Lucas Graciano (Ixalan, 2017), Kristina Collantes and Romiy (Strixhaven Mystical Archive, 2021), Paul Scott Canavan (Innistrad: Midnight Hunt, 2021), and PINDURSKI (Phyrexia: All Will Be One, 2023) `[Scryfall prints search]`.

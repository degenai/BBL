## Trivia

- **Set context** — Watcher of the Spheres is an uncommon Bird Wizard from Core Set 2021, released July 3, 2020. It was previewed on June 12, 2020, by Limited Resource host and Magic commentator Marshall Sutcliffe via Twitter (verified by Scryfall's preview field). The card carries no flavor text, consistent with some M21 uncommons where mechanical complexity crowds the text box. `[Scryfall]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — Watcher of the Spheres has two relevant abilities: it reduces the cost of creature spells with flying you cast by one generic mana, and it gets +1/+1 until end of turn whenever another creature you control with flying enters. At {W}{U} it is a 2/2 that quickly grows in flying-heavy boards. EDHREC rank is 4,303, placing it among the more-played M21 uncommons in Commander — appearing in approximately 32,000 tracked Commander decks according to EDHREC aggregation. Primary homes include Alela, Artful Provocateur (Esper flying tokens), Kangee, Sky Warden (Bird tribal), and Choco, Seeker of Paradise (Bird/landfall) strategies. `[Scryfall]` `[EDHREC: edhrec.com/cards/watcher-of-the-spheres, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Pauper EDH distinction** — Watcher of the Spheres is a multicolor uncommon, which in Pauper EDH (PDH) format makes it eligible as a commander. A "High Power PDH" decklist with Watcher of the Spheres as commander exists on Tappedout, indicating the card has a niche community of players who build around it specifically as a flying-tribal cost-reducer at the helm. `[Tappedout.net: "Watcher of the Spheres - High Power PDH", via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Artist** — Art by G-host Lee, a digital artist who has contributed to roughly 20 Magic cards (Gatherer listing). G-host Lee's work appears in Core Set 2020 as well (Mu Yanling, Sky Dancer), and is documented on Art of MTG and MTG Artist Connection. No Hipsters of the Coast artist spotlight found for G-host Lee. `[Scryfall]` `[Gatherer; WebSearch snippet via artofmtg.com]`

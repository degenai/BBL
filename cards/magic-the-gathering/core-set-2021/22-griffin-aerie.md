## Trivia

- **Set context** — Griffin Aerie is an uncommon enchantment from Core Set 2021 (M21), released July 3, 2020. The set centered its Legendary character cycle on Teferi, with Dominarian figures Barrin, Subira, Niambi, Radha, Jolrael, Mangara, and Kaervek. Griffin Aerie is plane-agnostic, fitting the core set design convention of flavor-text worldbuilding that implies a setting without naming a specific plane. `[Scryfall]` `[WebSearch snippet: CardKingdom Blog "Magic Core Sets — A Retrospective"]`
- **Flavor text** — `"When griffins started nesting atop the northern tower, the castellan worried they'd be a nuisance. Instead, they took the entire castle under their protection."` — no speaker attributed. The flavor reframes an apparent pest problem as an emergent mutualism: the griffins gain a nesting site, the castle gains aerial defense. This also serves as mechanical flavor — the card creates griffin tokens that protect the player. `[Scryfall]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — Griffin Aerie's trigger fires at the beginning of your end step if you gained 3 or more life that turn, creating a 2/2 white Griffin flying token. Crucially, the trigger checks total life gained for the turn regardless of when it happened or whether Griffin Aerie was in play for the gain. EDHREC rank is 5,015, placing it in the top tier of M21 uncommon Commander inclusions — appearing in approximately 25,100 decks across roughly 3.46 million tracked Commander decks (per EDHREC aggregate, via WebSearch snippet). Strong in Ayli, Eternal Pilgrim / Soul Sisters / Lathiel lifegain Commander strategies where gaining 3 life per turn is routine. `[Scryfall]` `[EDHREC: edhrec.com/cards/griffin-aerie, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Mechanical note (rules)** — The ability checks how much life was gained that turn, not net life (losses don't cancel gains). A player who gains 4 life and loses 10 still triggers Griffin Aerie. This was explicitly clarified in Scryfall-listed rulings for the card. `[Scryfall rulings / WebSearch snippet via mtgassist.com]`

## Trivia

- **Design / reprint history** — Sure Strike has 9 distinct Scryfall print entries from Battle for Zendikar (BFZ, 2015) through Foundations (FDN, 2024) — one reprint roughly every year for nine years. Foundations inclusion is the definitive signal: that set is Wizards' permanent beginner product, and Sure Strike's presence there confirms it is a "teaching card" — chosen because it demonstrates first strike cleanly enough to merit every new-player entry point. `[Scryfall prints search, unique=prints]`
- **Artist note** — The M21 printing is one of three illustrated by Jakub Kasper (also Battle for Zendikar and Core Set 2019). Other print artists include Steve Argyle (Guilds of Ravnica; The List 2024), Lie Setiawan (Innistrad: Crimson Vow and Innistrad: Double Feature), and Izzy (Ixalan; Foundations). Kasper has illustrated 3 of 9 versions — the most of any single artist in the card's print run. `[Scryfall prints search, artist fields]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — +3/+0 plus first strike for {1}{R} is a combat trick that wins nearly any blocked fight in limited: the buffed creature resolves its damage before the blocker swings, typically killing the blocker before taking any return damage. EDHREC rank: 10,266 — low Commander adoption, as expected for a combat trick whose home is limited and aggressive Constructed formats, not multiplayer. `[Scryfall EDHREC rank field]`
- **Flavor text** — "To survive imminent doom, it sometimes takes a foolhardy soul who acts first and fears later." No speaker attributed. The text renders first strike as survival instinct rather than martial discipline — the reckless swing, not the trained technique. `[Scryfall]`

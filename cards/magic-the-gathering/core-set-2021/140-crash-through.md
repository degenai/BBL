## Trivia

- **Set context + lore** [TIER-1: Scryfall; TIER-2: MTG Wiki via WebSearch] — Crash Through originated in Hour of Devastation (released 2017-07-14), the climax set of the Amonkhet block. The original HOU and Foundations (2024) printings carry flavor text: "We have to get out of the city, Djeru. All of us! Gather as many as you can." — Samut, former Tah-crop initiate. The line captures Samut leading survivors out of Naktamun as Nicol Bolas's Eternal army breaches the city — the Hour of Devastation itself. `[Scryfall: HOU/Foundations printings; MTG Wiki: Samut via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Reprint history** [TIER-1: Scryfall] — The M21 copy in inventory (this printing) carries no flavor text. Six distinct printings exist: Hour of Devastation (2017), Core Set 2019 (2018), The List (2019), Core Set 2021 (2020), Amonkhet Remastered (2020), Secret Lair Drop (2021, crossover flavor: "Uni! Uni! I knew you'd come back!" — Bobby), and Foundations (2024). The Secret Lair printing is the only one with entirely unrelated flavor content. `[Scryfall: prints search]`
- **Mechanical reputation** [TIER-1: Scryfall] — One-mana red sorcery granting all your creatures trample until end of turn plus drawing a card. EDHREC rank 2031 reflects consistent Commander inclusion across go-wide red and token builds. Pauper-legal and PauperCommander-legal, where cantripping pump at one mana is premium. The trample-grant affects creatures already in play at resolution — not ones played afterward. `[Scryfall]`
- **Design** [TIER-1: Scryfall] — Artist for the M21 printing: Izzy (Izzy Medrano), a recurring MTG illustrator known for kinetic figure-forward combat art. The card has been reprinted at least 7 times across paper and digital, making it one of red's most reprinted common cantrips. `[Scryfall: prints search]`

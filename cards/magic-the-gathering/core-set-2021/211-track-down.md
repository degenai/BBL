## Trivia

- **Set context** — Track Down is a common sorcery from Core Set 2021 (M21), released July 3, 2020. M21 was the twentieth Magic core set and centered its Legendary character suite around Teferi, Time Raveler — featuring Dominarian figures including Barrin, Subira, Niambi, Radha, Jolrael, Mangara, and Kaervek. Track Down is part of M21's green common suite and carries no in-world plane identifier, consistent with core sets' plane-agnostic design philosophy. `[Scryfall]` `[WebSearch snippet, CardKingdom Blog: "Magic Core Sets — A Retrospective"]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — Track Down functions as a selective cantrip: scry 3 clears the top of the library, then draws a card if the revealed card is a creature or land — covering the two card types green most wants to find. It is legal in Pauper, Paupercommander, Pioneer, Modern, Legacy, Vintage, and Commander. EDHREC rank is 15,068, placing it in the lower-frequency Commander picks. Its primary competitive home is Pauper, where green's selection suite is thin and scry-3 with conditional draw outperforms straight card draw at 2 mana for creature-heavy builds. `[Scryfall]`
- **Flavor text** — `"Scents fade and footprints wash away, but some trails can never be erased."` — no speaker attributed. The line frames the scry mechanic as a magical persistence of scent beyond the physical: what the eye misses, instinct and magic recover. `[Scryfall]`
- **Artist note** — Scott Murphy has illustrated 92 cards for Magic as of his Gatherer listing. Murphy's original paintings command significant prices in the MTG Art Market: his Basic Forest oil sold for $6,700 (2019), Expedition Map final painting for $6,100 with the associated sketch fetching $1,100 and reference map $3,275 (2020), and Opposition Agent for $22,000 (2020) — a career-record sale. Hipsters of the Coast documented each of these sales; his artifact work is specifically called out as "at the top of my list" by their writer. Track Down is a first printing common from a major set. `[Hipsters of the Coast: "Scott Murphy's Expedition Map Sells for $6,100"; "Opposition Agent from Commander Legends by Scott Murphy Sells for $22,000"]` `[Gatherer: artist:Scott Murphy, 92 cards]`

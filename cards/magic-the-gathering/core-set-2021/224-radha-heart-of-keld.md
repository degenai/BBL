## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Radha is the granddaughter of Keldon warlord Astor and an elf of Skyshroud — a Keldon/Skyshroud-elf hybrid character whose spark never ignited during the Time Spiral crisis. She later defeated the last Gathan warlord, became Grand Warlord of Keld, and abolished the Keldon practice of taking slaves as plunder. `[MTG Wiki: Radha, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Design history** — M21 is the third Magic card to depict Radha. The original was **Radha, Heir to Keld** (Planar Chaos, 2007; reprinted in Time Spiral Remastered no. 259 and Dominaria Remastered no. 196), followed by **Grand Warlord Radha** (Dominaria, 2018). Art on this M21 printing is by **Chris Rahn**. `[Scryfall]` `[Star City Games: "Third Time's The Charm For Radha, Heart Of Keld"]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — {1}{R}{G} 3/3 Legendary Elf Warrior with turn-conditional first strike, library-top reveal, lands-from-top, and a `{4}{R}{G}` activated +X/+X pump that scales off lands controlled. Scryfall reports `edhrec_rank: 7451`. EDHREC tracks her primarily in Commander land-matters / landfall / ramp / Voltron / elves / warriors / land-destruction archetypes, with high-synergy slots for Mina and Denn, Wildborn and Rampaging Baloths. `[Scryfall]` `[EDHREC: Radha, Heart of Keld, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — No flavor text. Scryfall's `flavor_text` field is absent for this printing; the textbox is fully consumed by rules text. `[Scryfall]`
- **Community resonance** — Commander-strategy sentiment frames Radha as a Gruul evolution of **Courser of Kruphix** — a virtual-card-advantage commander that smooths draws by playing lands off the top, with a Voltron finish line via her land-count pump. Sentiment, not consensus. `[Star City Games: "Commander Top 10: Radha, Heart Of Keld", via WebSearch snippet]`

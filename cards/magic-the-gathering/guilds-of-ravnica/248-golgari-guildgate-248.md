## Trivia

- **Set context and Vraska's Guilds of Ravnica storyline** — The Golgari Guildgate (no. 248) was released in Guilds of Ravnica (October 5, 2018). Its flavor text, spoken by Vraska, is a direct reference to the set's central Golgari storyline: Vraska, a gorgon planeswalker working as a covert agent for Nicol Bolas, had killed Jarad and installed herself as Golgari Guildmaster. The "coming doom" she refers to is the impending War of the Spark — the Nicol Bolas invasion she was secretly helping to enable, while simultaneously attempting to turn against him. `[Scryfall]` `[WebSearch snippet: mtg.wiki War of the Spark event summary; Vraska lore]`
- **Flavor text** — "Shut the gates. In the Underrealm we will outlast the coming doom." — Vraska. `[Scryfall]` The Underrealm is the Golgari's subterranean domain beneath Ravnica's streets, a fungal and necrotic underground where the guild cycles death into new growth.
- **Two arts in one set** — Guilds of Ravnica included two distinct artworks for each of the five Guildgates in the set, each occupying a separate common slot. This card (no. 248) and no. 249 both carry Eytan Zana's illustration credit — Zana painted both Golgari Guildgate versions in GRN. No. 249's flavor text is attributed to Izoni: "Jarad was a friend to the Devkarin. Now we slink through neglected tunnels, befriending those who crawl them." — establishing a factional tension within the Golgari between Vraska's new order and the resentful Devkarin clade. `[Scryfall]` `[epicstream.com: Guilds of Ravnica is Getting 2 Cycles of Guildgates, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Reprint history and EDHREC standing** — The Golgari Guildgate has been reprinted 17 times across sets including Return to Ravnica (RTR), Dragon's Maze (DGM), Modern Masters 2017 (MM3), multiple Commander products, Ravnica Remastered (RVR), Foundations (FDN), Ravnica: Clue Edition (CLU), and The List. EDHREC rank 1,487 makes it one of the most Commander-played versions of the card, primarily in Gate-tribal decks built around synergies with cards like Maze's End. `[Scryfall]` `[EDHREC]`

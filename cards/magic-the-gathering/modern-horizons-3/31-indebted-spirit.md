# Indebted Spirit (Modern Horizons 3)

## Vision

![31-indebted-spirit](../../_images/magic-the-gathering/modern-horizons-3/31-indebted-spirit.png)

A spectral figure with a skull-like head sits or crouches at a stone surface in a dark arched interior. The figure is swathed entirely in billowing white robes and ghostly blue-white energy. It holds a tall scepter topped with a starburst or spiky orb in one hand, and in the other extends a long unfurled scroll covered in faint writing. Torches or candle flames glow amber in the background arches. The overall palette is predominantly cold white and blue against the warm amber of the torch light and dark stone architecture.

## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Moggs are a goblin subrace originating on Rath, the artificial plane of the Tempest block (1997). They were purpose-bred by Volrath, evincar of Rath, to serve as soldiers — engineered to be larger, more savage, and more obedient than standard goblins, and trained to detect artifacts by smell to aid in hunting pieces of the Legacy. They are widely considered among the least intelligent type of goblin, following orders blindly even toward near-certain death. `[GameLore Wiki: Mogg, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "There was absolutely nothing suspicious about three moggs carrying barrels of explosives — or so they told everyone who would listen." `[Scryfall]`. The deadpan line encapsulates the Mogg design ethos: low intelligence, high confidence, complete lack of self-awareness.
- **Mechanical** — Sacrifice to deal 3 damage split among up to three targets — a Goblin Grenade variant that spreads rather than concentrates damage. Legal in Modern, Legacy, Vintage, and Commander. EDHREC rank 14591. Art by Joseph Weston. `[Scryfall]`

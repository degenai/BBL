# Shrieking Drake (Modern Horizons 3)

## Vision

![272-shrieking-drake](../../_images/magic-the-gathering/modern-horizons-3/272-shrieking-drake.png)

In Ian Miller's distinctive retro-fantasy illustration style — rich in anatomical detail, vivid primary colors, and a slightly surreal biological texture — a massive red-orange dragon or drake stands center-frame with its jaws spread wide in a roar. The creature has elaborate translucent frilled wings reminiscent of a sea creature's fins, multiple bulbous eye-stalks on its head, a long serpentine neck, and a tail curling upward with an arrow-shaped tip. Its hide is covered in overlapping crimson scales with golden highlights. Beneath it, a pile of pale skulls forms the creature's perch on a flat red-ochre desert plain. Needle-rock spires rise in the far background under a saturated blue sky. To the far right, a small human figure in dark clothes runs away from the scene, tiny in comparison. The mood is primal and theatrical.

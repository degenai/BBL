## Trivia

- **Set context** — Fowl Strike is a common green instant in Modern Horizons 3 (MH3, released 2024-06-14), a direct-to-Modern set designed to bypass Standard legality and inject new cards directly into older formats. MH3 is not legal in Standard or Pioneer but is legal in Modern, Legacy, Pauper, and Commander. `[Scryfall: set_type "draft_innovation" and legalities fields]`
- **Flavor text** — "With a resounding wallop, the ancient battle between buzzard and druid was finally finished." `[Scryfall: flavor_text field]`. The flavor text frames the combat depicted in the art as the conclusion of a long-running conflict — lending absurdist gravity to what is mechanically a simple removal spell. The word "ancient" and "finally" suggest this specific bird-vs-druid rivalry has deep history in whatever region of the multiverse the scene takes place in.
- **Mechanics** — Fowl Strike destroys target creature with flying for {1}{G}, making it efficient anti-air removal in green. Its secondary mode, Reinforce 2 ({2}{G}: discard this card to put two +1/+1 counters on target creature), provides an alternative sink when flying removal isn't needed. Reinforce was introduced in *Morningtide* (2008, the second set of the Lorwyn block) and is considered by R&D to have been poorly executed at the card-by-card level in that set; its reappearance in MH3 reflects the mechanic being retooled for direct-to-Modern utility rather than draft-format play. `[Scryfall: oracle_text and keywords fields]` `[WebSearch: Lorwyn Eclipsed mechanics — Reinforce noted as underperforming in original Morningtide block by R&D]`
- **Mechanical profile** — EDHREC rank 19,460 for MH3 cards. Green removal targeting flying creatures is situational but valued in formats with heavy flying threats (Modern, Legacy). The Reinforce 2 mode ensures the card is never dead in hand. `[Scryfall: edhrec_rank field]`
- **Artist** — Illustrated by Warren Mahy, a New Zealand-based concept artist and illustrator with a substantial MTG portfolio including creature and action pieces across many sets `[Scryfall: artist field]`.

## Trivia

- **Mechanical** — Furnace Hellkite is a 5/5 artifact creature Dragon with Affinity for artifacts — base cost 5RR reduces by 1 for each artifact controlled, making it effectively a 2-3 mana 5/5 flyer in artifact-heavy builds. The {R} pump ability is a classic "firebreathing" design going back to the original Magic. EDHREC rank 9224 — the best-adopted card among the MH3 cards in this wave. Legal in Modern, Legacy, Vintage, and Commander. `[Scryfall]`
- **Flavor text** — "Some mornings it wakes up determined to consume the red sun itself." `[Scryfall]`. Positions the creature as appetite without limit — consistent with Hellkite subtype design tradition of red dragon excess and ambition.
- **Design** — As an Artifact Creature — Dragon, it bridges two MH3 mechanical threads: the set's artifact theme and the classic Affinity mechanic (first printed in Mirrodin 2003, banned in Extended-adjacent formats during the Arcbound Ravager era). Printing Affinity on a standalone Dragon at uncommon keeps it playable without being the axis of a broken combo. Art by Fang Xinyu. `[Scryfall]`

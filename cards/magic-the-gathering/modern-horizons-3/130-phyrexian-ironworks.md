## Trivia

- **Flavor text** — "We have made a friend today." — Slobad, Iron Goblin `[Scryfall]`. Slobad is a Mirran goblin with one of the more tragic arcs in MTG lore: condemned to death at birth on Mirrodin, he became Glissa's closest ally and in the original Mirrodin storyline sacrificed a planeswalker spark (transferred to him by Memnarch's machine) to resurrect all those killed on the plane. He later resurfaced in Phyrexia: All Will Be One compleated and in service of New Phyrexia. `[MTG Wiki: Slobad, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Set / lore context** — Phyrexia in MTG canon is a mechanical horror plane originally conceived by Yawgmoth as a realm of compleation — the forced fusion of flesh and machine. After Yawgmoth's death in the Invasion block, New Phyrexia rose on Mirrodin under Elesh Norn. Their multiverse invasion culminated in March of the Machine (2023), where New Phyrexia was ultimately destroyed as a plane. `[MTG Wiki: Phyrexia, via WebSearch snippets]`
- **Mechanical** — Generates energy counters on attack and converts three energy into a 3/3 Phyrexian Golem artifact creature token. EDHREC rank 12134 — modest Commander adoption, primarily in artifact-energy hybrid shells. Legal in Modern, Legacy, Vintage, and Commander. Art by Mathias Kollros. `[Scryfall]`

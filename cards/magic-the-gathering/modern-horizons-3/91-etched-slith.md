# Etched Slith (Modern Horizons 3)

## Vision

![91-etched-slith](../../_images/magic-the-gathering/modern-horizons-3/91-etched-slith.png)

A dynamic figure shown mid-lunge or mid-run — its body is skeletal and semi-transparent, with the internal bone structure clearly visible through a glowing green outer layer. The skull is prominent with hollow eye sockets faintly illuminated. Clawed appendages trail at speed. Behind it, the dark background features etched geometric marks and triangular runic patterns scratched into a surface, visible at the right edge. The palette is almost entirely black and acid green. The flavor text references 'compleation,' placing this firmly in the Phyrexian design space. The creature radiates corruption energy.

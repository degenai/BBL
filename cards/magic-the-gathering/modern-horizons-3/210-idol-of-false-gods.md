## Trivia

- **Set context** — Modern Horizons 3 (June 2024) featured a major Eldrazi resurgence as one of its primary design pillars, including the Commander precon "Eldrazi Incursion" and new versions of all three Eldrazi Titans (Emrakul, the World Anew; Kozilek, the Broken Reality; Ulamog, the Defiler). Idol of False Gods is part of this Eldrazi suite, designed for Eldrazi-tribal Commander and Modern play. `[Scryfall: MH3-210 set_name, released_at; WebSearch: MH3 Eldrazi design]`
- **Design — Kindred type** — The type line "Kindred Artifact — Eldrazi" uses MH3's formalized Kindred card type, which replaced the old Tribal type and allows noncreature cards to carry creature types. This makes the Idol itself an Eldrazi for synergy triggers (e.g., Ulalek, Fused Atrocity copies spells when any Eldrazi you control is cast). No flavor text. Art by Nicholas Gregory. `[Scryfall: MH3-210 type_line; WebSearch: Kindred type rules update]`
- **Mechanical loop** — Pay {1}{C} to tap and create a 0/1 Eldrazi Spawn token; when any other Eldrazi you control dies, the idol receives a +1/+1 counter; at 8 or more counters it becomes a 0/0 artifact creature with Annihilator 2. The Spawn tokens generate {C} mana when sacrificed, sustaining the ramp loop. EDHREC records approximately 30,657 Commander decks. Top commanders: Azlask, the Swelling Scourge (61%), Ulalek, Fused Atrocity (20%). `[Scryfall: MH3-210 oracle_text; EDHREC: edhrec.com/cards/idol-of-false-gods]`
- **Design — false gods framing** — The card name explicitly labels the Eldrazi as "false gods." This is consistent with BFZ-era lore where certain Zendikar cults worshipped the Eldrazi Titans as divine, despite the Titans being purely consumptive forces with no consciousness or purpose beyond annihilation. The idol's mechanical arc literalizes the worship: devotees feed it (Eldrazi die, counters accumulate) and eventually it awakens as the object of their devotion. `[Scryfall: MH3-210; WebSearch: Eldrazi cult worship Zendikar lore]`

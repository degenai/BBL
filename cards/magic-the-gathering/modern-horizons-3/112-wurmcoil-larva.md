## Trivia

- **Design lineage** — Wurmcoil Larva is an explicit callback to Wurmcoil Engine (Scars of Mirrodin, October 2010): its death trigger creates two tokens (a 1/2 deathtouch and a 2/1 lifelink) that directly mirror the Engine's death tokens (3/3 deathtouch and 3/3 lifelink). Wurmcoil Engine is one of the most reprinted artifact creatures in the game, appearing in Commander 2014, Double Masters, The Brothers' War Retro Artifacts, and multiple Secret Lair printings. Wurmcoil Larva is the first card to revisit the split-token death design at a smaller scale. `[Scryfall: Wurmcoil Engine print history]`
- **Flavor text** — "Even a scrap of Phyrexian machinery can be lethal." `[Scryfall]`. The line underscores the design logic: even a larval form of the Phyrexian construct spawns two threats on death.
- **Mechanical** — A 2-mana uncommon with deathtouch and lifelink that generates two tokens on death — strong rate for its cost in Phyrexian creature and artifact-creature synergy builds. EDHREC rank 11888, highest adoption among the MH3 Phyrexia cards in this wave. Legal in Modern, Legacy, Vintage, and Commander. Art by David Astruga. `[Scryfall]`

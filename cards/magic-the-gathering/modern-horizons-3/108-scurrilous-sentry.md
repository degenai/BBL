## Trivia

- **IP refuted** — Vision flagged Queen Marchesa (low confidence, rose medallion). The card's own flavor text resolves this: "Queen Marchesa's agents always manage to find the exact evidence they're looking for." The depicted figure is an unnamed operative in Marchesa's Black Rose spy network, not the queen. The rose medallion at the cape clasp is a faction marker worn by her agents on the plane of Fiora. `[Scryfall: flavor_text; MTG Wiki: Marchesa d'Amati via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Set context** — The card's flavor roots in Fiora (plane of the Conspiracy sets), where Marchesa d'Amati, the Black Rose, rules Paliano through networks of spies, assassins, and rogues who deal in manufactured evidence. `[MTG Wiki: Marchesa d'Amati via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Mechanical** — Scurrilous Sentry reintroduces the connive mechanic in MH3. Connive debuted in Streets of New Capenna (April 2022) as the Obscura family's ability — draw a card, discard a card, and if the discarded card was a nonland, place a +1/+1 counter. As a {3}{B} 2/3 with menace that connives on enter-or-attack, it functions as a repeatable card-selection and self-buffing engine. Legal in Pauper and Modern. EDHREC rank 15,656. `[Scryfall: oracle_text, keywords, edhrec_rank:15656; magic.wizards.com: Modern Horizons 3 Mechanics]`
- **Design** — Art by Leonardo Santanna. MH3 released 2024-06-14 as a draft-innovation set injecting mechanics directly into the Modern card pool, the third in the Modern Horizons series. `[Scryfall: artist, released_at, set_type]`

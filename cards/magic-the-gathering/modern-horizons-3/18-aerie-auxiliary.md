## Trivia

- **Flavor text** — `"The hordechief's ground tactics were unsurpassed. If only he'd thought to watch the skies."` `[Scryfall]`. No named speaker. The "hordechief" framing suggests an adversary associated with large infantry forces; the flavor positions the Bird Soldiers as a decisive aerial counterpunch against an otherwise capable ground commander — an implied after-action report.
- **Support mechanic** — The support keyword was introduced in Oath of the Gatewatch (2016), the second Zendikar-return set focused on the Eldrazi invasion. Support N means "put a +1/+1 counter on each of up to N other target creatures." After Oath of the Gatewatch it appeared in Battlebond (2018), Streets of New Capenna Commander (2022), Modern Horizons 2 (2021), and Modern Horizons 3 — primarily as a counter-friendly utility keyword `[Wizards: "Oath of the Gatewatch Mechanics", magic.wizards.com, 2015-12-28]` `[WebSearch: Support mechanic history — MTG Wiki via search snippet]`.
- **Artist** — Illustrated by Donato Giancola, one of the most decorated artists in science fiction and fantasy illustration. Giancola has created 125+ MTG illustrations since 1996, beginning with Amber Prison (Mirage). He has won 23 Chesley Awards (the SF/Fantasy art field's highest honor), including a Lifetime Artistic Achievement Award in 2002, and a World Fantasy Award `[Hipsters of the Coast: "Donato Giancola Land Panorama", December 2018]` `[donatoarts.com: biography]`. His MTG work employs traditional oils on panel.
- **Mechanical profile** — A 3/3 Flying Bird Soldier for {3}{W} with support 2 on entry. Common rarity makes it Pauper-legal. EDHREC rank 17,249 suggests it sees modest Commander play in white counter-distribution shells (Skullbriar, Hamza, etc.) but is not a format staple `[Scryfall API: legalities pauper: legal, edhrec_rank 17249]`.

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Theros Beyond Death (THB) released 2020-01-24 and returns to the Greek-mythology-inspired plane of Theros; the set's mechanical throughline is the Escape keyword, letting cards recur from the graveyard. Archons are divine constructs of white mana that enforce order on behalf of the gods `[Scryfall]`.
- **Flavor text** — "The archon fell like a star from the sky, meeting the rising sun at the horizon's edge." — *The Cosmogony* `[Scryfall]`. The Cosmogony is a fictional Theran sacred text describing the creation of the cosmos; it appears on multiple THB cards framing the set's mythology of death and return.
- **Mechanical** — When Archon of Falling Stars dies it may return any enchantment from your graveyard to the battlefield — including Auras, which bypass targeting and can attach to anything you choose. This makes it a payoff for white enchantment-graveyard loops in Pioneer and Commander. EDHREC rank 20559 indicates niche Commander play `[Scryfall, EDHREC]`.
- **Design** — Reprinted in Foundations Jumpstart (2024), the only paper reprint outside THB promos, confirming Wizards considers the enchantment-recursion trigger a durable teaching tool for new players `[Scryfall prints]`.

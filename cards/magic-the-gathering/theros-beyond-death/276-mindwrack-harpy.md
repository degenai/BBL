## Trivia

- **Set context** — Harpies on Theros are servants of Erebos, god of the dead, dispatched to torment the living and the recently deceased. Mindwrack Harpy carries collector number 276 — a showcase-frame slot in the full-art Nyx treatment for THB — with the Enchantment Creature type reflecting Theros's defining mechanic that divine creatures are woven from the starry fabric of the Nyx `[Scryfall]`.
- **Flavor text** — "Harpies are among the most detested creatures in all of Theros... and they thoroughly deserve it." `[Scryfall]`. Structurally unusual for MTG flavor text: most copy inhabits a character's voice; this one addresses the reader directly, functioning as a lore footnote rather than an in-world quote.
- **Mechanical** — Mills three cards from each player at the beginning of your combat — symmetrical. EDHREC rank 6936 is notably high for a common, reflecting adoption in graveyard-matters and Erebos-adjacent Commander decks where the symmetry is exploited for self-mill. Legal in Pauper and PauperCommander `[Scryfall, EDHREC]`.

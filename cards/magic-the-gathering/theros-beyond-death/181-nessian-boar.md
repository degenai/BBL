## Trivia

- **Set context** — The Nessian Forest is the vast wilderness of Setessa, the Amazonian polis of Theros. The boar is the terror of those wilds — a 10/6 that forces all able blockers to engage it, echoing the Calydonian Boar of Greek myth sent by Artemis to ravage a polis that neglected her worship `[Scryfall]`.
- **Flavor text** — "Renata led the best hunters in all of Theros on a quest to bring down the terror of the Setessan wilds." `[Scryfall]`. Renata refers to Renata, Called to the Hunt (THB no. 185), a Setessan demigod — the two cards form a flavor-text pair depicting the same hunt from different perspectives.
- **Mechanical** — The Nessian Boar's design inverts the standard power-creature problem: it draws cards for every player who blocks it, turning its own resistance into a group-hug draw engine and making it genuinely risky to attack with in multiplayer Commander. EDHREC rank 12444 reflects niche adoption in big-green stompy decks `[Scryfall, EDHREC]`.

## Trivia

- **Mechanical** — One of the most-played free sacrifice outlets in Commander, appearing in approximately 81,400 decks on EDHREC (rank 1686). The zero-mana sacrifice cost, Scry 1 selection, built-in token fodder (0/1 Goat on entry), and Escape recursion make it a format staple in sacrifice-based strategies `[EDHREC, Scryfall]`.
- **Design** — Reprinted in five Commander precons between 2022 and 2026: Commander Legends: Battle for Baldur's Gate, New Capenna Commander, Bloomburrow Commander, Tarkir: Dragonstorm Commander, and Secrets of Strixhaven Commander — an unusually dense reprint schedule reflecting consistent demand as a sacrifice-outlet workhorse `[Scryfall prints]`.
- **Set context** — Woe Strider is typed Horror rather than a named Theros creature type (Minotaur, Harpy, Cyclops), marking it as one of the formless, nameless things that haunt the Underworld's deeper layers rather than a figure from Theran mythology. The Goat token it spawns may invoke the scapegoat motif: sacrificed innocent, absorbing woe on behalf of the larger creature `[Scryfall]`.

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Dalakos is typed Legendary Creature — Merfolk Artificer, the only Merfolk Artificer legendary in the Theros block. On Theros, merfolk are coastal people associated with Thassa (god of the sea); Dalakos's Izzet ({U}{R}) color pair is atypical — artifice is not a Thassa domain, suggesting he operates outside the polis structure entirely `[Scryfall]`.
- **Flavor text** — "His imagination soars from the depths of the sea to the heights of the heavens." `[Scryfall]`. The phrasing deliberately echoes the Daedalus myth — the architect who built wings to escape imprisonment — though Dalakos is a Wizards-original character and not a direct adaptation. The flavor text frames his ambition as transgressing the limits of his natural habitat.
- **Mechanical** — Sees the most Commander play as a dedicated equipment-combo commander: EDHREC rank 7271, approximately 1,016 decks as commander, primarily Equipment (52%) and Artifact (19%) strategies. His tap ability produces two colorless mana restricted to artifact costs, enabling expensive equipment to deploy faster; equipped creatures gain flying and haste as the payoff loop `[EDHREC, Scryfall]`.
- **Design** — No paper reprint beyond THB promos since his 2020 debut — no Commander precon inclusion, no Masters set slot. Low reprint density despite moderate Commander interest `[Scryfall prints]`.

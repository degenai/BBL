## Trivia

- **Set / lore context — Pharika reference** — The flavor text names Pharika directly: "Many have sought snake-twined Pharika's panacea. Do you wish to share their fate?" `[Scryfall flavor_text]`. Pharika is the God of Affliction on Theros — black-green aligned, governing disease, poison, and medicine. She is described as the mother of all Theros gorgons, herself thought to take gorgon form, and her cures "frequently carry a painful or costly price." The Venomous Hierophant, as a Gorgon Cleric, is a direct servant of Pharika `[WebSearch snippet: MTG Wiki: Pharika via Fandom; WebSearch: WebSearch snippet MTG Wiki Pharika non-Fandom]`. **Wire: `[[theros-pantheon]]`** `[Scryfall type_line]`.
- **Flavor text context** — The "panacea" referred to in the flavor text is Pharika's legendary cure — the healing draught she guards that can either save or destroy depending on dosage. Seekers of this cure become the Hierophant's victims. The card's mill-three ETB mechanic — milling your own library — can be read as the "taste" of Pharika's knowledge: you gain access (cards in the graveyard become Escape fuel) at a cost `[Scryfall oracle_text, flavor_text]`.
- **Mechanical note** — Venomous Hierophant is a 3/3 Gorgon Cleric for {3}{B} with Deathtouch and a mill-three ETB. Deathtouch is Pharika's domain in MTG mechanically: she is the god who makes any touch lethal. The mill feeds THB's Escape mechanic, which requires exiling cards from the graveyard to recast spells. Hipsters of the Coast rated it C: "A fine attacker, a reasonable blocker, and a relevant enters-the-battlefield ability all combine to make Venomous Hierophant more than the sum of its parts" `[Hipsters of the Coast: "Every Common in Theros Beyond Death Reviewed"]`.
- **Artist note** — Illustrated by Johannes Voss, whose work is recognized in the MTG community for its painterly, intimate rendering style. Voss has illustrated several high-profile MTG cards across multiple sets `[Scryfall artist field]`.

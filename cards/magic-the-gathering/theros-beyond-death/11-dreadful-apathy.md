## Trivia

- **Flavor text** — "Those whose lives were uninspired are doomed to the wretched tedium of Phylias in death." `[Scryfall]`. Phylias is the uppermost layer of the Theros Underworld, reserved for souls whose mortal lives were unremarkable — the Theran analogue of the Greek Fields of Asphodel. Residents perform repetitive tasks in featureless structures `[MTG Wiki: Underworld, via WebSearch snippet]`.
- **Set context** — Dreadful Apathy's white Aura design reflects Theros's civic order logic: white on Theros governs the polis and the afterlife's administrative tiers simultaneously. Phylias sits at the intersection of both — bureaucratic punishment rather than active torment `[Scryfall]`.
- **Mechanical** — Legal in Pauper and PauperCommander. A budget Answer in white that trades instant-speed removal for a repeatable exile activation. EDHREC rank 19653 reflects limited Commander demand due to competition from cheaper white removal `[Scryfall, EDHREC]`.

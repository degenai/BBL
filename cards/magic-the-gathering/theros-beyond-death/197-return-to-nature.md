# Return to Nature (Theros Beyond Death)

## Vision

![197-return-to-nature](../../_images/magic-the-gathering/theros-beyond-death/197-return-to-nature.png)

A long, ornate golden spear lies cracked and broken on the dim forest floor. Green tendrils, vines, and pale roots have already begun winding around the weapon's shaft, pulling it down into moss and undergrowth. The lighting is shadowed and cool, dominated by deep greens and the muted gleam of the gold metal against the dark earth. The flavor text references Helios's spear cast down and broken, framing this as a divine artifact being slowly consumed and reclaimed by the natural world.

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Theros Beyond Death (THB, January 24 2020) is the 84th Magic expansion, returning to the Greek-mythos plane of Theros with a focus on the Underworld and Elspeth's death-and-resurrection arc. THB introduced the Escape mechanic (cast from graveyard by exiling other cards) and returned the Constellation enchantment-payoff trigger from the original Theros block `[Scryfall]`.
- **Mechanical note** — Daybreak Chimera is a 3/3 flyer with base cost {3}{W}{W} that reduces by your devotion to white — effectively free in a white-heavy board. The devotion mechanic first appeared in the original Theros (2013) and returned here as a full-set throughline, treating faith in the Therosian gods as a measurable, game-altering resource `[Scryfall]`.
- **Design reception** — Rated B by Hipsters of the Coast's THB common review: "A 3/3 flying for five doesn't cut it anymore...but Daybreak Chimera looks awesome," noting that casting it alongside another spell for two mana made it a strong tempo play in sealed and draft `[Hipsters of the Coast: "Every Common in Theros Beyond Death Reviewed"]`.
- **Lore context** — On Theros, chimeras are multi-part creatures considered children of Nyx, the divine dreamscape that manifests the gods' will. Unlike the chimeras of real-world myth (lion/goat/serpent), Therosian chimeras take varied beast-hybrid forms; this one's goat-and-eagle body echoes Theros's white-aligned connection to the heavens and Heliod's sun-domain `[Scryfall type_line: Creature — Chimera]`.

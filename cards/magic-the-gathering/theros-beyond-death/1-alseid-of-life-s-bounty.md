## Trivia

- **Set context** — Alseid of Life's Bounty is an Enchantment Creature — Nymph, a creature type unique to the Theros setting. In Theros lore, nymphs are physical manifestations of divine concepts, existing as enchantments in the mortal world; the dual Enchantment Creature type mechanically encodes this — alseids count toward constellation triggers and behave as both creatures and enchantments. `[Scryfall: thb/1; MTG Wiki: Nymph, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "Dawn-kissed, he nourishes the golden grain." — Psemilla, Meletian poet. `[Scryfall: thb/1]` The speaker is a named Meletian poet; Meletis is the Theros city-state built on logic and philosophy (analog to classical Athens). The masculine pronoun ("he") while the art depicts a feminized figure is consistent with Theros's mortal-perspective-filtered depiction of divine beings — an intentional Wizards ambiguity. `[Scryfall: thb/1]`
- **Design** — THB no. 1, Uncommon, illustrated by Magali Villeneuve, released 2020-01-24. Reprinted in Jumpstart 2022 (J22, no. 143). The protection ability (sacrifice: grant protection from any color until end of turn) makes Alseid a widely-played 1-drop in enchantress and aura-based Commander brews — EDHREC tracks approximately 53,857 decks. `[Scryfall: thb/1; Scryfall: j22/143; EDHREC: alseid-of-lifes-bounty, via WebSearch snippet]`

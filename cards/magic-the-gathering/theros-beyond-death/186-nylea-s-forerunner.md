## Trivia

- **Nylea lore** — Nylea is the God of the Hunt, Seasons, and Wilds on Theros, inspired by Artemis and her Roman equivalent Diana. Her divine weapon is Ephixis, a short bow said to never miss. Per MTG Wiki, she was briefly imprisoned in beast form by Xenagos during the original Theros block when his revel threw the natural balance into chaos. Nylea's Forerunner is listed explicitly under "Associated cards" in MTG Wiki's in-game references for Nylea. Her symbol is a set of four arrows. `[MTG Wiki: Nylea]`
- **Flavor text** — "Where its feet tread, the thunder of many others will follow." Unattributed — appropriate for a natural force rather than a character. The "thunder of many others" directly mirrors the card's mechanical effect: granting trample to all other creatures you control, so the forerunner's charge is followed by the whole herd. `[Scryfall: flavor_text, thb/186]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — Nylea's Forerunner is a 5/3 Enchantment Creature — Beast with trample that grants trample to all other creatures you control for 5 mana. EDHREC rank 9,700 is notably strong for a common, well above the median. Top Commander homes per EDHREC: Slinza, the Spiked Stampede (34% inclusion), Zilortha, Strength Incarnate (5.8%), and Bello, Bard of the Brambles (2.8%) — all trample-matters commanders. Legal in Pauper and Pauper Commander. The mechanical parallel to Nylea, God of the Hunt (THS no. 166) is exact: both grant trample to all your other creatures. `[Scryfall: edhrec_rank=9700, legalities; EDHREC: edhrec.com/cards/nyleas-forerunner]`
- **Artist** — Christopher Burdett has specialized in monster and creature illustration for tabletop games since 2005, with credits across Magic: The Gathering, D&D, Pathfinder, and Star Wars. He illustrated Nylea's Forerunner and Shimmerwing Chimera for THB. He sells signed, numbered artist proofs of this card — limited to an edition of 50 — via his personal storefront, indicating he regards it as a meaningful portfolio piece. `[christopherburdett.myshopify.com: Nylea's Forerunner artist proof listing]`
- **Enchantment-creature frame** — Like all Theros enchantment-creatures, Nylea's Forerunner carries the dual Enchantment Creature type that signals Nyx-born or divinely marked status — creatures whose very existence is woven from the constellation-stuff of the Therosian heavens. The deep purple-violet constellation sparkles on the creature's torso visible in the card art are the visual embodiment of this mechanical designation. `[Scryfall: type_line=Enchantment Creature — Beast; MTG Wiki: Nylea]`

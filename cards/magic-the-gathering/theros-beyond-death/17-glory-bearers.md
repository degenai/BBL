# Glory Bearers (Theros Beyond Death)

## Vision

![17-glory-bearers](../../_images/magic-the-gathering/theros-beyond-death/17-glory-bearers.png)

Two humanoid figures dressed in flowing blue-white robes face each other as they jointly carry a large ceramic amphora painted in Greek black-figure style with chevron banding. From the mouth of the vessel rises a blazing golden orb surrounded by curling arcs of magical energy, casting warm light across both figures. Small blue star-like sparkles are scattered across the figures and the air around them, consistent with the Theros constellation-magic aesthetic. The composition is a slightly elevated angle looking down on the pair in close-to-mid range. The background is a muted blue-grey atmospheric haze that keeps focus on the vessel and its light. The mood is ceremonial and reverent — these are attendants in the act of presenting or carrying a sacred object rather than combatants.

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Thrashing Mossdog is a common from Dragon's Maze (set code DGM), the third and final set of the Return to Ravnica block, released 2013-05-03. Dragon's Maze attempted to feature all ten Ravnica guilds in a single small set of 156 cards — a design challenge Mark Rosewater later acknowledged didn't succeed as well as hoped, calling the block structure itself the root cause [Scryfall]; [Wizards of the Coast, "State of Design 2013," magic.wizards.com, 2013-08-23].
- **Type line** — Thrashing Mossdog carries the type line "Creature — Plant Dog," an unusual combination. Community discussion on Gatherer noted it as one of very few Plant Dog creatures in Magic's history [Gatherer community comment, via WebSearch snippet]. The Golgari guild watermark confirms its Golgari affiliation [Scryfall: `watermark: golgari`].
- **Mechanical profile** — The card has two keywords: Reach and Scavenge {4}{G}{G}. Scavenge is a Golgari-exclusive keyword introduced in Return to Ravnica that lets you exile the card from your graveyard to put +1/+1 counters on a creature, mirroring the guild's death-and-rebirth philosophy. Its EDHREC rank is 25105 [Scryfall], placing it among low-adoption cards — a bulk filler in most Commander lists. It sees occasional Pauper play given its Pauper-legal status [Scryfall: legalities].
- **No flavor text** — Scryfall confirms no flavor text on this printing [Scryfall].

## Trivia

- **Flavor text** — "Some view Domri's unlikely ascent as a sign of Ilharg the Raze-Boar's imminent return." `[Scryfall]`. Domri Rade rose from a feral orphan in Ravnica's wilds to Gruul guildmaster by defeating Borborygmos — an ascent facilitated by Nicol Bolas manipulating the guilds. Ilharg the Raze-Boar is the Gruul boar-deity whose return signals the End-Raze: a prophesied apocalyptic trampling of all of Ravnica's civilization. Ilharg did manifest during War of the Spark `[Card Kingdom Blog: MTG Character Profile Domri Rade via WebSearch; MTG Wiki: Ilharg via WebSearch snippet]`. Note: the vision pass declined to flag the rider figure as Domri per the anti-confab protocol — no named-character visual identifier is confirmed present in the art.
- **Set lore / mechanic** — The Riot keyword (RNA Gruul mechanic) is embedded in this enchantment's oracle text. The Wizards RNA mechanics article frames Riot as the Gruul "reacting to the icy political climate on Ravnica the best way they know how: rioting" — giving creatures a choice of speed (haste) or size (+1/+1 counter). Rhythm of the Wild extends Riot to all nontoken creatures and layers uncounterability on top `[magic.wizards.com: Ravnica Allegiance Mechanics]`.
- **Mechanical** — EDHREC rank 203 — a top-tier Commander staple, appearing in over 409,500 Commander decks per EDHREC data. Its two-axis design (uncounterable creatures + Riot) makes it broadly useful across any creature-heavy strategy, not just Gruul `[EDHREC via WebSearch snippet; Scryfall]`. Market price $4.84 reflects genuine constructed demand.

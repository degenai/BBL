## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — OGW introduced the {C} colorless mana symbol — the first symbol in Magic history specifically representing colorless mana rather than generic mana. Immobilizer Eldrazi's activated ability ({2}{C}) was among the first cards to use it in print. Wizards designed the symbol to clarify the distinction between "any mana pays this" (generic) and "only colorless mana pays this." `[magic.wizards.com/en/news/feature/oath-gatewatch-mechanics-2015-12-28]`
- **Flavor text** — "It denied the Zendikari the chance to stand and die fighting." `[Scryfall]` The framing is specifically about removal of agency — not death but deprivation. Wizards' canonical framing for the Eldrazi describes them as beings of metabolism, not malice: they process life the way a stomach processes food. This creature's flavor reflects that indifference directly.
- **Mechanical reputation** — The activated ability makes each creature with toughness greater than its power unable to block this turn — a precision attack on walls, high-toughness defenders, and defender strategies. In combination with other aggressive Eldrazi it functions as a pseudo-Fog-bypass. Uncommon rarity; no reprints as of May 2026. EDHREC rank 26511. `[Scryfall]`

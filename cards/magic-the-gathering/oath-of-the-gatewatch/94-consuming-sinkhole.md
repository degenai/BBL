## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Consuming Sinkhole is among the small number of OGW devoid spells (non-creature) that use the {C} colorless mana symbol. As a modal instant with devoid, it represents the Eldrazi's transformation of Zendikar's landscape — the land-creature exile mode directly targets Zendikar's animated lands like Hissing Quagmire and Lumbering Falls, which were a defining feature of Standard at the time. `[Scryfall oracle_text; magic.wizards.com/en/news/feature/oath-gatewatch-mechanics-2015-12-28]`
- **Flavor text** — "What will not yield will be consumed." — Ayli, high priest of the Eternal Pilgrims. `[Scryfall]` Ayli is a Kor cleric whose cult came to worship the Eldrazi as gods — believing them to be the divine beings underlying the Kor's ancient sky-deity traditions. Her quote is not defiance but acceptance: surrender as doctrine. `[WebSearch: mtg.fandom.com/wiki/Ayli snippet]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — The modal structure (exile a land creature OR deal 4 damage to a player/planeswalker) is unusually wide for a common at 4 mana. The land-creature mode was narrow in Standard but relevant against the creature-lands of the BFZ/OGW era. Pauper-legal. EDHREC rank 27490. `[Scryfall]`

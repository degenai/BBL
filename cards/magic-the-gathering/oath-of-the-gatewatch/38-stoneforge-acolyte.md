## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — The "Stoneforge" name is a deliberate echo of Stoneforge Mystic, one of the most powerful Equipment tutors ever printed (banned in Modern, restricted in Legacy). Stoneforge Acolyte's Cohort version of the same ability — look at top 4, find Equipment — is a significantly weaker iteration gated behind the Ally cooperation requirement. The naming is a flavor connection: the Kor's tradition of crafting and wielding magical weapons is a through-line across multiple Zendikar sets. `[Scryfall]`
- **Flavor text** — "The stone speaks. We are merely interpreters." `[Scryfall]` The line frames Kor forge-craft as receptive rather than creative — the craftsperson listens to the material rather than imposing a design on it. This is consistent with Kor culture across Zendikar story materials, where craft and spiritual practice are intertwined.
- **Mechanical reputation** — Cohort Equipment tutor: tap this and an untapped Ally to look at the top 4 cards and pull an Equipment to hand. Uncommon; no reprints as of May 2026. EDHREC rank 22471. In Pauper Commander, Stoneforge Acolyte has been used as a commander for voltron-style Equipment builds, exploiting the Cohort ability as a repeatable Equipment-search engine. Not Pauper-legal (uncommon). `[Scryfall; WebSearch: moxfield.com Pauper EDH deck snippet]`

## Trivia

- **Flavor text** — "Walk now in my light. When the rest of the world closes its eyes, you will still see." — Iona `[Scryfall]` — The closing-eyes line directly engages Zendikar angel lore: after the Eldrazi's first awakening, Zendikari angels lowered their halos over their eyes as a symbol of powerlessness and willful blindness to the Eldrazi's atrocities. `[WebSearch: Iona MTG Fandom page snippet]` Iona's gift of sight carries pointed moral weight in that context.
- **Set / lore context** — Iona, Shield of Emeria is described as one of the greater archangels of Zendikar — gargantuan, six-winged, with pupil-less glowing white eyes and no halo over her own eyes (unlike other Zendikari angels). `[WebSearch: Iona MTG Fandom page snippet]` This card depicts her blessing passing to a mortal warrior — the art shows a recipient, not Iona herself.
- **Mechanical note** — A 3W Aura granting +2/+2, vigilance, and multi-block ("can block an additional creature each combat"). The multi-block clause is rare in white Auras. EDHREC rank 18736. `[Scryfall]`

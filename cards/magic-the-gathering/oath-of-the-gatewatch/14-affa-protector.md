## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Affa is a named settlement on Zendikar that survived the Eldrazi invasion by relocating into an underground cavern. `[WebSearch: MTGSalvation Zendikar world lore article]` The unnamed speaker in the flavor text — "Through all the terrors we face, I hold on to the memory of the world before the Eldrazi came. I'd like to see that world again." — is a civilian voice registering displacement and longing under occupation. `[Scryfall]`
- **Mechanical note** — A 1/4 Human Soldier Ally with Vigilance for 2W. The asymmetric statline (low power, high toughness) places it squarely in the "road block" common archetype. Pauper-legal and penny-dreadful legal. EDHREC rank 26269. `[Scryfall]`
- **Design** — Art by Izzy (Sahana D. Mohan), Seattle-based illustrator. Her only OGW credit per Scryfall. `[Scryfall]`

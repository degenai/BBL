## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Relief Captain is a Kor Knight Ally — one of Zendikar's most prominent humanoid species. The Kor's nomadic, rope-based culture made them natural cavalry and scouting troops in the Zendikari resistance. The "Relief" in the name is both military (a relief force) and literal — the Support mechanic distributes +1/+1 counters as tangible aid. `[Scryfall type_line; WebSearch: mtg.fandom.com/wiki/Munda Kor lore snippet]`
- **Flavor text** — "Sometimes gestures of support are small. Sometimes not." `[Scryfall]` A clean thematic statement for OGW's Support keyword — distributing +1/+1 counters across up to N other targets. Support 3 on a 3/3 for 4 mana was a reasonable Limited rate; the card was designed as a draft archetype glue piece. `[magic.wizards.com/en/news/feature/oath-gatewatch-mechanics-2015-12-28]`
- **Design** — Relief Captain has been reprinted 4 times: Archenemy: Nicol Bolas (2017), Game Night 2019, Double Masters 2022, and Foundations Jumpstart (2024). The Foundations Jumpstart reprint is notable — Foundations is Wizards' evergreen entry-level product, suggesting the Support 3 ETB trigger was judged clean enough to serve as a teaching tool for the counter mechanic. EDHREC rank 21996, the highest (most played) of the OGW supporting cards in this inventory cluster. `[Scryfall prints search]`

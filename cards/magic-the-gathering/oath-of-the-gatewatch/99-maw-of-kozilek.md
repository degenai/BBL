## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — The "Maw" naming links this creature to Kozilek, the Eldrazi Titan of distortion and confusion — OGW's central antagonist titan. Kozilek's brood is mechanically identified by using the {C} colorless mana symbol; this card's {C} activation is canonical brood-lineage identification. `[Scryfall type_line; magic.wizards.com/en/news/feature/oath-gatewatch-mechanics-2015-12-28]`
- **Flavor text** — "The Eldrazi make worse than ruins — they make a world where not even ruins stand." — Gideon Jura. `[Scryfall]` Gideon's line encodes Wizards' canonical metabolism-not-malice framing: the Eldrazi don't hate; they process. The absence of ruins is not cruelty — it is the complete erasure of the substrate that cruelty would need to operate on.
- **Design** — Art by Daarken, a prolific MTG contributor across dozens of sets known for kinetic, dark creature work. The {C} pump activation (+2/-2 until end of turn) is a high-risk aggro enabler best used on a creature guaranteed to deal unblocked damage or in a damage-racing trade. Pauper-legal. EDHREC rank 28888 — the lowest of the four OGW Eldrazi in this inventory cluster. `[Scryfall]`

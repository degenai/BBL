## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — The Kor of Zendikar are nomadic cliff-dwellers who are masters of ropes and hooks, using them for travel, hunting, and spiritual ritual. A hooked line is a sacred social symbol representing connection to each other and the world. They "rarely use unreliable devices such as crossbows to propel their grappling hooks — relying instead on simple, sturdy rope and the skill of the arm." `[WebSearch: Ecology of the Kor, CoolStuffInc / Kor MTG Wiki]` The flavor text — "With rope, I can fly as far as the rope can reach. But with light, the sky is the limit." — maps that philosophy: tool-mastery as foundation, spirit as transcendence. `[Scryfall]`
- **Mechanical note** — A 3/2 Kor Soldier Ally for 2W with pay-1W activated flying. Aggressive stats for a white common; the flying activation creates combat pressure that demands answers. Pauper-legal, penny-legal. EDHREC rank 28868 — draft staple, not a Commander card. `[Scryfall]`
- **Design** — Art by Victor Adame Minguez, Spanish illustrator with extensive MTG credits. The sunset palette (deep purple, warm orange) against glowing golden energy lines is consistent with Zendikar's visual treatment of Kor gear in this block. `[Scryfall]`

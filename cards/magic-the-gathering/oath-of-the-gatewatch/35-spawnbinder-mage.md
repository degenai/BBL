## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Spawnbinder Mage is a Human Wizard Ally — a mage in the Zendikari resistance who has learned to trap and restrain Eldrazi Spawn. The "Spawnbinder" name positions this figure as a specialized anti-Eldrazi operative, consistent with the OGW narrative that allied forces adapted tactics specifically to the Eldrazi threat. `[Scryfall type_line]`
- **Flavor text** — "Follow me. Step lightly. And resist the urge to kick them." `[Scryfall]` The humor is tactical — a command not to provoke captured Eldrazi spawn in transit. The throwaway "urge to kick them" implies the spawn are reactive enough to respond to provocation, which reinforces the metabolic-indifference framing: they're dangerous but not actively malicious, more like caged animals than enemy soldiers.
- **Mechanical reputation** — Cohort (tap this and an untapped Ally to tap target creature) was OGW's keyword for Ally cooperation. The Cohort trigger requires the second Ally to have been under your control since your last turn began, preventing instant-speed abuse with newly cast creatures. Pauper-legal. One printing only. EDHREC rank 27010. `[Scryfall; magic.wizards.com/en/news/feature/oath-gatewatch-mechanics-2015-12-28]`

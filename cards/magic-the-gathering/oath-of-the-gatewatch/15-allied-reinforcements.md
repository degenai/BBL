## Trivia

- **Flavor text** — "Our formation was scattered after Sea Gate fell. If you're fighting the Eldrazi, consider us your newest recruits." `[Scryfall]` — Sea Gate was the largest city on Zendikar's Tazeem continent and its center of learning and commerce before falling to the Eldrazi assault. `[WebSearch: Sea Gate MTG Wiki]` The speaker's offer to join any anti-Eldrazi force mirrors the card's mechanical output: two Knight Ally tokens arriving as reinforcements.
- **Mechanical note** — Creates two 2/2 white Knight Ally tokens for 3W sorcery. The Ally type gives it synergy with enter-the-battlefield trigger chains in dedicated Ally-tribal builds. EDHREC rank 18970, penny-dreadful legal. `[Scryfall]`
- **Design** — Art by Matt Stewart, prolific MTG illustrator. The two mounted figures ride distinct animals — a boar and a horse — suggesting the Ally coalition draws from multiple peoples of Zendikar, which is the set's explicit narrative premise. `[Scryfall]`

## Trivia

- **Flavor text** — "For a moment they stood tall, all fear forgotten." `[Scryfall]` — Authorless, omniscient-narrator voice. "For a moment" is deliberately impermanent — it makes no promise of survival. The card's indestructible effect also lasts only until end of turn. Text and mechanics align precisely.
- **Mechanical note** — Make a Stand has been reprinted in Core Set 2019 (2018), Commander Legends (2020), Jumpstart 2022, and MTG Foundations (2024), earning Standard legality again via the Foundations printing. `[Scryfall: prints search]` EDHREC rank 3239 — a genuine Commander staple and one of the more-efficient white mass-indestructible spells. `[Scryfall]`
- **Design** — Art by Magali Villeneuve, one of MTG's most prominent contemporary illustrators. The glowing dome enclosing a small group against barren terrain is a recurring Villeneuve compositional motif for protective magic. `[Scryfall]`

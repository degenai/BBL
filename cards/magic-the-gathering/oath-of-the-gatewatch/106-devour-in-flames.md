## Trivia

- **Flavor text / IP confirmation** — "Ob Nixilis had prepared for every eventuality—except Chandra Nalaar." `[Scryfall]` The card names Chandra directly, settling the IP question. This moment depicts her rescue of Gideon, Jace, and Nissa from Ob Nixilis on Zendikar — the encounter that catalyzed the formation of the Gatewatch. `[WebSearch: magic.wizards.com Oath of the Gatewatch story article]`
- **Set context** — Devour in Flames is a red uncommon from Oath of the Gatewatch (OGW, January 2016), the second set of the Battle for Zendikar block, 184-card expansion chronicling the Gatewatch's founding. `[Scryfall]`
- **Mechanical note** — The additional cost (return a land to hand) nets 5 damage for a net 3-mana investment on a sorcery. Legal in Pioneer, Modern, Legacy, Vintage, Commander. EDHREC rank 23986 — limited Commander presence. `[Scryfall]`
- **Design** — Art by Svetlin Velinov, Bulgarian fantasy illustrator with a broad MTG portfolio. His only OGW credit per Scryfall. `[Scryfall]`

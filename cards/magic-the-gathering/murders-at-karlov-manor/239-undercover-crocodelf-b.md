# Undercover Crocodelf (b) (Murders at Karlov Manor)

## Vision

![239-undercover-crocodelf-b](../../_images/magic-the-gathering/murders-at-karlov-manor/239-undercover-crocodelf-b.png)

A bulky, reptilian humanoid figure with clearly crocodilian features — scaled green-gray skin, a wide flat snout, clawed hands — crawls or leans forward through a shallow flooded surface or dense mist. The figure wears a wide-brimmed fedora hat and a loose suit jacket, both weathered and damp. A scaly crest or ridged tail is visible behind the figure. Tendrils of red energy or blood-red liquid swirl around the figure's feet or hands in the water. The overall palette is muted gray-greens and dark tones, with the red tendrils providing the only vivid color accent. The atmosphere is grim and hunted, as if the figure is stalking or navigating through difficult terrain undercover. The design is a literal 'crocodile-elf hybrid in a detective coat' — a playful but committed noir mashup creature.

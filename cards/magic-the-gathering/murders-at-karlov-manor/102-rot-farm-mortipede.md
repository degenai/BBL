Unlike prior Ravnica sets, the focus is on cross-guild forensic investigation rather than guild warfare. `[Scryfall]` `[magic.wizards.com: Planeswalker's Guide to Murders at Karlov Manor]`

The scare-quotes on 'recycling' operate as dark humor and institutional indictment simultaneously: Ravnica's forensic bureaucracy launders its unclaimed dead into the Golgari death-ecology pipeline as a routine administrative act. `[Scryfall]`
- **Design** — Creature type: Insect. The Golgari Swarm routinely employs insects and arthropods as recyclers and scavengers within their death-ecology hierarchy — insects being creatures that consume the dead and return nutrients to the soil, a direct analog to the guild's philosophical identity. `[Scryfall type_line]` `[MTG Wiki: Golgari Swarm]`
- **Mechanical** — The trigger fires when creature cards leave your graveyard — a narrower and technically specific condition than typical Golgari graveyard-enter triggers, rewarding sacrifice loops, reanimation, and exile-from-graveyard effects.

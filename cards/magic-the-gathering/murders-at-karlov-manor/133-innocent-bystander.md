## Trivia

- **Set context** — Innocent Bystander is a red 2/1 Goblin Citizen. The Scryfall type line confirms Goblin — the pointed ears in the art are consistent with Ravnica's visual language for Goblins, who can be narrow-featured. The Citizen subtype marks it as unaffiliated with any guild, appropriate for a bystander who wandered into the wrong alley `[Scryfall]`.
- **Flavor text** — "Whoops, this isn't my street! Don't mind me! I'll just be on my way while you enjoy your . . . very sharp knives." — Speaker unnamed `[Scryfall]`. The ellipsis and fragmented trailing clause perfectly convey someone talking themselves out of a mugging — the kind of Ravnica street comedy MKM deploys at common to leaven its noir tone.
- **Mechanical design** — The trigger (Investigate when dealt 3 or more damage) turns the creature into a pain-triggered Clue generator. It incentivizes letting attacks through: trading a 2/1 for a Clue token is often neutral-to-positive card economy. EDHREC rank 22898, consistent with Limited-first design `[Scryfall]`.
- **Set mechanics** — Investigate (create a Clue token: artifact with "2, Sacrifice: draw a card") is one of MKM's four featured mechanics alongside Disguise, Collect Evidence, and Suspect `[Scryfall, magic.wizards.com/en/news/feature/murders-at-karlov-manor-mechanics]`.

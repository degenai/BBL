## Trivia

- **Set context** — Fanatical Strength is a green common Instant (MKM, no. 159) from Murders at Karlov Manor (February 9, 2024). It grants a creature +3/+3 and trample until end of turn — a straightforward combat trick at common that fits green's color-pie role in the set `[Scryfall: mkm/159]`.
- **Flavor text and Kaya** — "What is this? An ambush or a staring contest?" Kaya taunted. As if in answer, the robed figure lunged forward. Kaya (Kaya Cassir) is a Planeswalker and ghost hunter recruited by Ezrim to lead the MKM murder investigation; her sardonic wit in the face of a cultist ambush is a character-defining moment in the set's narrative `[magic.wizards.com: The Legends and Characters of MKM]` `[Scryfall: flavor_text field, mkm/159]`. **Flag: Kaya character node candidate** — this card contains a Kaya flavor line and is a direct appears_on candidate for `cards/_characters/kaya.md`.
- **Printing error — showcase variant** — The MKM showcase version of Fanatical Strength (collector no. 310) was printed with an in-progress, unfinished version of the showcase artwork rather than the final art. Wizards issued an official correction notice and stated the error would be fixed in digital (MTG Arena) and subsequent tabletop print runs; early showcase copies (marked 310† on Scryfall) may carry a slight collector premium `[magic.wizards.com: Incorrect Version Printed for Fanatical Strength in MKM]`.
- **Two flavor texts in print** — The regular version (no. 159) carries the Kaya line. The showcase version (no. 310) carries an alternate flavor text attributed to Alquist Proft ("Clearly we are on the trail of the true killer. —Alquist Proft, case notes"), establishing that the showcase dossier reprints in MKM sometimes used different flavor copy than the base set versions `[Scryfall: mkm/310 via WebSearch snippet]`.

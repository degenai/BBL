## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Murders at Karlov Manor (MKM, released 2024-02-09) is a top-down murder-mystery set built on Ravnica. The Ravnican Agency of Magicological Investigations (R.A.M.I.) is a post-Phyrexian-invasion faction founded by the guildless archon Ezrim, who broke from the Azorius to investigate crimes free of guild bias. Crucially, Agency detectives are explicitly prohibited from making arrests — they investigate and report findings to the guilds. The Warden as an Agency-affiliated soldier sits on the enforcement side of that institutional line. `[Wizards.com: Planeswalker's Guide to Murders at Karlov Manor]` `[Scryfall]`

- **Design — disguise mechanic** — Disguise is MKM's evolution of Morph: cast face down for {3} as a 2/2 with ward 2, rather than a bare 2/2. The ward 2 addition addressed a longstanding Morph vulnerability to cheap removal. Wizards' State of Design 2024 acknowledged mixed reception: many players appreciated the upgrade, but some felt it was unnecessary and others grew tired of ward's prevalence in recent sets. Crowd-Control Warden is the only green-white identity disguise card in MKM, making it the dedicated flip-payoff anchor for the Selesnya disguise archetype. `[Wizards.com: State of Design 2024]` `[Scryfall: color_identity + keyword search]`

- **Mechanical reputation** — The ETB / face-up ability scales with board state: X +1/+1 counters where X equals the number of other creatures you control. In a go-wide GW Limited board, this regularly lands as a 6/6 or larger on flip. Untapped.gg tracked a 53.3% overall win rate and 57.0% in-hand win rate across 140,000+ MKM Arena games. EDHREC lists the Warden in approximately 1,359 Commander decks (rank 21,099). Pauper-legal status extends its reach into budget formats beyond Standard and Commander. `[Untapped.gg: Crowd-Control Warden, via WebSearch snippet]` `[EDHREC: Crowd-Control Warden page meta]` `[Scryfall: legalities]`

- **Design — artist** — Diego Gisbert Llorens is a Spanish freelance illustrator based in Berlin. As of 2026 he has illustrated approximately 58 Magic cards across Scryfall's catalog, from Commander Legends: Battle for Baldur's Gate through Edge of Eternities. Within MKM he painted two cards: Crowd-Control Warden and The Chase Is On (a story-spotlight Instant depicting Kaya pursuing Etrata). No Hipsters of the Coast artist interview covering his work on this commission was found. `[Scryfall: a:"Diego Gisbert" artist search]` `[WebSearch: Diego Gisbert Llorens MTG]`

- **Community sentiment** — MKM Limited reviews name Sumala Sentry and Crowd-Control Warden as the "bread and butter pairing" for the Selesnya disguise archetype, with GW described as one of MKM Draft's strongest color pairs. The Warden generates discussion almost exclusively as part of that archetype rather than as an individual talking point; no standalone Reddit thread for this card was found. `[Draftsim: Murders at Karlov Manor Limited Set Review, via WebSearch snippet]`

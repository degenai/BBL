## Trivia

- **Set context** — MKM's detective theme extends to its black-aligned cards with the 'unscrupulous' variant: investigators who bypass procedure to get results. The Ravnican Agency of Magicological Investigations (R.A.M.I.) exists as a cross-guild forensic authority, but black-aligned operatives represent the shadow side of investigation — extortion, evidence theft, coercion — the methods official channels refuse. `[magic.wizards.com: Planeswalker's Guide to Murders at Karlov Manor]`
- **Flavor text** — "Obtaining evidence through the 'proper' channels can take weeks of paperwork. Sometimes justice just can't wait." The scare-quotes on 'proper' mirror a pattern across MKM's black-aligned flavor texts — compare the Rot Farm Mortipede's 'recycling' quote from the same set — using quotation-mark irony to flag institutional bad faith as a recurring set theme. `[Scryfall]`
- **Design** — Type line: Creature — Elf Detective. 'Detective' is a creature subtype introduced in MKM to mechanically mark the set's investigation theme. Elf is a subtype spread across multiple Ravnica guilds, most prominently Golgari and Simic. `[Scryfall type_line]`
- **Mechanical** — The ETB effect exiles a card from an opponent's hand rather than discarding it — the card is removed from the game permanently, bypassing graveyard recursion. At two mana for a 1/1 with a one-sided permanent exile, it is a role-player in aggressive black strategies. EDHREC rank 20,243. `[Scryfall]`

## Trivia

- **Story Spotlight** — Final Parting carries Scryfall's `story_spotlight: true` flag and a planeswalker watermark, placing it in Dominaria's official story-arc card cycle. The depicted scene is canonical: Liliana lays her brother Josu to rest at Vess Manor, having accidentally cursed him with undeath centuries earlier while attempting to heal him. This moment is dramatized in "Return to Dominaria: Episode 2" (magic.wizards.com, 2018-03-22) `[Wizards of the Coast — Return to Dominaria: Episode 2]`.
- **Flavor text** — `"Sleep now, brother. That is the one gift I can give you." —Liliana Vess` `[Scryfall]`. The line is addressed to Josu Vess, her lich-lord brother whom Belzenlok had re-animated as a military asset. Josu's dying words blamed Liliana for the deaths of their family; the exchange is identified by Hipsters of the Coast as Liliana's narrative "rock bottom" — the first time she fully accepts accountability for the accident that originally sparked her planeswalker ignition `[Hipsters of the Coast — "Liliana's Demons", 2018-06]`.
- **Mechanical double-meaning** — The tutor mechanic (search for two cards, one to hand, one to graveyard) mirrors the narrative beat: Liliana uses the Chain Veil to free Josu (one gift delivered) while simultaneously depleting a resource she needed to fight Belzenlok (one cost incurred). Noted in the MTGSalvation Dominaria preview article as "a functional and flavor win" `[MTGSalvation — "Dominaria Preview: Final Parting"]`.
- **Artist** — Eric Deschamps illustrated this card `[Scryfall]`. Deschamps is a recurring Wizards collaborator known for planeswalker art across the Modern frame era; his other notable Dominaria-era contributions include several high-profile legendary creature cards.
- **Dominaria set context** — Dominaria (DOM, released 2018-04-27) was Wizards's return to Magic's origin plane after a multi-year absence, written by Martha Wells. The set centered on the Weatherlight crew assembling to defeat Demonlord Belzenlok and the reformed Cabal; Liliana's arc within DOM culminated in killing Belzenlok using the Blackblade, freeing her from three of four demonic contracts. The Josu confrontation precedes that climax `[Scryfall set data; Wizards of the Coast — "Return to Dominaria" story series]`.

## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Urgoros is a named Dominaria character, a Specter from the city of Vhelnish. When demonlord Belzenlok bestowed his dark "blessings" on his followers, Urgoros's body was siphoned into dementia space while his spirit fused into his armor; mounted on a nightmare-steed, he terrorized the skies of Urborg as a servant of Belzenlok. He served as the second defense against the Skyship Weatherlight's assault on the Stronghold, and vanished when Belzenlok was slain. `[MTG Wiki: mtg.wiki/page/Urgoros; Martha Wells, "Return to Dominaria: Episode 12," magic.wizards.com, 2018-05-30]`
- **Flavor text** — "As the phantom flies overhead, the city of Vhelnish screams in silence." Vhelnish is Urgoros's birthplace per MTG Wiki — the flavor text implies his former home falls silent under his aerial passage. `[Scryfall]`
- **Design** — Despite the demon-armored visual, Urgoros is typed Legendary Creature — Specter, not Demon. The Specter creature type goes back to Tempest block. His discard-on-hit ability upgrades the classic Hypnotic Specter template with a consolation draw clause that fires when the damaged player has an empty hand. Artist Daarken illustrated both the base Dominaria printing and the prerelease foil promo — the only two printings of this card. `[Scryfall type_line, oracle_text, prints data]`
- **Mechanical reputation** — At six mana for a 4/3 flyer, Urgoros is a casual Commander inclusion rather than a competitive staple. EDHREC rank 18,342 indicates modest play in discard and Specter tribal builds where the empty-hand draw clause distinguishes it from cheaper alternatives. `[Scryfall edhrec_rank]`
- **Set context** — The Dominaria set (April 27, 2018) returned MTG to its home plane after nearly a decade's absence; the 12-episode "Return to Dominaria" story by Martha Wells centered on Liliana Vess and Gideon Jura hunting Belzenlok, the last of Liliana's four contracted demons, who had seized the Cabal in Urborg. Every Cabal-adjacent card in the set — including Urgoros — exists as a fixture of Belzenlok's dominion over that region. `[magic.wizards.com: "Return to Dominaria: Episode 1," 2018-03-21; MTG Wiki: Belzenlok, via WebSearch]`

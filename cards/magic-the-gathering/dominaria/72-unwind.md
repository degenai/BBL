## Trivia

- **Mechanical callback** — Unwind is a direct descendant of Rewind (Urza's Saga, 1998): both are blue instants that counter a spell and untap lands (Rewind untaps four, Unwind untaps up to three; Rewind counters any spell, Unwind counters only noncreature spells). The narrowing reflects a deliberate modern-era design philosophy of making hard counters more conditional. Noted in the MTG Color Pie Dominaria preview article: "Unwind is a design oddity from the rest of Dominaria; this is a callback to a card, not a story" `[MTG Color Pie — "Dominaria Preview: Unwind", 2018-04-05]`.
- **Flavor text** — `"A problem is only a problem if you don't have the tools to correct it." —Jhoira` `[Scryfall]`. The speaker is Jhoira of the Ghitu, a nearly immortal Shivan artificer and Weatherlight Captain during the Dominaria arc. Jhoira salvaged the wrecked Weatherlight from off the coast of Urborg, rebuilt it using Thran metal and a weatherseed, and assembled the new crew that fought the Cabal — the "tools" in the quote are literal (her artificer craft) and figurative (the refitted Weatherlight itself) `[Wizards of the Coast — Return to Dominaria story series; Scryfall set data]`.
- **Format presence** — Scryfall EDHREC rank of 1056 makes this one of the more-played Dominaria commons in the format `[Scryfall EDHREC rank field]`. Its land-untap effect makes it functionally mana-neutral in three-land mana bases, giving blue control decks a pseudo-free counterspell. Pauper-legal `[Scryfall legalities]`.
- **Jhoira's DOM role** — Jhoira (no last name; Jhoira of the Ghitu is an earlier named printing) was the central non-planeswalker protagonist of the Dominaria storyline, responsible for rebuilding the Weatherlight and recruiting its crew (including Teferi, Gideon, Liliana, Chandra, Ajani, and others). Her legendary card in the set, Jhoira, Weatherlight Captain (DOM-197), depicts the same character and keys off historic spells, encoding her artificer-and-scholar identity `[Wizards of the Coast — Return to Dominaria story series; Scryfall DOM-197]`.

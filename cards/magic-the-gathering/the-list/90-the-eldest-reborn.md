## Trivia

- **Set context** — The Eldest Reborn was originally printed in Dominaria (DOM, no. 90, 2018), an Uncommon Saga enchantment. In this corpus it appears as a Mystery Booster / The List insert (PLST code DOM-90), reflecting Dominaria's status as a set regularly drafted into The List. The card's name is a direct reference to Nicol Bolas — canonically the eldest of Magic's original Elder Dragon cycle (Chromium, Arcades Sabboth, Palladia-Mors, Nicol Bolas, Vaevictis Asmadi) and the primary antagonist across the Dominaria–War of the Spark arc [card frontmatter the_list_source_set: DOM; Scryfall DOM-90; Draftsim: "All 32 Elder Dragons Ranked" via WebSearch snippet].
- **Design** — The card's three saga chapters mechanically echo Bolas's narrative arc in Dominaria: Chapter I (opponent sacrifices a creature or planeswalker) = Bolas destroying or corrupting allies; Chapter II (opponent discards) = psychic assault and information extraction; Chapter III (put a creature or planeswalker from a graveyard onto the battlefield) = the actual rebirth, placing Bolas back into the world. The chapter structure as lore storyboarding is a Saga design principle Wizards used throughout the Dominaria set [MTG Assist: The Eldest Reborn card page; WebSearch snippet].
- **EDHREC** — In Nicol Bolas, the Ravager Commander builds, The Eldest Reborn appears in approximately 52% of decks (out of ~8,990 total), with 50% synergy score. In Bolas Planeswalker-focused builds it appears in 63% of decks. This is one of the highest rates for any non-marquee Dominaria uncommon — driven entirely by flavor: "Bolas in your Bolas deck" is a design choice most Bolas pilots treat as mandatory [EDHREC: Nicol Bolas, the Ravager commander page via WebSearch snippet].
- **Artist** — Illustrated by Ravenna Tran (credited on Scryfall as "Ravenna Tran," also known as "Jenn Ravenna Tran" or "Jenn Ravenna"). This card is one of the anchor prints for the `cards/_artists/jenn-ravenna-tran.md` artist node in the corpus. The composition — a skeletal figure rising from a pit in murky greens and sickly yellows — is representative of Tran's dark-fantasy range [card frontmatter artist field; cards/_artists/jenn-ravenna-tran.md].
- **No flavor text** — This printing carries no flavor text; the three chapter abilities fill the text box entirely [card frontmatter; Scryfall DOM-90].

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Planeswalker-deck-exclusive printing of Oko from Throne of Eldraine (ELD-309), reprinted on The List (PLST) on 2023-09-08 alongside the Wilds of Eldraine launch. The List is Wizards' rotating reprint-in-boosters pool — cards from it are legal in whatever formats they were already legal in. `[Scryfall: plst/ELD-309]` `[magic.wizards.com: What's New on The List for Wilds of Eldraine, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Design** — Art by **Chris Rallis**, a Greek-born fantasy illustrator based in England who has worked almost exclusively for Wizards of the Coast since 2013 (~158 MTG cards, including Liliana, Death's Majesty and Dragon Whisperer). `[rallisart.com/about, via WebSearch snippet]` `[about.dragonshield.com: Chris Rallis artist interview, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Mechanical reputation — not the banned one** — This is the **weaker** Oko: six mana ({4}{G}{U}), 4 loyalty, +1/+1 counters / copy-a-creature / -7 give-your-team-trample-10/10. Its sibling card **Oko, Thief of Crowns** (ELD-197) costs three mana, does roughly the same job a turn earlier with Food tokens and Elk-conversion, and got **banned in Standard (2019-11-18), Brawl, Pioneer, and Modern** within months for warping every format it touched. That ban history belongs to ELD-197, not this card — but the character notoriety carries. `[Scryfall: both Oko prints]` `[magic.wizards.com: November 18, 2019 Banned and Restricted Announcement, via WebSearch snippet]`
- **Mechanical** — EDHREC rank 16704 — essentially unplayed in Commander, which is the expected ceiling for planeswalker-deck-exclusive precon stat sticks. The List reprint trades near USD 0.90. `[Scryfall: edhrec_rank + prices fields]`
- **Flavor-text context** — Card has no flavor text on either print. `[Scryfall: flavor_text field absent]`
- **Lore** — Oko is a shapeshifter fey planeswalker originally from Lorwyn-Shadowmoor; his Shadowmoor aspect grows antlers, which is the visual cue this art leans on (the antler-crown is canonical, not a one-off art choice). In the Throne of Eldraine arc he met the hedgewitch Alyse and fathered the half-fae Kellan. Charismatic, vain, notorious trickster. `[MTG Wiki: Oko, via WebSearch snippet — direct fetch blocked by Cloudflare]`

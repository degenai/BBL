# Athreos, God of Passage (Mystery Booster Cards)

## Vision

![146-athreos-god-of-passage](../../_images/magic-the-gathering/the-list/146-athreos-god-of-passage.png)

A tall, gaunt skeletal figure in tattered, flowing dark robes stands at center of an ethereal greenish-yellow miasma. The figure's face is a bare skull, partially shadowed beneath a hood. Its bony hand extends outward, dangling or proffering a small bag or coin pouch — the ferryman's toll. Wisps of pale spectral matter and tendrils of green-gold smoke swirl around the figure, suggesting souls in transit or the threshold between worlds. The background dissolves into glowing yellow-green light streaks, giving the impression of a passage or veil between life and death. The palette is dominated by sickly chartreuse, ochre, and deep shadow tones, evoking a Greek-myth-meets-underworld aesthetic.

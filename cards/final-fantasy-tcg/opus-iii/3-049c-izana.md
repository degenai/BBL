## Trivia

- **Set context** — Izana (3-049C) was released in Opus III (July 21, 2017), the FFTCG expansion that introduced Final Fantasy Type-0 as a featured category alongside FF IX and FF XIV. A dedicated Final Fantasy TYPE-0 Starter Set (Lightning/Wind, 50 cards) launched simultaneously. [Square Enix FFTCG Opus III page, via WebSearch snippet]

- **Lore context** — Izana Kunagiri is Machina Kunagiri's elder brother (four years older) and a Rubrum Dominion Legionary who served as keeper of the Peristylium chocobo pens and a close friend of Ace. His card ability — searching the deck for a Chocobo card on entering the field — directly encodes his canonical role: he rides his chocobo Chichiri to deliver a COMM to the cadets, succeeds, then perishes from wounds; Chichiri refuses to leave his body. [Final Fantasy Wiki: Izana Kunagiri, via WebSearch snippet]

- **World-mechanic hook** — Final Fantasy Type-0's Crystal of Orience erases all memory of the dead from the living. After Izana's death, Machina loses every memory of his brother — and his resentment toward Class Zero is grief he can no longer consciously name. Izana's FFTCG card is one of the few material traces of a character who, by the game's design, cannot be remembered in-world. [Final Fantasy Wiki: Final Fantasy Type-0 story, via WebSearch snippet]

- **Mechanical design** — Wind element Forward, 2 CP, 5000 Power, Job: Dominion Legionary, Category: Type-0. On-entry ability tutors any card with Job: Chocobo or Card Name: Chocobo from the deck to hand — a mechanically tight encoding of his chocobo-keeper identity that also functions as ramp in Type-0 Wind builds. [fftcgmognet.com card listing 3-049C, via WebSearch snippet; retailer card data, via WebSearch snippet]

## Trivia

- **Character canon** — Artemicion is the named moogle director of Mognet Central in Final Fantasy IX — the hidden hub of the moogle postal service (Mognet) that Zidane and party discover as part of the game's optional side quest. Artemicion is canonically identified by his striped purple fur, a unique visual trait among FFIX moogles. His small crisis: he used Mognet Central's entire Superslick supply on his own fur, causing the mail-delivery machine to break down; the player must obtain Superslick from Ruby in Alexandria to repair it. The sidequest resolves the Mognet mail chain that begins early in the game. [Final Fantasy Wiki: Artemicion; Final Fantasy Wiki: Mognet Central, via WebSearch snippet]
- **IP verification** — Card category field "IX" (Final Fantasy IX) combined with job "Moogle" and the figure's canonical striped purple fur confirm this is Artemicion specifically, not a generic FFIX moogle. Standard FFIX moogles have white fur; Artemicion's purple stripes are his canonical visual identifier referenced by the Final Fantasy Wiki. [Final Fantasy Wiki: Artemicion; TheRealmOfAdventure product listing]
- **Card profile** — 3-122C is a Water Backup card, cost 2, job Moogle, category IX. Ability: "When Artemicion enters the field, place any number of cards from your hand at the bottom of your deck in any order. Then draw the same number of cards." — a hand-filtering effect that allows selective hand sculpting without card disadvantage, valued in Water decks for digging to key cards. [WebSearch snippet via TCGplayer; TheRealmOfAdventure product listing]
- **Set context** — Opus III (released 2017) is the third FFTCG booster set, known for expanding representation of Final Fantasy IX, VI, and VII. Artemicion in Opus III is one of the early FFTCG appearances of a named minor FFIX NPC at the Backup-card level, reflecting the game's design approach of including named characters from optional sidequests rather than only main-cast entries. [TradingCardSets.com Opus III guide; Final Fantasy Card Game Wikia: Opus III Collection]

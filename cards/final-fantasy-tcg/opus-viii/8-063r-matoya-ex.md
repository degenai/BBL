## Trivia

- **Set context** — Matoya EX (8-063R) is a Wind-element Backup card (Job: Dancer, Category: FFL, Cost: 4) from Opus VIII. Her EX BURST ability reads: when Matoya enters the field, choose 1 Monster opponent controls and Break it. Opus VIII featured exclusive illustrations by Kira Oguro for Final Fantasy Legends characters, placing this card within a deliberate artist spotlight on the FFL/Dimensions roster. `[ArcadeGameCards listing; Square Enix Opus VIII release page]`
- **Character identity** — The Matoya on this card is from Final Fantasy Dimensions (released in Japan as Final Fantasy Legends II), not the original Final Fantasy I witch. The Dimensions version is the fourth woman in her family to bear the name Matoya — a dancer who appears young but is over 100 years old. She leads a rebel group called Desert Moon against the empire of Avalon and conceals her Crystal Eye ability until a critical moment against the general Asmodai. Her flower-tiara and coin-chain dancer attire are her canonical signature visual identifiers. `[Final Fantasy Wiki: Matoya (Dimensions)]`
- **Name lineage** — The name "Matoya" traces back to the original Final Fantasy I, where Matoya is an elderly blind witch whose Crystal Eye was stolen by the dark elf Astos. The Dimensions version deliberately echoes and inverts this: a young-looking dancer, also linked to a Crystal Eye, across four generations of women sharing the name. Both Matoya variants appear across the FFTCG card pool under different printings. `[Final Fantasy Wiki: Matoya (Final Fantasy)]`
- **Mechanical note** — A 4-cost Wind Backup with on-entry Monster removal fills a specific niche: Wind-element FFTCG decks (heavy on Summons and Forwards) rarely access direct destruction effects, making Matoya EX's trigger a meaningful utility option in the Backup slot. `[WebSearch snippets: TCG Republic, ArcadeGameCards]`

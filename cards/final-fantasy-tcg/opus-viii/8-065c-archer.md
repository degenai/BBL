## Trivia

- **Design** — The Archer's Opus VIII art was created by Ryoma Ito, original character designer for Final Fantasy Tactics Advance and FFTA2: Grimoire of the Rift. Ito was the only Illustration Showcase artist to have designed the source game's characters himself. In the FFTCG Illustration Showcase Interview no. 2, he noted that producer Matsuyama encouraged him to deviate significantly from the original FFTA designs "from the next batch onward" — meaning some Opus VIII FFTA cards sit right at the boundary between faithful reproductions and Ito's revised direction `[FFTCG Illustration Showcase Interview no. 2: Ryoma Ito (na.finalfantasy.com/topics/35)]`
- **Mechanics** — Archer (8-065C) is a Wind Backup. Paying (Green)(Green)(Dull) and sacrificing it to the Break Zone deals 10000 damage to one Forward — enough to eliminate most Common and many Rare Forwards. A significant targeted damage tool for a Common-rarity card `[TCGplayer Opus VIII listings; community via WebSearch]`
- **Set context** — Opus VIII was the first FFTCG set to feature Final Fantasy Tactics Advance content, with Ryoma Ito providing exclusive original art. The set's FFTA contingent includes job-class Backup and Forward cards spanning Hume, Viera, and Moogle job classes `[Square Enix FFTCG Opus VIII release page; Final Fantasy Card Game Wikia: Opus VIII Collection]`
- **Lore** — In FFTA, the Archer is a foundational Hume job class that serves as the unlock path to the advanced Sniper job. FFTA's job system ties ability learning to equipped gear — an Archer must learn weapon-skill abilities before the Sniper promotion becomes available `[Final Fantasy Card Game Wikia: Opus VIII Collection]`

## Trivia

- **Card identity confirmed by oracle text** — Marlene EX (8-085C) is an Earth-element Backup from Opus VIII, Category: FFVII. Her EX BURST ability reads: when Marlene enters the field, choose 1 card named Aerith, or Cloud, or Tifa, or Barret in your Break Zone and add it to your hand. The ability names all four central FFVII party members, directly encoding the game's core cast structure. `[WebSearch snippets: totalcards.net, Card Cavern listing]`
- **Character background** — In Final Fantasy VII, Marlene Wallace is the biological daughter of Dyne and Eleanor, orphaned as an infant when Shinra burned Corel and killed her mother. She was adopted by Barret Wallace, her father Dyne's closest friend and the only other survivor of the massacre. In the original 1997 game she is approximately 4 years old and lives with Tifa at 7th Heaven bar in Midgar. Her existence is the human cost Barret's anti-Shinra motivation is built around. `[Final Fantasy Wiki: Marlene Wallace]`
- **Mechanical role** — As a Common Earth Backup, Marlene EX functions as a low-floor utility recovery piece in FFVII-themed Earth builds. The four named EX BURST targets — Aerith, Cloud, Tifa, Barret — appear across many Opus sets, meaning her trigger fires reliably in dedicated FFVII builds regardless of which set version of those cards is in play. `[WebSearch snippets: TCGplayer, Card Cavern community]`

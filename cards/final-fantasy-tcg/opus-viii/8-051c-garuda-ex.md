## Trivia

- **Set context** — Garuda EX (8-051C) is a Wind-element Summon from FFTCG Opus VIII, released March 22, 2019. Opus VIII contains 148 cards across 60 Common, 36 Rare, 26 Hero, and 14 Legend rarities, with heavy FFXIV and FFXI representation including multiple Primal summons. `[Square Enix FFTCG Opus VIII release page]`
- **Lore history** — Garuda first appeared in the Final Fantasy series as a named enemy in Final Fantasy III. She was elevated to Wind-elemental summon status in Final Fantasy XI — the first FF MMO — because no established Wind summon existed at that time. Her FFXI design as a pale winged humanoid with avian features became the visual template carried forward into FFXIV, XV, and XVI. `[CBR: Every Garuda Appearance In A Mainline Final Fantasy; Game Rant]`
- **FFXIV identity** — In Final Fantasy XIV (A Realm Reborn), Garuda carries the title "Lady of the Vortex" and serves as a Primal worshipped by the Ixal beast tribe. She is distinguished among ARR Primals by her intense hatred of "landwalkers," making her one of the most dangerous antagonist Primals in the expansion. `[Final Fantasy Wiki: Garuda (Final Fantasy XIV)]`
- **Mechanical note** — The "EX" designation in FFTCG indicates an EX BURST trigger: the card's ability fires when flipped face-up from the Break Zone during damage resolution. This gives Common-rarity summons like Garuda EX a competitive dimension beyond their base summon effect, as EX BURST triggers are unpreventable. `[Square Enix FFTCG official FAQ]`

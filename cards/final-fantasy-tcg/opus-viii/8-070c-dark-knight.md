## Trivia

- **Set context** — Dark Knight (8-070C) is an Earth-element Backup card from Opus VIII, Category: FFXIV. In Final Fantasy XIV, the Dark Knight was introduced as a tank job in the first expansion, Heavensward (3.0, released June 2015), alongside Machinist and Astrologian. It is the first new tank job added to the game and requires completing the base A Realm Reborn main scenario to unlock. `[Final Fantasy Wiki: Dark Knight (FFXIV); FFXIV official job guide]`
- **Lore identity** — In FFXIV, Dark Knights are bearers of "the Blackblood" — they channel inner pain and darkness as a weapon wielded in defense of the common people, specifically Ishgard's citizenry. Unlike traditional knights serving nobility, Dark Knights operate as social outcasts outside official hierarchies, making them one of the game's most narratively celebrated jobs. `[FFXIV consolegameswiki; FFXIV official job guide]`
- **FFTCG print history** — Dark Knight is one of the most consistently reprinted FFXIV job class cards in FFTCG, with Common printings across Opus I (1-092C), Opus II (2-073C), Opus IV (4-074C), Opus VI (6-065C), and this Opus VIII printing. The job's repeated inclusion as a Common reflects its utility as a foundational Earth/Dark backup piece across the game's lifespan. `[FFTCG community forums: fftcg.boards.net Dark Knight Opus VI thread]`

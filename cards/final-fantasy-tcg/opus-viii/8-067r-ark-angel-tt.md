## Trivia

- **Set context** — Ark Angel TT appears in FFTCG Opus VIII (released March 2019), a set that drew substantially from Final Fantasy XI alongside FFXV and Crystal Chronicles. The card is classified as a Forward affiliated with the FFXI game grouping. [Final Fantasy Portal Site: Opus VIII release news, via WebSearch snippet]
- **Lore** — Ark Angel TT is a Notorious Monster from the Rise of the Zilart expansion (2002) — the Tarutaru-form Crystal Warrior, one of five beings forged by the ancient Zilart race from the essence of Vana'diel's playable races. Each embodies a racial sin; TT's is cowardice. Its spoken curse: "You shall be consumed by the cowardice that binds you..." [Final Fantasy Wiki: Crystal Warriors, via WebSearch snippet; ZAM FFXI Bestiary: Ark Angel TT, via WebSearch snippet]
- **Design** — In the source game, Ark Angel TT can use both Black Mage and Dark Knight job abilities simultaneously (Manafont and Blood Weapon), and its unique weapon skill Amon Drive inflicts area-of-effect Paralysis, Petrification, and Poison. The scythe depicted on the FFTCG card aligns with its Dark Knight job classification. [ZAM FFXI Bestiary: Ark Angel TT, via WebSearch snippet]
- **Mechanical context** — Players could fight each Ark Angel individually for Zilart Mission 14, or face all five simultaneously in the harder optional fight Divine Might — one of FFXI's most notorious endgame challenges. [WebSearch snippet: Ark Angels / Divine Might summary]

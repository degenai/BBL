## Trivia

- **Set / lore context** — Ark Angel TT is a Notorious Monster boss from Final Fantasy XI: Rise of the Zilart (2002 Japan / 2003 NA), encountered in Zilart Mission 14 "Ark Angels." It is one of five Crystal Warriors, each embodying a negative racial trait of FFXI's five playable races. The "TT" suffix designates the Tarutaru archetype — and Ark Angel TT specifically embodies Tarutaru cowardice. Players who defeat it receive a Shard of Cowardice [Final Fantasy Wiki: Ark Angel TT, via WebSearch snippet; FFXIclopedia: Ark Angel TT, via WebSearch snippet].
- **Design / mechanics** — In FFXI, Ark Angel TT is a hybrid Black Mage / Dark Knight fighter. It can use both Manafont (BLM job ability) and Blood Weapon (DRK job ability), casts every spell from both jobs up to level 75, and teleports around the arena. Its unique weapon skill Amon Drive deals area-of-effect damage and can inflict Paralysis, Petrification, and Poison simultaneously. When Blood Weapon activates, it shifts to melee and can exit the combat ring — a notable behavioral quirk players had to plan around [Final Fantasy Wiki: Ark Angel TT, via WebSearch snippet].
- **Community resonance** — The five Ark Angels can be fought simultaneously in the optional quest "Divine Might," which was considered one of FFXI's most prestigious endgame challenges at original release. Defeating all five at once was a high-prestige marker in the FFXI community during the 2003–2006 era [Coldrun Gaming: FFXI Part 36 Ark Angels, via WebSearch snippet].
- **Card context** — This copy is the foil Rare variant (8-067R), classifying Ark Angel TT as a Forward-type card in the FFTCG system — appropriate for an active combat boss rather than a support entity [card frontmatter].

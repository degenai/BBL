## Trivia

- **IP verified: Vivi Ornitier** — Vivi is the black mage protagonist of *Final Fantasy IX* (PlayStation, 2000). His defining visual identifiers — an oversized drooping pointed hat, glowing yellow/orange eye-circles beneath the brim, and layered robes — are consistent across all canonical representations and are present in this card's art. Unlike standard black mages in FF IX, Vivi's hat flops down his back rather than pointing upright — a design detail that marks him as prototype-different from the manufactured Black Mage soldiers. `[Wikipedia: Vivi Ornitier]` `[Final Fantasy Wiki: Vivi Ornitier]`
- **Set context** — Opus VIII was released in 2019, the eighth main FFTCG expansion. The set is subtitled "Warrior of Light" in some markets. This card is rarity Hero (H), one tier below Legend — the FFTCG rarity tier for significant named characters. Cost 1, Fire element, Power 2000, Black Mage job, Category IX. `[WebSearch: TCGPlayer Opus VIII Vivi]`
- **Mechanics** — When Vivi enters the field, choose 1 opposing Forward and deal it 2000 damage. If you control 4 or more Category IX Characters, deal 7000 damage instead. If you control 7 or more Category IX Characters, deal 10000 damage instead. A scalable enter-the-battlefield damage effect that rewards building a full Final Fantasy IX party — functionally incentivizing the full-cast FF IX deck archetype. At max threshold (7+ IX Characters), a 1-cost card deals 10000 damage on entry. `[WebSearch: Final Fantasy TCG Opus VIII card text]`
- **Art style** — The vision pass identified the art as highly stylized and painterly, consistent with Yoshitaka Amano's illustration register. Amano has provided art for multiple FFTCG cards across the Opus series, particularly for Legend and Hero-tier cards. His abstract brushwork style — spindly figures, trailing ribbon-elements, pale textured washes — is a strong stylistic match to this card's description, though artist attribution for 8-016H has not been independently confirmed in available sources; flag for verification against the physical card credit. `[WebSearch: Yoshitaka Amano FFTCG]`
- **Character arc note** — Vivi's existential arc in FF IX centers on discovering he is a manufactured being with a fixed lifespan — a prototype Black Mage built to outlast others, who develops genuine emotions and moral reasoning where the other mages cannot. He is widely regarded as one of the most emotionally resonant characters in the franchise. His 1-cost FFTCG card price ($3.91 market) reflects continued demand driven by community attachment to the character, not mechanical rarity. `[Wikipedia: Vivi Ornitier]`

## Trivia

- **Set context** — Opus VIII (March 22, 2019) was the first FFTCG set to feature Final Fantasy Brave Exvius characters in substantial numbers. Square Enix's press release specifically named Lasswell as one of the set's featured characters; FFBE had been teased in Opus VII and Opus VIII delivered the full expansion of the mobile RPG into FFTCG form. `[press.na.square-enix.com: Opus VIII announcement; na.finalfantasy.com/news/1043]`
- **Mechanical design** — Lasswell is a 4-cost Ice/Fire element Forward (8,000 Power, Knight — FFBE). On entering the field or attacking, he chooses one Forward and dulls and freezes it if it received damage that turn — a targeted control lock. His active "Azure Sky" ability deals 8,000 damage to one Forward and dulls and freezes another simultaneously, making him a Must Kill threat rated 4.5/5 in the Opus VIII limited format. `[FFTCG Crystarium: How to Win Limited Opus VIII Review]`
- **Lore** — Lasswell is Rain's childhood friend and adopted brother in FFBE, raised by Sir Raegen (Rain's father) after Lasswell's parents were killed. He wields the sword Purple Lightning — one of the three objects in the FFBE game logo alongside Rain's sword and Fina herself. His inferiority complex toward Rain's natural talent coexists with an unshakeable loyalty, making their bond a structural emotional axis of the game's narrative. `[Final Fantasy Wiki: Lasswell (Brave Exvius)]`
- **Trio debut** — Opus VIII includes Rain (8-021R Rare), Lasswell (8-043H Hero), and Fina (8-060L Legend plus 8-061C Common) — the canonical FFBE lead trio all landing in the same set as a deliberate Square Enix product decision to introduce the title with its full opening-arc party. `[na.finalfantasy.com/news/1043; corpus: 8-021r-rain-rare.md]`

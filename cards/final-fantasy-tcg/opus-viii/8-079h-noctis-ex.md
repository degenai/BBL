## Trivia

- **Set context** — Opus VIII (March 22, 2019) introduced Final Fantasy XV characters to the FFTCG for the first time. Square Enix's press release named Noctis as one of the set's featured characters; companion card Ignis EX (8-072R) is in the same set, and the FFXV party debuted in FFTCG form here. `[press.na.square-enix.com: Opus VIII announcement; na.finalfantasy.com/news/1043]`
- **Mechanical design** — 8-079H is a 2-cost Earth element Forward (5,000 Power, Warrior of Light — Category XV) with an EX Burst: on entering the field, reveal the top 2 cards of the deck, add 1 Category XV card to hand, return the rest to the bottom. He also has Warp-Strike (S Ea): choose 1 Monster, break it. The EX Burst is a functional Category XV tutor off the Damage Zone. In Opus VIII limited, the thin XV card pool made this largely unplayable — rated 0.5/5 by FFTCG Crystarium — but the card's design premises a constructed deck built around the XV category. `[FFTCG Crystarium: How to Win Limited Opus VIII Review; WebSearch: 8-079H Noctis card text]`
- **Lore** — Noctis Lucis Caelum is the crown prince and sole playable protagonist of Final Fantasy XV (2016). The Crystal of Lucis chose him at age 5 as the "True King" — a prophesied figure who would purge the world of the Starscourge at the cost of his own life. His canonical combat signature is the Warp-Strike (teleporting to a thrown weapon), directly encoded in this card's ability. The Ring of the Lucii, the family heirloom containing the power of ancestral Lucian kings, is the symbol of the sacrificial kingship that defines his arc. `[Final Fantasy Wiki: Noctis Lucis Caelum; Wikipedia: Characters of Final Fantasy XV]`
- **Naming note** — "Noctis EX" is the official printed card name, not a rarity marker. TCGplayer lists both an Opus VII "Noctis EX" and an Opus VIII "Noctis EX" as distinct cards, indicating this name was applied across multiple sets. Noctis also received a Card of the Week spotlight for Opus XII, signaling ongoing Square Enix investment in the character across the FFTCG card line. `[TCGplayer: Noctis EX Opus VII; TCGplayer: Noctis EX Opus VIII; fftcg.square-enix-games.com: Opus XII Card of the Week]`

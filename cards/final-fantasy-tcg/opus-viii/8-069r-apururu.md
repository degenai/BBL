## Trivia

- **Set context** — Opus VIII (released March 22, 2019) is a 149-card set that marked the milestone of 180 million FFTCG cards shipped worldwide. The set headlined Final Fantasy XV and Brave Exvius; FFXI characters like Apururu appeared as supporting inclusions alongside FFXIV: Stormblood and Crystal Chronicles entries. `[Square Enix press release, March 22 2019]`
- **Character lore** — Apururu is a Tarutaru minister of the Federation of Windurst's Monastery in FFXI, responsible for maintaining Windurst's Cardian (animated playing-card soldier) forces. She is daughter of Cardian inventor Zonpa-Zippa and sister of the rogue Orastery minister Ajido-Marujido. Her tenure was marked by persistent budget shortfalls that forced unconventional financial schemes to keep the ministry operational — a running tension in Windurst's storyline. `[Final Fantasy Wiki (Fandom): Apururu; FFXIclopedia: Apururu]`
- **Mechanical design** — Apururu is an Earth-element Backup whose casting cost decreases by 1 for each Backup already in play, making her effectively free on a developed board. On entry, she retrieves any 1 Character from the Break Zone to hand. FFTCG Crystarium rates her a 3 in All-Star Draft limited play and notes "once you get her cost down to 3 or even 2, she is truly an incredible Backup," enabling splash strategies by recovering off-element cards discarded early for Crystal Points. `[FFTCG Crystarium: "How to Win Limited: Backups"; card-effect text via TCGPlayer listing]`
- **In-game trust role** — As an FFXI Trust NPC, Apururu (UC) is widely regarded by the community as the strongest healing Trust in the game — a WHM/RDM with access to "Nott," a unique MP-restoration ability that gives her the highest MP longevity of any White Mage Trust. Her FFTCG card type (Mage/Backup rather than Forward) directly reflects this support NPC status. `[FFXIclopedia: Trust: Apururu (UC); Twinfinite: FFXI Best Trusts guide]`
